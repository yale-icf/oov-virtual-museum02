# Transcription

**Title (top right):**
Water Engine

**Text (bottom of card):**
Come all ye Cully, my Water Engine buy,
to Pump your flooded Mines, and Cole Pit dry;
Some Projects are all Wind, but ours is Water,
And tho at present low, may rise hereafter.

**Notable markings:**
- Four red heart symbols in the upper left corner (indicating this is the Four of Hearts from the playing card deck)
- Engraved illustration showing two men operating a water pumping engine near a body of water, with buildings and landscape in the background
- Border line around the entire card
- Aged, worn paper with visible creasing and discoloration consistent with historical artifact
