## Notable Markings:

- **Soviet State Emblem:** Red hammer and sickle within a wreath (upper left)
- **Red Star:** Decorative element at top center
- **Serial Numbers:** No. 055726 (appears multiple times - top right, bottom left)
- **Series Designation:** Series 23 (appears twice)
- **Class:** 150 (appears twice, bottom)
- **Decorative Elements:** Industrial imagery including factory, hydroelectric dam, combine harvester, ornate border designs
- **Color Scheme:** Red and brown/sepia tones on cream background
- **Year:** 1948 (center bottom)
