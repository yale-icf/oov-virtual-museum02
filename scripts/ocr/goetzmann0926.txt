# Transcription

**Connecticut Land Company, fs.**

HARTFORD, Sept. 5th, 1795.

THIS CERTIFIES, that Calvin Austin of gla[s]field, in the County of Hartford, Estate of Connecticut—
is entitled to the Trust and Benefit of Three Thousand—

Twelve Hundred Thousandths of the Connecticut Western
Reserve, fs called, as held by John Caldwell, Jonathan
Brace, and John Morgan, Trustees in a Deed of Trust, dated
the Sixtieth Day of September One Thousand Seven Hundred and
Ninety Five, to hold said Proportion or Share to exist the said
Calvin Austin—
to his Heirs and Assigns, according to the Terms, Conditions, Co-
venants and Exceptions contained in the said Deed of Trust, and
in certain Articles of Agreement entered into by the Persons com-
posing the Connecticut Land Company; which said Share is
transferrable by Assignment, under Hand and Seal, witnessed by
two Witnesses, and acknowledged before any Justice of the Peace
in the State of Connecticut, or before a Notary Public, or a
Judge of the Common Pleas in any of the United States, and to
be recorded by the Clerk of the Board of Directors.
