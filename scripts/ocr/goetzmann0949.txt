# Transcription: Kousanie Tea Company, Limited Share Certificate

## Document Text:

**KOUSANIE TEA COMPANY,** "LIMITED"

Registered under Act XIX. of 1857 of the Legislative Council of India.

CAPITAL RUPEES 3,45,000, DIVIDED INTO 3,450 SHARES OF 100 EACH.

No. [illegible]

This is to Certify that

**W. Campbell & Co.**

of **Jature**

is at the date hereof a Registered Shareholder of the

**Kousanie Tea Company, Limited** in respect of one SHARE numbered **[One Thousand Eight Hundred and] Seven**

and that the said **W. Campbell & Co.** is entitled as a

PROPRIETOR thereof to all the benefits, advantages, powers, and privileges attending the same, and will so continue until Transfer or assignment made and registered according to the Deed of Settlement of the said Company.

**K. M. Clarter** — Manager.

**Z. M. Ruffels** — Managing Director.

Kousanie, **23d April 1874**

---

## Notable Markings:

- **One Anna stamp** (purple) affixed to upper right corner
- **Ornamental decorative border** on left side (company seal/emblem)
- **Handwritten entries** filling in certificate details
- **Two signatures** at bottom (Manager and Managing Director)
- **Share number:** 1807
- **Decorative typography** throughout with ornamental flourishes
