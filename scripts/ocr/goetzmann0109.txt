# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Ai my oyt smak

Door't breken van de koord ben ik
bedroefsd gevallen, dog't is mijn loon; ik moeft
met de truckflok niet mallen.

**English translation:**

Ai my taste/flavor

By breaking of the cord I have
fallen into sorrow, but it is my reward; I must
not play/trifle with the trick-block.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Card number "9" at top left (Nine of Hearts - Negen Harten)
- A heart symbol in the upper center portion
- Cherub/putti figure in the center of the composition
- Lantern at the bottom
- Classical architectural elements (buildings, tower) in background
- Ornamental border frame around entire image
- This is from a historical Dutch playing card series called "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Cards) from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Tableau)
