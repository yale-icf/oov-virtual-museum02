**Notable markings:**

- Serial Number: **N° 161,517** (appears twice on the bond)
- Two official seals with Romanian heraldry (red/circular stamp visible)
- Signatures of official representatives at bottom
- Blue decorative border with ornamental patterns
- Eagle emblem at bottom center
- Numbered coupons 31-60 attached to the right side (small denomination coupons numbered 60, 50, 40 through 31)
- Each coupon bears the same serial number: **N° 161,517**
- Text indicates this is a **Bearer Bond** ("au porteur")
- Date reference: **1929**
