## English Translation:

**Bética**

ANONYMOUS COOPERATIVE AGRICULTURAL AND INDUSTRIAL COMPANY

Established in Seville on December 15, 1924 by deed executed before Notary D. Ilíc. Sánchez Blanco y Sánchez with a

SHARE CAPITAL OF 17,000,000 PESETAS

ISSUE OF 6,000,000 PESETAS IN MORTGAGE BONDS DIVIDED INTO 12,000 BEARER TITLES OF 500 PESETAS NOMINAL VALUE EACH, AUTHORIZED AT THE EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL SHAREHOLDERS' MEETING OF JUNE 30, 1931, AND FORMALIZED BY PUBLIC DEED BEFORE NOTARY DON FÉLIX SÁNCHEZ BLANCO Y SÁNCHEZ IN SEVILLE ON SEPTEMBER 21, 1931.

**Bond Details:**
BOND No. [number] 500 PESETAS

The bearer of this Bond is entitled to seven percent annual interest, free of present and future taxes. Payable in gold pesetas in semi-annual installments due on March 31 and September 30 of each year. Starting from March 31, 1932, until the date of amortization, which shall occur within a maximum period of thirty years. By lottery drawing, in a minimum amount of two hundred thousand pesetas annually, beginning September 30, 1934, the Company may increase said amount for amortization and retains the right to retire all bonds from circulation by prior payment of the same, without need for drawing, coinciding with the date set for the same.

AMENDED AT VALLE                                   SEVILLE OCTOBER 1, 1931

THE PRESIDENT.                    [signature]                    THE SECRETARY.

The S.A. "Azucarera de Sevilla"

- **Serial Numbers:** Various coupon numbers visible (50-59 range noted)
- **Decorative Border:** Ornate geometric frame with "500" in corners
- **Allegorical Figure:** Female figure with agricultural/industrial imagery in top section
- **Coupon Sheet:** Attached at bottom with multiple detachable coupons for interest payments
- **Stamps/Seals:** Multiple official stamps and "JUSTIFICADO" marking visible
- **Date Discrepancy:** Document mentions establishment in 1924 but formalization in 1931
