# Transcription

## Original (Russian):

**Header:**
Билет Государственной Комиссии погашения долгов
на Капитале Безсрочном

**Main Text:**
Билет Государственной Комиссии погашения долгов
1854. на Капитале Безсрочном ЛИТ. № [unclear]

КАНЦЕЛЯЛЬ [left side]                                ДОХОДЪ [right side]
500 РУБЛЕЙ                                           50 РУБЛЕЙ

Свидетельствует, что означенный ниже лицо
заимствовал у Государственного Российского казначейства именем
[text continues in cursive, partially illegible]
триносящий ценнервысаго Дохода по Шести на Сто
на нижеследующие периоды ежегодно разделяемый
платежные со 15-го по 31-го Генваря и со 15-го по 31-го Июля

Справедливее [signature/mark]

**Footer:**
Вписано в Журнал лиц. 1 стр. 1851 года 1854.
[Signatures and administrative marks]

## English Translation:

**Header:**
Certificate of the State Commission for the Repayment of Debts
on Perpetual Capital

**Main Text:**
Certificate of the State Commission for the Repayment of Debts
1854. on Perpetual Capital LIT. № [unclear]

OFFICE [left side]                                   INCOME [right side]
500 ROUBLES                                          50 ROUBLES

[This] certifies that the aforementioned person
has borrowed from the State Russian Treasury
on behalf of [text continues, partially illegible]
bearing perpetual income at Six per Hundred [6% per annum]
for the following periods, divided annually
payable from the 15th to 31st of January and from the 15th to 31st of July

Verified [signature/mark]

**Footer:**
Entered in Register of Persons, Vol. 1, page [unclear] of 1854.
[Administrative signatures and marks]

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Imperial Double-headed Eagle** emblem at top center with crown
- **Ornamental border** with repeating geometric patterns on top and bottom
- **Decorative circular seals/vignettes** arranged vertically on left and right margins
- **Handwritten signatures** at bottom (Administrator and Bookkeeper of the Commission)
- **Handwritten notation** indicating date and registration: "Вписано в Журнал лиц." (Entered in Register)
- **Serial number designation:** ЛИТ. № [unclear in scan]
- **Currency designation:** Both Российским серебряным монетою (Russian silver currency) referenced in description
