# Transcription

**GERMANY.**

Lottery begins January 21, 1817,
Two Grand Prizes of 20,000 Guineas;
And FORTY other Capitals!
ALL IN STERLING MONEY!
NO STOCK PRIZES!

BARON von DONDER-DRONK SMOKE-OFF
BEHOLD, at Fortune's invitation,
A hero of the German nation;
He's smok'd you out, and to partake
Of British cheer and British cake,
Has left his native land to roam,
And gain a Prize in Britain's home,
To care and grief he bids defiance,
A Lottery Prize is his reliance.
