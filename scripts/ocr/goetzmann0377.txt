# Transcription of A&P Stock Certificate

**Header/Title Section:**
- "100" (top left and right corners)
- Serial Number: 458293 (top left)
- "A&P" (center, within circular emblem with two winged figures)

**Main Title:**
"THE GREAT ATLANTIC & PACIFIC TEA COMPANY"
"INCORPORATED UNDER THE LAWS OF THE STATE OF MARYLAND"

**Certificate Body Text:**
"This is to Certify that

MRS LEONORE ROMNEY

is the owner of

CUSIP 390064 10 3

ONE HUNDRED

FULL PAID AND NON-ASSESSABLE SHARES, OF THE PAR VALUE OF $1 EACH, OF THE COMMON STOCK OF

The Great Atlantic & Pacific Tea Company, Inc. [hereby called the "Corporation"] transferable on the books of the Corporation by said owner in person or by duly authorized attorney when surrender of this certificate properly endorsed. This certificate is not valid until countersigned by the Transfer Agent and registered by the Registrar.

In Witness Whereof, the Corporation has caused this certificate to be signed with its corporate seal.

Dated: JAN 2 1974"

**Signature Lines:**
- "H.O. Julsell" (Treasurer) - with signature
- "M.A. Audsley" (Chairman of the Board) - with signature

**Notable Markings:**
- Official corporate seal (circular, green, center bottom)
- "FIRST NATIONAL CITY BANK" (left side, vertical text)
- Bank routing/reference marks
- "AMERICAN BANK NOTE COMPANY" (bottom center)
- Serial number: 458293
- CUSIP number: 390064 10 3
