**English translation:**

$ 5 = zł 44.57

REPUBLIC OF POLAND.

BOND

3RD SERIES DOLLAR PREMIUM LOAN

VALUE OF 5 DOLLARS
OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

OR 44.57 ZLOTYS.

THE 3RD SERIES DOLLAR PREMIUM LOAN IS ISSUED BY VIRTUE OF THE LAW OF 1 FEBRUARY 1930
(JOURNAL OF LAWS OF THE R.P. NO. 10, ITEM 65). INTEREST ON THIS LOAN AT THE RATE OF 4 PERCENT PER ANNUM IS PAID SEMI-ANNUALLY
ON FEBRUARY 1 AND AUGUST 1 OF EACH YEAR THROUGH AUTHORIZED BANKS ABROAD.

[Extended terms regarding bond conditions, lottery provisions, and repayment terms]

THIS BOND SHALL BE SUBJECT TO PRESCRIPTION ON 1 FEBRUARY 1971. RENEWAL AFTER 30 YEARS FROM THE DATE
ON WHICH IT BECOMES PAYABLE SHALL NOT BE PERMITTED. COUPONS OF THE BOND BECOME PRESCRIBED
AFTER 5 YEARS FROM THE DATE THEY BECOME PAYABLE, AND PREMIUMS AFTER 10 YEARS FROM THE DATE OF THEIR DRAWING.

Warsaw, 1 February 1931

DIRECTOR OF THE MINISTRY OF THE TREASURY:
Department of State Debt:
[Signatures]

Commission for Control
of State Loans:
[Signatures]

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **1222277** (bottom center)
- Exchange rate notation at top: **$ 5 = zł 44.57**
- Official signatures from the Ministry of the Treasury and State Loan Control Commission
- Ornate decorative borders with floral/classical motifs and large numeral "5" in corners
- Printed by: "POLSKA WYTWÓRNIA PAPIERÓW WARTOŚCIOWYCH WARSZAWA" (Polish Security Printing Works, Warsaw)
