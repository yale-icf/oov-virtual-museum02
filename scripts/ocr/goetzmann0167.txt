# Transcription

New Year's Lottery begins
21st This Month
(JANUARY).

All the Prizes will be Paid in Sterling Money.

LAST LOTTERY.
No. 2,656,
£20,000
WAS
Sold in Shares
BY
SWIFT
And Co. who also sold
NINE other CAPITALS of £1,000,
£500, &c. all in SHARES.

2 of 20,000 Guineas
AND UPWARDS OF
40 OTHER CAPITALS.
