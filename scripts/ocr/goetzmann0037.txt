# Transcription

**Title:** Corral Fishery

**Main Text:**
Corral that Beautious product only found,
Beneath the Water, and above the Ground,
If Killʼd for as it ought from thence might Spring,
A Neptunes Pallace for a British King.

**Notable markings:**
- Diamond suit symbol in upper left corner
- Engraved portrait of a figure in period dress (King of Diamonds) in upper left
- Ship with full sails in the harbor scene
- Multiple figures on shore examining coral and shells
- Coastal landscape with mountains in background
- This is page 10 of 51 from a set of South Sea Bubble Playing Cards (King of Diamonds)
