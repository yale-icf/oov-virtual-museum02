# Shanghai-Nanking Railway Net Profit Sub-Certificate

**No. E3036**

Shanghai-Nanking Railway

ISSUE by the
BRITISH & CHINESE CORPORATION LIMITED,
of a Series of Net Profit Sub-Certificates.

---

**NET PROFIT SUB-CERTIFICATE.**

THE BRITISH & CHINESE CORPORATION, LIMITED, hereby certifies that this is No. E3036 Net Profit Sub-Certificate and one of the Sub-Certificates to Bearer not exceeding in the whole in number 32,500 entitled to such benefits as are secured thereto by a Declaration of Trust dated the 19th July, 1904, and made between the British and Chinese Corporation Limited of the one part and the Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation of the other part.

IN WITNESS whereof The British and Chinese Corporation, Limited, has caused its Common Seal to be hereunto affixed this Second day of December, 1904.

*The Common Seal of The British and Chinese Corporation, Limited, was affixed hereto in the presence of*

[Signature]
Director.

[Signature]
Secretary.

NOTE.—These Sub-Certificates cannot continue beyond July 9th 1953 or earlier Redemption. The possession of this Sub-Certificate entitles the possessor to the inspection of a copy of the said Declaration of Trust at the London Office of the said Banking Corporation.
