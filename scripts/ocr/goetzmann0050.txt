# Transcription

**Title (top):**
Hemp & Flax

**Main text (bottom):**
Here Hemp is Sown'd for Stubborn Rogues to Die in,
And foster Flax, for tender Skins to Lye in:
But Should the usefull Project be defeated;
the Knaves may prosper but the Fools are cheated.

**Notable markings:**
- Red heart symbol in upper left corner
- Engraved illustration in classical style depicting workers in a field with buildings, trees, and birds in sky
- Border frame around entire card
- Hand-written or cursive script for the poetic text at bottom
