# Transcription

FOR THE PAYMENT OF

No. 79          Comptroller's-Office, July 1st 1809

I hereby certify, that The Honourable [unclear] Poole Esqr.
is entitled to receive the Sum of FIVE POUNDS of
Lawful Money, out of any Funds appropriated for the
Payment of Interest on the liquidated Debt of the State of
Connecticut

I will Pow.

[Signature - illegible]                                    Compt.

5/.                           HARTFORD: PRINTED BY HUDSON AND GOODWIN.                           5/.
