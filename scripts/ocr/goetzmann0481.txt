## Notable Markings:

- Serial number: **№059944**
- Denomination: **125 Rubles in Gold** (also noted as 500 Fr., 404 Marks)
- Imperial double-headed eagle with crown in center
- Ornamental border design typical of Russian Imperial bonds
- Signature lines for authorized officials
- Stamp/seal impressions (partially visible)
- Printer mark: "239 Pl. Netherlandsh" (Netherlands printer mark)
- Dating notation indicating 1894-1904 period
- Multiple official seals and stamps throughout the document
- Decorative corner emblems and security features
