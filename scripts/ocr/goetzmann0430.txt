## English Translation:

**GOVERNMENT OF THE CHINESE REPUBLIC**

**8% TREASURY BONDS OF 1921**

**LUNG-TSING-U-HAI RAILWAY**

**50,000,000 FRANCS**

Divided into 100,000 Bonds of 500 francs each.

**BOND FOR 500 FRANCS**

No. 012913

[The detailed conditions outline that these bonds are bearer bonds issued by the Government of the Chinese Republic, bearing 8% annual interest, payable semi-annually. The bonds are secured by the General Company of Railways and Tramways in China for the Lung-Tsing-U-Hai Railway line. Legal provisions include conditions regarding redemption, payment procedures, and obligations of the issuing parties.]

Signed in Brussels, July 1st, 1921

**THE MINISTER OF THE CHINESE REPUBLIC**
IN BRUSSELS

[Signature and red seal with Chinese characters]

**THE GENERAL COMPANY
OF RAILWAYS
AND TRAMWAYS IN CHINA**

**THE DELEGATE**

[Signature]

**AND GENERAL DIRECTOR
OF THE LUNG-TSING-U-HAI RAILWAY UNION**

[Signature]

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 012913 (visible on bond)
- **Red Official Seals:** Two red square seals with Chinese characters (chops) - one from the Chinese Ministry and one from the Railway Company
- **Signatures:** Three handwritten signatures (Minister, Delegate, and Director General)
- **Date:** Bruxelles (Brussels), 1er juillet 1921 (July 1, 1921)
- **Ornamental Border:** Elaborate green decorative border with floral and geometric patterns typical of 19th-century financial documents
- **Printer Mark:** "A. VERSCHUEREN - ANVERS-BRUXELLES" and "Dessin d'époque" at bottom
