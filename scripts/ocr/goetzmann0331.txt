## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 07.399 (printed and visible throughout document)
- **Decorative Border:** Elaborate ornamental frames with floral motifs in gold/tan coloring
- **Company Vignette:** Oval medallion at top center with "COMPAGNIE D'INGUARAN"
- **Watermark/Background:** Large watermarked text reading "INGUARAN" visible across the certificate
- **Printer Mark:** Bottom of document indicates "IMP. BITTNER-THIERRY, 42 RUE LAFITTE, PARIS"
- **Multiple Coupon Stubs:** 16 perforated coupons arranged in grid pattern at bottom of document, each numbered sequentially
- **Official Seals/Stamps:** Visible stamp/seal impression in upper right corner of main certificate
