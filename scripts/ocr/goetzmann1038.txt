# Continental Bill of Exchange No. 85 Transcription

**Printed for 30 Dollars at five Sums, Journous Dollar, Numb. 85**

United States of AMERICA, 25. Day of November 1778

AT Thirty Days Sight of this First Bill, Second, Third and Fourth not paid, pay to Mr. Moses Frazier or Order, Thirty Dollars, in One Hundred and Fifty Livres Tournois, for Interest due on Money borrowed by the United States.

To the Commissioner or Commissioners of the
United States of America, at Paris.

Countersigned, Nathaniel Appleton
Commissioner of the Continental Loan-Office in the
State of Massachusetts Bay

S. Hopkinson
(Treas. of Loans.)

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **85** (appears in top right)
- Signature: **S. Hopkinson** (Francis Hopkinson, Treasurer of Loans)
- Countersignature: **Nathaniel Appleton** (Commissioner of Continental Loan-Office, Massachusetts Bay)
- Ornamental borders and decorative elements on left margin
- Handwritten annotations and stamps on left side [partially illegible]
- Standard format indicating this is the "First" of four bills issued simultaneously
