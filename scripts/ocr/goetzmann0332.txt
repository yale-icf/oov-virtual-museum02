## English Translation:

**COMPAGNIE OCCIDENTALE DE MADAGASCAR**

ANONYMOUS COMPANY WITH 7,000,000 FRANCS

WITH A CAPITAL OF THREE MILLION TWO HUNDRED THOUSAND FRANCS
DIVIDED INTO 32,000 SHARES OF 100 FRANCS General [meeting]
extraordinary

Established according to Statutes dated 4 May and 17 June 1895, modified by the General Assemblies of shareholders on 27 September 1898, 30 December 1900, [unclear dates] 22 and 30 June 1910

HEAD OFFICE IN PARIS

SHARE OF ONE HUNDRED FRANCS TO BEARER

Fully paid up

No. 16,102

ONE ADMINISTRATOR                ONE ADMINISTRATOR

[Signatures]

Paris, 16 SEPTEMBER 1910

## Notable Markings:

- **Red circular stamp** on left side (company seal, partially legible)
- **Purple circular stamp** at top center (official verification mark)
- **Medallion/coat of arms** on right side (classical female figure with shield)
- **Two handwritten signatures** by administrators (bottom center, illegible)
- **Serial number**: 16,102
- **Date**: 16 SEPTEMBRE 1910
- **Ornamental border**: Elaborate engraved decorative frame with scrollwork and classical motifs
- **Printer marks**: D. RICHARD IMP., E. TOMÁZE DEL.
- **Repeated company name** at bottom in four sections (typical of detachable stock certificates)
