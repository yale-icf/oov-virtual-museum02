# Transcription

**Original (English):**

---

**New York State Per Cent Stock**

STATE OF NEW YORK, COMPTROLLERS OFFICE,

Albany, 10 October 1817

BE IT KNOWN, That the People of the State of New York, [have] Walter Berry of the State of Connecticut or his Assigns, the Sum of Three Thousand Dollars, bearing Interest at the rate of seven per Centum per Annum, from the first ___ day of October exclusively, payable quarterly, on the first Mondays in the Months of January, April, July and October; being Stock created in pursuance of an Act of the Legislature, entitled, "an Act to create a public and transferable Stock, and to lay and collect additional Taxes for the use of this State;" passed April 7th 1815; the Principal of which Stock is reimbursable as the said Act provides. Which debt is recorded in this Office, and is transferable only by appearance in person, or by Attorney, according to the rules and forms instituted for that purpose.
