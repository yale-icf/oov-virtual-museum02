THIS DEBENTURE IS ISSUED UNDER THE AUTHORITY OF CLAUSE 54 OF THE ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION OF THE LIVERPOOL CORN TRADE ASSOCIATION, LIMITED, OF A RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS DATED THE 26TH OF MAY, 1897.

THE

**Liverpool Corn Trade Association,**
LIMITED.

INCORPORATED UNDER THE COMPANIES' ACTS, 1862 TO 1893.

CAPITAL: £60,000, in 400 Shares of £150 Each.

Registered Office:
8, BRUNSWICK STREET, LIVERPOOL.

ISSUE of £100,000 C. PERPETUAL MORTGAGE DEBENTURES, carrying Interest at £3½ per Centum per Annum.

No. 291     C. PERPETUAL MORTGAGE DEBENTURE. £100.

The Liverpool Corn Trade Association, Limited, [acknowledges receipt of] One Hundred Pounds, being part of their Perpetual Mortgage Debenture Stock, and hereby agrees to pay to the bearer or registered holder for the time being the sum of One Hundred Pounds, or other the registered holder for the time being to be made on the 1st day of August.

The Association will, during the continuance of this security, pay to each registered holder interest thereon at the rate of £3½ per centum per annum. By equal half-yearly payments, on the 1st day of February and the 1st day of August, the 1st of such half-yearly payments to be made on the 1st day of August.

The Association doth hereby charge with the said principal money and interest all the present and future property of the Association, both real and personal, including uncalled capital, by way of security to the intent that the "C" Debenture and the other debentures forming part of the above-mentioned authorized issue of £100,000 shall rank, pari passu, as a charge upon the said property and future property of the Association, called only in the event of the Association being unable to pay the 1st and £10,000 in Debentures which are charged upon the said property in priority to the Debentures respectively named.

The Debenture is issued subject to and with the benefit of the conditions endorsed hereon, which are as to hereinafter set out.

**Given** under the Common Seal of the Association,

this 16th day of June, 1897
