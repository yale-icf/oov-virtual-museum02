## English Translation:

This Receipt must be exchanged before _______________
(This date will be added three months from the date of signature)

COUNCIL OF THE DISTRIBUTED PUBLIC DEBT OF THE FORMER OTTOMAN EMPIRE

Receipt to Bearer No. 100,014

of a nominal amount of { Ltqs 2.20
                       { £ 2.0.0
                       { Frs fr. 50.—

THREE POUNDS STERLING _______________

Issued by the Council of the Distributed Public Debt of the Former Ottoman Empire, in accordance with the provisions of Article IV, paragraph o, of the Contract concluded on 13 June 1928 between the Government of the Turkish Republic and the Representatives of holders of Ottoman Public Debt, ratified by the Grand National Assembly of Turkey by law passed on 1 December 1928.

EXCHANGEABLE

against a Bearer Title of

Representative Bonds of Arrears of Annuities of the Ottoman Public Debt "Series A" 1928

In the absence of exchange within a maximum period of one year from the date of its issue, the present Receipt shall be forfeited for the benefit of the debtor States.

It is incumbent upon the interested bearers to group together in order to present for exchange Receipts of a total amount corresponding to the nominal amount of Ltqs 22, or £ 20, or Frs fr. 500 of the unit bonds of the Representative Bonds of Series A.

The present Receipt is created only for the purposes of the banking exchange operation. It cannot be considered as a debt security. It does not produce interest.

AMSTERDAM, 22 APR. 1930

FOR THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRIBUTED PUBLIC DEBT
OF THE FORMER OTTOMAN EMPIRE:

The President,                        For | BANK OF PARIS AND THE NETHERLANDS
[signature]                          BY PROXY
                                    [signature]
