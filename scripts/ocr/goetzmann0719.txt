## English Translation:

CERTIFICATE OF INSCRIPTION
Of 6 Percent Russian Funds in Bank Assignations / Inscription Certificate in Russian 6 Percent Bank Assignations

No. 1326. R⁺ 1000:—

The holder of this is entitled to an inscription of ONE THOUSAND RUBLES at 6 percent Russian Funds, payable in BANK ASSIGNATIONS, inscribed in the Debt-Book at St. Petersburg, in the name of the ADMINISTRATION OFFICE, established in Amsterdam, under the direction of

HOPE AND COMPANY,
KETWICH AND VOOMBERGH,
AND

Wed. W. BORSKI / Widow W. BORSKI

The interests are received and paid at the holder's/bearer's expense and risk on Coupons, according to Article 6 of the published Notice.

The HOLDER of this Certificate shall at all times be entitled to claim the original INSCRIPTION in return, against return of this Certificate with the matured Coupons, and shall receive PROOF for new COUPONS, and against payment of costs as mentioned in Article 10 of the Notice.

Amsterdam, 11 July 1825.

Ten Half-yearly COUPONS delivered, valid until 11 July 1829.
