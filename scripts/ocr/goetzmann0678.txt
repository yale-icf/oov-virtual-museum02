## English Translation:

**Header:**
IMPERIAL RUSSIAN GOVERNMENT.
NIKOLAEVSKY RAILWAY.

**Subtitle:**
ISSUE:
555,500 BEARER BONDS.

**Terms:**
of one hundred twenty-five rubles,
or five hundred francs, or twenty pounds sterling,
or two hundred thirty-six guilders.

Of these, 400,000 bonds at one per page, numbered 600,001–1,000,000,
and 155,500 at five per page, numbered 1,000,001–1,155,500.

Issued by Imperial decree of 25 March 1869 (*)

**Main Bond Section:**
BOND
No. 046607

of 125 rubles = 500 francs = 20 pounds sterling = 236 guilders.

[Followed by extensive terms and conditions describing 4% annual interest, payment locations, and redemption procedures]

**Payment locations mentioned:**
In Narva, In Gottingen & Co., In Amsterdam, at Count Hopé & Co., In London, at Barings & Co.

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 046607
- **Red circular seal:** "50e COMPLEMENT DE 1919" (50th Anniversary commemoration seal, affixed in red)
- **Official stamps and seals:** Double-headed Imperial Russian eagle at top center
- **Blue postal stamp/cancellation mark** in upper right corner
- **Handwritten annotations:** Various marks and signatures throughout
- **Ornate decorative border** with geometric patterns around entire document perimeter
- **Date of issue:** 25 March 1869
- **Issue amount:** 555,500 bonds totaling 69,437,500 rubles / 277,750,000 francs / 11,110,000 pounds sterling / 131,098,000 guilders
