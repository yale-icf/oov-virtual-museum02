## Notable markings:

- Serial number: **No. 224S** (appears twice - upper right and left)
- Check number designation: **224S**
- Location: Virginia City, Montana Territory
- Date: 186[6] (final digit unclear but likely 1866)
- Engraved vignettes: Frontier scene with miners/prospectors (left) and stagecoach with horses (upper right)
- Signature line present but unsigned (blank check)
- Company name and agent information printed in formal script
- Publisher mark: "Strobridge & James, 93 Fulton, N.Y." (visible in small print at bottom left)
