# Transcription

**AFRICA.**

Lottery begins January 21, 1817.
Two Grand Prizes of 20,000 Guineas;
And FORTY other Capitals!

ALL IN STERLING MONEY!
NO STOCK PRIZES!

---

KANKO SAMBO.

A sable son of Afric's shore,
To share this evening's festive store,
On England's coast behold.
Fond hopes within his bosom rise,
That he shall gain a splendid Prize
In this Month's Lottery Gold!

---
