# SECOND NATIONALIST GOVERNMENT LOTTERY LOAN
## OF THE
### FIFTEENTH YEAR OF THE REPUBLIC OF CHINA, BEING THE YEAR 1926.
#### BOND FOR FIVE DOLLARS, CANTON CURRENCY.

The Regulation of the Lottery Loan for financing the construction of the Port of Whampoa was sanctioned by the Nationalist Government Mandate of the 25th June, 1926.

A summary of the said Regulations appears below:—

**Amount issued:—** $10,000,000.
**Denomination:—** $5.
**Security:—** The loan is secured on the revenue of the Nationalist Government, and the Central Bank of China, which is the Government Depository, unconditionally guarantees the payment of prizes and the repayment of principal at maturity.

**Repayment:—** The loan is repaid in thirty monthly instalments, without interest.
- 1st to 10th month 2½ of the bonds are drawn each month.
- 11th " 20th " 3% " " " "
- 21st " 30th " 3½ " " " "

Public drawings for prizes are held on the 5th day of each month, at the General Chamber of Commerce, Canton, and directly after the winning numbers are drawn a second drawing will be made from the said winning numbers for the awarding of prizes.

The following prizes will be drawn each month:—
- 1 First Prize......................... $ 50,000.— | 50 Sixth Prizes, each $ 30............ $ 1,500.—
- 2 Second Prizes, each $ 2,000...... $ 4,000.— | 100 Seventh " " $ 15............. $ 1,500.—
- 5 Third, " $ 500............. $ 2,500.— | 300 Eighth " " $ 10............. $ 3,000.—
- 10 Fourth, " $ 100............. $ 1,000.— | 600 Ninth " " $ 5.............. $ 3,000.—
- 20 Fifth, " $ 50............... $ 1,000.—

All bonds, which have been drawn for repayment of principal, can be used as security for loans at 50 cents each, in addition to the repayment of principal.

Drawn Bonds should be presented to the Central Bank of China, Canton, for repayment of principal and payment of prizes five days after the drawing, and not later than three months thereafter.

**Use of Goods:—** The Bonds are transferable, and may be bought, sold or mortgaged, or used as security or reserve fund for public transactions.

Dated the 1st day of the 8th Month of the 15th Year of the Republic of China, being the 1st of August, 1926.
