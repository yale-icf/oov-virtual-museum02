# Transcription

**Title (top of card):**
Settling Colonies in
Accadia North America

**Verse (bottom of card):**
He that is Rich, and wants to fool away,
A Sporting Sum, in North America;
Let him Subscribe himself a Headlong Sharer,
And Apes Ears, Shall Honour him or Bearer.

**Notable markings:**
- Nine red diamonds arranged in a 3x3 pattern in the upper left corner (King of Diamonds suit indicator)
- Engraved illustration showing colonial settlement scene with European settlers interacting with Native Americans, ships in harbor, and landscape features
- Card appears aged with visible wear and discoloration consistent with 18th-century playing cards
- This is identified as part of the "South Sea Bubble Playing Cards" series, a satirical commentary on the financial speculation of the early 1720s
