# Transcription

To Constable Samuel Searl &
The Within List Contains a
Each Parishon Here in Named is
affected as their Proportion of the
Fine Levied on the Sixth Class in the
town of Rowley for not procuring a man
to Serve in the Continental Army year
agreeable to a Hole of the General
Court. Each Parishon [tax] is in the Line
with their name and the Same is
Commited to you to Colect

august 15th 1782

Thomas Mighill    Select
Daniel Spafford    men of
Joseph Poor        Rowley
Nathaniel Pickard
Isaac Smith

---
