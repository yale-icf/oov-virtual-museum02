## English Translation:

**Header:**
Certificate No. 79948                  Year 1822                    Certificate No. 25263

ROUBLES 960                            448 Sterling

PERPETUAL INCOME AT 5 PERCENT.

Certificate of perpetual income on capital invested in the State Debt Book by the Commission for the Repayment of Debts, consisting of two hundred sixty roubles in silver, equivalent to 448 pounds sterling.

Registered 1st March 1822.

Book L. page [117] 2nd Loan Lip. [unclear]

[The main body contains detailed conditions regarding:
- Transfer and inheritance of the certificate
- Semi-annual payment of interest from the State Treasury in Saint Petersburg or Warsaw
- Rules regarding loss, theft, or damage of the certificate
- Provisions for disputed claims
- Conditions for redemption or conversion]

**Signatures:**
For the Director [signature]
Accountant [signature]
