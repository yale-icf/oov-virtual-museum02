**1.** THE COLOMBIAN NATIONAL RAILWAY COMPANY, LIMITED (hereinafter called "The Company") will on the 1st day of July, 1929, or on such earlier day as the principal monies hereby secured become payable, in accordance with the Conditions endorsed hereon, pay to [handwritten: Santiago Lopez Duran] or to the Registered Holder for the time being hereof, the sum of **One Hundred** Pounds. [illegible]

**2.** The Company will in the meantime, and until payment of the said principal sum, pay to such Registered Holder interest thereon, or on so much thereof as shall for the time being remain unpaid at the rate of 6 per cent. per annum, by half-yearly payments on the 1st day of January and the 1st day of July in each year, the first of such half-yearly payments to be made on the 1st day of July, 1910, for interest from the 1st day of January, 1910.

**3.** The Company declares that this Debenture is one of the Debentures referred to in the Deed of Trust, dated the 23rd day of January, 1901, and made between the Company of the one part and ROWLAND HILLS and EDWARD EYRE WILLIAMS of the other part.

**4.** This Debenture is issued upon and subject to the Conditions endorsed hereon, and the Company will duly and punctually perform, observe, and comply with all such Conditions, and also all the provisions of the said Deed of Trust so far as the same respectively are or ought to be performed, observed, or complied with by the Company.
