## Original (Dutch):

**Uitgestelde Schuld.**

No. 6231                                                                                                    Uitgezal    f. 500

**Bewijs van Voldoening**
**AAN DE WET.**

———————————————

**UITGESTELDE SCHULD.**

A1 zoo aan de COMMISSIE, bij Besluit van Z.K.H., in dato 7 Juni 1814, No. 23, belast met de Werksaamheden voortvloeiende uit de Wet van 14 Mei 1814, gebleken is: dat door [P.C. Vallings] als C. Pareren & [unclear]

De zake

[Several handwritten annotations and signatures]

behoorlijk is voldaan aan den inhoud van Art. 5, 12 en 18 van de Wet voornoernd, voor de Kapitalen op het Oude Grootboek der Hollandsche Schuld, ingeschreven ten name van C. Niemanstedt [?]

Het dorpe

blijkens AFSCHRIJVINGS-BILLET No. [?] van het GROOTBOEK zal deze Rekening overeenkomstig Art. 16 der Wet, op het Nieuwe Grootboek der UITGESTELDE SCHULD, ten voorlichreven hoofde worden overgebracht ter Somma van [Veertien Honderd Guldens] welke misdien reeds zal deelen in de kans der Eerste Uitlooting van dezelven.

En is hierbjj overeenkomstig Art. 14 van het Besluit van Z.K.H., in dato 28 Juli 1814, No. 23, afgegeven een Restant-Renverfaal in blanco, groot [Honderd en Dertig Gulden] [unclear]

Uitgegeven uit kracht van Art. 12 der Wet van 14 Mei 1814, door de COMMISSIE voornoernd.

En is deze ten eerste maal Afgezegd door een onderteekend.

N. [unclear] 1814                                                    [Signature]

Amsterdam,                                                           [Signature]
den Mei... 1815

## English Translation:

**Deferred Debt.**

No. 6231                                                                                                    Issued    f. 500

**Certificate of Compliance**
**WITH THE LAW.**

———————————————

**DEFERRED DEBT.**

As it has appeared to the COMMISSION, by Decree of His Royal Highness, dated June 7, 1814, No. 23, charged with the business arising from the Law of May 14, 1814, that by [P.C. Vallings] as C. Pareren & [unclear]

Proper compliance has been made with the content of Articles 5, 12, and 18 of the aforementioned Law, for the Capital amounts in the Old General Ledger of the Hollandsche Schuld [Dutch Debt], registered in the name of C. Niemanstedt [?]

As evidenced by the WRITE-OFF TICKET No. [?] of the GENERAL LEDGER, this account shall, in accordance with Article 16 of the Law, be transferred to the New General Ledger of the DEFERRED DEBT, under the aforementioned principal, for the Sum of [Fourteen Hundred Guilders], which sums shall henceforth participate in the drawing of the First Lottery of the same.

And hereby, in accordance with Article 14 of the Decree of His Royal Highness, dated July 28, 1814, No. 23, there is issued a Remainder-Return Note in blank, for the amount of [One Hundred Thirty Guilders] [unclear]

Issued under the authority of Article 12 of the Law of May 14, 1814, by the aforementioned COMMISSION.

And this is hereby certified for the first time by an undersigned.

N. [unclear] 1814                                                    [Signature]

Amsterdam,                                                           [Signature]
May... 1815

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 6231
- **Amount:** f. 500 (Guilders)
- **Decorative Border:** Green ornamental frame with repeating pattern
- **Dates:** June 7, 1814 (decree date); May 14, 1814 (law date); July 28, 1814; May 1815 (signature date)
- **Official References:** Multiple article citations (Art. 5, 12, 14, 16, 18)
- **Handwritten Annotations:** Multiple manuscript additions, signatures, and corrections throughout
- **Status:** Appears to be a formal government debt certificate with official seals/printing
