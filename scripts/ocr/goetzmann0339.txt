## English Translation:

**Header:**
COAL MINE COMPANY OF UJO-MIERES
COMBINED CONCESSIONS OF UJO-MIERES AND RIOSA

Bearer Certificate No. 72049 / Certificate to Bearer

**Left side (Spanish):**
Anonymous Society
CAPITAL
15,000,000
PESETAS GOLD
DIVIDED INTO 600,000 SHARES OF 25 PESETAS GOLD

Certificate of Five Shares
of 25 Pesetas gold each share
COMPLETELY PAID UP

No. 370,241

One Administrator [signature]

**Right side (French):**
Anonymous Society
CAPITAL
15,000,000
PESETAS GOLD
Divided into 600,000 shares of 25 pesetas gold

Certificate of Five Shares
of 25 Pesetas gold each
FULLY PAID UP

to No. 370,245

The Administrator [signature]

**Body text:**
This Company is constituted and domiciled in Ujo [for the] coal business [and was] established publicly on the 3rd of December 1903 before the Notary of Madrid Don José Moreno de la Cruz and la Parra.

**Bottom (French):**
This Company was constituted on 3 December 1905 by public deed executed before Don José Menéndez y de Parra, Notary in Madrid, and has its registered office in Ujo (Asturias).
