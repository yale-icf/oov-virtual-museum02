**English translation:**

THE ROCK ISLAND COMPANY.

CERTIFICATE of $ 1000.–
COMMON STOCK.
No.: 52108

HOLDER (This is entitled to Trust Cévone Shares of $ 100.– in The Rock Island Company, under the further terms and conditions of the Company for the management of the Administration Office of American Funds, established by

BROES and GOSMAN,
TEN HAVE and VAN ESSEN
JARMAN and ZOONEN,
at AMSTERDAM

and legalized by the undersigned Notary.

The PAYMENTS on the accumulated shares will be paid for the account and risk of the HOLDER in America, and exclusively only.

The exchange of CERTIFICATES with overvalued Dividend vouchers, against the original Bank note, can be agreed simultaneously effective from 1 October 1897.

Witness by me
Amsterdam,

Company for the management of the Administration Office aforementioned,

Notary,
Hereby [?] send Dividend vouchers of up to 20, with COUPON.
