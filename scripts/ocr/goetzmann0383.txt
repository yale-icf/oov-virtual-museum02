# Japanese Wartime Patriotic Bond Transcription

## Original Text:

**Main Certificate (Upper Section):**

戦報４
(Top right corner)

回四第
組ノ壹拾參

**Central Gold Seal:**
083986

附金増割

昭和十七年八月
日本勧業銀行
株式會社

崇情國報時戰
圓五金

用預収情タ宛簽此
儲金部八大於遠
ナリモ定資基森林行規時臨

**Red Seals (Left and Right):**
[Two circular red stamps with landscape/architectural imagery]

**Coupon Section (Lower Section):**

禁　附
組ノ壹拾參
(第4回時戰)
(禁圓五)

083986

金増割
圓

---

## English Translation:

**Main Certificate:**

War Report 4

Fourth Series
Group 47 (or similar notation)

[Gold seal number:]
083986

Attached Gold Increase Coupon

August, Showa Year 17 [1942]
Japan Industrial Bank
Joint Stock Company

Wartime National Patriotic Bond
Five Yen

[Instructions regarding deposit of coupons, storage of principal in designated sections, and procedures...]

**Coupon Section:**

Prohibited - Attached
Group 47
(4th Issue, Wartime)
(Prohibited: 5 Yen)

083986

Gold Increase Coupon
Yen

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 083986 (appears in both main certificate and coupon section)
- **Issue Date:** August 1942 (Showa 17/昭和十七年八月)
- **Issuing Institution:** Japan Industrial Bank (日本勧業銀行)
- **Red Seals:** Two circular red stamps with traditional Japanese landscape/architectural designs
- **Denomination:** 5 Yen (金圓五)
- **Document Category:** 戦報４ (War Report 4 - indicating 4th issue)
- **Institutional stamps/marks:** Multiple red and blue circular seals throughout
