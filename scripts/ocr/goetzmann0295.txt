**English Translation:**

ROMANIAN BANK
ANONYMOUS SOCIETY
SOCIAL CAPITAL LEI 160,000,000.

DIVIDED INTO 320,000 SHARES AT 500 LEI EACH, FORMED THROUGH 8 EMISSIONS, THE LAST OF WHICH AUTHORIZED THROUGH THE EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL MEETING OF SHAREHOLDERS OF DECEMBER 21, 1919, PUBLISHED IN THE OFFICIAL MONITOR NO. 199 - 1919.

THE COMPANY IS CONSTITUTED BY VIRTUE OF THE AUTHENTICATED ACT OF THE LIPOV TRIBUNAL, NOTARY SECTION ON JANUARY 19, 1906, AND AUTHORIZED CONFORMABLY TO THE AUTHENTICATED ACT OF THE LIPOV TRIBUNAL, NOTARY SECTION ON JULY 15, DECEMBER 1910, PUBLISHED IN THE OFFICIAL MONITOR NO. 294 - 1910.

**Notable Markings:**

- **Serial Number:** 011666
- **Stamps:** Purple circular stamp at top left reading "OPTAT EMISIUNEA XI-4"
- **Stamps:** Additional purple and black postal/official stamps at top of document
- **Signatures:** Two handwritten signatures by administrators and control official
- **Decorative Elements:** Ornate art nouveau border; classical allegorical figures (left and right margins); industrial/agricultural scene (center); steamship vignette (bottom center); denominations "500" repeated in corners
- **Date and Location:** "BUCURESTI 1920" (Bucharest 1920)
- **Printer:** "LITH. CARTEA ROMÂNEASCĂ S.A. SEDIUL RĂSIOESCU BUC."
