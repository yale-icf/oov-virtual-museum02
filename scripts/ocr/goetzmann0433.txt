## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 02756
- **Series:** 191 (Сто девяносто первая)
- **Denomination:** 1,000 Rubles (Тысяча рублей)
- **Interest Rate:** 4% annually (40 rubles per year)
- **Imperial Double-Eagle Seal:** Central ornamental emblem
- **Orange/Red Stamp:** Visible in upper left area (possibly customs or cancellation mark)
- **Watermark/Background:** Faint watermark visible throughout
- **Decorative Border:** Art Nouveau design in pink and brown with ornamental corner pieces showing "1000" in each corner
- **Bond Status:** Bearer bond (На предъявителя) - payable to holder
- **Final Coupon Date:** December 1924
