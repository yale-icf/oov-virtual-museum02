**Title (top right):**
Royal Fishery of Great Britain

**Verse (bottom):**
They talk of distant Seas, of Ships, and Nety,
And with the Stile of Royal Gild their Baits;
When all that the Projectors, Hope or Wish for,
Is to catch Fool, the only Chubs they Fish for.
