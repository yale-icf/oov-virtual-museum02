**Anno Regni vicesimo quarto Georgii II. Regis.**

Year One thousand seven hundred and forty nine, transferrable as aforesaid on the Ninth of September, One thousand seven hundred and fifty one: and whereas the Governor and Company of the Bank of England have required to advance and pay into the Receipt of his Majesty's Exchequer the Sum of One million twenty six thousand four hundred twenty six Shillings, and Six Pence, at the latest times, and in the proportions before mentioned, upon Condition that Exchequer Bills be drawn on or before the time the full Principal Sums are proposed to be advanced; which Bills are to be made forth at the said Receipt, and Surpluses, Energies, and Overplus upon commonly called the Sinking Fund, at an Interest, not exceeding the Rate of Three pounds per Annum, to be paid out of the said Sinking Fund, and to commence from the dated Times on which the several Sums are proposed to be advanced; and that the mutual Sums continued in such Exchequer Bills shall be repaid to them out of the First Proceeds of Surpluses of the said Sinking Fund, that shall be applied to the Repayment of the Principal of the National Debt; and whereas, since the making of the said Proposal, the Sum of Eighteen thousand two hundred and fifty eight pounds, in Bank Annuities, is, by this present bred and allowed to be subscribed to the proprietors thereof, to that the principal Sum to be advanced by the said Governor and Company, to pay on the remaining unsubscribed part aforesaid, will amount to the Sum of One million thirteen thousand one hundred sixty eight Pounds, Four Shillings, and Six Pence: Now we, Four Majesty's most faithful and loyal Subjects, the Commons of Great Britain in Parliament assembled, being of Opinion that it will be of Advantage to the Public to accept of the said Proposal, and being also desirous to lessen the Interest of the National Debt, as far as may be, constituted only Justice and Prudence Fruit;
