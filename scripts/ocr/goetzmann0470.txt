# Transcription of United States of Brazil 5% 20-Year Funding Bond of 1931

## Main Text Transcription:

**UNITED STATES OF BRAZIL**

**5 PER CENT. 20-YEAR FUNDING BOND OF 1931**

**DUE OCTOBER 1, 1951**

No C
82940                                                                                                82940
No C

United States of Brazil, wheresoever issued the Government for value received, promises to pay to the bearer hereof on the first day of October, 1951 (unless before said date this bond shall have been duly called for redemption), and payment of the accruing upon prior date, pursuant to the principal sum of

**ONE HUNDRED DOLLARS ($100)**

in gold coin of the United States of America of the present standard of weight and fineness existing October 1891, with interest thereon from the first day of October, 1931, at the rate of five per cent. per annum, payable semi-annually on the first and last days of October in each and every year until this bond shall be paid. Principal and semi-annual interest on this bond shall be made only upon presentation and surrender of the attached interest coupons as they severally mature. Both principal and interest shall be paid without exchange or other deduction to any person in London, England, at the office of a paying agent to be nominated by Dalton, Read & Co., in advising of Bankers buying such bonds, or in New York, at the office of the City Bank Farmers Trust Company, or such other paying agent as the United States of Brazil may designate.
