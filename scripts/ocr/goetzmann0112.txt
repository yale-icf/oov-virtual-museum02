# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Koop brillen voor blinde Astronomisten

Koop Brillen voor de blind gchuildc Astronomisten,
Die eerst in de milervogt hun geld en goed verkristen.

**English translation:**

Buy spectacles for blind Astronomers

Buy Spectacles for the blind cheated Astronomers,
Who first in the thousand nights squandered their money and goods.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Numbered "2" in upper right corner
- This is part of a satirical playing card series (Pasquins Windkaart/Pasquin's Wind Cards)
- The image depicts a figure in 17th-century dress wearing a wide-brimmed hat and spectacles, holding a box and gesturing toward birds (including an owl) and a kite in the sky
- Engraved in the typical style of Dutch satirical prints from this period
