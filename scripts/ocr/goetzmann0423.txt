## Historical Context Notes:

This is a **Prize Loan Bond (Prämien-Obligation)** from the Kingdom of Serbia dated June 15, 1888, denominated in 10 gold francs/dinars. The trilingual format (German/Serbian/French) indicates it was marketed to investors across Central Europe, the Balkans, and France during the late Austro-Hungarian period. Prize bonds combined fixed interest with lottery drawings, making them attractive investment instruments for small savers in pre-World War I Europe.
