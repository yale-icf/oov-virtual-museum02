# Transcription of Chinese Imperial Government 4.5% Gold Loan of 1898 - Interest Coupon Sheet

## Original Text (as printed on coupons):

**CHINESE IMPERIAL GOVERNMENT**
**4½ % GOLD LOAN OF 1898**

[Each coupon contains:]
- Interest denomination: **£ 2.5 S.** (or similar fractional amounts)
- Text: "CHINESE IMPERIAL GOVERNMENT"
- "4½% Gold Loan of 1898"
- Reference to bearer bond conditions
- Ornate decorative borders with engraved patterns

## Notable Markings:

- **Cancellation marks:** All visible coupons (approximately 17-18 in lower row) are cancelled by **hole-punches** (round black holes punched through each coupon)
- **Layout:** Coupons arranged horizontally in a continuous strip
- **Color:** Orange/tan printing with black engraved ornamental borders
- **Format:** Interest coupon sheet format (lower row of a two-row sheet)
- **Associated document:** Companion to upper row (reference: goetzmann0699) forming complete coupon sheet for £100 Sterling bearer bond
- **Origin markings:** Printed in China and/or Germany

**Note:** Due to image resolution and the nature of interest coupon sheets, specific serial numbers and some fine print details are [illegible] in this scan.
