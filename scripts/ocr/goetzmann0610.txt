## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 10113
- **Interest Rate:** 3% per annum

- **Type:** Bearer obligation (Obligación al Portedor)
- **Series:** A
- **Decorative Elements:** Ornamental borders and medallions in corners
- **Printer Mark:** "AMERICAN BANK NOTE COMPANY, NEW YORK" (visible at bottom)
