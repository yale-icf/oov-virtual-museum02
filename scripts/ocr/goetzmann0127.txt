# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

'k Wierdt'ijk door't Actiespel,
schoon't was een snode v'ond,
Maar't geld bruijd door de zeetwever
te vens naar den grond.

**English translation:**

I didn't escape through the lawsuit scheme,
though it was a wicked discovery,
But the money was destroyed by the saltwater
towards the ground at the same time.

**Notable markings:**

- Card designation: "7" and club symbol (☘) in upper right corner
- Engraving-style illustration of a well-dressed man in 18th-century clothing holding a plate or tray with coins spilling out
- Latin text caption below the illustration in italicized script
- This is page 49 of 55 from Pasquins Windkaart (Pasquin's Wind Card), a satirical playing card series from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Tableau)
