## English Translation:

**SOCIETY OF AGAVE PLANTATIONS
OF ANNAM**

Registered Office in SAIGON
Anonymous company with capital of 9,750,000 Francs
Divided into 5,500 shares of 500 francs each.

Statutes filed at the office of M. Baugé, Notary in Saïgon (Cochinchina), March 27, 1924

Share of 500 francs

[Stamped text]

Registered Office in SAIGON

Capital allocated...

Share of 500 francs to Bearer

FULLY PAID

One Administrator                                              One Administrator

Document No. 05863

Saïgon, [date] 1925

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** N° 05863
- **Signatures:** Two administrator signatures present (one legible as "O. Cavell")
- **Official Seal:** Red circular "SOCIÉTÉ ANONYME" stamp with border text
- **Date:** Saïgon, 1925
- **Border:** Ornate red decorative frame with floral and geometric motifs
- **Color scheme:** Red ornamental text and borders on pale green/cream stock
- **Additional markings:** Various stamped and printed notarial references (some partially illegible due to overlay)
