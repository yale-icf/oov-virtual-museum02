# Transcription of Chinese Republic 8% Railway Equipment Loan 1922 Treasury Note Coupon Sheet

## Document Header (repeated across all coupons):

**Original (French):**
GOUVERNEMENT DE LA REPUBLIQUE CHINOISE
EMPRUNT POUR LE MATERIEL DE CHEMIN DE FER
8% DE 1922

**English Translation:**
GOVERNMENT OF THE CHINESE REPUBLIC
LOAN FOR RAILWAY EQUIPMENT
8% OF 1922

## Coupon Details (visible on each coupon):

**Original:**
Première série de l'Emprunt de Spécialité de 40,000,000 de Livres Sterlings
Bon De Tresor De £20 Valeur 01086

**English Translation:**
First series of the Specialty Loan of 40,000,000 Pounds Sterling
Treasury Note for £20 Value 01086

## Coupon Numbers Visible:
Numbered 20 through 1 (from left to right, top to bottom):
- Top row: 20, 19, 18, 17, 16
- Second row: 15, 14, 13, 12, 11
- Third row: 10, 9, 8, 7, 6

Each coupon contains handwritten dates and signatures [mostly illegible due to image quality]

- Blue ornamental border frame around each coupon
- Serial/reference number visible: 01086
- Multiple handwritten signatures and dates (1930s era, largely illegible)
- Official stamps/seals (partially visible, details unclear)
