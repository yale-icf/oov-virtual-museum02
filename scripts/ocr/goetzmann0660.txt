## English Translation (Key Sections):

**Main Title:**
"KINGDOM OF GREECE
GOLD LOAN GUARANTEED AT 2½% OF 1898
To the honor of the Peoples of France, Great Britain and Russia
ANONYMOUS BEARER BOND 2,500 DRACHMAS
No. 43,842"

**Terms Summary:**
"This loan is guaranteed by France, Great Britain and Russia.

The capital and interest of this loan will be paid in Gold at the Bank of England and other European financial centers.

Interest is payable on the 15th of April and 15th of October of each year.

The bond will be redeemed by drawings by lot. The first drawing will take place within thirty years of the issue and will be finished by 1928.

If resources permit, by option of the Greek Government, a portion of the debt may be redeemed before the dates fixed by the drawing..."
