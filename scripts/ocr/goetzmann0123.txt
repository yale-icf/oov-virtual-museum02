# Three of Clubs - Dutch Windkaart Playing Card

**Original (Dutch):**

z Dagt voor mij'n Achtè winst een
heer lyk heidze kopen.
Maar, ach! zij doen ze 't end mij op
drie beenen lopen.

**English translation:**

I say, for my eighth profit a
gentleman-like dignity I would buy.
But, alas! they make me at the end walk on
three legs (i.e., with a cane/staff).

**Notable markings:**

- Number "3" with club symbol (♣) in upper right corner
- Decorative border frame around entire card
- Engraved illustration showing an elderly figure in 17th-century dress, wearing glasses and a hat, leaning on a long staff/cane, standing in a landscape with buildings in the background
- Small house visible in the middle distance on the left side
