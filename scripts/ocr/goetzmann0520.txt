# Transcription

## Original (Dutch/French):

**LEFT COLUMN (Dutch):**

CERTIFICAAT
Van 6 per Cents
RUSSISCHE FONDSEN
IN
ZILVER.

N°. 670.—

HOUDER dezes is geregtigd tot eene Inscriptie in het GRONDBOEK
nomens ƒ 6 per Cents Russische Fondsen, betaalbaar in ZILVER, ingeschreven in het Groot-Boek te St. Petersburg, ten name van het ADMINISTRATIEBURO, opgericht te Amsterdam, onder directie van

HOPE en COMP.,
KETWICH en VOOMBERGH,
EN
WED. W. BORSKI.

Waarvan de Interessen voor rekening en risico van den HOUDER en ontvangen en uitbetaald zullen worden op Coupons, ingevolge Art. 6 van het uitgegeven Bericht.

De HOUDER van dit Certificaat zal te allen tijde geregtigd zijn de originale INSCRIPTIE terug te vorderen, tegen terugave van dit Certificaat, met de onderwijl afgestelde HALVE JAARLIJKS COUPONS, tegen betaling van de onkosten, in Art. 10 van het Bericht vermeld.

Amsterdam, den 19 der Maand 1857

Gezien bij mij
S. Gimmelman
Notaris

Hierbij afgegeven Halfjarige COUPONS, tot 19 Juli 1859 en Calon.

## English translation:

**LEFT COLUMN:**

CERTIFICATE
Of 6 per Cent
RUSSIAN FUNDS
IN
SILVER.

No. 670.—

THE HOLDER of this is entitled to an Inscription in the REGISTER
in the name of ƒ 6 per Cent Russian Funds, payable in SILVER, registered in the Grand Book at St. Petersburg, in the name of the ADMINISTRATIVE BUREAU, established in Amsterdam, under the direction of

HOPE and COMP.,
KETWICH and VOOMBERGH,
AND
WED. W. BORSKI.

Of which the Interest for the account and risk of the HOLDER shall be received and paid on Coupons, according to Art. 6 of the published Notice.

The HOLDER of this Certificate shall at all times be entitled to reclaim the original INSCRIPTION in return, against return of this Certificate, with the half-yearly COUPONS detached in the meantime, against payment of the costs mentioned in Art. 10 of the Notice.

Amsterdam, the 19th of the Month 1857

Seen by me
S. Gimmelman
Notary

Half-yearly COUPONS hereby issued, up to 19 July 1859 and Calon.

---

**RIGHT COLUMN:**

CERTIFICATE
OF RUSSIAN INSCRIPTION
at 6 per Cent
IN
SILVER.

R° 500:—

THE BEARER is entitled to an Inscription of CENTRAL Rent with nominal capital at 6 per Cent, of the Russian Debt of Switzerland, payable in SILVER, inscribed on the Grand-Book at St. Petersburg, in the name of the ADMINISTRATIVE BUREAU, established in Amsterdam, under the direction of

HOPE and COMP.,
KETWICH and VOOMBERGH,
AND
Widow W. BORSKI.

Of which the Interest shall be received for account and risk of the BEARER and paid against delivery of the Coupons, according to Art. 6 of the Prospectus.

The BEARER of this Certificate may at any time reclaim the original INSCRIPTION, against return of the Certificate, with the Coupons not yet due belonging thereto, and the ACKNOWLEDGMENT annexed thereto to obtain payment of the FEES, upon payment of the costs mentioned in Art. 10 of the Prospectus.

Amsterdam, the 19th of May 1857

[Signature]
Ferdinand Mombault
Patr. Wooels.

Delivered Six-Month Income COUPONS, up to 19 July 1859 with Calon.

## Notable markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 670 (left side), R° 500 (right side)
- **Date:** 19 November 1857 (Amsterdam)
- **Signatures:** S. Gimmelman (Notary - left), Ferdinand Mombault and Patr. Wooels (right)
- **Coupon validity:** Half-yearly coupons issued until 19 July 1859
- **Decorative border:** Ornamental frame in classical style surrounding entire document
- **Language:** Bilingual Dutch and French text in parallel columns
- **Denomination:** 500 Roubles in Silver (Zilver/Argent)
- **Interest rate:** 6% per annum
