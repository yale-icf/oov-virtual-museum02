# Transcription

**Order for _____ Dollars, at five Livres Tournois Dollar, No. 814**

United States of AMERICA, 21 Day of November 1778

At Thirty Days Sight of this First Bill, Second, Third and Fourth not paid, pay to Jeremiah Green Esqr
or Order, Sixty Dollars, in Three Hundred Livres Tournois, for Sinage on Money borrowed by the United States.

To the Comptroller or Commissioners of the
United State of America, at Paris.

Countersigned Nath. Appleton
Comptroller of the Commissioners' Loan-Office in the
State of Massachusetts Bay

H. Atkinson
(Treas. of Loans.)

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **814** (printed at top right and handwritten)
- Date: **21 Day of November 1778** (handwritten)
- Payee: **Jeremiah Green Esqr** (handwritten)
- Amount: **Sixty Dollars** / **Three Hundred Livres Tournois** (handwritten and printed)
- Signatures: **H. Atkinson** (Treasurer of Loans, signed in cursive on right)
- Countersignature: **Nath. Appleton** (Comptroller, countersigned)
- Decorative border elements on left and right margins
- Official printed header and formatting typical of Revolutionary War-era financial instruments
