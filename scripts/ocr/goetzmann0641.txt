## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** CC 04238717 (appears on all coupons and main bond)
- **Denomination:** 10,000 Dinars (Deset Hiljada Dinara)
- **Coupon Values:** Four coupons at 3,190 Dinars each
- **Issue Date:** 1. X 1987 (1 October 1987)
- **Coupon Payment Dates:** 1987-1990 (1987 implicit), 1990, 1991, 1992, 1993
- **Bilingual Text:** Serbian Cyrillic and Bosnian/Croatian Latin scripts
- **Photographic Portrait:** Group of five individuals (workers/youth) on bearer bond
- **Issuing Authority:** Republican Secretariat for Finance, Sarajevo
- **Government Seal:** Visible on upper portion of bearer bond
- **Printer:** Grafička Realizacija "Svjetlost" SaRS, Sarajevo (visible at bottom left of coupon sheet)
