# Transcription

**CONVERSION OF SPANISH DEBT.**

**Passive Stock.**

Certificate No. 457                                                £14. 3. 4

The BEARER hereof is entitled to the sum of Fourteen Pounds Three Shillings and Four Pence, SPANISH FIVE PER CENT. PASSIVE STOCK; and on the presentation of a sufficient number of these Certificates, forming together any of the undermentioned amounts, *we engage to deliver, in exchange,* a Bond of the Spanish Government.

**AMOUNTS OF THE BONDS.**

£42. 10. — £85. — £170. — £255. — £510. — £1020.

Entd. [illegible]                                                [illegible]

London, 4th May, 1835.
