# Transcription of New York Central Rail Road Company Bond

**UNITED STATES OF AMERICA.**

**No. 1890** | **STATE OF** | **NEW-YORK.**

**$1000.** | **$1000.**

---

## Main Text:

**The New-York Central Rail Road Company.**

*Hereby certify, that the person to whom this Certificate is issued, being a Stockholder in one or more of the several Railroad Companies authorized to be consolidated into one great Organization, under the Act forming the main road, is entitled for the consideration herein stipulated, to the sum of* **ONE THOUSAND DOLLARS**, *to be paid out of the revenues, arising from the said Road, and the maintaining their Head Office at the city of Thirty years in the... [text continues with bond terms], with interest from that day at the rate of **SIX per cent per annum**, payable semi-annually on the last day of May, and the first day of November in each year, in the manner of the mystive Interest Warrants hereby apportioned. Upon equal and similar being payable in the City of New York. This Certificate is only transferable on the books of the company upon surrender of this Certificate, and in written assignment, under the Act forming the consolidation of certain Rail Road Companies pursuant to the resolution, and in accordance with such proviso. The whole thereof to the...*

*In testimony whereof, this Certificate, in pursuance of the order of the Board of Directors of the said New-York Central Rail Road Company, and in behalf of the officers even signed by the President and Treasurer of the said Company, the first day of August, in the year eighteen hundred and fifty-eight.*
