# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Heer

De schop, en 't blad papier in de eene schaal gelegen,
Kan, door de Graversshoop, een goud beurs overwegen.

**English translation:**

Lord/Sir

The spade and the sheet of paper placed in one scale,
Can, through the Engraver's heap, outweigh a purse of gold.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Title at top: "Heer" (Lord/Sir)
- Spade symbol (♠) in top right corner
- Engraved illustration in woodcut or engraving style typical of 17th-century Dutch playing cards
- Text formatted as couplet/verse at bottom of card
- Part of "Pasquins Windkaart" series from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Theatre)
