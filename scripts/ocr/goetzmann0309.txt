**Notable markings:**

- **Red seal/stamp** in upper center of certificate (official authorization mark)
- **Serial number:** 385,529
- **Multiple handwritten signatures** on the main certificate
- **Dated:** Paris, 4 February 1886
- **Postmarks and revenue stamps** on the coupon sections indicating various payment dates (third and fourth installments)
- **Engraved decorative elements** including vignettes of canal engineering work and allegorical figures
- **Printer attribution:** "STERN, Graveur, PARIS" at bottom
