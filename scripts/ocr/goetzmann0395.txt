# Transcription

## Original (Dutch and Chinese):

**Left margin (vertical):**
REGERING VAN DE CHINEESCHE REPUBLIEK
8 pct. SCHULD LETTER VAN 1923
LUNG-TSING-U-HAI-SPOORWEG

**Top center (main heading):**
REGERING VAN DE CHINEESCHE REPUBLIEK
8 PCT. SCHATKISTBILLETTEN VAN 1923
LUNG-TSING-U-HAI-SPOORWEG
F. 16.667.000.

VERDEELD IN 16.667 BILLETTEN VAN F. 1000.

**Center section:**
SCHATKISTBILJET AAN TOONDER N° 11638 GROOT
F. 1000.-NEDERLANDSCH COURANT

**Additional text at bottom left:**
N.V. NEDERLANDSCH SYNDICAAT VOOR CHINA

**Notable text block (lower portion):**
8 % - DE PAGAMENTI DEN INTEREST EN DE WINSTBEWIJS MOETEN PLAATS-HEBBEN...
[followed by extensive terms and conditions in Dutch]

## English translation:

**Left margin:**
GOVERNMENT OF THE CHINESE REPUBLIC
8% BOND CERTIFICATE OF 1923
LUNG-TSING-U-HAI RAILWAY

**Top center:**
GOVERNMENT OF THE CHINESE REPUBLIC
8% TREASURY BILLS OF 1923
LUNG-TSING-U-HAI RAILWAY
F. 16,667,000.

DIVIDED INTO 16,667 BILLS OF F. 1,000 EACH.

**Center:**
BEARER TREASURY BILL NO. 11638 FOR
F. 1,000.-DUTCH GUILDER CURRENCY

---

## Notable markings:

- **Serial number:** No. 11638
- **Signatures:** Two handwritten signatures visible (lower left and center-right)
- **Red seals:** Two Chinese character seals (chops) in red ink
- **Date:** 1923
- **Printer mark:** N.V. NEDERLANDSCH SYNDICAAT VOOR CHINA (lower center)
- **Ornamental borders:** Elaborate teal and red decorative geometric and floral patterns framing the entire document
- **Central vignette:** Landscape engraving showing a bridge and railway infrastructure (top center)
