# Transcription

**Title:** Insurance on Horses

**Main Text:**
You that keep Horses to preserve your Life,
And Pads to please your Wives, and Mistresses,
Insure their Lives, and if they Die we'll make
Full Satisfaction, or be bound to Break.

**Notable markings:**
- Four of Diamonds suit symbol (red diamond) in upper left corner
- Playing card format typical of South Sea Bubble satire cards from early 18th century
- Engraved illustration showing men examining dead horses with standing figures and a mounted horse in background
- Historical print with hand-colored elements (red diamonds on card suit)
