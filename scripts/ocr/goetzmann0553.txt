**SIX PERCENT FIRST MORTGAGE DEBENTURE.**

NEW RUSSIA COMPANY, LIMITED [Hereafter called the Company]
will on the 1st day of October aforesaid and on similar date in the principal amount therein mentioned (if punctually paid) in accordance with the conditions endorsed hereon, pay to the bearer of this Debenture unless then registered and in such case to the registered holder hereof the sum of £100 sterling in London and the Company will pay interest thereon at the rate of 6 per cent per annum, by equal half yearly payments on the 2nd April and the 1st day of October in each year in accordance with the coupons annexed hereto unless forfeited by non-presentation and surrender of such Debenture.

By virtue of the Trust Deed mentioned below these Debentures are specially secured upon a portion of the company's property consisting of estates in the Government of Catherinaslav, Russia; on part of which property are situated the principal Collieries and works of the Company. The said estates comprise an area of about 1,300 acres and are more particularly described in the Deed of Mortgage and are issued upon and subject to the conditions endorsed hereon and to those contained in the Deed of Trust dated the 22nd day of March, 1901, and made between the Company of the one part and Mr. Right Honourable Alfred Lyttleton, M.A.; M. P., and Charles Cohn, Marine Eng'r, by the other part, and on the condition that the bearer or registered holder hereof has the same may do, stand thereby.

Given under the Common Seal of the Company
this sixth day of April 1901.
