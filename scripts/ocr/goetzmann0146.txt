# Transcription

**CHARON.**

HERE's CHARON, the Ferryman, come for his fee !
Let's try if the surly old rogue will agree
To postpone my passage still longer, and take
A plate of roast beef, or a piece of Twelfth Cake ;
'Tis hard to depart from a table well spread,
O'er Stygian streams to be row'd with the dead !
More willing would I be to gain, if I stay,
A Prize in the Lott'ry next Valentine's Day.
