# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

t Dusster
Lachebock

Zo min als ik den loop der zeven hooft
Planeten
Versta; zo min kan ik het eind der
Acker sweten.

**English translation:**

The Dark
Laughing book

As little as I understand the course of the seven chief
Planets,
So little can I understand the end of
Earthly labors.

**Notable markings:**

- "7" in upper right corner (card designation - Seven of Diamonds)
- Diamond symbol in upper right
- Engraved/woodcut illustration showing a man in 17th-century dress holding what appears to be a astronomical or mathematical document
- The image is part of "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Cards) from "Het Groote Tafereel" - a Dutch satirical playing card series
- Page number indicates this is card 39 of 55
