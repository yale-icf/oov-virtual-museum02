**Main Body:**

United States of AMERICA, 12 Day of November 1778

AT Thirty Days Sight of this First Bill, Second, Third and Fourth
not paid, pay to Jesse White
or Order, Thirty-six Dollars, in One Hundred, and Eighty Livres
Tournois, for Interest due on Money borrowed by the United States.

To the Commissioner or Commissioners of the
United States of America, at Paris.

Countersigned, Nath. Appleton
Commissioner of the Continental Loan-Office in the
State of Massachusetts Bay
