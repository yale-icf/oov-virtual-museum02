# Transcription: Portuguese External Debt Certificate of Interest

**LETTER B.**

**Portuguese External Debt D°.6944**

## CERTIFICATE OF INTEREST

ON THE SUM OF £100, CAPITAL OF THE THREE PER CENT. BOND NUMBER AS ABOVE.

---

By virtue of the authority granted to the undersigned by the Decree of His Majesty the King of Portugal, of which the following is a Translation:

**"DECREE.**

"Making use of the authority granted to my Government by Article 2nd of the law of 26th instant: I am pleased to grant to the holders of Three per Cent. Bonds of the External Debt issued under the Decree of 18th December, 1852 (the documents of the Deferred Debt not included) the eventual right to an increase of Capital: the right to an increase of such Bonds, to which they are to correspond—and to take place only under the hypothesis mentioned in said article. The Board of Public Credit will cause to be signed by the Financial Agency in London the Certificates alluded to in the present Decree, which are to be signed by the Councillor Guterres Candido of the War Department, and will have it thus method, and cause the same to be carried into effect. *Palace at Cintra on the 28th July, 1856.*" THE KING.

"Countersigned, JOSÉ JORGE LOUREIRO."

---

I, the Financial Agent of the Portuguese Government in London, do hereby certify that the holder of the Bond Letter B, No. 6944..........., of £100, issued under the Decree of 18th December, 1852, will be entitled to receive an additional annual interest at the rate of one per cent. per annum, or any smaller sum, upon the capital of said Bond, in terms of and in the case provided for in Article 2nd of the Law of 26th July, 1866, transcribed below, and of the agreement entered into in London on the 13th December, 1855.

---

We, Dom Pedro, by the grace of God, King of Portugal, and Algarves, make known to all our subjects that the Cortes have decreed, and we have sanctioned the following law:—

**ART. 1st.**

I. Decree [text regarding postponement of the Three per Cent. Bonds of the various Classes of Documents of the External Funded Debt, in addition to the terms of the Decree of the 18th December, 1852] [text regarding nominal Capital in Deferred Bonds with interest at the rate of three per cent. per annum from January 1850, for over £100 of nominal Capital of said.]

2nd. To the holders of Four per Cent. Bonds issued under the law of the 1st of April, 1854, £2 nominal Capital in Deferred Bonds, with interest at the rate of four per cent. per annum from the 1st of January, 1858, for over £100 of nominal Bonds of said.

3rd. To the holders of Debentures, £100 nominal Capital in Bonds of three per cent. per annum from the 1st of January of the present year, for every £100 nominal Capital of said.

**The Government is likewise authorized to grant to the holders of the External Three per Cent. Debt issued under the Decree of 18th December, 1852, in exchange for the**

[Additional articles continue with detailed provisions regarding interest payments, railways, concessionary arrangements, and revocation of prior laws]

**ART. 5th.**

The agreement entered into in London on the 13th December, 1855, between the Finance Minister and Mr. Richard Thornton, is hereby approved, with reference to the terms specified.

---

Signed with signature and seal:

**JOSÉ JORGE LOUREIRO.**
(Sealed with the Large Seal of the Royal Arms.)

---

**WHIFFING, LONDON.**
