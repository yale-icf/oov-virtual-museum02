It has been agreed and settled as follows:

1° The Social Company Y. ROFÉ & Co. sells to the contracting party of the second part, who accepts:

**Suez Canal Bonds 3% 2nd Series**

(the numbers will be designated in the definitive deed in accordance with art. 3)

at the fixed price of P.T. _________________
payable in the following manner:
P.T. _________________ in cash, upon signature of the present deed and the balance in _________________
installments of P.T. _________________ each, the last of which being P.T. _________________ due on the first of
each month, beginning the following month.

2° The present preliminary deed shall bind the House Y. ROFÉ & Co. only after confirmation by it notified to the purchaser by separate letter.

This letter shall concern the principal conditions of the present contract.

The purchaser shall, in case he should find any difference whatsoever between the conditions of the present contract and those which shall be stated in the confirmation letter, notify his claims by registered letter to the House Y. ROFÉ & Co., within 30 days following the date of the Confirmation letter. In the absence of which the conditions stated in this last letter shall be irrevocable to the purchaser.

3° After payment of the final installment of the agreed price, the House Y. ROFÉ & Co. shall deliver to the purchaser, with a regularly receipted note which shall constitute the definitive deed of sale, the bond certificates mentioned, the ownership of which shall be acquired at that moment only and lawfully under Art. 338 of the Mixed Civil Code.

The delivery of the aforementioned bonds shall take place exclusively at the Registered Office of the Social Company Y. ROFÉ & Co., in Cairo, 15 days after payment of the balance of the agreed price.

The purchaser shall return to the House Y. ROFÉ & Co. the present contract together with all related documents, accompanied by a regular discharge signed by him establishing receipt of the bonds.
