## BOND TEXT (Main Body)

1556 BROADWAY CORPORATION, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York (hereinafter called "the Corporation"), for value received, acknowledges itself indebted and hereby promises to pay to the bearer hereof, or if this bond be registered, to the registered holder hereof, on the first day of April, 1963 (unless this bond shall have been called for previous redemption as provided herein, and in the indenture hereinafter mentioned),

and to pay interest thereon at the rate of six per cent. (6%) per annum, payable semi-annually on the first day of April and the first day of October in each year, and such interest hereon until maturity hereof (unless this bond shall have been called for previous redemption) being payable only upon surrender of the interest coupons hereto attached as they severally mature.

This bond is one of a series of bonds issued under and pursuant to an indenture dated the principal office of Interstate Trust Company, in the Borough of Manhattan, City and State of New York, or at the option of the bearer or registered holder hereof, in the manner and upon the conditions and subject to the limitations provided in the indenture herein, any taxes which, under any present or future law of any State, Territory or possession of the United States of America, or of the District of Columbia, the bearer or holder hereof, as a resident of any said state, territory or possession, is, or shall be, obligated to pay by reason of the ownership hereof, upon or on account of the principal or interest of this bond, shall be paid by the Corporation, as an additional sum to the dollar of the principal amount hereof in any year, provided that application for such reimbursement shall be made to the Corporation as provided in the provisions above referred to by [illegible].

For the payment of any such tax, or in case default be made in the payment of any interest hereon as it First Mortgage Leasehold Sinking Fund Gold Bonds, all of like tenor (except as to designated number and denomination), issued and to be issued in an aggregate principal amount equal to Seven Million Four Hundred Thousand Dollars ($7,400,000) at any time outstanding, under the indenture dated the 1st day of April, 1928 (hereinafter referred to as the "Indenture"), and duly executed and delivered, mortgaging, transferring and conveying unto Irtus [Trustees?] of Columbia University in the City of New York, as lessor, to Bethlehem Engineering Corporation, as lessee, dated February [date], [illegible], mortgaging, transferring and conveying unto [Trustees?] of Columbia University in the City of New York as lessor to Bethlehem Engineering Corporation, as lessee, by assignment dated May 20, 1924, recorded in the Office of the Register of New York County on July 11, 1924 as Liber 3456 [illegible]; and also by leases from the Trustees of Columbia University, in the City of New York, as lessor, to Bethlehem [Engineering Corporation?], as lessee, all the real property situate, lying and being in the Borough of Manhattan, City and State of New York, and more particularly described in the mortgages aforesaid, together with all the improvements thereon, and all the appurtenances thereto, all of which real property is particularly described in the mortgage, and the rights and interests thereon is payable at the office of agency of the Corporation, in the Borough of Manhattan, City and State of New York, or at the option of the bearer or registered holder hereof, at the principal office of Interstate Trust Company, in the Borough of Manhattan, City and State of New York.

[The text continues with extensive legal terms regarding the sinking fund, redemption conditions, and bond holder rights. Much of this middle section is overprinted with red text that is largely illegible in the scan]

[Continuing from legible portions:]

...This bond shall pass by delivery and registration by the Trustee hereto until such registration by being in like manner transferred to bearer and thereupon transferability by delivery shall be restored, but this bond may again and from time to time be registered and transferred to bearer as before. The registered holder hereof shall be entitled to the benefit of the lien created by the mortgage hereinabove mentioned, which shall continue to be transferable by delivery, and payment to the bearer thereof shall discharge the Corporation with respect to the principal and interest thereon.

[Additional legal text regarding bond conditions, the Corporation's representations, and transferability...]

This bond shall not be entitled to any benefit hereof, and shall not be valid as against any purpose until it shall have been authenticated by the execution of the certificate hereon endorsed by Interstate Trust Company, under said indenture.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, 1556 Broadway Corporation has caused this bond to be signed by its President or a Vice-President, and its corporate seal to be hereunto affixed and attested by a Secretary or an Assistant Secretary and coupons for such interest, bearing the facsimile signature of its Treasurer, to be hereto attached, all as of the first day of April, 1928.

1556 BROADWAY CORPORATION.
