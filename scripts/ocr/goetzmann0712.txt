COUNCIL OF PUBLIC DEBT DISTRIBUTED OF THE FORMER OTTOMAN EMPIRE

Receipt to Bearer No. 125,515

of a nominal amount of { Ltqs 4.40
                       { £ 4.—
                       { Frs 100.—

Issued by the Council of Public Debt Distributed of the Former Ottoman Empire, in accordance with the provisions of Article IV, paragraph a) of the Contract entered into on June 13, 1928 between the Government of the Turkish Republic and the Representatives of Holders of Ottoman Public Debt, ratified by the Grand National Assembly of Turkey under the terms of a law voted on December 1, 1928.

EXCHANGEABLE

against a Bearer Title of

Representative Bonds of Arrears of Annuities of Ottoman Public Debt "Series A" 1928

In the event of failure to exchange within a maximum period of one year from the date of its issuance, the present Receipt shall be prescribed for the benefit of the debtor States.

It is incumbent upon the interested bearers to group together in order to present for exchange Receipts of a total amount corresponding to the nominal amount of Ltqs 22, or £ 20, or Frs 500 of the unit titles of the Representative Bonds of Series A.

The present Receipt is created only for the purposes of the banking operation of exchange. It cannot be considered as a credit title. It is not productive of interest.

AMSTERDAM April 22, 1930 — 19

For the BANK OF PARIS AND THE LOW COUNTRIES
(Bank Head Office)
BY PROXY

FOR THE COUNCIL OF PUBLIC DEBT DISTRIBUTED
OF THE FORMER OTTOMAN EMPIRE

The President,
