# Transcription

**JOINT ACCOUNT** | Consolidated £3 *per Cent.* Annuities.
**ACCEPTANCE AND DIVIDENDS.**

*[Dated]* 25th 1831

Know all Men by these Presents, That We *James Woldswyn of Eyelt Brokham Road Edwarg Palombes Clarke of Leyningfiam Lodgh in Norfolk Solicitors* 

do jointly and each of us doth separately for ourselves, and for the Survivor of us, make, constitute, and appoint *David Brown Wolseley Barclay Chefter Lee Benan Henry Turchell Barclay Simon and [unclear] Sutton James all of London Bankers*

our true and lawful Attorney, *[manuscript insertion - illegible]* for us and in our Names and on our behalf, and also for and in the Name and on the behalf of the Survivor of us, to accept all such Transfers, as are or may hereafter be made unto us, of any Interest on Share in the Capital or Joint Stock of the £3 *per Cent. Annuities,* erected by an Act of Parliament of the Twenty-fifth Year of the Reign of His Majesty King George II. entitled, *[text continues]* transferable at the BANK OF ENGLAND, to be charged on the Sinking Fund, &c. and by several subsequent Acts. Also to receive and give Receipts for all DIVIDENDS that are now due, and that shall hereafter become due and payable for the same, for the Time being.

And to do all lawful Acts requisite for effecting the Premises; hereby ratifying and confirming all that *[manuscript insertion - illegible]* shall do therein by Virtue hereof. And in Case of our Deaths, *Attorney* *[manuscript insertion - illegible]* by virtue of, or under Colour, or in pursuance thereof; shall so far as the Governor and Company of the Bank of England are interested or concerned, be as binding upon our respective Executors and Administrators, as the same would have been upon us if living, unless notice in writing of our Deaths, shall have been given previously to the said Governor and Company of the Bank of England: and unless such Notice be given, we hereby severally covenant promise and engage and bind ourselves and our respective Executors and Administrators, to and with the said Governor and Company of the Bank of England, that our respective Executors and Administrators, shall and do allow, ratify and confirm, as good, valid and effectual, Attorney *[manuscript insertion - illegible]* after our decease, so far as the said Governor and Company of the Bank of England, shall or may be in any way or manner interested therein. In Witness whereof we have hereunto set our Hands and Seals the

Day of *[blank]* in the Year of our Lord One Thousand Eight Hundred and Thirty *[illegible]*

*Signed, sealed, and delivered, in the Presence of us by the above-named.*

---

**N.B.** If executed out of Great Britain to be attested by His Majesty's Consul, or Vice Consul, a Magistrate, or Notary Public, in addition to the usual Witnesses.

You are desired to attend to the Instructions for executing Letters of Attorney which are on the other side.

*Printed at the Bank of England, J. Oldham Superintendent.*
