# Transcription of The Common Fund Company Limited Scrip Certificate

**Header Text:**
100 | 100

**Main Body Text:**

The Holder of this SCRIP has the right to

CALL FOR

ONE HUNDRED SHARES of £20 each

THE COMMON FUND COMPANY LIMITED

and to receive Certificate of Full Stock on paying to the Company the par cost of Twenty Pounds per share, in one sum.

**Date and Signature:**
London, 20th December, 1869

for the Company

R.W. Millz, Registrar.

**Side Panels (Left):**
CONVERTIBLE INTO STOCK AT PAR BY ALL THE COMPANY'S BANKERS IN EUROPE AND AMERICA

OFFICES: WASHINGTON, BERLIN, PARIS, LONDON

**Bottom Text:**
INCORPORATED UNDER THE COMPANIES' ACTS, 1862-67.

**Footer Attribution:**
ALFD DOUBLE & CO'S, SERGEANTS INN & 40, FLEET ST. LONDON

**Serial Number:**
H170 (appears twice, upper left and upper right)

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **H170** (printed in red)
- Signature: **R.W. Millz, Registrar** (handwritten)
- Decorative engraved globe illustration (center lower) showing North and South America
- Ornamental border frames on all four corners
- Multiple decorative vignettes on left margin (heraldic and allegorical designs)
- Cherub/angel figure in upper center
