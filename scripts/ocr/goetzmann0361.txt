# Transcription

**Original:**

DUCATI 100                                    DUCATI CENTO

CEDOLA

Del Banco giro di Venezia
Che Sarà ricevuta Come dinaro effettivo
da tutte le Casse Regie
e Pubbliche
in pagamento delle Contribuzioni
daziarie fondar ie e qualunque altra
Come pure dalla Cassa del Banco giro
per il Cambio in partita.

IN CORRELAZIONE
del proclama del primo Ottobre 1798.

PER DUCATI CENTO
da L. 8.

[Signatures] Giovannelli        Porzi

Schiantavello

N. 1175

---

**English translation:**

DUCATS 100                                    DUCATS HUNDRED

CEDULA

Of the Banco Giro of Venice
Which Shall be received As effective money
by all Royal
and Public Cash Offices
in payment of contributions
customs, land taxes, and any other
As well as by the Cash Office of the Banco Giro
for exchange in account.

IN CORRELATION
with the proclamation of October 1, 1798.

FOR DUCATS ONE HUNDRED
at L. 8.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Two imperial double-headed eagle seals/emblems (Habsburg)
- Serial number: N. 1175
- Two signatures: Giovannelli and Schiantavello (with unclear middle signature)
- Ornate decorative border on left margin
- Handwritten monetary notation and signatures
