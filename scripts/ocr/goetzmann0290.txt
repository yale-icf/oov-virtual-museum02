**Page 94 - Anno Regni vicefimo quarto Georgii II. Regis.**

And whereas feveral Perfons, not being timely apprized of the Notice given by Advertifement in their feveral Annuities, being in his Majefty's Colonies in America, and other Parts beyond the Seas, of from unavoidable Accidents have been deprived inheriting the fame; be it therefore enacted by the Authority aforefaid, That the Sums which were ftanding in the refpective Names of the following Perfons, on the Twenty eighth Day of February, One thousand feven hundred and forty nine, fhall be intitled to the Benefit of the Claufe of the Houfe of Commons, paffed the Twenty ninth Day of November, One thousand feven hundred and forty nine, videlicet, Charles Aythorpe of Bofton in New England, to the Sum of Five thoufand three hundred twenty eight Pounds in Bank Annuities, John Erving of the fame Place to the Sum of Six thoufand Pounds in Bank Annuities, Daniel Huger of South Carolina to the Sum of Eight thoufand Pounds in Old South Sea Annuities, John Smith to the Sum of Eleven thoufand Pounds in Old South Sea Annuities, and to the Sum of Two thoufand one hundred Pounds in New South Sea Annuities, inftead of the like Sums fubfcribed to the Second Revolution paffed in the fame Year, Ralph Willet to the Sum of Three thoufand Pounds in Old South Sea Annuities, Elias Mofes Defortis to the Sum of Three thoufand one hundred and fifty one Pounds, Joint Stock in the New South Sea Annuities, Hugh Holmes to the Sum of Four thoufand three hundred Pounds Old South Sea Annuities, John Saunderfen to Five hundred Pounds Old South Sea Annuities, John Armftrong to the Sum of Two hundred and twenty five Pounds Old South Sea Annuities, John Jolly to the Sum of One thoufand Pounds Old South Sea Annuities, Two thoufand Pounds New South Sea Annuities, One thoufand Pounds Bank Annuities, of the year One thoufand feven hundred and forty feven, and One thoufand Pounds Lottery Annuities, of the Year One thoufand feven hundred and forty feven, in as full and ample Manner, as if they had feverally and refpectively received the faid Terms on or before the faid Twenty eighth Day of February, One thoufand feven hundred and forty nine.

And be it further enacted by the Authority aforefaid, That the Sums that were ftanding in the Joint Names of Meffieurs Samuel Edwards Decratch, and Hugh Briggs, now Sir Hugh Briggs Baronet, on the Twenty eighth Day of February, One thoufand feven hundred and forty nine, fhall be intitled to the Benefit of the Claufe of the Houfe of Commons, paffed the Twenty first Day of March, One thoufand feven hundred and forty nine, videlicet, Twelve
