# Transcription

To know all men by these Presents that I Calvin Austin of Suffield in the County of Hartford and State of Connecticut for the Consideration of Eight Thousand Dollars unto to my full satisfaction of James K. Garnsey of the City and County of Hartford and State aforesaid, do herby [sic] assign and transfer unto the Said Garnsey the annuled [sic] Certificate for Three Thousand Twelve hundred Thousandths of the Connecticut Western Reserve (So called) to which I am entitled under the Deed of Trust and Articles of Agreement of the Connecticut Land Company to have and to hold the same Certificate for Three Thousand Twelve hun dred Thousandths to the Said of Garnsey his heirs and assigns, Subject to the Terms, Conditions, Covenants and Exceptions contained in said Deed of Trust and Articles of Agreement.... And I hereby by myself my heirs Executors, and Administrators that at and until the enjoying and delivering of this Present I am will entitled to the assigned hum bers, and have good right to see assign and transfer the same in manner aforesaid... In Witness Whereof I have hereunto Signed my Name and affixed my Seal this Twentieth Day of December in the year Seventeen hundred and Ninety Six—

Signed sealed and delivered
in presence of —
**Calvin Austin**

[Witnesses:]
Ebenezer Gaulking
Alfred Boyd

---

Hartford County, p. Suffield, Dec. 20th 1795. Personally appeared Calvin Austin signer and sealer of the above instrument, and acknowledged the same to be his free act and deed

Before me — [unclear] Hatheway [unclear] State
