**Notable markings:**

- **Serial number:** No. 032,638
- **Two signatures:** P. de Cordonat (left) and one illegible signature (right)
- **Official seal:** Blue circular stamp (center-left) with coat of arms and text regarding stamp duty
- **Orange circular stamp:** (center-right) indicating stamp duty paid by subscription
- **Printer credit:** Marcel Charles Verneau S.C.Y. Paris (bottom)
- **Decorative elements:** Two engraved tropical landscape scenes (top left and right), central map silhouette of Phu Quoc island, and landscape photograph (bottom)
