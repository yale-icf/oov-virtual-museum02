## English Translation:

**FRANCO-PERUVIAN SOCIETY**

OF THE MINES OF THE PROVINCE OF CASTRO-VIRREYNA

IN COMMANDITE

UNDER THE BUSINESS NAME: CROSNIER and Co.

Constituted by deed before Mr. POULD and his Colleague, notaries in Paris, on April 8, 1833

---

No. 1,735 | 500 FR.

**Share of Five Hundred Francs**

Issued to Mr. Blaryez François Charles Bouguier
residing in Paris, Rue d'Argenteuil No. 22

Paris, January 1st, 1852

THE CONTROLLER FOR THE FRANCO-PERUVIAN SOCIETY, THE MANAGER,

[Signature] A. Fauld | [Signature]

**Repaid by the Liquidator**

---

**EXCERPT FROM THE STATUTES**

ART. 7. The social fund is fixed at the sum of 1,000,000 francs; it will be divided into 2,000 shares of 500 francs each.

[Additional statute text follows in two columns]
