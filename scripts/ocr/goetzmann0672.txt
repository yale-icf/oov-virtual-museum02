## English Translation:

IMPERIAL RUSSIAN GOVERNMENT.
NIKOLAEVSKY RAILWAY.

ISSUE
OF 600,000 OBLIGATIONS,

each
OF ONE HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE RUBLES,
or FIVE HUNDRED FRANCS, or TWENTY POUNDS STERLING,
or TWO HUNDRED THIRTY-SIX DUTCH GUILDERS,
of which 400,000 obligations one per sheet, numbered №1-400,000,
and 200,000 five per sheet, numbered №400,001-600,000,

for initial capital of
75,000,000 RUBLES, or 300,000,000 FRANCS, or 12,000,000 POUNDS STERLING, or 141,600,000 GOLD GUILDERS.

This issue is authorized by IMPERIAL Ukase of 15 June 1867. (*)

OBLIGATION

№207521

for 125 rubles = 500 francs = 20 pounds sterling = 236 guilders.

[The obligation gives the holder the right to receive at designated banks the interest and principal according to terms specified herein. Interest payments at 4% annually, payable semi-annually on 2/14 January and 2/14 July...]

[Terms specify payment locations in St. Petersburg, Paris, London, Amsterdam, and other financial centers]

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** №207521
- **Red Circular Stamp:** "50 COMPLEMENT / DE DROIT / 9 PARIS 12 / 1868" (50 Francs tax/duty mark, Paris, 1868)
- **Imperial Eagle Coat of Arms:** Central emblem with crown
- **Circular State Seal:** Bottom right and left corners
- **Date:** 1867
- **Signatures:** Two signatures at bottom (illegible but present)
- **Ornate Decorative Border:** Elaborate frame with geometric and circular patterns throughout
- **Multiple Currency Conversions:** Listed in rubles, francs, pounds sterling, and guilders
