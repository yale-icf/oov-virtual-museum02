# Transcription of Peruvian Republic Congressional War Loan Certificate (1823)

## Original Text (Spanish):

De la fecha en ___ mes- serán satisfechos por la tesorería de la Cámara de Comercio, renta y [unclear] ___ pesos con mas su respectivo interés al cinco por ciento mensual á D. Santiago Campo — por ser-parte de la accion de mil [unclear] ___, con que ha contribuido en el emprestito de cien mil pesos determinado por el Soberano Congreso para subvenir á las necesidades públicas, y dar al ejército un impulso capaz de terminar la guerra; y cuyo pago de principal y reditos será irremisiblemente practicado con preferencia á toda otra deuda nacional. Lima [unclear] Enero de 1823. 4.° 2.°

[Lower left section contains handwritten notations and signatures that are largely illegible]

[Dated notation]: 24 Mayo 1823

[Signature]: [unclear signature]

[Additional notation at bottom]: S° Sem° Campaor [or similar]

## English Translation:

From the date in ___ month- will be satisfied by the treasury of the Chamber of Commerce, income and [unclear] ___ pesos with its respective interest at five per cent per month to D. Santiago Campo — for being part of the action of one thousand [unclear] ___, with which he has contributed to the loan of one hundred thousand pesos determined by the Sovereign Congress to meet public needs and give the army an impetus capable of ending the war; and whose payment of principal and interest will be irremissibly executed with preference over all other national debt. Lima [unclear] January 1823. 4.° 2.°

## Notable Markings:

- **Official seal** at top left (circular seal of the Republic of Peru)
- **Handwritten entries** and signatures throughout (many illegible due to age and script)
- **Serial/reference numbers** in margins: "15.12" (top right)
- **Multiple signatures** at bottom, including what appears to be "Santiago Campo"
- **Date notations**: January 1823, May 24, 1823
- Document reference codes: "4.° 2.°"
