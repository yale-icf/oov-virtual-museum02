# Transcription

**Title (top):** York Buildings

**Main text (bottom):**
You that are blest with Wealth, by your Creator,
And want to drown your Money in Thames Water;
Buy but York Buildings, and the Cistern there,—
Will sink more Pence, than any Fool can spare.

**Heraldic element (top left):**
Shield containing five trees (spade symbols)

**Notable markings:**
- This is a playing card from the South Sea Bubble series
- Identified as Five of Spades
- Heraldic coat of arms in upper left corner featuring five trees arranged in a shield
- Flag or banner visible in the building illustration reading "York Building"
- Hand-colored engraving with period satirical commentary on the York Buildings Company investment scheme
