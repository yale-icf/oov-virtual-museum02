## English Translation:

**Header:**
189 RUB. [left] | 189 rhs. [right]

**Main Title:**
4½% BOND LOAN OF 1912
SOCIETY OF THE
KAHETIAN RAILWAY COMPANY

for a nominal capital of 13,230,000 rubles = 1,400,000 pounds sterling,
with unconditional guarantee of the IMPERIAL Russian Government
regarding the indicated interest payment and redemption of the bonds.

**Bond Details:**
No. A 14533                    BOND                    No. A 14533

FOR ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY-NINE RUBLES - 20 POUNDS STERLING
TO THE BEARER.

[Body text describes the terms: The bond is secured by the property and revenues of the Kaketian Railway Company, guaranteed by the Imperial Russian Government. Interest is paid semi-annually at 4½%. The principal and interest are exempt from Russian taxes. Payment occurs on February 16/March 1 and August 19/September 1 of each year. Located in St. Petersburg, 1912]

**Payment Schedule:**
Interest payable on February 16/March 1 and August 19/September 1 of each year.
St. Petersburg, 1912

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. A 14533 (appears twice, left and right)
- **Imperial Seal:** Double-headed eagle (Российский герб) in circular stamp, top left and right
- **Ministry Seal:** М.Ф. (Ministry of Finance) seal visible in top right corner
- **Decorative Elements:** Ornate Art Nouveau border with geometric and floral patterns
- **Text Stamps:** "ЧЕТЫРЕ ШИЛЛИНГА" (Four Shillings) stamp in red, top right
- **Signatures:** Multiple handwritten signatures visible at bottom (appears to be officials of the Kahetian Railway Company and Imperial delegates)
- **Denominations:** Marked as "189 РУБ." / "189 rhs." (rubles) and "£20s." in corner medallions
- **Condition:** Document shows age with some wear and faint markings typical of 1912-era bonds
