# La Platense Flotilla Company Limited - Share Warrant Transcription

## Main Certificate Text

**LA PLATENSE FLOTILLA COMPANY, LIMITED**

SHARE CAPITAL £1,000,000.          CAPITAL ACTIONS £1,000,000.

100,000 SHARES OF £10 EACH FULLY PAID UP          DIVISÉ EN 100,000 ACTIONS DE £10 ENTIÈREMENT LIBÉRÉES

Share Warrant to Bearer for ONE SHARE No 15821          Certificat au Porteur D'UNE Action No 15821

This is to Certify that the Bearer of this [warrant] is the Proprietor of ONE fully paid Certificat est Propriétaire dans Action at Borse numbered as above in the Capital of the Company thereof and is entitled to the Inheritance and all other Regulations of the Company for the time being.

La Platense Flotilla Company Limited.          La Platense Flotilla Company Limited.

Given under our hand at [location] this 3rd hand 1896

[Signatures]
**JENNY [?]** - Chairman
**W.P. Witheren** - Secretary

Warrant Number: **821**
