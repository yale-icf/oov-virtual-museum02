**English Translation:**

I, the undersigned KORNELIS VAN DEN HELM BODDAERT,
as Director and qualified [representative] of the money-lenders (geld-Opschieters) for various Plantations in the Colony of Essequibo and Demerara, according to the Mortgages filed at the Secretariat there, in proper Order for the benefit of this fund, having been executed by the Company's Seal and signed by the Secretary of the Colony under my supervision, I acknowledge in this capacity, according to the printed Project and Condition (the contents of which are written here below) to have received

from [name]

A Sum of [Five Hundred Pounds at slant or similar amount]

Which sum I, the undersigned, promise to the same [person] or his heirs and rightful claimants to refund after the expiration of the term in the Plan mentioned here below, and meanwhile shall pay out the Interest at Five percent per annum commencing primo January 1760, reducing accordingly year by year, until the full and Effectual payment of the Mortgages, of which the first expiration shall be on primo January 1760, and so continuing every year, and that of all free-money without any deduction from Capital or Interest to be able to pay, the Interests being paid at the Office of the undersigned, to the fulfillment of all which the Moneylenders above their special Mortgages have bound, their Persons and goods, under constraint according to Law.

In Witness thereof signed by me
Middelburg [month] 1760
[Various later endorsements and annotations]

Conform Art: 5 paid for 3 Years [due] January 1770

[Signatures:]
Johann Phillip Ste.                    [?] Schoraitz
