# Transcription of Buenos Aires Bond Document

## Original (Spanish):

**DEUDA INTERNA**
DE LA PROVINCIA DE BUENOS AIRES

SERIE A

**100 CIEN PESOS**
A MONEDA NACIONAL

ESTE TITULO GAMA EL 5% DE INTERÉS ANUAL

T-1 1/3 DE AMORTIZACIÓN ANUAL ACUMULATIVA

Los intereses se pagarán por trimestres vencidos en las oficinas de Tesorería de la Provincia, en la Capital y demás ciudades donde haya recaudadores, cada tres meses contados del día de su emisión, en conformidad con las prescripciones del decreto respectivo.

La Plata, [fecha legible]

[Multiple coupon stamps visible on right side and bottom, each marked:]
$100$ $100$
133,050
[Serial numbers varying: A 147, A 146, A 145, etc.]
5% 1 3

---

## English Translation:

**INTERNAL DEBT**
OF THE PROVINCE OF BUENOS AIRES

SERIES A

**100 ONE HUNDRED PESOS**
IN NATIONAL CURRENCY

THIS TITLE EARNS 5% ANNUAL INTEREST

1/3 ANNUAL CUMULATIVE AMORTIZATION

Interest shall be paid quarterly on due dates at the offices of the Treasury of the Province, in the Capital and other cities where there are collectors, every three months counted from the date of issue, in accordance with the provisions of the respective decree.

La Plata, [date]

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 143,050 (visible on main document)
- **Ornamental border:** Red decorative frame with detailed patterns
- **Official seal:** Circular seal impression visible (center-left)
- **Orange stamp:** Visible near center of document
- **Coupon sheet:** Extensive grid of detachable coupons (right side and bottom), each numbered sequentially (A 147 through A 21 visible), marked with $100$, 133,050, and 5% 1/3 denominations
- **Signatures:** [Illegible manuscript signatures at bottom]

- **Issuing location:** La Plata (visible at bottom)
