# BOLIVIAN GOVERNMENT LOAN OF 1872 - CERTIFICATE TRANSCRIPTION

**BOLIVIAN GOVERNMENT LOAN OF 1872.**

**CERTIFICATE.**

**IN RESPECT OF RATEABLE PROPORTION OF ULTIMATE BALANCE OF TRUST FUND.**

We the undersigned John Horatio Lloyd & Alfred James Lambert, the Trustees of that portion of the net proceeds of the 5 per Cent Government Loan raised in the year 1872 by the Republic of Bolivia, which was intended and appropriated towards payment for the works in the Prospectus of that Loan mentioned as they proceeded.

**Hereby Certify** that the holder of this Certificate is in respect of the undermentioned Bond and Loan named below that is to say

| No. of Bond | Date of Bond | Amount of Bond |
|---|---|---|
| 242 | 12th February 1872 | £400 |

entitled to a rateable proportion of the ultimate balance of the said trust fund. Payment of the said rateable proportion of the said ultimate balance of the said trust fund will be notified by Advertisement when the same shall become available for distribution.

Dated this 17th day of June 1880.

[Signatures]
**TRUSTEES.**

John Horatio Lloyd
J. Lambert

---

**NOTE.—**This Certificate is delivered in the holder of the aforenamed Bond, in pursuance of the judgment of Her Majesty's Court of Appeal, dated 20th June, 1879, in the Action "Wilson v. Church and others," and the Judgment of the House of Lords, dated the 23rd March, 1880, in the Action of the "National Bolivian Navigation Company and others v. Wilson and others."

---
