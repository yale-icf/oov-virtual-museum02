## English Translation:

**Upper Certificate:**

War Report 4th Issue
Denomination: 10 Yen

Serial No. 0951840

Gold Increase Dividend
Including Shares August, Showa Year 17
Form Japan Industrial Bank

Wartime Patriotic Bond Certificate
10 Yen

For deposit collection, this security is based on a fixed amount with interest adjustment regulations established by the Finance Ministry, determining the principal and interest payments.

**Lower Stub:**

Black Attachment
Denomination: 10 Yen
Serial No. 0951840
Gold Increase Dividend
10 Yen 4th Issue War Report
(10 Yen Bond)

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number**: 095184 (appears in red in both sections)
- **Date**: August 1942 (Showa 17 - 昭和十七年八月)
- **Issuing Institution**: Japan Industrial Bank (日本勧業銀行)
- **Red Official Seals**: Two circular red seals (one appears to show Mount Fuji with cherry blossoms; the other shows administrative markings)
- **Red Rising Sun Symbol**: Military emblem in upper left corner
- **Aircraft imagery**: Military aircraft depicted in upper right
- **Decorative borders**: Traditional Japanese ornamental design with floral and architectural elements
- **Page marking**: "戦報4" (War Report 4) appears in upper right corner and bottom left
