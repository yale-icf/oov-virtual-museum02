Inscripta La Escritura de L'misión de Obligaciones Hipote-carias al doce de Noviembre de mil novecientos treinta y uno, en el Registro de La Propiedad de Lora del Río, en el tomo treinta y siete, de Villanuevas del Río, al folio ciento cuarenta vuelta, finca número mil quinientos ochenta y cinco, inscrip-

## English translation:

The Deed of Issuance of Mortgage Obligations is registered on November 12, 1931, in the Land Registry of Lora del Río, in volume thirty-seven, of Villanuevas del Río, at folio one hundred forty verso, property number one thousand five hundred eighty-five, third registration, and in the Commercial Registry of Seville, on November 17, 1931, at folio two hundred twenty-eight verso, of volume thirty-eight of the Book of Companies, sheet number one hundred thousand four hundred sixty-five, fifth registration.

Reintegrated with the Tax Stamp in the Matrix.

[Large decorative text in center]: 500 PESETAS
