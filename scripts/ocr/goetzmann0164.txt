# Transcription

**POLICEMAN.—Hollah! What are you two boys talking about, eh? I think you are entering into a very wicked conspiracy to overturn the Government. I have just overheard ye talking about the Bank. Now I con't want you to say anything that will prejudice yourselves on your trial; at the same time I am willing to hear anything you have got to say in explanation of your conduct concerning your talk about the Bank. I have no doubt you meant the Bank of England.**

**FIRST BOY.— Bank of England! What be that? Whoy, it was that mud bank we were a talking on.**

**SECOND BOY.—To be sure it was. Don't you see, Mr. Policeman, it's a country bank,— and we were a-going to make a run upon it, by troving to jump through it!**
