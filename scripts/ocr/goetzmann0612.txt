# Transcription

FOR THE PAYMENT OF

No. 3107    Comptroller's-Office, Oct 2d 1789

I hereby certify that Andrew Kingsbury
is entitled to receive the Sum of Five Shillings Lawful Money, out of any Funds appropriated for the Payment of interest on the liquidated Debt of the State of Connecticut.

Ralph Pomeroy, Compt.

HARTFORD: PRINTED BY HUDSON AND GOODWIN.

---

## Notable markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 3107
- **Signature:** Ralph Pomeroy, Comptroller (handwritten)
- **Date:** October 2, 1789
- **Cancellation:** Small hole punched through center of document
- **Ornamental borders:** Decorative vine and geometric patterns along all edges
- **Denomination:** Five Shillings
- **Printer attribution:** Hudson and Goodwin, Hartford
- **Denomination markers:** "5/." appears in upper corners
