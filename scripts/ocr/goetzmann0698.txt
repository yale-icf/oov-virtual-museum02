## LEFT SECTION: EXTRACTS

**Extracts**
from
the Agreement referred to in the Bond.

[English text - Left column]
The Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation and the Deutsche-Asiatische Bank as Joint Agents for the purposes herein contained.

The whole amount of the Loan shall be forty five years and repayment of the whole shall be made by a yearly sinking fund, which shall be applied to the purchase or redemption at par of the Bonds in question in connection with the Chinese Imperial Government attached to the Chinese Imperial Government at the end of forty five years the whole amount shall be repaid.
