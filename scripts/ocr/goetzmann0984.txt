# Transcription of Province of Nova Scotia Government Redeemable Stock Certificate

**DOMINION OF CANADA**

No C1515                                                                No C1515

**Province of Nova Scotia**

**GOVERNMENT REDEEMABLE STOCK.**

**BEARING INTEREST AT 3½ PER CENT PER ANNUM.**

REDEEMABLE AT PAR IN LONDON ON 11TH JULY 1930. THE GOVERNMENT HAVING THE OPTION OF REDEEMING THE SAME AT ANY TIME AFTER ONE MONTH'S NOTICE BY ADVERTISEMENT IN THE TIMES NEWSPAPER, OR BY POST TO THE THEN STOCKHOLDERS AT THEIR REGISTERED ADDRESSES.

**£1000**

This is to Certify that the Bearer of this Certificate

is entitled to

**ONE THOUSAND POUNDS**

Province of Nova Scotia Government Redeemable Stock, with Interest thereon at the rate of £3 7/10 per cent per Annum, Commenceable at Same National Provincial Bank of England Limited.

THE REVENUE OF THE PROVINCE OF NOVA SCOTIA ALONE ARE HEREBY IN RESPECT OF THE STOCK AND THE DIVIDENDS THEREON, AND THE CONSOLIDATED FUND OF THE UNITED KINGDOM, AND THE CONSOLIDATED REVENUES OF HIS MAJESTY'S TREASURY ARE PLEDGED FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE STOCK OR OF THE DIVIDENDS THEREON, OR FOR ANY MATTER RELATING THERETO — 42 Geo. II, cap. 28, sec. 3.

**Countersigned for**

**THE NATIONAL PROVINCIAL BANK OF ENGLAND LIMITED.**

**Dated,** 17th October 1910

**Registrar**

This Certificate is transferable by delivery unless the name and address of the owner be registered with the Bank. When the coupons attached are exhausted a further supply may be obtained on lodgement of this certificate at the National Provincial Bank of England Limited, 112, Bishopsgate Street, London, E.C.

This certificate may be pledged as security in the manner prescribed by the Bank's conditions. When so pledged it must be left with the Bank.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: No C1515 (appears twice, upper corners)
- Handwritten date: "17th October 1910"
- Handwritten signature and notation (lower right): Appears to be registrar's signature
- Stamp: "CANCELLED" (visible in purple/blue ink)
- Provincial coat of arms of Nova Scotia displayed prominently
- Eight coupon sections attached at bottom (numbered for interest payments at £17.10.0 each)
- Ornamental red/orange decorative border throughout
- Printed by The National Provincial Bank of England Limited
