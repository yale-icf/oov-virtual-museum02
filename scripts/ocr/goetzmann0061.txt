# Transcription

**Title:** Greenland Trade

**Main Text:**
This Project was to catch, to Cut and Boil Vilge Whales, and other Monstrous Fish to Oil, A Stinking Bubble, th'e of late so Dear, Yet now the Greatest Sharers stink, for Bear.

**Notable markings:**
- Red diamond suit symbol in upper left corner (indicating this is a playing card from the Two of Diamonds)
- Engraved illustration depicting two figures on a shore with a large cauldron, a sailing vessel in the background, and coastal landscape with cliffs
