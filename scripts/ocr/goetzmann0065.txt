# Transcription

**Title (top center):**
Rock Salt

**Card suit (top left):**
Seven of Diamonds (seven red diamond symbols arranged in traditional playing card pattern)

**Main text (bottom of card):**
You that are willing to Preserve your Meat,
In Winter Savty, and in Summer Sweet;
Encourage this Salt Project, and your Cow
Will turn to some Account, at least to Brine.

---

## Notable markings:
- Playing card format with diamond suit indicator
- Engraved illustration showing salt harvesting/processing operation with multiple figures, salt pans, and rocky formations
- Aged parchment appearance with visible wear
- This is part of a satirical playing card series related to the South Sea Bubble financial scandal
