# Transcription

**CONVERSION OF THE EXTERNAL DEBT OF PORTUGAL,**
**UNDER THE DECREE OF 18th DECEMBER, 1852.**

---

**PROVISIONAL CERTIFICATE OF DEFERRED STOCK.**

---

No. [1463]                                                                                          [Date]

In conformity with Article 25th of the Regulations approved of by a Decree of Her Most Faithful Majesty the Queen of Portugal, under date of 23d March, 1853, by which the Financial Agency in London is authorised to carry out the Conversion of the External Funded Debt of Portugal, in pursuance of the previous Decree of 18th December, 1852:

This is to Certify THAT the HOLDER hereof is entitled to the Sum of

**Three Pounds** [amount in handwriting - illegible]

**THREE PER CENT. DEFERRED STOCK,** bearing Interest from the 1st of January, 1863, which Sum proceeds from the above Conversion.

---

This Certificate will be exchanged on presentation with others, making up a sufficient amount, for Bonds of any of the following Series, viz. £50, £100, £200, or £500, into which the said Stock is divided; and for any remaining Balance a fresh Certificate will be given.

---

FINANCIAL AGENT OF THE PORTUGUESE GOVERNMENT IN LONDON.

WHITING, LONDON.

---
