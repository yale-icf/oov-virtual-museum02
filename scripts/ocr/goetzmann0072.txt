# Transcription

**Title:** Salt-Petre

**Main Text:**

Come all ye Black Infernal Powder Makers,
And Rocketeers that deal in Squibs and Crackers,
Buy Petre Stock, Let me be your Adviser,
& will make you [tho' not Richer] much & Wiser

**Card Markings:**

Four of Clubs (shown in top left corner with club symbols)

---

## Notable Visual Elements:

- Engraved illustration showing a saltpetre production scene with figures, a barrel, a cart, a building in the background, and landscape elements
- Classical engraving/etching style typical of early 18th-century playing cards
- Text in cursive script at bottom of card
- Four club symbols in upper left corner indicating this is a Four of Clubs card from the South Sea Bubble satirical playing card set
