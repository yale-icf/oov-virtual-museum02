# Transcription

**MONSIEUR MARPLOT.**

HERE comes Monsieur Marplot, half famish'd, from France,
To eat our Twelfth-Cake and teach John Bull to dance;
But all his fine bows, his congees, and grimaces,
Will ne'er introduce him to Johnny's good graces.
Should Britain's StateLott'ry now grant him a Prize,
He'd fix on the City's rich Freeholds his eyes:
But st-p, Monsieur Meagre, by gar, understand,
Old England won't spare you one inch of its land.
