# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

De dwaasheid dogt de kunst te delven in het graft,
Maar vrouw Voorzigtighheid keerd deze rampen af.

**English translation:**

Foolishness thinks it can bury art in the grave,
But the lady Foresight turns away these disasters.

---

## Notable markings:

- Number "9" in top right corner (indicating Nine of Spades)
- Circular marking with "ø" symbol in lower left
- Engraved in style typical of Dutch playing cards from the 17th century
- The image shows a figure holding a mirror and a map/chart, with a spade symbol in the upper right
- Horizontal line shading throughout (woodcut/engraving technique)
