# DERBY CANAL

THIS Ticket, or Instrument, is to certify, that Matthew How of Derby in the County of Derby (Hatter)

is entitled to the share No. 320 in this undertaking; and that her name and proper addition, with the number of the said share, are fairly and distinctly entered in the book kept by "The Derby Canal Company" for that purpose, agreeable to the directions of the act passed in the last session of Parliament, intituled, "An Act for making and maintaining a Navigable Canal from the River Trent at or near Swarklton Bridge, to and through the Borough of Derby, to Little Eaton, with a Cut out of the said Canal in or near the said Borough, to join the Erewash Canal near Sandiacre; and for making Railways from such Canal to several Collieries in the parishes or liberties of Denby, Horfley, and Smalley, all in the county of Derby." Given under the Common Seal of the said Derby Canal Company, at their second General Assembly, held the seventh day of October, in the year One Thousand Seven Hundred and Ninety Three.

N.B. There must be Duplicates of all Transfers of Shares, each of them executed both by the Seller and the Purchaser, one part whereof must be delivered to the Clerk to the *Derby Canal Company*, and entered by him before the purchaser can have any share of the profits of this undertaking, or be entitled to vote as a proprietor.
