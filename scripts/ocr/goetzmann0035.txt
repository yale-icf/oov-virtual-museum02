# Transcription

**Title:** Holy Island - Salt

**Text at bottom:**

Here by mixt Elements of Earth and Water,
They make a Mud, that turns to Salt hereafter;
To help the Project on among Change Dealers!
May all bad Wives like Lot's become Salt Pillars!
Since crowds of Fools delight to be Salt Sellers.

**Notable markings:**

- Red heart symbol in upper left corner (indicating this is the King of Hearts suit from a playing card deck)
- Engraved illustration showing allegorical figures and salt-making processes
- Figure of a suited king in period dress in upper left corner
- Landscape scene with figures engaged in salt production in the main composition
- This is page 8 of 51 from a set of South Sea Bubble satirical playing cards
