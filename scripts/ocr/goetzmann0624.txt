**Right side (Formal assignment text):**

I Henry Philips Do hereby assign and transfer to Alexander Wiltocks Three — Shares in the Capital or Joint Stock of the Insurance Company of the State of Pennsylvania.

In witness whereof I have hereunto set my Hand the Twentieth day of March 1797.

Witness:
John Lewis

[Signature]: Henry Philips
