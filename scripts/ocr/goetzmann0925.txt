**English translation:**

The document is dated April 25, 1903 (Meiji 36) and represents a share certificate for the Kokubun Petroleum Association. The right table shows 10 shares with installment payment records, with amounts of 50 Yen for most installments.

**Notable markings:**
- Red seal/stamp (upper right of left section)
- Multiple red circular seals/stamps in the table (bottom row)
- Handwritten signature: 請川忠右衛門
- Reference number visible: 738 and "1903" in upper right corner
- Handwritten notation at top right: "National Prosperity Petroleum"
