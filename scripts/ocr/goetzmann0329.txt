## Notable markings:

- **Serial Numbers:** 1095201 – 1095250
- **Two circular stamps/seals** at top of document (appears to be official authority marks)
- **Three signatures** by: Administrator, President, and Controller (all handwritten, largely illegible)
- **Ornate decorative border** with geometric and interlace patterns in Art Nouveau style
- **Illustration:** Central figure of a robed woman (likely Dacia or Romania personified) holding a model of a building, symbolizing the company's construction/building mission
- **Date stamp:** IANUARIE 1946 (January 1946)
- **Archival marking:** "Doct. la Sorbona VII" (handwritten at top left)
- **Library/collection mark:** Numbers visible in bottom left corner
