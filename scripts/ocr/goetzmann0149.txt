# Transcription

**HERCULES.**

HERE's HERCULES, fam'd for his wonderful feats,
Reduc'd to a pavior, now mending the streets;
He rams till he's faint; so, his spirits to cheer,
Because it's Twelfth Night, has a pot of good beer.
Poor dog! was he prudent to husband his mite,
How soon might he be in a much better plight!
The Lott'ry, which draws on next Valentine's Day,
Maymake the rogue happy without more delay.
