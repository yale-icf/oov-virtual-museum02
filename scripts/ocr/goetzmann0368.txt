# Transcription

**Original (Spanish):**

Ferro-Carril y Almacenes de Depósito de Santiago de Cuba
CÉDULA ACCIÓN.

No. 7272                                                                                                    $100.

DE CIEN PESOS.

Cédula de Propiedad de una Acción de cien pesos para [illegible] Gabriel de Llamas.

que habiendo entregado según lo que consta un supuesto en las Estatutos y Reglamento de la Asociación.

Santiago de Cuba, 20 de abril de 1861.

J.M. de Llaguno, Presidente.    Lino [?] Contador.

Cuba Fec. 20 de 1861.    J.S. Lozano, Secretario.

---

**English Translation:**

Railroad and Warehouses of Deposit of Santiago de Cuba
SHARE CERTIFICATE.

No. 7272                                                                                                    $100.

OF ONE HUNDRED PESOS.

Property Certificate of one share of one hundred pesos for [illegible] Gabriel de Llamas.

who having delivered according to what is verified one supposed in the Statutes and Regulations of the Association.

Santiago de Cuba, April 20, 1861.

J.M. de Llaguno, President.    Lino [?] Contador [Accountant].

Cuba Feb. 20 of 1861.    J.S. Lozano, Secretary.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: No. 7272
- Face value: $100 (Cien Pesos)
- Three signatures: J.M. de Llaguno (President), Lino [?] (Contador/Accountant), J.S. Lozano (Secretary)
- Date: April 20, 1861
- Engraved vignette of a steam locomotive with passenger cars in center
- Ornamental border and decorative corner designs throughout
- Shareholder name: Gabriel de Llamas
- Printer mark at bottom: "F.H. Eytinge's, Bank N.Y." [partial, faint]
