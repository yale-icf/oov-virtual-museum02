4½% LOAN OF THE CITY OF WARSAW OF THE YEAR 1931

"Zł. in zł. 99,188 (Zloty in zloty, ninety-nine) [illegible] or

No. 0038738                                                    No. 0038738

The loan of the City capital of Warsaw is issued by virtue of the decision of the Municipal Council dated 10 June 1930, approved by the Government Commissioner for the affairs of obligations of the City of Warsaw by decree dated 10 June 1930.

The obligations bear interest at 4½% per annum.

Interest is payable without deduction of Polish income tax on the value of movable securities.

The obligations are issued for a total amount of 55,912,500 French francs or 19,527,243.75 zlotys or 11,352,993.75 Swiss francs in denominations of:

Warsaw, 1 June 1931.

The loan of the City of Warsaw is issued by virtue of the resolution of the City Council of 10 June 1930, approved by the Government Commissioner for matters of conversion of obligations of the City of Warsaw, by letter of 10 June 1930.

Obligations are interest-bearing at the rate of 4½ percent per annum.

Interest is payable without deduction of Polish income tax on securities.

Obligations are issued for the amount of: French francs 55,912,500 or Zloty in zloty 19,527,243.75 or Swiss francs 11,352,993.75 in denominations of:

[Various denominations listed]

Warsaw, 1 June 1931.

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** № 0038738 (appears on both left and right sides)
- **Denomination:** Bearer bond (au choix du porteur / według wyboru okazicieła)
- **Currency options:** French francs, Polish zlotys, or Swiss francs
- **Date:** 1 June 1931 / 1 czerwca 1931 r.
- **Bilingual format:** French (left) and Polish (right)
- **Decorative elements:** Ornamental border with architectural vignettes of Warsaw (Town Hall on bottom left, Parliament building on bottom right)
- **Signatures:** Two signature lines at bottom for President of City of Warsaw and Municipality member [signatures appear to be present but illegible in image]
- **Coat of arms:** Central heraldic emblem of Warsaw at top
- **Interest rate:** 4.5% per annum
- **Total loan:** 55,912,500 French francs (or equivalent in zloty or Swiss francs)
