# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

tot Antwerpen bij A. Di. Civili
bij de Bourse 1728

[Coat of arms with "SPIRO SPERO" motto]

I. B. Tongelins Sculpist Antw:

Ck Onderschreve —, —, —, belove en obligiere my, mits een Constante premie, die tot myn contentement hebbe ontfangen, aen [handwritten: Mijn Heer Grammond?] ofte ordre, op het verthood deser tuschen heden en den eersten transportdagh naer [handwritten: Anno 1730 vijfde maande] 1730 — te leveren en te transporteeren [handwritten: Jan 28 —] Actien, inde Generacle Keyserlyke Indise Compagnie althier, onder conditie, van aen my benefens het transport te rembourfseren alle her gene nu reels is, en mildschen noch op die Actien in die tustschen-teyt sal gefourniert worden, en boven dien noch te gelyek aen my te betaelen [handwritten: andere Summe van Vierhondert en Vijftig ponden] voor tweede Premie a [handwritten: Dateerend Voordagh per Cento Agto] op ieder Actie, fullende daer regens aen de gene die dese Actien sal aenvoerden, ende betaelen, ter selver tyde uyt-keeruen mijn bovenfchreve alle voor de Compagnie in die tutschen-teydt op die Actien ceniger wyfe sal divideren, rembourleren, ofte geven, al beleere duyck meer als zijn bovenfchreve te doene naer betaelichge, maer [handwritten: voor de tweede Male den 1730] voorseyt, dese Actien aen my niet opgevraeght fijnde, en op den eersten transpot-dagh naer de op-vraeging niet getransporteerde betaelt fijnde, waer toe den op-vraeger gehouden is, dan blyft dese Obligatie ten mynen laste Nulle, en van geender waerde meer, fijnde dan die Actien gelyck oock den contanten premie, die my voor nu voor al-too is opgeleverd, en onder-roepelyek toebeboort, met alles wat van de Compagnie sal ontfangen. Aldus gecontractaert ter interventie van [handwritten: Makelaar Wijkman?]

onder verbintenisse van myn Persoon en goederen in Antwerpen den [handwritten: Twelfde April —] zeventien hondert [handwritten: en twintig]

[Signature] Jean Van Lancker

voor Actien à 192 P. C. in optie tot [handwritten: Eerste October 1730] inden tustschen tyde 2 daghen voor transpot op te vraegen

[Handwritten annotations and signature: Carlo Brittele / Jean Crampel?]

## English translation:

In Antwerp by A. Di. Civili
at the Exchange 1728

[Coat of arms with "I BREATHE I HOPE" motto]

I. B. Tongelins Sculptor Antwerp:

I, the undersigned —, —, —, promise and obligate myself, with a constant premium, which I have received to my satisfaction, to [handwritten: My Lord Grammont?] or order, on the obligation hereof between now and the first delivery day after [handwritten: Anno 1730 fifth month] 1730 — to deliver and transport [handwritten: January 28 —] Shares in the General Imperial Indian Company here, under the condition that I will reimburse my beneficiary for the transport of all that is now due, and further still on those Shares in the interim period shall be furnished, and beyond that still to pay equally to me [handwritten: another Sum of Four Hundred and Fifty pounds] for a second Premium at [handwritten: Dated Postage of Per Cent Ajo] on each Share, with the understanding that for those delivering these Shares and paying, at the same time the above-mentioned all for the Company in the interim period on the Shares anything shall divide, reimburse, or give, all much less than his above-mentioned to do toward settlement, but [handwritten: for the second time the 1730] aforesaid, these Shares not having been requested of me, and on the first delivery day after request not being transported [and] paid for, whereby the requester is obligated, then this Obligation remains to my charge as Null and void of any value more, whereby those Shares like also the cash premium, which I for now for all time have received, and subordinately belong to me, with all that the Company shall deliver. Thus contracted with the intervention of [handwritten: Broker Wijkman?]

under obligation of my Person and goods in Antwerp the [handwritten: Twelfth April —] seventeen hundred [handwritten: and thirty]

[Signature] Jean Van Lancker

for Shares at 192 P.C. in option until [handwritten: First October 1730] in the interim period 2 days for transport to be requested

## Notable markings:

- **Coat of arms seal**: Central oval seal with imperial eagle, crown, and crossed insignia with "SPIRO SPERO" (I Breathe I Hope) motto
- **Artist signature**: "I. B. Tongelins Sculpist Antw:" (Sculptor in Antwerp)
- **Multiple handwritten annotations and corrections** throughout the document in cursive script
- **Signatures**: Jean Van Lancker (main signature), with additional names Carlo Brittele and Jean Crampel (?) at bottom
- **Serial/Reference numbers**: 192 P.C., dates of 1728, 1730, and 1730
- **Document condition**: Aged parchment with wear and foxing marks
