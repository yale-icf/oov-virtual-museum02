# Transcription of Commonwealth of Pennsylvania Five Per Cent Stock of 1825

**No. 71**

**Five per cent. stock of 1825.**

*Treasury Department of Pennsylvania, November 18, 1829.*

---

**Be it known,** That there is due from the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, unto **James P. Parker** in trust, of Philadelphia, or his assigns, the sum of **one hundred dollar**

bearing interest at **Five per centum per annum**, from the **first day of August** 1829, payable semi-annually at the **Bank of Pennsylvania**—that is to say, one-half part thereof on the first day of February, and the remaining half part on the first day of August, in each year, being **STOCK created in pursuance of an act of Assembly passed the eleventh day of April, 1825**, entitled "An act authorizing a loan;"—the principal of which Stock is reimbursable and payable in Fifteen Years after the first day of January, 1825: which debt is recorded in this Department, and is transferable on the books of the Auditor General or at the Bank of Pennsylvania, by appearance in person or by attorney, according to the rules and forms instituted for that purpose.

**Dollars, 100.**

---

**COUNTERSIGNED,**

[Signature] **Mahon**
*State Treasurer.*

[Signature] **David Mason**
*Auditor General.*

---
