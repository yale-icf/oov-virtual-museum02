# Notable markings:

- **Certificate Number:** 01529
- **Circular seal (center-bottom):** "SOCIÉTÉ DES GRANDS HÔTELS INDOCHINOIS" with monogram "GHI"
- **Stamp (upper left):** "INDOCHINE" circular stamp with coat of arms
- **Signatures:** Two handwritten signatures (Administrator and Board Delegation representative)
- **Decorative border:** Ornate Art Nouveau-style frame with geometric and floral motifs in gold/tan color
- **Watermark text (faint, background):** "GRANDS HOTELS" and "INDOCHINOIS" (security feature)
- **Printer mark:** "SAIGON — IMP. A. PORTAIC" (Saigon printer attribution)
