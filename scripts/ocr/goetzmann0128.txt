# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Zo kde 8 door midden snij, zo hou
ik maar twee nullen,
Die niet zo goed zijn, als de Wind-
negotios prullen;

**English translation:**

If you cut the 8 in half, then I keep
two zeros,
Which are not as good as the Wind-
negotiation trinkets;

**Notable markings:**

- Suit symbol: Eight of Clubs (8♣) in top right corner
- Text partially visible in upper right: "Actie" and "agtig"
- Part of the Pasquins Windkaart series (satirical playing cards about the South Sea Bubble financial speculation)
- Simple rectangular border frame
- Engraved illustration in classical style typical of 17th-century Dutch prints
