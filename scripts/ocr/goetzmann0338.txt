# Transcription of Compañía Petrolera Mexicana "Faros de Aztlán" Stock Certificate

## Original (Spanish):

**Header:**
Compañía Petrolera Mexicana
"FAROS DE AZTLÁN"

**Main Text:**
50
ACCIONES

TÍTULO NUM
0245
Del Num 012201
Al Num 012250

VALOR 50 PESOS
Oro Nacional

CAPITAL SOCIAL
$ 250,000 ORO NACIONAL

DURACIÓN 50 AÑOS          DOMICILIO CIUDAD DE MEXICO

El portador de este título representa Compañía Acciones de a un peso oro nacional cada una, totalmente pagadas de las doscientas cincuenta mil en que está dividido el capital de la Compañía según la escritura de constitución social otorgada ante el Notario Público num. 14, Don Francisco Merino Ortiz, el día 24 de junio de 1916 con todos los derechos y obligaciones que expresan los estatutos.

México 1º de octubre de 1916

El portador
[Signature: Asiferi Gambiruis]

El Secretario
[Signature: Not Rosales Morming]

El Tesorero
[Signature: [illegible]]

---

## English Translation:

**Header:**
Mexican Petroleum Company
"LIGHTHOUSES OF AZTLÁN"

50
SHARES

CERTIFICATE NUM
0245
From Num 012201
To Num 012250

VALUE 50 PESOS
National Gold

SOCIAL CAPITAL
$ 250,000 NATIONAL GOLD

DURATION 50 YEARS          DOMICILE MEXICO CITY

The bearer of this certificate represents shares of the Company at one peso national gold each, fully paid from the two hundred fifty thousand into which the capital of the Company is divided according to the deed of social constitution granted before the Public Notary No. 14, Don Francisco Merino Ortiz, on the 24th of June 1916 with all the rights and obligations expressed in the bylaws.

Mexico 1st of October 1916

The President
[Signature: Asiferi Gambiruis]

The Secretary
[Signature: Not Rosales Morming]

The Treasurer
[Signature: [illegible]]

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Numbers:** Certificate No. 0245, Share Numbers 012201–012250
- **Decorative Elements:** Ornamental Aztec-inspired borders; oil derrick illustration in center top; prominent Aztec warrior figure in profile on right side; temple/pyramid imagery
- **Seal/Stamp:** Yellow/gold institutional seal in bottom left corner (partially visible)
- **Handwritten Signatures:** Three signatures present (President, Secretary, Treasurer)
- **Date:** October 1, 1916 (issued during Mexican Revolution under Carranza presidency)
