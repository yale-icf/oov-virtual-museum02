# Stock Purchase Warrant Transcription

**No NCW5418**

---

**STOCK PURCHASE WARRANT**

**TO PURCHASE COMMON STOCK OF**

# GENERAL INVESTMENT CORPORATION

*Incorporated under the Laws of the State of Delaware*

**WARRANT TO PURCHASE ___/___ SHARES**

---

THIS CERTIFIES that the bearer is entitled to purchase at any time on or after September 14, 1929 (without limit) all or any part of

_____ SHARES

full-paid and non-assessable shares of COMMON STOCK of GENERAL INVESTMENT CORPORATION, a corporation of the State of Delaware (herein called the "corporation"), as such stock may be constituted at the time of purchase, at the price of $8 per share, upon surrender of this warrant accompanied by full payment of the purchase price in cash at the principal office of the corporation in Boston, Massachusetts. This warrant does not entitle the holder to any rights whatsoever as a stockholder of the corporation. It is expressly agreed that the shares of common stock of the corporation agreed to be sold hereunder are shares of the common stock of the corporation as such stock may be constituted at the time of purchase and that delivery of the number of shares of common stock called for herein, in good delivery hereunder whether or not the claim is made, shall constitute full performance of all the obligations of the corporation hereunder with respect to the exercise of the rights or interests represented by the shares of common stock of the corporation authorized and outstanding at the date hereof or at any other time prior to such purchase and irrespective of any dividends, sales, exchanges or other disposition of stock of the corporation of any class, any change of authorized capital or reclassification thereof, the sale or lease by the corporation of all or any part of its property, or any change whatsoever; provided, however, that if while this warrant is in effect the corporation shall have recapitalized by subdivision or combination of its outstanding shares of common stock into a greater or lesser number of shares, then, in each such case, the number of shares of common stock purchasable upon exercise of this warrant shall be increased or decreased (as the case may be) in proportion to the change through such subdivision or combination in the number of outstanding shares of common stock of the corporation, and provided further, that if while this warrant is in effect the corporation shall consolidate or merge with or into any other corporation, then, as a condition of such consolidation or merger, adequate provision shall be made whereby the holder of this warrant shall thereafter be entitled to purchase, in lieu of the shares of common stock of the corporation theretofore purchasable upon exercise of this warrant, such shares of stock, securities or assets as would have been purchasable upon the exercise of this warrant had such consolidation or merger not taken place. The existence of this warrant shall not prevent the lawful dissolution of the corporation. Whenever in this warrant the corporation is referred to, it shall be deemed to include any corporation into or to which the corporation may have been merged or consolidated. This warrant is exchangeable at the option of the bearer at the principal office of the corporation in Boston, Massachusetts for other similar warrants of different denominations entitling the bearer thereof to purchase an equal aggregate number of shares of common stock of the corporation as specified in this warrant. Every holder of this warrant, by accepting the same, consents and agrees with the corporation and with every subsequent holder of this warrant as follows: Title to this warrant shall be registered by the absolute owner of this warrant and is hereby granted power to transfer absolute title thereto by delivery thereof to a purchaser thereof for value (present or accrued); without notice of prior defenses or equities or claims enforceable against his transferor; every bearer of this warrant shall be presumed to be such a purchaser thereunder unless the contrary be established; every bearer of this warrant waives and renounces all his equities and rights herein in favor of every such purchaser and every such purchaser shall acquire title hereto and to all rights represented hereby; the corporation and any warrant agent shall treat any bearer who is a purchaser hereof of the character above described, as the absolute owner thereof for all purposes without being affected by any notice to the contrary; the corporation and any warrant agent shall be entitled to accept the surrender of this warrant from the bearer hereof and such surrender shall constitute full and complete discharge of all liability of the corporation and any warrant agent hereunder; the corporation does not make any contract with any bearer hereof except as contained in this warrant and any performance by the corporation with respect to the bearer hereof of any agreement contained with respect to this warrant, shall be due performance of such agreement. On the back of this warrant is a form of subscription which must be used upon exercise thereof.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF the corporation has caused this warrant to be signed in its behalf by the undersigned warrant agents.

**GENERAL INVESTMENT CORPORATION**
*(Boston, Mass.)*
