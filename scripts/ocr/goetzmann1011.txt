# Transcription

**Original:**

OORSPRONKELIJKE KWITANTIE AFGETEEKEND. L.R. & Co.

AAN DEN INHOUD VOLDAAN.
AMSTERDAM, 19[--]

**English translation:**

Original receipt signed off. L.R. & Co.

Satisfied as to the contents.
Amsterdam, 19[--]

**Notable markings:**

- Rectangular stamp in upper left corner containing text "OORSPRONKELIJKE KWITANTIE AFGETEEKEND. L.R. & Co." (Original receipt signed off. L.R. & Co.)
- Diagonal stamp in green text reading "AAN DEN INHOUD VOLDAAN. AMSTERDAM, 19[--]" (Satisfied as to the contents. Amsterdam, 19[--])
- The year in the bottom stamp is partially illegible but appears to be from the early 20th century
- Page is otherwise blank/empty, representing the reverse side of the receipt
