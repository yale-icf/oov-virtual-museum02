## English Translation:

**IMPERIAL**
**RUSSIAN GOVERNMENT**

3 3/10 % CONVERSION BONDS
issued on the basis of the HIGHEST Decree of 6 March 1898
in exchange for 4 1/2 % mortgage bonds of the former Society of Mutual Land Credit
at the nominal capital of
EIGHT HUNDRED EIGHTY-FIVE MILLION FOUR HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED RUBLES
= 184,490,784 German imperial marks = 237,766,400 francs = 9,025,342 pounds sterling 12 shillings = 109,327,872 guilders

**3 3/10 % CONVERSION BOND**
No. 097530

**FOR ONE HUNDRED FIFTY RUBLES**
= 324 German imperial marks = 400 francs = 15 pounds sterling 17 shillings = 192 guilders

Issued by the State Loan Bank, [date]

**TO THE BEARER.**

[Extensive legal terms and conditions regarding interest payments, redemption procedures, and holder rights follow]

Issue based on the HIGHEST Decree to the Ministry of Finance on 6 March 1898.

[Additional regulatory provisions and conditions]

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** №097530
- **Denomination:** 150 Rubles (with equivalent values in German marks, francs, pounds sterling, and Dutch guilders)
- **Red circular stamps:** Two Imperial government seals/stamps visible on left and right sides
- **Ornamental borders:** Elaborate decorative green borders with geometric and floral patterns
- **Signature:** Director General of the State Debt Department (handwritten signature visible at bottom)
- **Date stamp:** "6 Марта 1898 года" (6 March 1898)
