# Transcription

**Title:** Bleeching of Hair

**Heraldic Symbol:** Ten of Clubs (shown in upper left corner)

**Main Text (caption at bottom):**

Here Dirty Brown, Dark, Red, and Yellow Hair, Are Bleach'd, to Colours that are Fine and Fair, Then Blended so, that half the Whores in Town, contribute to adorn one Addl'd Crown.

---

## Notable Elements:

- This is a satirical playing card from the South Sea Bubble era
- The illustration depicts a scene outside a building where hair bleaching is being performed
- Multiple figures are shown, including what appears to be a bleaching operation with fire/smoke and hanging hair
- The landscape in background shows mountains and vegetation
- The text is a crude commentary on the commercial exploitation of hair bleaching and its connection to sex work during the period of financial speculation
