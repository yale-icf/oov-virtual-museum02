# Transcription

**Left side:**

No. 262

W. Hastings

Received this Certificate,

Oct 10 1862

Wm. Hastings

---

**Center/Right side:**

SCRIP CERTIFICATE.

WESTCHESTER & PHILADELPHIA RAIL ROAD CO

SCRIP AUTHORIZED BY RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD,
September 29th, 1860.

This Certifies, That the Westchester and Philadelphia Rail Road Co.,
is indebted to Washington Hastings [or his assigns], in the sum of
Sixty Eight [unclear - possibly "Forty Eight"] Dollars,

which sum it promises to pay [him] or his assigns, on the first day of October,
A.D. One Thousand Eight Hundred and Sixty-five, with interest at the rate of six per
cent. per annum from this date.

Witness the signatee of the Treasurer and the Seal of the Company, this first day of
October, A.D. 1860.

[Signature]
TREASURER

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: No. 262
- Company seal/emblem visible in upper center and lower right
- Handwritten annotations throughout
- Treasurer's signature at bottom right
- Official seal impression near treasurer's signature
- Faded watermark visible in background
- Receipt notation on left margin dated "Oct 10 1862"
