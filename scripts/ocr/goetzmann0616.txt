## English Translation:

**CERTIFICATE**

No. 1266                                    CAPITAL R. 1000—

THE BEARER of this is entitled to a Capital of

ONE THOUSAND ROUBLES

in ASSIGNATIONS bearing 6% interest registered in the General Ledger of the
Russian Empire, in the names of:

GERARDUS BLANCKE & ZOON,
CHEMET & WEETJEN and
VANDENBROEKE & COMP.

CUSTODIANS

of the Funds, belonging to the Administration Office, under Main Direction of:

VAN VLOTEN & DE GYSELAAR,
BUYS & ZOON and
H. W. VAN DRIEL SLAM.

Established by:

[List of 44 merchant/banking names as above]

Whereby the interest shall be paid on Coupons, according to the notice dated
5 July 1824; issued by them; and the Bearer of this is authorized to exchange this CERTIFICATE,
with the addition of the not yet expired Coupons, at that Administration Office
in exchange for a receipt of equal Capital, for a similar registration in the
Russian General Ledger.

AMSTERDAM, 7 October 1824.

Counter-signed

[signature]                                [signature]

Herewith issued Ten half-yearly
Coupons.

---

## Notable markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 1266
- **Capital Value:** R. 1000 (one thousand Roubles)
- **Interest Rate:** 6% (2es pCt. / deux pour cent)
- **Date:** 7 October (7 Oktober) 1824
- **Two handwritten signatures** at bottom (counter-signatures)
- **Wax seal impression** visible in upper left corner
- **Note on coupons:** "Hierbij uitgegeven Tien halfjarige Coupons" (Herewith issued Ten half-yearly Coupons)
