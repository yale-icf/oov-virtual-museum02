# Transcription

**Original (French):**

This is a coupon sheet containing 64 individual coupons arranged in a 4×16 grid. Each coupon is identical in structure with the following text:

COMPAGNIE IMPERIALE

CHEMINS DE FER ETHIOPIENS
Action N° 01,927
[COUPON TYPE - varies by position]

The coupon types listed are:
- QUARANTE-HUITIÈME COUPON (48th Coupon)
- TRENTE-SIXIÈME COUPON (36th Coupon)
- VINGT-QUATRIÈME COUPON (24th Coupon)
- DOUZIÈME COUPON (12th Coupon)
- QUARANTE-SEPTIÈME COUPON (47th Coupon)
- TRENTE-CINQUIÈME COUPON (35th Coupon)
- VINGT-TROISIÈME COUPON (23rd Coupon)
- ONZIÈME COUPON (11th Coupon)

[And continuing in descending numerical order through to:]
- PREMIER COUPON (1st Coupon)

Each coupon displays a numerical value in the upper right corner (ranging from 48 down to 1).

---

**English translation:**

IMPERIAL COMPANY

ETHIOPIAN RAILWAY LINES
Share No. 01,927
[COUPON TYPE - varies by position]

The coupon numbers descend sequentially from the 48th Coupon to the 1st Coupon.

---

**Notable markings:**

- All coupons feature decorative ornamental borders
- Red/brown printing on cream-colored paper
- Share/Action number: 01,927 (consistent throughout)
- Systematic numbering system from 48 to 1
