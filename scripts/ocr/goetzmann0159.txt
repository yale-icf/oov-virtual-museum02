# Transcription

New Year's Lotteries begin JANUARY 23, containing
NINE PRIZES OF £21,000 EACH.
All Other Prizes Doubled.

MOGGY CLEISHBOTHAM.

The Season's best Compliments wait on us all,
And a rich New Year's Present from fam'd Coopers' Hall.
In truth where such good entertainment invites
What spirits are proof 'gainst such cheering delights?
On SWIFT I will wait, as I like to be doing,
For things good and plenty make excellent brewing.
Twenty-one Thousands! I'll pledge them in wine,
Should Fortune give orders that one shall be mine,
And we'll toast the Queen we All Love.
