# ENGLISH TRANSLATION:

**Conditions of Negotiation, for a Fund from which, under the Direction of DANIEL CHANGUION, to certain Planters in Rio Essequibo and Rio Demerary, for the continuation and improvement of their Plantations, a Sum of ƒ 40,000 shall be furnished for 10 Years at an Interest Rate of 6 percent per annum.**

**Article 1.**

The Planters in the aforementioned Colonies, who are inclined to take up some Monies for the improvement and continuation of their Plantations, and who have been accepted for this purpose by the Director, shall be obliged at their own cost to have said Plantations properly inventoried by Sworn Appraisers, including the Lands, Buildings, Works, Male and Female Slaves, and generally whatever else pertains thereto, with the exclusion of Furniture and other Items unnecessary for the operation of the Plantations, and the full and undisputed Ownership of such Inventory thereby proved by said Appraisers to be valued.

**Article 2.**

The Inventory thus determined, prepared, and valued as above shall be sent to Daniel Changuion, together with the necessary Mortgage Deeds, wherein it must be evident that said Mortgage has been duly registered at the Secretary's Office of the Colony, and that no other Special or General liens on said Effects exist, likewise that no liens shall be registered or advanced without the consent of the Money-lenders herein.

**Article 3.**

To those Planters who shall deliver the Inventory and Mortgage Deeds as aforesaid, shall be loaned one half or at most five eighths of the value at which their Plantations, with their appurtenances, have been assessed, at an Interest Rate of 5 percent per annum, which they undertake to pay annually above all other Charges on the Penne as mentioned in the Mortgage Deed.

**Article 4.**

The Money-borrowers, who can annually produce Valuable proof that their holdings have thereby been improved, shall have the liberty to make a new Valuation as mentioned above, of the increased value of the property, and to take out an additional one half of said improvement, provided such be communicated to aforesaid Daniel Changuion.

**Article 5.**

The respective Planter Money-borrowers bind themselves to address all their Products to be sent to [Daniel Changuion], to provide necessary Provisions, and to sign Bills of Exchange on Daniel Changuion, and also in a timely manner when sending their Products, to provide him with proper notice for the arrangement of the necessary affaires; provided that they are not permitted to fail in this or to sell their Products in the respective Colonies, or to divert them in other ways, in which case their Interest shall be due, and also their principal Capital with the Interest shall be immediately demandable and collectible from the Persons, Plantations with all that appertains thereto, and all other moveable and immoveable Goods, binding themselves hereto before the Court of Justice in the Colony, where the other [conditions] shall likewise be upheld, by voluntary Condemnation.
