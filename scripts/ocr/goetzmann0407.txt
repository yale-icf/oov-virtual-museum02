# Transcription of Norwich & Worcester Rail Road Company Bond

**Header Elements:**
- NUMBER: 468
- DOLLAR: 1000

**Main Title:**
United States of America
State of Connecticut

Norwich & Worcester
RAIL ROAD COMPANY

**Subtitle Text:**
WHOLE AMOUNT AUTHORIZED $500,000 — INTEREST PAYABLE SEMI-ANNUALLY

**Body Text:**
On the first day of March, 1851, the Norwich and Worcester Rail Road Company, formed to pay by Loan, bearing the date thereof, Connecticut Bank, a Bank Incorporated under the Laws of Connecticut, State of Connecticut, State of $1000, at the New England Bank, Exchange, in the City of Boston, with interest at the rate of $25 per cent per annum, payable semi-annually on the presentation of Dividend Warrant, under the seal and signature of the Directors of said Company, at said Bank until the 1st day of March, 1877. This Bond is transferable, and the holder is entitled to receive from the Treasurer of the said Company, at fixed periods, the interest due thereon.
