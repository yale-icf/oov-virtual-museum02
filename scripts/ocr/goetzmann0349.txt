# Transcription

**FOR THE PAYMENT OF**

No. 13,729 Comptroller's-Office, July 23d 1792

I hereby certify that Solomon Ensign Jr.
is entitled to receive the Sum of Thirteen Shillings & Six
pence

Lawful Money, out of any Funds appropriated for the
Payment of interest on the liquidated Debt of the State of
Connecticut.

L0. 13. 6                                    A. Kingsbury, Compt.

---

HARTFORD: PRINTED BY HUDSON AND GOODWIN.

---
