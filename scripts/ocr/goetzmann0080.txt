# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Vrouw

Men zet de schop vrij neer, ons
spelen is verbruijd
De blinde razernij van Quimquampoix
heeft uit.

**English translation:**

Woman [Lady]

One sets down the spade freely, our
playing is exhausted
The blind madness of Quincampoix
has ended.

**Notable markings:**

- Title at top: "Vrouw" (Woman/Lady)
- Spade suit symbol (♠) in top right corner
- The text at bottom appears to be in 17th/18th century Dutch
- This is identified as "Queen of Spades" from the Pasquins Windkaart (Wind Chart) playing cards series from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Theater)
- The reference to "Quimquampoix" (Quincampoix) appears to be referencing the Mississippi Bubble financial crisis
