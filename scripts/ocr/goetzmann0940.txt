*De Heer Dierenthis, Nyk*

**English Translation:**

We undersigned Directors and Administrator of the aforementioned Contract, do hereby Certify that

*Mr. Dierenthis, Nyk*

according to the stated Age, has been entered and registered on the small List, No. [unclear] in the Class of [unclear], under the Letter [unclear] as being the funding of this Portion in the form of ƒ10– funded by

[illegible signatures]

such that we recognize ourselves, on that account, to be interested for a Portion in the aforementioned Contract, which after comparison has been found to accord with the Original, which together with the Effects rests with us.

Done Amsterdam, the 31st October 1774.

Directors
[signatures]

Administrator
[signature]

NB. The Annual Report must be made in the Month of November, according to Art. 32 to 37 inclusive.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Date: 31 October 1774
- Location: Amsterdam
- Multiple handwritten signatures by Directors and Administrator
- Reference to a 10 Gulden (ƒ10) amount
- Official certification document format
- Handwritten annotations and corrections throughout
