# Transcription of Morris Canal and Banking Company Stock Transfer Receipts

## FIRST RECEIPT (Top)

**Morris Canal and Banking Company.**
**OF 1844.**

**AGENCY AT PHENIX BANK, CITY OF N.Y.** June 28th 1845

For Value Received, M do hereby assign and transfer unto

Hopkins & Berton
Esqrs

SHARES in the Capital Stock of the MORRIS CANAL & BANKING COMPANY
OF 1844, standing in [unclear] name on the Books of the said Company.

WITNESS,

[Signature - illegible]

---

## SECOND RECEIPT (Bottom)

**Morris Canal and Banking Company.**
**OF 1844.**

**AGENCY AT PHENIX BANK, CITY OF N.Y.** July 10 1845

For Value Received, I do hereby assign and transfer unto

E.S. Map
Esqrs

SHARES in the Capital Stock of the MORRIS CANAL & BANKING COMPANY
OF 1844, standing in [unclear] name on the Books of the said Company.

WITNESS,

[Signature - illegible]

---

## Notable markings:

- Ornamental decorative border on left side of both receipts
- Printed by: "George C. Mann, Blank Book Manufacturer, 63 Wall st. N.Y"
- Handwritten signatures and dates in cursive script
- Two separate transfer receipts printed on a single sheet
