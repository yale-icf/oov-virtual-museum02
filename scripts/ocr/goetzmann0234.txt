# American Trust Company Stock Certificate - Text Transcription

**AMERICAN TRUST COMPANY**

**INCORPORATED UNDER THE LAWS OF THE STATE OF MASSACHUSETTS, A.D. 1881**

"This is to Certify that"

**HARRIETTE S. FOSTER**

is the owner of

**NINE**

Shares

of the Capital Stock of the American Trust Company, Inc., transferable on the books of the Company in person or by Attorney on the surrender of this Certificate.

**Boston, JAN 14 1910**

[Certificate No.] **1951**

---

**Notable markings:**

- Large engraved vignette of an American bald eagle in flight (left side)
- Red diagonal overprint reading "NOT OVER NINE SHARES" across the face
- Ornate green decorative border throughout
- Date: January 14, 1910
- Facsimile signatures of two company officers at lower corners (labeled "TREASURER" and "PRESIDENT")
- Decorative rosette seal in center bottom
- Serial number: 1951
- Company incorporation date: A.D. 1881
- Location: Boston
- Printer attribution: "AMERICAN BANK NOTE COMPANY NEW YORK & BOSTON" (visible at bottom)
