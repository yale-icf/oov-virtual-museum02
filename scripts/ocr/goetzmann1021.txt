# Transcription of VOC Financial Documents (1622-1623)

## DOCUMENT 1 (Top Page)

**Original (Dutch):**

Ontfangersder Oost-Indische Compagnie gelieven te betalen
aen
de somme van
tot voldoeninghe vanden inhoudt deser Obligatie met den Intereste
van dien, synde vervallen den
Als te weten voor t'Capitael der selver Obligatie,          gl.
Ende voor         maenden Intereft van ditto Capitael
tegens              ten hondert int laer          gl.
Comt samen als vooren                                    gl.
Actum in Middelburgh.

[Handwritten section in brown ink:]
Ick ondergheftchreven bekenne vande boven-ftaende
[several lines of illegible/unclear handwriting]
voldaen te zijn, Actum in Middelburgh

Dominicus van Hoontshoerle

**English Translation:**

The Receivers of the East India Company are requested to pay
to
the sum of
for satisfying the contents of this Obligation with the Interest
thereof, which is due
Namely for the Capital of said Obligation,          gl. [guilders]
And for         months Interest of said Capital
at            per hundred per year          gl.
Total as above                                    gl.
Drawn up in Middelburgh.

[Handwritten receipt:]
I, the undersigned, acknowledge that the above-mentioned [sum]
[further text unclear]
has been paid to me, Drawn up in Middelburgh

Dominicus van Hoontshoerle

## DOCUMENT 2 (Bottom Page)

**Original (Dutch):**

Wy onderftchreven Reecken-meesters vande Camere der Ooft-Indifche Compagnie binnen Middelburgh, Bekennen midts defen
gheaccordeert te hebben, ende by den Ontfangers der voorftchreven Compagnie ontfanghen te welen, vanden Erfamen [?] ... de Somme van [?]
Welcke voorftchreven somme gan metren Interefse van dien jegens . . . ten hond. Int jaer gherekent, wy aen voorn. . . [several lines with heavy handwritten annotations and cross-outs]
oifte ligt, Actum in Middelburgh de[n] . . . Oct[ober] . . . 1622

£1333 : 6 : 8

[Signature: Jocvry? and Cornelis Jauckly]

**English Translation:**

We, the undersigned, Reckoning-masters of the Chamber of the East India Company within Middelburgh, do hereby acknowledge
that we have agreed and received by the Receivers of the aforesaid Company, from the Worthy [?] ... the sum of [?]
Which aforementioned sum shall be paid with Interest thereof at ... per hundred per year, being calculated, we as aforesaid [several lines with heavy handwritten amendments and deletions]
or otherwise, Drawn up in Middelburgh the ... October ... 1622

£1333 : 6 : 8

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Signatures:** Dominicus van Hoontshoerle (top document); Jocvry[?] and Cornelis Jauckly (bottom document)
- **Dates:** Top document dated 9 November/December 1623 (Amsterdam/Middelburgh); Bottom document dated October 26, 1622
- **Sum:** £1,333:6:8 Flemish (shown as "£1333 : 6 : 8" on bottom document)
- **Interest Rate:** 6% per annum
- **Annotations:** Both documents contain extensive handwritten annotations, amendments, and cross-outs in period handwriting
- **Serial marking:** "£1333 : 6 : 8" circled in lower left of bottom page
- **Archival markings:** Visible in upper right corner of top page
