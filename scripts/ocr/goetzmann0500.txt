## Original Language Note:

This document is written in **Dutch** (17th-century Flemish/Antwerp dialect). It appears to be a contract regarding the purchase and transfer of stock in the Dutch East India Company (VOC - Verenigde Oost-Indische Compagnie), dated from the early 18th century, despite the header indicating it was sold in Antwerp.

---

## Notable Markings:

- Decorative ornamental border framing the entire text block
- Central heraldic seal/coat of arms with "SPIRO SPERO" inscription
- Multiple handwritten annotations in margins (dates and reference numbers)
- Signature line at bottom (largely illegible in reproduction)
- Red wax seal impression (partially visible)
