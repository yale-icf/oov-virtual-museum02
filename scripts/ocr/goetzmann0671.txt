## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 277167
- **Red Seal/Stamp:** Large circular red official seal visible in upper right portion
- **Ornamental Border:** Elaborate decorative gold and black Art Nouveau border design
- **Signatures:** Three handwritten signatures (Manager, Assistant, and Accountant)
- **Printer Mark:** "239 FL. Netttal" at bottom
- **Imperial Eagle Seal:** Double-headed eagle emblem at top center
- **Bond Denomination:** Multiple currency equivalents listed (rubles, francs, marks, pounds sterling, guilders, dollars)
