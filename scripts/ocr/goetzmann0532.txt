# Transcription

**120** *Livres Tournois Journois Dollars Num.* **71**

United States of AMERICA, 4th Day of November 1778

AT Thirty Days Sight of this Sight Bill, Second, Third and Fourth not paid, pay to Mr. John Tompkins or Order, One Hundred and Twenty Dollars, in Six Hundred Livres Tournois, for Interest due on Money borrowed by the United States.

To the Comptroller or Commissioners of the U. S.
United States of America, at
North Boston [or Nantucket - unclear]

H. Atkinson
Treasurer
(Treas. of Loans.)

Commissioner of the Continental Loan Office in the State of Massachusetts Bay

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **No. 71** (top right)
- Amount: **120 Dollars** / **600 Livres Tournois** (top and body text)
- Payee: **M. John Tompkins** (handwritten)
- Date: **4th Day of November 1778** (handwritten)
- Signature: **H. Atkinson**, Treasurer of Loans (handwritten, right side)
- Counter-signature: **Nath. Appleton**, Commissioner of the Continental Loan Office in Massachusetts Bay (handwritten, lower left)
- Decorative borders and engraved elements along left and right margins
- Multiple color inks (green and brown/sepia) for printed and handwritten elements
