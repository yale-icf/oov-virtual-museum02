**English Translation:**

TODAY THE FIRST DAY OF THE MONTH OF MAY OF THE YEAR SEVENTEEN HUNDRED EIGHTY-SEVEN.

Appeared before me ABRAHAM VAN BERM, public Notary, resident at the Court of Holland, established in Amsterdam,
Mr. PIETER STADNITSKI and Mr. HENDRIK VOLLENHOVEN, as Directors,
And the Well-Honored Gentlemen ABRAHAM FOCK and ADRIAAN SCHARFF, as Commissioners
In the hereafter named Negotiation, Parties known to me, the Notary, within this City.

Declaring, that by the two aforementioned Gentlemen Directors, pursuant to the Report issued by them, from diverse Participants, according to signed Receipts, a Capital of Seven times Hundred Fifty Thousand Guilders Dutch Current Money has been expedited, against such yearly Interests as shall be stated hereinafter, in order that the same monies, collected according to a fixed subscription of One Million and Five times Hundred Thousand Guilders, pursuant to the Conditions, each bearing interest [details regarding terms], returning 10 pCt. per capita, though burdened with the deduction of the tenth payment, and to be protected with young Participants within this City the Negotiation of Provision, Premium, and Brokerage &c. as often as necessary, and furthermore that such Contracts would be constituted upon the lives of the following young Ladies, being between the ages of 5 and 8 years, and attested to by the men and Notary GERRIT HOUSMAN and Witnesses, by the two aforementioned Gentlemen Directors and by me the Notary, said Gentlemen first Parties having proceeded, the matter being stated and recorded, and which Acte, under the terms of this Negotiation, for the benefit of the Owners and interested parties, remains in the keeping of my Notary's office.

Pursuant to, and in fulfillment of the aforementioned issued Report, by the two aforementioned Gentlemen Directors to me the Notary, in the presence and before the two aforementioned Gentlemen Commissioners, have been handed over; As is also known to the Notary, likewise certified upon Fine [sealed],
