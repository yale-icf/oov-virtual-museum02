# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

**Left panel:**

[Seal: LEKDIJK BOVENDAMS]

Allonge, behoorende bij rentebrief folio 25 no 74, groot Een Duizend gulden (ƒ1000.-) uitgegeven door het Hoogheemraadschap Lekdijk Bovendams te Albrecht (Goedeland) waarvan de eerste ten bedrage van Vijf en twintig gulden (ƒ25.-) teken jare verschijnt op den 12 Januari.

Afgegeven 8 Januari 1944.
Dijkgraaf en Hoogheemraden van den Lekdijk Bovendams.

[Signature]
Dijkgraaf

[Signature]
Secretaris.

**Middle and right panels:**

Two columns of coupon records with letter codes (A.A.A.A through U.U.U.U) and corresponding payment dates and amounts (primarily "12 Jan" dates from 1944-2003, with values of 25.- guilders)

---

## English translation:

**Left panel:**

Allonge, belonging to interest-bearing bond folio 25 no. 74, for One Thousand guilders (ƒ1,000.-) issued by the Water Board of Lekdijk Bovendams at Albrecht (Goedeland), of which the first interest payment of Twenty-five guilders (ƒ25.-) shall be paid annually on 12 January.

Issued 8 January 1944.
Dijkgraaf and Board Members of Lekdijk Bovendams.

[Signature]
Dijkgraaf

[Signature]
Secretaris.

**Middle and right panels:**

Coupon payment records from 1944 through 2003, showing annual interest payments of 25.- guilders on 12 January.

---

## Notable markings:

- Official seal of Lekdijk Bovendams (top left)
- Two signatures (Dijkgraaf and Secretaris)
- Two columns of alphabetic coupon codes (A through U) with payment dates
- Dated: 8 January 1944
