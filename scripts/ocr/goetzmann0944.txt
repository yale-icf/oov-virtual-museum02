**English translation:**

[The subscriber list contains 30 numbered female names (60-89), each preceded by "Jufvr." (Miss/Madam)]

All those who have agreed as my Notary in the presence of the Gentlemen Comparants are recorded in the following list. The aforementioned three different weekend locations, where a Notary will receive an Aandeel and a Quittantie [receipt], are as Notaries appointed: [paragraph continues with legal details about the negotiation process]

[Articles I and II detail the issuance of 750 copies of bonds worth 1,000 Guilden each, with terms for interest payments on the 1st of November and 1st of May to the bearers of the bonds, and conditions regarding the transfer of coupons and receipts]

**Notable markings:**

- Document appears to be page 2 of a 3-page contract
- Contains numbered subscriber list (entries 60-89)
- Legal document in Dutch from 1787
- References to bond issuance by Pieter Hendrik Vollenhoven and Pieter Stadnitski
