# Grand Lottery Trade Card Transcription

**Grand Lottery**
**NOW DRAWING.**

---

**SCHEME**

Prizes of £9,000
Prizes of 20,000
Prizes of 10,000
Prizes of 5,009
Prizes of 8c. &c.

[Central image: Engraving of a classical female figure (Fortune) holding a banner labeled "PRIZES"]

---

**Interesting Questions:**

Is there any other possible mode by which I may gain, from a stake of a few Shillings only, nearly Two Thousand Pounds?

Have I no ambition to make my fortune by a mode of adventure, where the loss is trifling, but the gain may be great?

---

**NEXT DAY OF DRAWING**
**WILL BE**

**Thursday, Jan. 22.**

---

**TICKETS & SHARES**

Are on Sale at all the

**Licensed Lottery Offices,**

*And by their Agents in the Country.*

Evans & Ruffy, Printers, 29, Budge Row, Wallbrook.
