# Imperial Russian Government 4½% State Loan 1905

## Original (Russian):

**Header:**
281 РУБ. 50 КОП.                                500 REICHSMARK.

ИМПЕРАТОРСКОГО РОССИЙСКОГО ПРАВИТЕЛЬСТВА.

РОССИЙСКИЙ

4½% ГОСУДАРСТВЕННЫЙ ЗАЁМ 1905 ГОДА,

выпущенный на основании ВЫСОЧАЙШЕГО Указа 15 Декабря 1904 г.
на нарицательный капитал 231.500.000 рублей = 500.000.000 имп. герм. мар.
= 294.000.000 гульд. руб.л. = 23.375.000 фун. стер.

в количествах 20.000 акциях по 5000 имп. герм. мар. с № 000001 по № 030000, 60,000 акциях
по 3000 имп. герм. мар. с № 030001 по № 120000, 140.000 акциях по 1000 имп. герм. мар.
по № 120001 по № 260000, 60.000 акциях по 900 имп. герм. мар. с № 260001 по № 320000.

**ОБЛИГАЦИЯ
№319459**

**В ПЯТИСОТ ИМПЕРАТОРСКИХ ГЕРМАНСКИХ МАРОК
= 281 РУБ. 50 КОП. = 294 ГОЛЛ. ГУЛЬД. = 24 ФУНТ. СТЕРЛ. 7 ШИЛЛ. 6 П.,**

приносящая в Государственном Казначействе Российской Империи

**НА ПРЕДЪЯВИТЕЛЯ.**

204 Г. ПОЛН.                                                     4 24.7.06 Р.

1905 г.

## English Translation:

**Header:**
281 RUB. 50 KOP.                                500 REICHSMARK.

IMPERIAL RUSSIAN GOVERNMENT.

RUSSIAN

4½% STATE LOAN 1905,

issued on the basis of the HIGHEST Decree of December 15, 1904
with a nominal capital of 231,500,000 rubles = 500,000,000 Imperial German marks
= 294,000,000 guilders = 23,375,000 pounds sterling

in quantities of 20,000 bonds of 5,000 Imperial German marks each No. 000001 to No. 030000; 60,000 bonds
of 3,000 Imperial German marks each No. 030001 to No. 120000; 140,000 bonds of 1,000 Imperial German marks each
No. 120001 to No. 260000; 60,000 bonds of 900 Imperial German marks each No. 260001 to No. 320000.

**BOND
No. 319459**

**IN FIVE HUNDRED IMPERIAL GERMAN MARKS
= 281 RUB. 50 KOP. = 294 DUTCH GUILDERS = 24 POUNDS STERLING 7 SHILLINGS 6 PENCE,**

yielding interest at the State Treasury of the Russian Empire

**TO BEARER.**

[Followed by extensive detailed terms and conditions regarding the loan structure, interest payment schedules, redemption procedures, and administrative provisions]

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 319459
- **Red Imperial Seal:** Circular seal of the Russian Imperial Government (upper right)
- **Russian Double-headed Eagle:** Centered at top in ornamental frame
- **Decorative Border:** Elaborate blue and mauve ornamental border design throughout
- **Date:** 1905 г. (1905 year) at bottom center
- **Multiple signature lines** at bottom (appears to be for officials/guarantors)
- **Denominations printed in three currencies:** Rubles/Kopeks, German Marks, Dutch Guilders, and British Pounds Sterling/Shillings
- **Ornamental baroque-style flourishes** at corners and throughout document
- **Text indicating bearer bond status** ("НА ПРЕДЪЯВИТЕЛЯ" - "To Bearer")
