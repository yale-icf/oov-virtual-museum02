# Transcription

**Title (top of card):**
Lute-String

**Main text (bottom of card):**
These Crafty Managers have play'd, for Years,
the World as Many Tricks as Dancing Bears;
By Bubbling too; they've broke their Ancient Rules,
They first made Lute-strings, but they now make Fools.

**Notable markings:**
- Club symbol (♣) in upper left corner
- Page notation visible: "Page 42 of 51"
- This is identified as the King of Diamonds from a set of 51 South Sea Bubble Playing Cards
- Wood engraving style illustration showing multiple figures in 18th-century dress in an interior setting with shelving visible in background
- Wooden blocks/logs depicted in lower left corner
