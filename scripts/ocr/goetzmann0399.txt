WITH A CAPITAL OF 30,000,000 FRANCS

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 059,820
- **Signature:** M. Merhier (Administrator)
- **Red Circular Stamp:** "MINES D'OR DE NAM KOK / SOCIÉTÉ ANONYME" with decorative circular design (appears on top document and multiple coupons)
- **Printer:** P. FORVEILLE IMPRIMEUR DE TITRES PARIS-RODEZ
- **Document Border:** Ornate decorative border with repeating geometric patterns
- **Coupons:** 24 detachable interest/dividend coupons numbered from 1-24
- **Status Mark:** "DROIT DE TIMBRE" stamp visible in top right corner
- **Background:** Subtle crosshatch security pattern throughout
