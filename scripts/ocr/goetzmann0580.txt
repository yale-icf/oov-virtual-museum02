"PRINCIPALITY OF BULGARIA

BULGARIAN 5% STATE GOLD LOAN OF 1904

[Details about the loan being authorized by the Prince's ukase, ratified by contract with the Banque de Paris et des Pays-Bas on October 1, 1904, with the loan sanctioned by ukase dated November 15/28, 1904...]
