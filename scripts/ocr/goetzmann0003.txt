## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** № 022882
- **Series:** Б (B)
- **Three official signatures** at bottom (Director of State Debts, Ministry of Finance official, and Control officer)
- **Ornate decorative border** with floral medallions in corners and geometric patterns along edges
- **Cream and brown color scheme** with pink circular watermark elements
- **Date issued:** September 13, 1941
- **Denomination clearly displayed:** 5,000 leva at 5% interest
- **"На Приносителя" (To Bearer)** - indicating this is a bearer bond
