# ENGLISH TRANSLATION

**SPECIAL ZERO-INTEREST BOND
of the
PORTUGUESE EXTERNAL FUND
5TH SERIES**

Emission of 477,517 bonds, special zero-interest bonds, redeemable at par by lottery, over 198 semesters as per the table inscribed on the reverse, when the 3 percent interest bonds of the 4th series bearing the same numbers are redeemed, these special bonds being 387,517 bonds of the 1st obligation numbered 1 to 387,517, 477,517 to 477,517 and 18,000 bonds of 5 obligations each, from 387,516 to 477,515

**Bond Certificate for 1 Obligation**
No. 303:486

Réis 308,000 — Pounds 6-12-8 — Francos 166.67 — Marks 135.34 — Florins 79.34

**GENERAL OBLIGATION**

Having been authorized by the Portuguese Government through the law of 11 May 1902 to convert the external debt, consolidated and redeemable, into zero-interest bonds for the period of 5 semesters, and having been obligated by virtue of this law to convert zero-interest obligations into 3 percent interest bonds of the 4th series...

[Legal text continues describing the terms of amortization through public lottery, the role of the Public Credit Board (Junta de Crédito Público), the capital amount of 308,000 Réis, and the conditions binding both the bondholder and the Portuguese State, spanning multiple paragraphs of binding legal provisions.]

Signed in Lisbon, 22 November 1862
