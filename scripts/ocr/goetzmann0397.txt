# Transcription of Mexican Telephone Company Stock Certificate

**THE MEXICAN TELEPHONE COMPANY.**

NOT VALID unless countersigned by BOSTON SAFE DEPOSIT & TRUST COMPANY, Transfer Agent.

No. 16621 NOT OVER TWENTY-FIVE SHARES — **25** — Shares.

Capital, $2,000,000          200,000 SHARES, $10 Each.

ORGANIZED UNDER THE LAWS OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK.

This is to Certify, that **Harry E. Jacobe** of Cane Bridge is entitled to **TWENTY-FIVE**

Shares of Ten Dollars each of the full paid and unassessable Capital Stock of The Mexican Telephone Company, transferable only on the Books of the Company in person or by Attorney on the surrender of this Certificate.

New York City,                                **FEB 28 1905**

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial Number: 16621
- Large red stamp: "CANCELLED"
- Red stamp: "BY CERTIFICATE ISSUED FEB 28 1905" with additional stamps for "AMERICAN LOAN" and "AMERICAN COMPANY" references
- Date stamp: "APR 5 1905" and "FEB 28 1905"
- Left margin stamp: "FEB 2 1[?]" and reference to "Boston SAFE DEPOSIT" and "TRUST Transfer Agent"
- Signatures: Treasurer (illegible) and President (illegible/partially visible as "C. [illegible]")
- Notation: "ISSUED FOR PROPERTY PURCHASED"
- Recipient name: Harry E. Jacobe (or Jacoby) of Cane Bridge
