**English translation:**

**LEFT PAGE (Main Certificate):**

GENERAL COMPANY
OF
TRAMWAYS OF MOSCOW & RUSSIA
(JOINT STOCK COMPANY)

Established by authentic deed,
of 17 January 1885, before Me VAN HALTEREN, notary,
in Brussels,
published in the BELGIAN MONITOR on 4 and 7 February 1885.

Registered office: BRUSSELS

PRIVILEGED SHARE
OF

Two Hundred Fifty Francs

BEARER SHARE FULLY PAID

No. 00274

Conferring the rights stipulated in article 44 of the bylaws.

Brussels, 8 February 1885.

One Administrator,                    One Administrator,
[Signatures]

N.B. The sheet of attached coupons, being exhausted, will be replaced.

No. 11707. — BRUSSELS. — TYP. AND LITH. E. GUYOT, PACHÉCO STREET, 12

---

**RIGHT PAGE (Dividend Coupons):**

Multiple coupon tickets numbered 30, 29, 19, 28, 18, 27, 26, 25, 24, 23

Each reads:
GENERAL COMPANY
OF
TRAMWAYS OF MOSCOW & RUSSIA
PRIVILEGED SHARE No. 00,274
Dividend coupon

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **00274** (appears on main certificate and all coupons)
- Two signatures from administrators at bottom left
- Ornate decorative border around entire certificate
- Circular seal/stamp in upper left corner (partially visible, red coloring)
- Red stamp impression in upper right area of main certificate
- Printer information: E. GUYOT, Brussels
- Date: 8 February 1885
- Coupons detached on right side, numbered sequentially (23-30)
