## English Translation:

**IMPERIAL RUSSIAN GOVERNMENT.**

**RUSSIAN**

**4% STATE LOAN OF 1902**

Issued for the realization of principles in Russia regarding compensation for damages from China in Manchuria and in accordance with His Imperial Majesty's Decree of 1 March 1902.

On a nominal capital value in thousands of rubles = 451,050,000
= 393,000,000 German marks = 231,570,000 Dutch guilders = 19,257,00[?]

In quantities of 22,000 sheets of 5,000 German marks each, numbered 000001 to 022000; 15,000 sheets of 2,000 marks from № 022001 to 037000; 10,000 sheets of 1,000 marks from № 037001 to 047000; and 5,000 sheets of 500 marks from № 047001 to 052000.

**BOND** No. 116485

**FOR ONE THOUSAND IMPERIAL GERMAN MARKS**

At 4 percent = 1900 year rubles = 40 francs

Licensed by the State Loan Book, [department] - [page].

**TO THE BEARER.**
