# Transcription of City of Baku 5% Loan Bond, 1910

## Original Text (Russian, English, French):

**Header (Russian):**
5% ЗАЕМЪ ГОРОДА БАКУ 1910 ГОДА

**Serial Number:** №00603

**Main Title (Russian):**
5% ЗАЕМЪ ГОРОДА БАКУ 1910 ГОДА

в 26.999.973 рублей = 2.857.140 фунтамъ стерлинговъ = 71.999.928 франкахъ,
въ числе: № 1 до 71432 - въ 189 руб.; № 71433 до 84292 - въ 945 руб.; № 84293 до 84577 - въ 4725 руб.

заключенный по основанiй ВЫСОЧАЙШЕ утвержденного б/19 Октября 1909 года положенiя Совета Министровъ.

**Face Value Box:**
ОБЛИГАЦИЯ
ВЪ СТО ВОСЬМЬДЕСЯТЬ ДЕВЯТЬ РУБЛЕЙ
= 20 фунтамъ стерлинговъ = 504 франкамъ

НА ПРЕДЪЯВИТЕЛЯ,

приносящая дохода по ПЯТИ процентомъ, въ годъ, уплачиваемыхъ по полугодiямъ, 2/15 Января и 2/15 Июля каждого года, безъ удержанiя какихъ либо сборовъ государственныхъ, городских и другихъ какихъ-нибудь пошлинъ и сборовъ.

**English Section:**
5% LOAN
OF THE CITY OF BAKU, 1910,
for Rble 26,999,973 = £ 2,857,140 = frs 71,999,928

BOND
FOR ONE HUNDRED AND EIGHTY NINE ROUBLES
= Pounds sterling 20 = Francs 504
TO BEARER.

Bearing 5% per cent interest per annum.

**French Section:**
EMPRUNT 5%
DE LA VILLE DE BAKOU, 1910,
de Rble 26,999,973 = £ 2,857,140 = frs 71,999,928

OBLIGATION
DE CENT-QUATRE-VINGT-NEUF ROUBLES
= Livres sterling 20 = Francs 504
AU PORTEUR.

portant intérêt à raison d'un pour cent par an,

**Signature Line:**
Gorodskoy Golova (Mayor)
Le Maire

The Members of the Municipal Administration
Les membres du Conseil Municipal

**Footer:**
Баку, Май, Баку.
1910.

5% облиг. въ 189 руб. = 20 фунт. стерл. = 504 фр.
5% Bond for Roubles 189 = £ 20 = Francs 504.
5% Obligation de Roubles 189 = £ 20 = Francs 504.

---

## English Translation:

**Header:**
5% LOAN OF THE CITY OF BAKU 1910

for 26,999,973 roubles = 2,857,140 pounds sterling = 71,999,928 francs,
including: № 1 to 71432 - at 189 roubles; № 71433 to 84292 - at 945 roubles; № 84293 to 84577 - at 4,725 roubles.

Concluded on the basis of the HIGHEST APPROVED decree of October 6/19, 1909 of the Council of Ministers.

**Face Value Section:**
BOND
FOR ONE HUNDRED AND EIGHTY-NINE ROUBLES
= 20 pounds sterling = 504 francs

TO BEARER,

bearing income at FIVE percent per annum, paid semi-annually, on January 2/15 and July 2/15 of each year, without retention of any state taxes, municipal and other duties and fees.

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** №00603 (appears twice, top left and top right)
- **Face Value Box:** 189 РУБ. (in decorative oval, right side)
- **Denomination Mark:** "189" in box (right side)
- **Official Seals:** Circular municipal seal of Baku (bottom left)
- **Red Stamp:** "FOUR SHILLINGS" seal (center-right area)
- **Signatures:** 
  - Mayor (Gorodskoy Golova) - signature present
  - Members of Municipal Council - signatures present
- **Date:** Baku, May 1910
- **Ornamental Borders:** Elaborate decorative frame with classical motifs throughout
- **Document Type Indicator:** "EMPRUNT 5%" (French) and "LOAN 5%" (English) clearly marked
