## Notable markings:

- **Serial Number:** 035685 (appears twice, Bulgarian and French sections)
- **Signatures:** Two handwritten signatures in the lower section (names unclear)
- **Red/Pink Stamp:** Circular stamp visible in bottom right corner (partially visible/faded)
- **Date:** August 1917 (en Corps 1917 Août)
- **Denomination:** 100 Gold Leva (Сто Лева Zlatni / Cent Leva en Or)
- **Printer:** Giesecke & Devrient (bottom right)
- **Design Elements:** Elaborate green border with geometric patterns; central allegorical figure with caduceus (Mercury/commerce symbol); coat of arms shield
