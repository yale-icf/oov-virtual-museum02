# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Top text (partially visible):
"t Waal alle ... naar de ..."

Heart symbol with arrow in top right corner

Bottom text:
"Om al mijn geld door de Aties niet
te missen;
Gopi ik t'in ze, tot lokdas voor
de vissen."

**English translation:**

Top text: "[The] Waal all... towards the..." (incomplete/unclear)

Bottom text:
"So that I won't miss all my money through the Aties [or Athenians];
I throw it in there, as bait for the fish."

---

**Notable markings:**

- Engraved illustration in traditional Dutch Golden Age style
- Heart symbol (♥) with arrow in upper right
- Small publisher's mark or monogram in lower left corner of the engraving
- Frame border around the entire composition
- This is identified as Card 26 from "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Card) playing cards from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Tableau)
