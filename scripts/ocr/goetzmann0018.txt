# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

mites Dat Die hier ter Bank gereghsteerde
worde.

II.

Die meerder kome te dispoueren of
aste tschijden dan hy op sine Vistentinge te goede heeft / sal / Dan het te Deel
gedisboureerde/twee per cento. tot boete
betaelen / en sijne aftscbijdinge niet
halderen hoo? hy rerepareerde hecft.

III.

Die gelde inbrengen / moeten op
yder sali een brieste binden / getekent
met de specie en baer naam/om/500 Doo?
der ce Deel recbft inbrengen / inter aenlionts honde worden nackgesien / het te
weenig of ce Deel by beeerste oitatie be-
pouderde / te recbt ce honnen b?engen.

Aldus eenparig door de Vroetschap bij
provisie Gearrelsleert op den 2de October
1720, en more follio van den Stachuize
t'UtrechtGepubliceerd den 11en October
1720.

In heunisse van My/
E: V: HARS CAMP:

**English translation:**

must be registered at this Bank.

II.

Whoever wants to dispose of or
cut it then he has on his Account to good / shall / Then it to Part
distributed/two per cent. as fine
pay / and his cutting not
hold as he repaired has.

III.

Those monies bring in / must on
every salt a brief bind / signed
with the specie and their name/for/500 Door
of the Part right bring in / in the meantime hand are viewed / it too
little or the Part by first occasion be-
held / to right to honor bring.

Thus unanimously by the Vroetschap by
provisional arrangement on the 2nd October
1720, and more folio of the Stachuize
Published in Utrecht the 11th October
1720.

In witness of Me/
E: V: HARS CAMP:

---

**Notable markings:**

- Signature/initials: "E: V: HARS CAMP:" at bottom
- Date notations: "2de October 1720" and "11en October 1720"
- Roman numerals marking sections: II and III
- Wear and aging visible on page edges
