# Transcription

**The Lottery leads us past the Reach of Want.**

WHAT Parent but must feel his bosom pant,
To place his Offspring past the reach of want?
With what so likely can you realize
A sum sufficient, as a Lott'ry Prize?
The risk's a trifle, and the Wheel is rich;
Two THIRTY THOUSANDS are within your reach;
One TWENTY, and One TEN, the Scheme adora,
Three of FIVE THOUSAND yet remain undrawn;
While others, worthy of your strict attention,
Swell this brilliant list too long to mention!
A Golden Harvest now your hand invites;
Go reap the fruits, and revel in delights!
