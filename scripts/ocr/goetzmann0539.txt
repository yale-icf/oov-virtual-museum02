## Notable Markings:

1. **Weimar German Eagle Seal** - Red wax seal with imperial eagle at bottom center
2. **Serial Number Stamps** - "№ A.22399" appears in red text (top left and right)
3. **Library/Archive Stamp** - Purple rectangular stamp visible in upper portion (partially legible, appears to be an institutional marking)
4. **Circular Seal** - Embossed circular seal with heraldic design (right side, center)

## Key Details:

- **Denomination:** 100 Belgas = 500 Belgian Francs
- **Bond Number:** A.22399
- **Issue:** Belgian tranche of the 1930 Young Plan International Loan
- **Interest Rate:** 5½%
- **Languages:** German, English, French (trilingual document)
- **Issuer:** German Reich (Deutsches Reich)
- **Date Reference:** August 15, 1930 (visible in some text sections)
