UNITED STATES OF AMERICA
STATE OF NEW YORK

170 BROADWAY BUILDING
CORNER BROADWAY-MAIDEN LANE, INC.
FIRST MORTGAGE LEASEHOLD 4½% SINKING FUND GOLD BOND
No. B25

Secured by Leasehold Estate in the Improved Property at the Southeast Corner of Broadway and Maiden Lane in the Borough of Manhattan, City and State of New York.

CORNER BROADWAY-MAIDEN LANE, INC., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York (hereinafter called the "Company") for value received acknowledges itself indebted and hereby promises to pay to the bearer hereof, or if this bond be registered, to the registered holder hereof, on the first day of May, 1948 [unless this bond shall have been called for previous redemption], the sum of

FIVE HUNDRED DOLLARS ($500)

and to pay interest thereon at the rate of four and one-half per cent (4½%) per annum payable semiannually on the first day of May and the first day of November in each year, and interest hereon until maturity hereof (unless this bond shall have been called for previous redemption) upon presentation and surrender hereof at the office of the Trustee, or at the principal office of the Company in the Borough of Manhattan, City and State of New York, in gold coin of the United States of America or of equal to the present standard of weight and fineness; without deduction for any normal federal income tax (except the excess, if any of any normal federal income tax imposed upon the holders of this bond by the United States government), and without deduction for any other tax, duty or governmental charge whatsoever.

The Trustee hereunder named, or any paying agent of the Company may be required or permitted to pay hereon or to deliver or receive hereunder under any present or future law of the United States of America.

If default shall be made in the payment of any installment of interest due on this bond on the date thereof or in the payment of the principal hereof, then the registered holder hereof may, in the sole discretion thereof, offer mentioned, reimburse such bearer or registered holder hereof who shall be a resident of the District of Columbia or of certain States of the United States of America referred to in said indenture, any and all taxes assessed or to be assessed upon such bond or the interest hereon, provided that no such obligation shall exist nor shall any such reimbursement shall be made to the Company, as provided in said indenture, within thirty (30) days after payment of such and all other charges shall not have become due and payable.

The Company also agrees, in accordance with the provisions of the Sinking Fund Gold Bonds, all of the bonds (save of the outstanding member 2nd debentures) issued and to be issued in an aggregate principal amount of not exceeding one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) during the continuation of the indenture, under the lien and provisions thereof, dated May 1, 1925 (hereinafter referred to as the "Indenture"), and duly recorded and delivered, and conveying, with Trustees First Trust Company of New York, as Trustee, are upon the Indenture estate securities to hereby made to said indenture, as is full and complete in description of the property mortgaged, the nature and extent of the security hereby owned and the rights of the holders of said bonds and of the Trustee to enforce said security.
