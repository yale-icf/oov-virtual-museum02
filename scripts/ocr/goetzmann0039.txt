# Transcription

**Title (top):**
Lending Money upon Bottom-Ree

**Main text (bottom):**
Some lend their Money for the sake of More,
And Others borrow to Encrease their Store,
Both these do oft Engage in Bottom Ree,
But Gwie Sometimes the Bottome of the Sea.
