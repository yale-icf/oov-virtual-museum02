## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 1,176,790
- **Signatures:** Two signatures at bottom of document
- **Ottoman Seal:** Imperial tughra (seal) visible in center panel
- **Coupons:** Attached coupons visible on right margin (numbered 1,176,790)
- **Ornamental Border:** Red ornamental frame with Ottoman-style geometric patterns and "4½" markings in corners
- **Languages:** French (left), Ottoman Turkish/Arabic (center), English (right)
- **Date Format:** Both Gregorian (1st September 1903) and Ottoman calendar (1/14 September / 1er Septembre) dates shown
