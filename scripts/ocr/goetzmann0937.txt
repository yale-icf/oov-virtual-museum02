# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

CONTRACT VAN OVERLEEVING
Letter G

ONDER DE ZINSPREUKE

DETYD BAARD ROOZEN.

CONDITIEN en VOORWAARDEN, waar op de ondergeteckende Persoonen met den anderen geaccordeert en overeен gekomen zyn, wegens het beleggen van by hun te fourneerene penningen, in Obligatien op alzulke Negotatien, als door de Heeren dezer Directie en Administratie zal werden goedeurd, best aan de Intentie deze te zullen voldoen, en die te conflictueeren op Lyven by de Inteckening te noemen.

[Followed by 30 numbered Articles (ART. 1 through ART. 30) detailing conditions and terms regarding investments, obligations, and participant rights in what appears to be a mutual insurance or investment arrangement]

---

**English translation:**

CONTRACT OF OVERLEEVING
Letter G

UNDER THE DEVICE

THE TIME BEARDED ROOZEN

CONDITIONS and TERMS, upon which the undersigned Persons with the others have agreed and come to terms, concerning the investment of funds to be provided by them, in Obligations on such Negotiations as shall be approved by the Lords of this Directorate and Administration, best serving the Intent thereof to be fulfilled, and to be recorded on Lives by the Signing thereof.

[Followed by 30 Articles detailing the mutual conditions, investment terms, obligations of participants, distribution of portions, conditions of lives, inheritance provisions, and dispute resolution procedures]

---

**Notable markings:**

- "Copy" notation in upper left corner
- Document dated 31 October 1774
- Value: 10 Gulden
- Origin: Antwerp (Anvers)
- Multiple reference notations and marginal marks throughout the text
- Table of "Lyven" (Lives) with corresponding gulden amounts listed at bottom left
