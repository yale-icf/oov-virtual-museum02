From July 15 to July 18

3,000 Yen
Increment Fund

Purchase price is 10 yen per share, cutting from the distance, initial tax payment this year in October, first lottery draw this year in October, dividend payments April and October each year in two or more installments of 133 yen, 333 yen◎, 2,000 yen principal◎

## Notable Markings:

- Circular seal with "三千円勤增金" (3,000 Yen Increment Fund) in top right
- Mount Fuji (snow-capped mountain) as central decorative image
- Teal/dark blue background with cream/beige text and imagery
- Vintage printing style typical of early 20th century Japanese financial documents
- Text arranged in traditional vertical columns (right to left reading)
