# Transcription

**FOR A VALUABLE CONSIDERATION RECEIVED BY EACH.**

**IT IS MUTUALLY AGREED BY:**

**The New York Central and Hudson River**
**Railroad Company**

**and the holder of the foregoing Debt Certificate No. 1390**
**of the New York Central Railroad Company for**

**ONE THOUSAND DOLLARS**

that the principal hereof shall not be due nor payable until the first day of May 1902, with interest at the rate of Four percent per annum, payable semi-annually on the first of twenty-four and shall both principal and interest shall be payable in gold coin of the United States of America of the standard of weight and fineness of Decem[ber] 24, 1892. Registered certificates of $1,000 or multiples thereof will be issued in exchange for certificates of the same, under such regulations as the said Company may prescribe. The assent to this agreement by the holder of said Debt Certificate is evidenced in its name, be for the purpose of having this agreement registered therewith, and by the acceptance of the accompanying coupons.

**Witness the seal of the Company**
**and [signature] at of December 1892**

[Signature: illegible/cursive]
**TREASURER**
