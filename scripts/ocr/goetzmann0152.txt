# Transcription

**Christmas Sport.**

"Who'll play To-Night at Blind-Man's buff?"
"I will," says JANE,—"but don't play rough:"
And I'll be Blind-Man first," says NED—
"So stand now clear,—mind how you tread;
"And if a danger I come near,
"Cry out—Roast Beef! Roast Beef! for fear."

[ENGRAVING: Classical allegorical figure of Dame Fortune seated in a carriage, blindfolded, with attendant figures]

Tho' Dame Fortune is blind, yet her favors she shows
To her friends all alike, and no difference knows;
So that all who would gain them this Lottery, they must
Apply (or they'll lose them) 'fore JAN. TWENTY-FIRST.
