# Transcription

**PANDORA.**

**MISCHIEVOUS PANDORA** will find her mistake,
If she comes expecting a piece of Twelfth Cake,
I like not the baggage; she may, if she pleases,
Withdraw from our treat with her box and diseases.
'Tis open'd, alas! and the plagues fly about,
While Hope lies behind, and is loth to come out.
Hope! sure I mistake, 'tis a Ticket I see,
A Prize in the Lottery—I hope it's for me.
