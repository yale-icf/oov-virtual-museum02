# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Actie raadzel

Nog blinder was't geheime van de Achies, dan voorheen t. Diep zinnig raadzel van den dapp'ren Samson scheen.

**English translation:**

Active riddle

Yet more mysterious was the secret of the Aches than before, the deeply meaningful riddle of the brave Samson seemed.

---

- Text is in serif typeface typical of 17th-century Dutch printing
