# Transcription: The Spassky Copper Mine Limited Share Warrant

## English Text (Left side):

SHARE WARRANT TO BEARER
No 68868                           THE                           No 68868
TITRE AU PORTEUR

**THE SPASSKY COPPER MINE LIMITED**

CAPITAL £1,250,000    CAPITAL £1,250,000
IN 1,250,000 SHARES OF £1 EACH    EN 1,250,000 ACTIONS DE £1 CHACUNE

1 SHARE No _______                1 ACTION

This is to Certify that the Bearer is one of the Proprietors of ONE fully paid £1 Share, numbered as above in THE SPASSKY COPPER MINE LIMITED, subject to the Memorandum and Articles of Association of the Company and to the conditions endorsed hereon.

GIVEN under the Common Seal of the Company.

LONDON this _____ day of _____ 19_17.

[Signatures for DIRECTOR and SECRETARY]

## French Text (Right side):

Il est Certifié par les présentes que le Porteur de ce titre est propriétaire d'UNE Action entièrement libérée portant les numéro ci-dessus de THE SPASSKY COPPER MINE LIMITED, aux termes de l'Acte de Constitution des Statuts de la Société et des Conditions énoncées au verso du titre.

DONNÉ sous le Sceau de la Société.

Londres ___ jour de _____ 19_17.

---

## Notable Markings:

- Serial number: **No. 68868** (appears twice at top)
- Date: **May 10, 1917** (handwritten in the date field, though partially unclear)
- Red chop/stamp visible in upper right corner (company seal mark)
- Ornate purple/mauve decorative border with floral and geometric motifs
- Large ornamental "1" numerals in bottom corners
- Bilingual layout (English/French)
- Decorative allegorical female figure in center holding scrolls
- Common Seal impression referenced (though not clearly visible in reproduction)
