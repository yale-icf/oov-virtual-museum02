# Transcription

**Title:** Fatning of Hoggs

**Main text:**
Come all ye Bacon making Greasy Rogues,
that want Good Nurses, for your Meagre Hoggs,
Send them to us, and at a Small Expence,
We'll fat em up, with Offal, Blood, and Grains.

**Notable markings:**
- Six of Clubs suit symbol in upper left corner (six club symbols arranged in traditional playing card pattern)
- Engraved illustration showing two figures on the left (appearing to be vendors or promoters) and multiple pigs being fed on the right near a wooden structure
- Decorative header with horizontal line shading
- This is part of a satirical playing card series related to the South Sea Bubble financial scandal
- Document appears aged with typical period paper discoloration
