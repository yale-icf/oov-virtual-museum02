# Transcription

LINGO.—Agreeable Surprise.

"I'm here, Domine Felix."

___

Your learning, good Lingo, is vast and profound—
But if you'd be thought really wise,
Court Fortune, whose gifts, when her wheel shall go round,
May prove an "Agreeable Surprise."

___

The Lottery will be drawn 14th THIS MONTH,
(January) and following Day,

---
