# Transcription

**SOUTH AFRICA.**

Lottery begins January 21, 1817.
Two Grand Prizes of 20,000 Guineas;
And FORTY other Capitals!
All in Sterling Money!—No Stock Prices!

---

**THE HOTTENTOT VENUS.**

TO partake of your mirth, we're a visit once more
From the beauty and pride of the Hottentot Shore;
Her claim to a share of the cake may be granted,
But that's not the object you'll find that's most wanted.

A share in the Lottery's wealth, Sir, has drawn her,
If obtain'd, she'll remain in Old England's snug corner.
