LOUIS STANISLAS XAVIER,

(L. S.)

LE MARÉCHAL, DUC DE BROGLIE.

N°. 373

Wy ondergeschrevene J. BOURCOURD en Weduwe E: CROESE & COMP., als daar toe, gelyk in vorenftaande Obligatie breder ftaat gemeld, geauthenticeerd zynde, bekennen voor rekening van hunne Koninglyke Hoogheden de Prineen van Vrankvyk, ontfangen te hebben uit handen van

de Somma van EEN DUIZEND GULDENS Hollands Courant voor Aandeel in dezelve Obligatie, en zyn de Coupons hiertoe behorende getekend [illegible]

Actum Amsterdam, den 28. September, 1793.

f 1000::-

Deze gecontrafigneerd en ten Prothocole aangetekend, [illegible] behorende de door my getekende Aandeclen niet boven het getal van Twaalf Duyzend Nummers. En zyn de Letters Patentes dato 7 September 1793, benevens de-Originele Obligatie van dato 25 September 1793 gedeponeerd en [illegible] berusttende onder my.

**English translation:**

GIVEN AT HAMM IN WESTPHALIA (that our [illegible] ordinaries & made the countersigning of the Marshal Duke of Broglie, Minister of State, the twenty-fifth day of September in the year of Grace One Thousand seven hundred ninety-three, and of the Reign of the King the first.

LOUIS STANISLAS XAVIER,
CHARLES PHILIPPE.

(L. S.)

THE MARSHAL, DUKE OF BROGLIE.

No. 373

We undersigned J. BOURCOURD and Widow E: CROESE & COMP., as thereto, just as in the aforementioned Obligation further stated, authenticated hereby, acknowledge for the account of the Royal Highnesses the Princes of France, to have received from the hands of

the Sum of ONE THOUSAND GUILDERS Holland's Current [currency] for Share in the same Obligation, and the Coupons hereto belonging are signed [illegible]

Done Amsterdam, the 28th September, 1793.

f 1000::-

This countersigned and noted in the Protocol, [illegible] belonging through me the signed Shares not exceeding the number of Twelve Thousand Numbers. And the Letters Patent dated 7 September 1793, together with the-Original Obligation of date 25 September 1793 have been deposited and [illegible] remaining in my custody.

By order of their Royal Highnesses.

Keeper of the Royal Treasury.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **N° 373** (printed and handwritten)
- Date: **28. September, 1793** (printed and handwritten)
- Amount: **f 1000::-** (1000 Dutch Guilders)
- Multiple handwritten signatures and flourishes throughout
- Notation of currency: **Hollands Courant**
- Official stamp/seal: "Par ordre de leur Altesses Royales" / "Garde du Tresor Royal"
