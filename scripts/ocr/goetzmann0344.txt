**English Translation:**

COUNCIL OF THE PUBLIC DEBT APPORTIONED FROM THE FORMER OTTOMAN EMPIRE
PROVISIONAL RECEIPT TO BEARER NO. 0,288,837
issued in exchange for the withdrawal of a unit obligation with nominal capital of Frs. 500 or £ 19.17 or Liqs. 22
FROM THE 5% 1914 OTTOMAN LOAN.

In accordance with the decision dated June 9, 1933 of the Commission for the apportionment of the nominal capital of the Ottoman Public Debt, established by Article 49 of the Treaty of Lausanne, the present receipt has been issued in exchange for the withdrawal of the aforementioned obligation, to establish:

a) that Turkey's share was the subject of a special issue; that the shares of Italy (Dodecanese), Palestine, Iraq and States under French Mandate were entirely amortized;

b) and that, as far as the others are concerned, as of the date of extinction of the present receipt, the nominal capital remains solely engaged with the borrower; that the amounts indicated in the table below, representing the States which are the debtors, in proportion to the amounts shown opposite each of them.

[TABLE of debtor states and amounts]

The present provisional receipt is exclusively for the purpose of establishing the rights of holders of securities withdrawn from circulation on the debtor States. Ottoman, indicated below. This provisional title may not, by means of assignment of rights, be effective by means of the present provisional receipt, which could be used as a means for the placement or other operations of shares in the Ottoman Debt. The accounts concluded between the States for the settlement of their respective shares of operations, the modalities of which will be made known, in due course, to the holders, by publications of the Debt Council.

FOR CONTROL:
By Declaration of the Council:           FOR THE COUNCIL OF THE PUBLIC DEBT APPORTIONED
                                         FROM THE FORMER OTTOMAN EMPIRE.

The President:

Paris, October 16, 1933.
