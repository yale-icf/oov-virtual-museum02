# Transcription

**POMONA.**

POMONA, the Goddess, now comes to display
Her fruits, on an apple-stall spread, this Twelfth Day:

The crops of her orchard most surely must fail,
When thus she's reduc'd to be trading retail:
Small profits from dealing are slender supplies,
To those which next Valentine's Day may arise
From Fortune's State Lott'ry; for then she intends
With Two Hundred Thousands to welcome her friends.
