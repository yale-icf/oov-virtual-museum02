Every Subscriber to mention the Class in which he will subscribe, and to pay £.50 on every £.100 subscribed, into the Hands of the Treafers, Messrs. LOCKHART, Bankers, in Pall-Mall, on or before the First of November 1789; and the remaining 50 per Cent. to be paid on or before the First Day of January 1790, when the Name, Age, and Description of every Nominee shall be given, and the Deed of Trust executed.

The Annuities to be paid half yearly, at Messrs. LOCKHART's, the Treafers, to commence from the Time of Subscribing; and the First half-yearly Payment to be made on the Second Wednesday in February 1790, and the Second half-yearly Payment on the Second Wednesday in August following; and on the same Days in each ensuing Year.

To secure the regular Payment of the Annuities, Six HUNDRED THOUSAND DOLLARS, in the Funds of the United States of America, which, at the Par of Exchange, are equal to ONE HUNDRED AND THIRTY-FIVE THOUSAND POUNDS STERLING, bearing an Interest of Six per Cent. per Annum, will be vested in the Names of FRANCIS BARING, EDMUND BOEHM, and THOMAS HENCHMAN, Esquires, of London, in Trust, to receive the Interest on the fame, and apply it in Payment of the Annuities as aforesaid.

The aforesaid Funds to be legally vested in the said Trustees, and by them declared fo to be; and the Deed securing the Application of the Interest to the Payment of the Annuities, to be enrolled in one of his Majesty's Courts of Record at Westminster.

The Proprietors of the 600,000 Dollars will alfo place FIFTY THOUSAND POUNDS Capital Stock in the Three per Cent. Consolidated Annuities of Great-Britain, in the Names of the said Trustees,
