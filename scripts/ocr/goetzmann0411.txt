**English translation:**

Folio. 247: B:

Received by me, the undersigned, from Joan Baptista Cogels, Cashier of the General Imperial East India Company; the sum of [crossed out: three hundred and sixty guilders] Flemish currency for a distribution of [crossed out: six per cent or] [unclear] guilders Flemish currency per Share on [unclear] _____ Shares which I hold in the same Company in my name, Done in Antwerp //: [crossed out: August]. 1734:

gls. 360 = w.t

[Signature: C.J. De Witte(?)]

**Notable markings:**

- Coat of arms engraving at top center (imperial eagle with shield and insignia)
- Folio number: 247: B
- Annotation at top right: "P. Orange 1734"
- Amount notation: "gls. 360 = w.t" (360 guilders Flemish weight)
- Multiple handwritten corrections and crossed-out passages
- Signature of recipient (appears to read C.J. De Witte or similar)
- Document reference number: 247
