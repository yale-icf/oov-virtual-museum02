# English Translation:

(1)

WE CHARLES the Second, by the Grace of God Elected Roman Emperor, [etc.]. To all and every one, whom this present deed may concern, be it known, that We, together with Our Heirs and Successors, do hereby openly and publicly declare and make known by this letter of obligation and bond, that through the mediation and directed agency of Willem Gideon Deutz, of Amsterdam, from diverse Persons and Individuals, partly within the jurisdiction of the States of the United Netherlands and partly residing in other Places, in service to our commonly administered treasury, and in the form of a loan, has been brought into the hands of Our Imperial Treasury in current money, against appropriate securities, a certain Capital Sum, and Holy Roman money, amounting to seven hundred thousand guilders in Holland Currency, and this common sum having been computed at one hundred thousand Guilders Holland Currency, [which] to Willem Gideon Deutz., and the further and mutual Interested Parties, the which aforementioned loans taken together have been subscribed, obligated and concluded between Us, Our Heirs and Successors, and from that time forth against Us, Our Heirs and Successors, with knowledge and agreement, on the condition and understanding that, having received the aforementioned loan amount of Capital, in yearly Interests We shall pay at a rate to the existing and stipulated Interest pro rata, the previous nominal borrowed sum of seven hundred thousand guilders Holland Currency for fifteen Years within Amsterdam, in like Holland Currency Coinage, shall be repaid and discharged from Anno one thousand seven hundred seven and thirty, until Anno one thousand seven hundred seven and forty, and two thousand seven hundred eight and forty, and two thousand seven hundred nine and forty, one thousand seven hundred fifty, and one thousand seven hundred fifty-one, the Capital itself in each and every Year pro rata to be placed;
