# Transcription

**ANGER.**
*The Rubber lost by a Revoke.*

[Engraving of Lady Pennypoint standing beside a table with two lit candles]

**LADY PENNYPOINT.**

With a *knave* I was caught, and put quite to the blush,
All *honours* were *play'd*, yet of hearts I'd a *flush*,
But my partner, poor deal, was as cross as Old Nick,
And 'twas by his *revoking* he lost the odd trick.
If my lead he'd return'd, and left shuffling alone,
I'd have *won* the first point, and the *game* was our own.
My only resource to some Office to *pack*,
Take a *rubber* with Fortune, on *loo* turn my back.

Lottery begins 21st This Month (Jan.)—2 of 20,000 Guineas, and
40 other Capitals—all Sterling Money (no Stock Prizes.)
