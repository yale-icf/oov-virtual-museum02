# TEXAS AND GERMAN EMIGRATION COMPANY
## CERTIFICATE OF STOCK

**No. 572 FOLIO 92**

SHARES OF ONE HUNDRED DOLLARS EACH.

This is to certify, that the German Emigration Company is proprietor of TWOT shares, No. 2775 and 2776, in the Joint Stock of the Texas and German Emigration Company, constituted by articles of association, incepted and agreement, contained in Indentures, bearing date the fifteenth day of September, A.D. 1843.

This Certificate is transferable by delivery or assignment, vesting in the bearer all and assignee all the rights of the original proprietor, to be subject to the provisions of said Indentures, and to the right of said German Emigration Company, as their assigns, to redeem the Stock hereby represented, by paying to him, as bearer, as aforesaid in any Banking or Commercial House in the City of Galveston, for him, as bearer, the said sum with interest from the first day of January, 1844, at the rate of five per cent per annum, on or before the first day of May, A.D. 1855.

Given at the City of Houston, this fifteenth day of June, 1852.

Issued by authority of said Indenture, and in accordance with its requirements, by

Henry F. Fisher

AGENT OF THE GERMAN EMIGRATION COMPANY.

---

## MEMORANDUM.

The JOINT STOCK of the **Texas and German Emigration Company** consists in the lands and tenements, rights, interests and property vested, conveyed, put in, assigned and transferred by the **German Emigration Company** to the **Texas and German Emigration Company**, as per Indenture of September 15th, 1843, to-wit: 1st.—All their right and claim to about 1,200,000 acres of land in Harris & Matagorda Counties, Grant. 2nd.—All their right and title to their private property in and about New Braunfels, Fredericksburg, Indianola, and lands and tenements elsewhere in the State of Texas. 3rd. All the debts, demands and claims due them in any manner in the State of Texas.
