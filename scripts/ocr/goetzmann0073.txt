# Transcription

**Title (top of card):**
Sugar

**Verse (bottom of card):**
Fair Tattling Gossips, you that Love to; see,
Fine Sugar blended, with Expensive Tea;
Since you Delight in things, both Dear & Sweet,
Buy Sugar Shares, and you'll be Sweetly Bit.

**Notable markings:**
- Playing card suit indicator: Five of Clubs (shown in upper left corner with five club symbols)
- Engraved illustration depicting a sugar processing or trading scene with two cherub/putti figures and large vessels in what appears to be an 18th-century commercial interior
- This is part of a satirical playing card set related to the South Sea Bubble financial scandal
