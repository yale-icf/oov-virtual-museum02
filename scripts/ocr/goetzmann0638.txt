## Historical Context Notes:

This bond represents Yugoslavia's (then Bosnia-Herzegovina's) attempt to secure investment capital through a multi-lingual, internationally-tradeable security instrument. The presence of six languages reflects the document's circulation across Austro-Hungarian, German, French, and British financial markets in the pre-World War I period.
