# Document Transcription

## Original (Classical Chinese):

**Title Section:**
中華民國二十九年建設公債
第一期美金債票

**Main Text (reading right to left, top to bottom):**

民國二十九年建設公債第一期美金債票
本公債設立年九十二國民
票債金美期一第
遠利還本金全本還
利自是本是遠利利利
翼請定法頒約自自以以以以
中華民國作本分指額遠十自月年（二十一）美金
十九代語北旁銀行保額凡元分指額遠得本期自年
年五月日財政部備金上須繳納保護金時得

## English Translation:

**Title:**
Republic of China Year 29 Construction Bonds
First Period US Dollar Bond Certificate

**Primary heading:** Republic of China Construction Bonds, Year 29, First Period - US Dollar Bond

The document contains terms regarding bond repayment, interest schedules, and administrative provisions for this war-era security issued by the Nationalist Government.

## Notable markings:

- **Red stamp/seal** (center): Large square official government seal in traditional red lacquer style with Chinese characters
- **Pink/red stamp** (upper left corner): Denomination marking "伍圓" (Five Yuan/Dollars)
- **Multiple circular vignettes** (corners and sides): Illustrations of trains, bridges, industrial/infrastructure scenes, and maritime vessels
- **Decorative borders**: Ornate blue and white scrollwork, floral motifs, and classical Chinese design elements throughout
- **Denomination markers**: "伍" (Five) appears in ornamental frames on left and right sides
