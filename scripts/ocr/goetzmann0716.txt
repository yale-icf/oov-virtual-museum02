**Notable markings:**

- Serial Number: **1450669** (appears at bottom of main bond and on all three coupons)
- Three attached coupons numbered **18, 19, and 20** at bottom, each bearing the same serial number
- Exchange rate notation at top: **$ 5 = zł 44.57**
- Multiple official signatures and handwritten authorizations
- Ornate decorative borders and vignettes in blue ink throughout
- Each coupon specifies: "wartości 0,10 dolara, czyli 0,89 złotego" (value 0.10 dollar, namely 0.89 zloty)
- Coupon payment dates noted as "1 sierpnia 1940 r." (1 August 1940)
