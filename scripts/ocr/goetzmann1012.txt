**CORNER BROADWAY-MAIDEN LANE, INC.**, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York (hereinafter called the Company), for value received, hereby acknowledges itself indebted and hereby promises to pay to the bearer hereof, or if registered, to the registered holder hereof, at the office of **MANUFACTURERS TRUST COMPANY**, Trustee, in the Borough of Manhattan, City and State of New York, or at such other place as the Trustee may designate, on the **first day of May, 1975**, the principal sum of

**FIVE HUNDRED DOLLARS ($500)**

with interest thereon until maturity hereof (unless this bond shall have been called for previous redemption), payable semi-annually on the first day of May and the first day of November in each year, and interest hereon until maturity hereof (unless this bond shall have been called for previous redemption), at the rate of four and one-half per centum (4½%) per annum, on the presentation and surrender of the interest coupons hereto attached at the principal office of the Trustee in the Borough of Manhattan, City and State of New York.

The bonds are issued under and pursuant to the terms and conditions of a certain Indenture of Mortgage, Security Agreement and Indenture of Trust, dated as of the first day of May, 1952, executed and delivered by the Company to the **MANUFACTURERS TRUST COMPANY**, as Trustee (hereinafter called the Indenture), to which Indenture and the modification and amendment thereof (if any), reference is hereby made for a description of the property mortgaged, the nature and extent of the security for these bonds and the rights of the holders hereof, and for the definition of the terms herein used.

This bond is entitled to the benefit of a certain sinking fund established in the Indenture, and is subject to the conditions specified in said Indenture relative to the application of such sinking fund.

The principal of and interest on this bond are payable in gold coin of the United States of America of or equal to the present standard of weight and fineness without deduction for or on account of any tax or taxes which the Company, as the issuer on the principal thereof, may be required or permitted to pay or deduct therefrom.

The Company covenants, in the manner, upon the conditions and subject to the limitations provided in the Indenture hereto-fore mentioned, to redeem on such dates as therein set forth, any of these bonds issued by registered holder thereof who shall be a resident of the State of Columbia or of the United States of America, at the request of such registered holder by reason of such residence and the ownership of, or receipt of income from, this bond, provided that application for such redemption shall be made to the Company as provided in said Indenture, within forty (40) days after payment of the then last coupon.

This bond is one of a duly authorized issue of bonds of the Company known as its First Mortgage Leasehold 4½% Sinking Fund Gold Bond of the issue (unless and deduction) issued and to be issued in an aggregate principal amount not exceeding **FIVE MILLION DOLLARS ($5,000,000)**, all of which bonds are issued under and are secured, respectively, of the same date of issue, by the indenture of mortgage dated as of the first day of May, 1952, executed and delivered by the Company, and are entitled equally and ratably with all other bonds issued or to be issued under said Indenture to the benefits and security of the Indenture and the property mortgaged thereby.

Lessor to the Company, as Lessor, [unclear], or to be recorded in the office of the Register of the County of New York. Reference is hereby made to the Indenture, for full and complete description of the security mortgaged, the nature and extent of the security.

This bond is entitled to the benefit of a certain sinking fund established in the Indenture and, upon the operation of said sinking fund, the principal of this bond, or any installment of interest due on this bond or, the Worthlessness or observance thereof at the times hereinafter specified in said Indenture shall be applied toward such principal or interest, and in case of default in the payment of the principal hereof, be declared, void and ceased to become due [illegible] in the manner, at the time of times and with the effect provided in said Indenture.

The bond shall be subject to the Company's obligation to effect and set to the security, in addition to the issue and effect thereof. But this bond is a corporate obligation only and to secure it whatsoever, either directly or through any trustee, receiver, personal or other agent shall be liable on the payment of the principal of or interest on this bond, for the enforcement thereof, except as may be prescribed by law, except as may be prescribed, or the enforcement of the Company or, that American corporation as follows of any original or contribution or by the amendment of any assessment, penalty, subscription or limitation affecting obligations and provisions thereof, all as provided for in the Indenture.
