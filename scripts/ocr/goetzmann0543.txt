# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Deze CONTRACTEN zyn te bekomen by RICARDO en DE LARA op J'et Wesperveld over het Wardhuis, N°. 70. Te Amsterdam.

Ondergetekende bekenne Verkogt te hebben aan [handwritten: A. L. Duviaux Dieu-Duzundt] — Ponden Sterlings Capitaal Actien van de Geconsolideerde [?] Ianns — van Engelandt, ten Pryze van [handwritten: Diegenen zynde] en [?] [unclear] — 50½ zynde Restanten van, de Undeling, uitgegeven in de Maand Januarij laats[til]. welken — 2000 — algemene, mits darentegen de Rekening te geven, op de Rescontre van [?] aankomende te laten Leveren, binnen Londen tot den gemelden Prys. En, zo middelerwyl eenige Uitzdelingen gegeven worden, zullen dezelven zyn ten profyte van den Koper, en tot afkorting van den bovengemelden prys, en in tegendeelal alle Bylagen en Inroepingen ten zynen laste, naar den gemeenen styl; alles op zodanige voorwaarden, als by het Reglement in de Maana December 1803 gearresteerd, fraan vermeld, waarnaar [?] Ondergetekende aanneeme zig te geaargagen, en voorts te goeder trouwwe.

Gedaan in Amsterdam den [handwritten: 13 Mei] A°. 1805

[Signature - illegible]

---

**English translation:**

These CONTRACTS can be obtained from RICARDO and DE LARA at J'et Wesperveld over het Wardhuis, No. 70, Amsterdam.

The undersigned acknowledges having sold to [handwritten: A. L. Duviaux Dieu-Duzundt] — Pounds Sterling Capital Stock of the Consolidated [?] Anns — of England, at the Price of [handwritten: various terms] — 50½ being Remainders of the Division, issued in the Month of January last[illeg.], which — 2000 — in general, on condition that the account be rendered, at the Rescontre of [?] to be delivered, within London at the aforesaid Price. And if, in the meantime, any distributions are issued, they shall be for the benefit of the Buyer, and in reduction of the abovementioned price, and conversely all Attachments and Claims shall be at his charge, according to common practice; all on such conditions as are fixed in the Regulation of December 1803, as mentioned, whereafter the Undersigned undertakes to conduct himself, and furthermore in good faith.

Done in Amsterdam on [handwritten: 13 May] A.D. 1805

[Signature]

---

**Notable markings:**
- Handwritten insertions throughout, particularly names and numerical amounts
- Signature at bottom (largely illegible)
- Date: May 13, 1805
- Reference to Ricardo en De Lara as brokers
- Address: Wesperveld over het Wardhuis, No. 70, Amsterdam
