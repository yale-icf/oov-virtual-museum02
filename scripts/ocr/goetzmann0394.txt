# Transcription

**SYLVIA OF OHIO.**

**LITTLE MIAMI RAILROAD COMPANY**

This is to Certify that James G. King's Sons, Trustees, are the proprietors of Ten Shares of Fifty dollars each of the Capital Stock of **The Little Miami Rail Road Company**; transferable only on the Books of said **Company** on the surrender of this Certificate.

**CINCINNATI** June 17th 1862

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number**: 9,40,44 (top left ornamental cartouche)
- **Share Denomination**: 10 Shares (top right ornamental cartouche)
- **Date**: June 17th, 1862
- **Location**: Cincinnati
- **Signatures**: [Two signature lines at bottom - one marked "Secy" (Secretary) on left, one marked "Pres." (President) on right]
- **Printer attribution**: Rawdon, Wright & Hatch, Cincinnati (visible at bottom)
- **Decorative elements**: Ornamental border with circular pattern, elaborate header with engraved railroad locomotive and passenger car, allegorical labor vignette at bottom showing workers with horse and plow
