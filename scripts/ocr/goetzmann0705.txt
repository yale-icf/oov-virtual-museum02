**Header:**
Series D — UNIFIED 5% DEBT OF THE CITY OF NAPLES — Capital L. 1000

**Main Body:**
Under the guarantee of the R. Government for payment according to the terms of the Law of 14 May 1881, N° 198 (Series 3°) and the Regulation approved by Royal Decree of 15 October 1881, N° 438 (Series 3°).

BEARER BOND N° 20027 / BEARER TITLE N° 20027

Emission of L. It. 86,053,310 of Capital, approved by the Municipal Council of Naples in the sessions of 20 October, 3 and [date] November 1880, with approval of the Provincial Deputation on 5 December 1880.

Nominal Capital One Thousand Lire / Nominal Capital One Thousand Lire

with the annual income of 5%, computable from 1 January 1881, the semi-annual payments of which shall be paid abroad on 1 July of each year and voluntarily from the coupons attached hereto.

THE MAYOR / [Signature]

Naples, 20 November 1881

SEEN FOR CONTROL AND FOR REGISTRATION OF THE TITLE
THE GOVERNMENTAL DELEGATE / [Signature]
