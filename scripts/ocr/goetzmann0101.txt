# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Cindelaos.

De Zuidze Hesperven dagt ik ver-
geefs, te vangen; zy knapt het geld, en laat de lege
hengel hangen.

**English translation:**

Cindelaos.

In vain I attempt to catch the Southern Star; she snaps the money, and leaves the empty fishing rod hanging.

**Notable markings:**

- Heart symbol in upper right corner
- Decorative border frame around the entire image
- Engraved illustration showing a man in 17th-century dress holding a fishing rod with a cherub/cupid figure below
- Plant/tree foliage in lower left corner
- Water depicted at bottom of composition
