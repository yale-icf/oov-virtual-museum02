# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Daarstaat koets en Paarden

Mijn koets en paarden zijn mij door den wind ontglipt.
Terwijl de teugel ook op 't laast mijn hand ontslipte.

**English translation:**

There stands a carriage and Horses

My carriage and horses have slipped away from me by the wind.
While the reins also finally slipped from my hand.

**Notable markings:**

- Number "67" in top right corner
- Text "Six of Diamonds" appears to reference this as card #67 from the Pasquins Windkaart deck
- Engraved illustration showing a man in 17th-century clothing holding a diamond shape and a kite string in a landscape setting
- This is page 38 of 55 from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Picture/Theater)
