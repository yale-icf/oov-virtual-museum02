# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Uyttoght of mijn lasten Ring.

Wie stenen koopt bij nagt laat zig altoos bedotten, Zo ging't in Zwingnempoix ook met al de Achtezotten

**English translation:**

"Display of my burdensome Ring."

"Whoever buys stones at night always gets deceived. So it went in Zwingnempoix too with all the Fools."

**Notable markings:**

- Number "20" in upper right corner
- Engraved illustration in Dutch Golden Age style showing a man in period dress holding a kite and cloth
- Landscape background with trees and buildings
- Page number indicates this is from a series (Page 42 of 55, Ten of Diamonds from Pasquins Windkaart playing cards)
