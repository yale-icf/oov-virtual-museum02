# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

*Top text:*
"k Zoek Zuid nog West,
Naar't oosten! best"

*Bottom text:*
"Het Zuid en't West heft zo veel kwaad
gedaan,
Dat nooythun naam weer op't Kompas
moet staan."

---

**English translation:**

*Top text:*
"I seek South and also West,
Toward the East is best"

*Bottom text:*
"The South and West have done so much harm,
That their names should never again appear on the Compass."

---

**Notable markings:**

- Card number "4" visible in top right corner
- Ornamental diamond shape in upper right
- Small ships and maritime elements in background landscape
- This is identified as the Four of Diamonds from Pasquins Windkaart playing cards
