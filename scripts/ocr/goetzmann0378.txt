# Hungarian Fund Bond Transcription

## Text Content:

**Header (top left):** Dated New York

**Header (top right):** One February 1852

**Main Title:** HUNGARIAN FUND

**Subtitle:**
On demand one year after the establishment in fact of the INDEPENDENT HUNGARIAN GOVERNMENT, the holder hereof shall be entitled to ONE DOLLAR payable at the Manhattan Banking or at other U.S. Agencies at London or New York or to exchange the same in sums of fifty Dollars or over, for Certificates bearing 5 per cent interest, payable in July or quinquennial instalments from one year after said event.

**Signature line:** [Signature - appears to read "B. W. Smith"]

**Footer/Printer mark:** Danforth, Bald & Co. New York & Phila'a [Philadelphia]

## Notable Markings:

- **Ornamental denomination medallions:** Number "1" in decorative circular frames (left and right)
- **Letter markers:** "C" in circular seal (left); "N°" in circular seal (right)
- **Allegorical figures:** Four engraved illustrations depicting classical and allegorical female figures representing liberty and virtue
- **Heraldic emblems:** Decorative coat-of-arms designs in upper corners
- **Serial number:** "28149" visible in red ink (right margin)
- **Ornamental borders:** Extensive decorative scrollwork throughout
