# Transcription

Fortune invites you to choose
One, Two, or all the
Three Prizes of £50,000.
Four Prizes of £220,000.
&c &c making
a Grand Total of
£314,400
In Stock and Money.

SWIFT & T

11 Poultry, 12 Charing Cross &
31 Aldgate High Street

(All the Capitals
May be Drawn) 7th Nov't

DB

# Notable markings:

- Royal crown emblem at top of carriage illustration
- "FR" monogram on the side of the carriage
- Initials "DB" at bottom left
- Decorative scrollwork and flourishes throughout the document
- Engraved illustration of a horse-drawn lottery carriage with a winged figure (Fortune) riding on top
