# Transcription

**No. 4696**

**LONDON AND STRAND BRIDGE**

**These are to Certify that John Colegate of Buckingham Street Strand, Victualler, Esqr. is a Proprietor of and entitled to ONE SECURE being No. 4696, Entered in the Registry Book of the above Undertaking and is by Virtue thereof One of the Company of Proprietors of the STRAND BRIDGE, Incorporated by Act of Parliament for establishing the said Bridge, and in pursuance thereof, Given under the Common Seal of the said Company**

**London this 30th day of December 1809**

[Signatures present but largely illegible]

**A Memorial of every Sale or Transfer of this Share must be entered by the Clerk of the Company**

---

## Notable markings:

- **Serial number:** No. 4696
- **Golden/gilt seal:** Large oval seal affixed to document (center-left), imprinted with bridge imagery
- **Decorative borders:** Ornamental scrollwork and twisted rope patterns on left and right margins
- **Date:** 30 December 1809
- **Document type:** Share certificate printed on vellum
- **Signatures:** Two signatures present at bottom right (largely illegible due to cursive script and image quality)
