# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

*Top text:*
"Een Spagie mm of meer
Scheelt weing daar mij aert."

*Bottom text:*
"Mijn rotte kist doet mij thans met
sesje pronken',
't Behoitzy het geld zwel, maar myzje
eer, 'die is verzounken'."

**English translation:**

*Top text:*
"A Spaniard more or less
Changes little in my nature."

*Bottom text:*
"My rotten chest now makes me
show off with a sixpenny piece;
It holds the money well, but my honor,
that is sunken."

---

**Notable markings:**

- Card number "6" visible in upper right (indicating Six of Spades)
- Spade symbol in upper right corner
- Engraved illustration in Dutch Golden Age style
- Page notation suggests this is from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Theater) - a series of Dutch satirical playing cards known as "Pasquins Windkaart"
