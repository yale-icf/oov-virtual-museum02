# REGLEMENT OP DE WISSELBANK BINNEN UTRECHT

**Original:**

REGLEMENT
OP DE
WISSELBANK
Binnen UTRECHT,

Gearresteert op den 7en Oktober 1720.
en Gepubliceert den 11 dito.

[Ornamental coat of arms with lions and shield]

UTRECHT.
Gedrukt by Jacob van Poolsum, Stads Drucker,
tegen over 't Stadhuis, 1720.

---

**English translation:**

REGULATION
FOR THE
EXCHANGE BANK
Within UTRECHT,

Approved on the 7th of October 1720,
and Published the 11th of the same month.

[Ornamental coat of arms with lions and shield]

UTRECHT.
Printed by Jacob van Poolsum, City Printer,
opposite the Town Hall, 1720.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Page number "244" in a circle at top right
- Decorative coat of arms featuring two lions supporting a shield with vertical stripes, surrounded by ornamental flourishes
- Printed in Utrecht by Jacob van Poolsum
- Date: 1720
- This is page 1 of 7 of a bound collection titled "Het groote tafereel der dwaasheid" (The Great Mirror of Folly) relating to Dutch speculative mania
