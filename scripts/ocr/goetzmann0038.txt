# Transcription

**Title (top):** Irish Sail Cloath

**Caption (bottom):**
If good S.t Patricks Friends should raise a Stock,
And make in Irish Looms true Holland's Duck,
Then shall this Noble Project by my Soul—
No longer be a Bubble, but a Bull.
