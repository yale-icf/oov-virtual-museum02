**Title:** Manuring of Land

**Text at bottom:**

A Noble Undertaking but abus'd,
And only as a Tricking Bubble us'd,
Much they Pretend to, but the Publish Fear,
They'll never make Corn Cheap, or Horse Dung Dear.
