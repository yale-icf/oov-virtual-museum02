# Transcription

United States Lottery No. 16m769.

CLASS the FIRST.

THIS TICKET entitles the Bearer to receive such Prize as may be drawn against its Number, according to a Resolution of CONGRESS, passed at Philadelphia, November 18, 1776.

---

## Notable markings:

- **Serial number:** 16m769 (printed in upper right, in decorative box)
- **Class indicator:** "I." (printed at lower left)
- **Signature:** "D. Jackson" (manuscript signature, lower right)
- **Decorative elements:** Ornamental scrollwork border on left side; decorative star pattern borders on top and right edges
- **Date:** November 18, 1776 (printed text)
- **Location:** Philadelphia (printed text)
