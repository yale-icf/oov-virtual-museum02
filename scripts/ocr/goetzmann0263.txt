# Transcription

**Anno Regni octavo Georgii Regis.**

Whereas, aforesaid; Be it further Enacted by the Authority aforesaid, That the Commissioners of the Stamp'd Creations, or any Three of one of them now being, or the Lord Treasurer, or any Three of any of the Commissioners of the Treasury at the time being, shall and they are hereby enabled and impowered to cause such Callies of Pro or Allignment, as aforesaid, or other Callies, to be risen upon and stricken in the Name of Names of such Person or Persons, as they the said Commissioners of the Treasury, or any Three of more of them, or the Lord Treasurer, or any Three or more of the Commissioners of the Treasury for the time being, shall think fit to entrust in this behalf, as Part of the said Sum now preceding Widowhood Thousand Pounds, which hass intended to be raised by the said foregoing Act; and that upon such producing a further of such Calley of Callies to the said Cashere of the Governor and Company of the Bank of England for the time being, a Receipt or Receipts in Quiting shall be given by the said Cashere to the Person or Persons to be entrusted, his or their Signe or Signes, producing or tendering the same, shall in neper thence be handed and adged to be Contributors within the Meaning of the Act in part before recited; and their Names, and the sum contained in such Callies respectively, shall be fairly entred in the Book of Books of the said Comptroller for the time being, and into the Duplicates or triplicates thereof, to be transmitted into the Office of the Auditor of the Receipt, or the Exchequer; and that such Person or Persons to be entrusted, or the Person or Persons by them or those appointed, in the Bodies Politick or Corporate, who (no Affiance or Signings of the Person or Persons to be entrusted) shall deliver up or tender such Calley or Callies, his or their Successors, Administrators, Successors and Assigns, shall have, receive, and enjoy, and he entitled in his or their Name, to have, receive, and enjoy, the respective Annuity or Annuities to be payable on by in respect of the said Sum of One hundred two thousand six hundred eighty two Pounds, Seven Shillings, Farthings, and every or any Part thereof, out of the Yearly Fund by the last mentioned Act letted and stablished, and shall have the like Estate and Interest therein, from the said Feast of Saint John Baptist, One thousand seven hundred and twenty one, and all other Benefits and Advantages in these thereof, as if such Contributions had been specially made in Ready Money, according to that Act.

And for the more speedy, raising good the Subsiding Sums enabled by the Authority aforesaid, lawfull to any One or Commissioners of any Three or more of them now being, or any Three or more of them now being, or any Three or more of the Commissioners of the Treasury for the time being to call upon such Commissioners of the Treasury, or any Three or more of them respectively, by Warrant to be raised upon such other Callies, or upon such Money thereof, as aforesaid, by Specie of Justices, or where, or for such Commissioners of the Treasury, or they Creature thereof, to be reasonable; all which Annuities of One purposed Pounds, Seven Shillings, Pence, shall nevertheless be Parliament as to to the last other Annuities, which have been and is hereby granted.

And as it hereby enacted, he raised, aforesaid, for or hundred two thousand six hundred eighty two Shillings, and Eleven Pence as the time shall be raised, for King good the Sinking Fund the said Exchequer Bills, the Company, as is above mentioned, is revoked, a provide to the contrary notwithstanding.

Provided always, and be aforesaid, & one all specified as set forth, upon One Act of this Actituled, An Act for granting a Land-Tax to be raised in and of the Year One thousand seven and in much manner as herein granted, as shall arise and raised to be made on the Act last by that Act or any other Act transferred or to be transfer[red]
