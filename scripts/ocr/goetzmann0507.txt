**Notable markings:**

- Wax seal impression in upper right corner (appears to be an official Amsterdam stamp)
- Printed header with decorative typography
- Date notation: "DONDERDAG, 16 OKTOBER" (Thursday, October 16, 1822)
- Multiple columns of numerical price data throughout
- Various abbreviations: p.ct. (per cent), dito. (the same), pCt. (percent), M.E. (unclear abbreviation)
