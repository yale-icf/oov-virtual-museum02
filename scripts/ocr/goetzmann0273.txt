# Transcription

**336          Anno Regni octavo Georgii Regis.**

**Which Duties shall be paid Down in Ready Money by the respective Importers of such Plates, from time to time, before Landing of the same respectively.**

And be it Enacted by the Authority aforesaid, That the said Duties upon Plates Imported, shall be raised, levied, recovered, and paid, and be brought into the Exchequer, by such Rules, Maps, Means, and Methods, and under such Penalties and Forfeitures, and in such Manner and Form, as the Duties upon Plates hereby taken away, or any of them, might have been raised, levied, recovered, and paid, if the same or any of them had continued.

**How appropriated.**

Provided always, and it is hereby Enacted by the Authority aforesaid, That the Duties to arise upon the Importation of Plates, pursuant to this Act (the necessary Charges of Management excepted) shall be appropriated and applied, as near as may be, to the same Uses and Purposes, to which the former Duties upon Plates by this Act taken away, were applicable or ought to have been applied if this Act had not been made.

**How redeemable.**

Provided also, and it is hereby Enacted, That the Duties by this Act charged upon Plates Imported, or any part thereof, shall be redeemable by Parliament, in the time and Manner as the said former Duties on Plates hereby taken away, or any of them, were redeemable; and in all Cases where any of the said former Duties on Plates were to cease or determine, a proportional Part of the Duties on Plates hereby charged, shall likewise cease and determine.
