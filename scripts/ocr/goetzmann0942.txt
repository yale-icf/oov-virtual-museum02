# Transcription of French Consular Certificate of Egyptian Financial Document, Alexandria, 1866

## Original (French and Arabic):

**Top of page (French):**
£195,000 -- au 8 Novembre 1865
au change de 69½ au £.p. 2100 "

**Stamp (upper left):**
H.M.'s [unclear] 1866

**Reference number:**
N°106

**Arabic text (main body - top section):**
[Lengthy Arabic text - appears to be official financial/governmental declaration]

**English/French text (middle section):**
"This here Application to Cashed a Billet & S/C.
Mohammed Sharif Pasha Minister of Finance
Alexandria 8 May 1866
To Minister del Affaires Etrangeres
Je demande l'exequatur

[Several illegible lines with handwritten annotations and crossed-out text]

No letter Destination to the Dominion
A Man Travelling. Ouziah Solde
as Solium de Affaires Etrangeres
Loyalites

6 Mars 1866

Councel [Consul?] General
Samplles Ambesse

**Signature:**
D'Boigne

---

## Notable markings:

- **French Consulate seal** (circular stamp, center-lower portion): "CONSULAT GENERAL DE FRANCE / ALEXANDRIE"
- **Multiple handwritten signatures** (various official parties)
- **Serial number:** N°106
- **Date stamps and official notations** throughout document
- **Crossed-out and amended text** indicating official review and processing
- **Reference to monetary value:** 195,000 Piastres at exchange rate of 69½ to £1 (equivalent to 2,100 £)
