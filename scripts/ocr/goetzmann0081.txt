# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Kauegl

Prins Freddryks
mars, naar Vrianen

Ik slaprins Freddryks mars, om met
al de Schiegeleken.
Als wrind zoldaeten, naar Vrianen toe
te trekken.

**English translation:**

King [of Spades]

Prince Frederick's
march, toward Vriane

I sleep Prince Frederick's march, in order to meet
all the [soldiers/troops].
As wind soldiers, toward Vriane to
proceed.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Title "Kauegl" at top
- Spade suit symbol (large, solid black spade) in upper right
- Engraved illustration of a figure in 17th-century dress holding a large barrel/drum, with a feathered hat
- Background landscape showing a fortified city and military encampment
- This is page 3 of the Pasquins Windkaart (Pasquin's Wind Cards) from *Het Groote Tafereel*, a Dutch satirical playing card series
- The engraving style is characteristic of Dutch woodcut/engraving from the 17th century
