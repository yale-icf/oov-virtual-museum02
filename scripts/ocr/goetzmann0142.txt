# Transcription

**FARMER CUT-CLOSE.**

THE jolly Farmer lends his willing hand,
By his industry to enrich our land;
Equally willing is he to regale
At Christmas, or Twelfth-Day, with cake and ale;
He tries the Lottery, for it's free to all,
And gains Five Thousand, though his risk was small;
Sells off his stock, enabled to retire
At ease, an independent country squire.
