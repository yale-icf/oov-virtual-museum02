# Transcription

**Title (top):**
Purchaseing of Estates illegally detain'd

**Verse (bottom):**
You that have Dormant Titles to Estates,
Fill on your Cloyst Shelves or feed the Rats,
Sell them to us we'll gratify your Spite,
And Plague the Rogue that Rob you of your Right.
