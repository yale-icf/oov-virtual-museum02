## English Translation:

**COUNCIL OF THE PUBLIC DEBT DISTRIBUTED FROM THE FORMER OTTOMAN EMPIRE**

**PROVISIONAL BEARER RECEIPT No. 0,951,240**

Delivered in exchange for a unit bond of a nominal capital of Frs. 500 or £ 20 or Ltqs. 22

**OF THE OTTOMAN LOAN UNIFIED CONVERTED DEBT 4°/o.**

In accordance with the decision dated June 9, 1933 of the Commission for the distribution of the nominal capital of the Public Debt of the Former Ottoman Empire, established by virtue of Article 49 of the Treaty of Lausanne, the present receipt has been delivered, in exchange for withdrawal of the following bond, by the enterprise

**DEUTSCHE BANK UND DISCONTO-GESELLSCHAFT HAMBURG BRANCH**

Given that Turkey has been subject to a special issue; that the share-holdings of Italy (Dodecanese), Palestine, Iraq and neighboring States, as a result of these events, at the date of issuance of the present receipt, the unamortized nominal capital of the aforementioned loan is found to be reduced to the amounts specified in the table below, which correspond to the States which are the debtors thereof at the ratio of the gold exchange.

**DEBTOR STATES**

[Table with same structure and data as above]

This provisional receipt is issued exclusively to establish the rights of the holders of bonds withdrawn from circulation against the debtor States of the Ottoman Loan. The Debt Council will not be able to proceed, by means of this receipt, to amortize the nominal capital at their charge. Future operations that could be carried out by means of this provisional receipt, with a view to the application of the Agreements concluded with the States that it replaces, will be brought to the attention of the public through notices published by the Debt Council.

**FOR CONTROL,**

By Delegation of the Council:

**FOR THE COUNCIL OF THE PUBLIC DEBT DISTRIBUTED FROM THE FORMER OTTOMAN EMPIRE,**

The President:

Paris, October 1st, 1933.
