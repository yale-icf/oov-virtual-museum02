## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 009951
- **Issue Number:** 88th Issue (第88回)
- **Denomination:** 55 Yen (金五拾圓)
- **Date:** Showa 35 (1960)
- **Circular seals/stamps** in red at bottom left and right corners
- **Official bank seal** (日本勧業銀行) present
- **Ornamental decorative borders** throughout in traditional Japanese style
- **Denomination marking stamp** (貯18) appears in four corners
