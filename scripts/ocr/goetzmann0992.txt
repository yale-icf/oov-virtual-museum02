# English Translation

**Company of the Economic Railways of Eastern Egypt**

[Each coupon box contains the same structure with varying obligation numbers (110-120) and payment dates, alternating between July 1st and January 1st from 1952-1957]

Coupon of eight francs eighty-two centimes
Payable [on specified date] in: Cairo, Alexandria, Paris, London, Amsterdam, Brussels and Geneva

[Right side English text:]
"...is only from the day of commencement of the conceded lines mentioned in article 1 of [the agreement covering] 50 kilometres) and from the date of the lines which may hereafter be conceded in [the] article.

The lines previous to their general working [shall be examined] by sections of 25 kilometres, and the guarantee shall be available from the date of such examination for each..."

# Notable Markings:

- **Bond Serial Number:** 10592 (consistent across all coupons)
- **Coupon Numbers:** 110–120 (semi-annual coupons)
- **Decorative border:** Blue ornamental frame around each coupon box
- **Payment locations:** Stamped in capital letters on each coupon
- **Currency:** Swiss/French francs (8.82 francs per coupon)
