## English Translation:

[Series of dividend receipts (Quittantie) for 1/1063 share of the Alum and Iron Works Garphytte and Beata Christina, covering years 1776-1786, issued in Amsterdam. Each entry shows:]

"Received from the Directors of the Alum- and Iron Works Garphytte and Beata Christina, the sum of [blank] for one year's dividend on a portion in these Works, over the year [date]. Adam Amsterdam [year]."

"This receipt serves as the receipt number 27."

"By procuration of the Directors."

---

**Notable Markings:**

- Multiple signatures in cursive: "M. Cornt" (repeated on each receipt)
- Serial number references: "No. 27" 
- Annual dating from 1776-1786
- Location stamp: "Adam Amsterdam" (Amsterdam)
- Document reference number in header: "No. 27"
