**English translation:**

ROMANIAN BANK
ANONYMOUS SOCIETY
CAPITAL STOCK LEI 350,000,000.
EMISSION VIII-A

IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE DECISION OF THE EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF 12 FEBRUARY 1938, PUBLISHED IN THE OFFICIAL GAZETTE NO. 57 OF 10 MARCH 1938, AND REGISTERED IN THE COMMERCIAL REGISTER ON 12 FEBRUARY 1938, THE BANK'S SHARE CAPITAL WAS INCREASED THROUGH A NEW EMISSION OF 140,000 LEI. OF WHICH 35% WAS PAID IN CASH AND 65% IN STATE SECURITIES. IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE DECISION OF 2000 SHARES OF 500 LEI EACH.

BY ORDER OF THE IFOV COURT, PUBLIC NOTARY SOCIETY AUTHENTICATED NO. 4437 OF 11 DECEMBER 1910, PUBLISHED IN THE OFFICIAL GAZETTE NO. 232 OF 8 JANUARY 1911...

THE DURATION OF THE SOCIETY IS UNLIMITED.

BEARER TITLE
ONE SHARE
NOMINAL VALUE LEI 500

No 0560076

ADMINISTRATORS                    SIGNATURE FOR CONTROL
[signatures]

BUCHAREST 1938
