# Document Transcription

## Original (English):

**LEFT PAGE:**

MEXICAN
FIVE PER CENT. DEFERRED STOCK.

Letter D.

Bond for
£500 Sterling,

Bond Warrant
For 2000 Acres,

BEARING INTEREST FROM
OCTOBER, 1847.

Whereas The President of the Republic of Mexico did, under date of the 15th of April, 1837, with the concurrence of the Council of Government, issue a Decree for the creation of a National Consolidated Fund, with interest at the rate of Five Per Centum per annum, to be paid and distributed immediately through London Banking houses, to be paid half-yearly.

Whereas, at a General Meeting of the Mexican Bondholders held in London, pursued to public advertisement, on the 9th day of November, 1837, it was resolved, by the Bondholders then present, and the great bulk of the absent Bondholders, that a Committee should be appointed to arrange, adjust and settle the affairs of the Mexican Government at the Court of London; having in the same aid on behalf of and Government, by virtue of their said deed and authority vested in him, entered in full cancellation of said Deferred Stock, into the following Agreement for the purpose of conversion, Bond Deferred a copy in Spanish, and a translation in English, together with a copy of said Agreement, are hereto annexed:

And Whereas all other, they, it is the above named America De Verragas [illegible], all British and Mexicans, at the Court of London, acting under the authority aforesaid, do hereby annul this Obligation, superseding and having had been and other issue of a Bond for Five Hundred Pounds sterling, or a Warrant for Two Thousand Acres in the Republic of Mexico, issued under authority of the said Decree, and whereas the Government in London, are duly authorized to redeem convert for conversion of the proper amount, according to the terms of said Decree and Agreement as herein set forth of the Mexican Government, providing for the application of all monies to be received from the respective Governments of Mexico and England towards the payment of the principal or interest of the whole overdue thereof, as they shall deem fit, and whereas the Government of Mexico, at the Court of London, do hereby annul this Obligation, superseding and having had been and other issue of a Bond for Five Hundred Pounds sterling, or a Warrant for Two Thousand Acres in the Republic of Mexico, issued under authority of the said Decree:

And it is also further Bristler, That the holder of this Bond or Land Warrant shall be entitled in any time, until the first day of October, 1847, to receive in exchange for the same Two Thousand Acres of Land, comprised in the Republic of Mexico; and that, in case of the non-acceptance of such offer, the whole of the above Acres per Bond issued will be appointed in the amount of interest thereon, calculating from the first day of October, 1847, until the date of giving possession of said land, subject accordingly to the conditions and regulations herein contained.

And it is also further Bristler, That the holder of this Bond or Land Warrant shall be entitled in any time, until the first day of October, 1847, to receive in exchange for the same Two Thousand Acres of Land, comprised in the Republic of Mexico; and that, in case of the non-acceptance of such offer, the whole of the above Acres per Bond issued will be appointed in the amount of interest thereon, calculating from the first day of October, 1847, until the date of giving possession of said land, subject accordingly to the conditions and regulations herein contained.

And it, said America De Verragas, acting as aforesaid, do hereby guarantee and confirm to the holder of this Bond or Land Warrant, all and singular the Hypotecations, Securities, and Stipulations, originally granted to the Holders of the Deferred Stock issued under the authority of the said Decree, with all and every other and further advantages, powers, and privileges granted by the same, to the full benefit and effectual discharge of the obligations created by the Bond or Land Warrant, to grant and to pay at all times, and under all circumstances, as follows:

In Witness whereof, I, the said America De Verragas, have hereunto set my hand in London, this 20th day of September, 1837.

[Signature]

Safe Thereof Certifie, that the foregoing is the Signature of Don America De Verragas, the person named in the above Bond as the special Agent of Mexico appointed, by the Government of Mexico, for the purpose of conversion, Bonds of the Mexican Government proceeding from the Loans effected in London, or for internal Revenue, represented by Deferred Stock and Warrants, or Deferred Warrants otherwise, having the same, at the time of his Signature to this Instrument, full power and authority so to do as is by the Government of Mexico.

Consolidated Fund and Court, Paid of this date,

London, this 20th day of September, 1837.

[Signature]

[AGENTS FOR THE UNDERWRITERS OF THE MEXICAN GOVERNMENT]
