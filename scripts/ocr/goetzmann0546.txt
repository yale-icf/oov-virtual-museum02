**Main heading:**
БЕЗСРОЧНЫЙ
[Bezstochny - Perpetual/Without Term]

**Visible handwritten and printed text (partial/legible portions):**

Дарпелъскъ Банкъ городѣ №
АМСТЕРДАМ
в Бельгаде
[unclear text]

2 Sem 1909
[Multiple semester/half-year notations with dates:]
- 1 Sem 1906
- 2 Sem 1906
- 1 Sem 1907
- 2 Sem 1905, 1 Sem 1906, 2 Sem 1906, Sem 1907

**Handwritten cursive text (largely illegible due to image quality and overlapping stamps):**
- References to "Пруссия" (Prussia)

- Official signatures and endorsements

## Notable Markings:

1. **Oval Revenue Stamps:** Approximately 80-100 circular stamps densely covering the left margin and portions of the document body, dated from 1850s-1870s, in purple, red, and blue ink, bearing denominations like "1 Sem," "2 Sem" with years (1861-1915 range)

2. **Rectangular Stamps (right margin):** Purple rectangular stamps with "SEM" notations and dates (1894-1912 range)

3. **Central Engraving:** Mountain landscape vignette (Alpine scenery) serving as security feature

4. **Handwritten Endorsements:** Multiple cursive annotations recording successive coupon/interest payment collections

5. **Institutional markings:** Amsterdam bank stamp visible

**Document Condition:** Heavily worn, aged paper (cream/beige), extensive use evidenced by overlapping stamps and annotations spanning decades of financial transactions
