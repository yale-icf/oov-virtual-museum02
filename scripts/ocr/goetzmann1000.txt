## Notable markings:

- Soviet state emblem (hammer and sickle within wreath) on upper left
- USSR shield emblem (CCCP) on lower right
- Large "50" denomination on lower left
- Ornate decorative border in green and cream colors
- Fine line work/guilloche background pattern throughout
- Multiple serial numbers in red text
- Small text in various Cyrillic scripts (Russian, Ukrainian, Kazakh, and others) indicating the loan's coverage across Soviet republics
- Page indicator: Page 1 of 2
