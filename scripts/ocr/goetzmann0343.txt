**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **073774**

- Blue circular stamp (right side) with authorization date: "DROIT DE TIMBRE PERÇU ET AUTORISATION INSÉRÉE AU JOURNAL OFFICIEL 3 AVRIL 1928"
- Multiple coupon strips at bottom with sequential numbering (Coupons 31-40 visible)
- Signatures of administrator and board delegation representative (illegible)
- Ornate decorative borders with classical motifs and allegorical figures
- Date notation: 3 AVRIL 1928 (3 April 1928)
