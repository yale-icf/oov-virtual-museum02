# TRANSCRIPTION

## CONDITIONS.

1. This Debenture is one of a series of 5,000 Debentures for securing the aggregate principal sum of £500,000, issued, or about to be issued, by the Company.

2. The Company shall appropriate in each year a sufficient proportion of the net revenue of the Company for that year remaining, after providing for the seven-fold subscription (if necessary) of the First Mortgage Debentures for the time being of the Company, to pay interest at the rate of 5 per cent. for that year upon all the Debentures of this series, and shall apply the same accordingly, or in case the revenue shall not be sufficient for that purpose then shall it split the whole of the revenue equally amongst all the Debentures issued to the holders of the Debentures of this series of interest for that year upon their Debentures.

3. The interest upon the said Debentures shall not be cumulative, and the net revenue of any year shall only be subject to payment of the interest for that year.

4. A register of the Debentures will be kept at the Company's registered office, wherein there will be entered the names, addresses, and descriptions of the holders, and particulars of the Debentures held by them respectively.

5. The registered holder will be regarded as exclusively entitled in benefit of the Debenture, and all persons that act accordingly, and the Company shall not be bound to enter in the register notices of any trust or to recognize any other person as in any other person as in any other person.

6. Every transfer of this Debenture must be in writing under the hand of the registered holder, his executors, or administrators. The transfer must be delivered at the registered office of the Company with a fee of 2s. 6d. and such evidence of identity or title as the Company may reasonably require, and thereupon the transfer will be registered.

7. In the case of joint registered holders the principal moneys and interest payable hereunder shall be deemed to be owing to them upon a joint account.

8. The principal moneys and interest payable hereunder will be paid without regard to any equities existing between the Company and any intermediate holder hereof, and the receipt of the registered holder for such principal moneys and interest shall be a good discharge to the Company.

9. The principal moneys hereby secured shall immediately become payable if an order is made or an effective resolution is passed for the winding up of the Company.

10. The registered holder of this Debenture will be entitled to receive notices of and to vote at Meetings of the Company as provided by its Articles of Association.

11. In the event of its becoming necessary either by reason of any requirement of the Government of Costa Rica in that behalf, or otherwise, to establish a sinking fund for the purpose of redeeming the Debentures of this series, the same shall be established and managed in accordance with, or otherwise, arising in relation to the establishment of such sinking fund and which may require, the direction of a meeting of the Debenture holders, of which notice shall be duly given as aforesaid, and the decision of a majority of Debenture holders present in person or by proxy at such meeting shall be deemed to be the decision and shall be binding upon all the Debenture holders of this series.

12. A notice may be served by the Company upon the holder of this Debenture either personally or by sending it through the post in an envelope addressed to such holder at his registered address.

13. Any notice served by post shall be deemed to have been served at the time when it was posted, and in proving such service it shall be sufficient to prove the address on the envelope enclosing the notice, and the posting of it.
