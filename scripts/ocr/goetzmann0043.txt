# Transcription

**Title:** Cleaning the Streets

**Main Text:**
A Cleanly project, well approv'd, no doubt,
By Stroling Dames, and all that walk on Foot:
This Bubble well deserves the Name of best,
Because the Cleanest Bite of all the rest.

**Notable markings:**
- Three spade symbols in a vertical column on the left margin (indicating this is the King of Diamonds from a South Sea Bubble playing card set)
- Decorative flourishes at the end of lines
- Period engraving/woodcut style illustration showing a street cleaning scene with a cart, workers, and buildings
