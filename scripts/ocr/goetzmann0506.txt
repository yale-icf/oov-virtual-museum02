# Transcription of Potosian Land Grant Certificate

## LEFT PAGE (English Version)

**Class C. No. 193 / Class C. No. 193**

**Grant of 400 Acres.**

[Header with coat of arms: "POTOSIAN LAND GRANT" / "STOCK UNIT" / "400 Acres"]

**Copy of the Original Grant from the King of the Mosquito Shore and Nation, to GEORGE FREDERICK II, D.G., King of the Mosquito Shore and Nation.**

By virtue of these Presents, We hereby grant and devise unto His Excellency Colonel the Right Honorable Lord Cochrane, and to his Heirs, Executors, Administrators and Assigns, all that certain parcel or tract of Land, situate, lying and being in that part of the Potosian Republic called Popayan Province, containing Four Hundred Acres of Land in the County of Popayan in a weight line, down the South, until it touches the 1st parallel of South Latitude.

To have and to hold the said parcel of Land, with all and singular the Houses, Buildings, Improvements, Hereditaments and Appurtenances thereunto belonging or in any wise appertaining, to him the said Lord Cochrane, and to his Heirs and Assigns, to the only proper use, benefit and behoof of him the said Lord Cochrane, and his Heirs and Assigns forever.

In Witness Whereof, We have caused our Great Seal to be hereunto affixed. Given under Our Hand, Seal and Signature, in the City of the Mosquito Shore, this [date] Day of [month], in the year of our Reign.

**By His Majesty's Command:—WILLIAM BOWES.**

[Additional official documentation regarding transfer and validation]

**Certified by:**
- WILLIAM SMITH & Co., Chancing Street, Whitechapel
- Deputy Clerk of Lieutenancy of the High Court of Chancery

[Extended legal text regarding the grant conditions and authority]

**Grant of 400 Acres.**

*Viz.* George Mac Gregor, Esquire, do hereby grant unto the Bearer hereof is entitled to Four Hundred Acres of Land in that part of the Popayan Republic called Popayan Province, in consideration of his having received the sum of Four Hundred Pounds Sterling as a proportioned part of the original subscription for the Copy of the Original Grants as in any other original Grants to the Potosian Republic, in manner as will not aid relief of the Potosian Republic, now bearing date, but to immemorial and exclusive possession of the said land in virtue hereof. And we hereby promise that the Bearer shall and will be entitled to the said Lands and Tenements, and deliver to the Bearer the Grant sealed with the seal aforesaid.

*Edinburgh, April 3rd., 1824.*

[Signatures]

## Notable Markings:

- **Coat of Arms:** Heraldic seal at top center of document
- **Serial Numbers:** No. 193 (appears on both sides)
- **Signatures:** Multiple handwritten signatures on both pages
- **Date:** April 3rd/3e Avril, 1824 (Edinburgh/Edimbourg)
- **Official Stamps/Seals:** Various certification marks
- **Languages:** Bilingual (English/French with Spanish title) presentation
- **Notation:** "Entered Vol. / Pd. [?]" at bottom of left page
