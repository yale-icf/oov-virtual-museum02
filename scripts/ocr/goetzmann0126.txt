# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Bombario gy zyteenGidt, dat is gewis,
't Heb dorst naar Actidrank, maar
giet myn mond juist mis.

**English translation:**

Bombario pours eagerly, that is certain,
He has thirst for Actidrank, but
it just misses my mouth.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Card designation: "Six of Clubs" (indicated by the club symbol with "6" in the upper right corner)
- Page number visible: appears to be from a playing card series called "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Cards)
- Small circular seal/stamp in lower left corner (partially legible, text unclear)
- The engraving is rendered in typical 17th-century Dutch woodcut/engraving style with fine line hatching
