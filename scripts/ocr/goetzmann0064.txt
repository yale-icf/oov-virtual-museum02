# Transcription

**Title (top of card):**
Insurance on Ships &c

**Text at bottom of card:**
In vain are all Infurances, for Stall
the Raging Wind, must answer heavens Will;
To what Wife purpose do we then Infure,
Since Some must Loose what e'er the Sea Devour

**Card suit indicator (top left):**
Six of Diamonds (shown as six red diamond symbols arranged in two columns)
