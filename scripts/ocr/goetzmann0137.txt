# Transcription

**QUEEN.**
**TITANIA.—A Midsummer Night's Dream.**

On the moon's silver beams, the fairy Titania,
Is yet, the gay Queen of delight,
To visit you, daughters and sons of Britannia,
An usher to preside for the night!
Now obey my commands, let the hours glide sweetly,
And each take their cake and their wine,
Let song, jest, and dance, go on jocund and featly,
And each toast his charmer divine;
And as your time flies, while fondly conversing,
Let the Lottery come in for its share,
Remember the Tickets are Fortune's dispersing,
Then haste and buy one for your fair.
