## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 06181
- **Imperial Seal:** Red circular seal of Imperial Russian Government (top right)
- **Company Seal:** Circular seal with Imperial Russian Eagle (top left)
- **Date:** Moscow, 1901
- **Signatures:** Multiple official signatures in Russian, German, and Dutch sections
- **Bond Value:** 2,000 German Marks (926 Rubles 80 kopeks; 1,180 Dutch Guilders; 97 Pounds Sterling 18 Shillings)
- **Interest Rate:** 4% per annum
- **Decorative Border:** Ornate Art Nouveau border with corner designs marked "4000" and "2000"
- **Denomination:** Visible in corners (4000 marks)
