# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Kromme Cinquen

De kromme Cinquen, daar ik mij op durf vertrouwen,
Zullen, vrees ik, mij in't eind nog eens berouwen.

**English translation:**

The Crooked Five

The crooked five, on which I dare to rely,
Will, I fear, in the end still cause me to rue.

**Notable markings:**

- Number "5" in top right corner
- Diamond symbol (suit marker) in top right
- Engraved illustration in period style showing a figure in 17th-century dress with a smaller figure in background
- Text in calligraphic script at top left reading "Kromme Cinquen"
- Caption text in Dutch at bottom of engraving
