## Notable Markings:

- Serial number range: 291101–291150
- Total value denomination: 50,000 (Polish marks)
- Date of approval: February 6, 1922
- Photographic vignette at top center: Oil derricks labeled "COLUMBIA" at an oil field in Galicia
- Decorative Art Deco border with ornamental motifs
- Water tower illustrations on left and right sides
- Printer attribution (bottom): "ZAKLADY GRAFICZNE E.D-S K. KOZIAŃSKIM W WARSZAWIE"
- Red stamp/seal visible in upper left corner (partially illegible)
