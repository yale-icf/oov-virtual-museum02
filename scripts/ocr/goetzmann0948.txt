## Right Page (Railway Agreement)

**For the information of Bondholders, some extracts from the Railway Agreement mentioned in the Bonds are here set out. The Agreement is dated the 25th July, 1908, and will be deemed to be the only material portion relating to the Bonds or the security for the same.**

**I.—RAILWAY AGREEMENT**

**Article 2.** The capital so provided shall be wholly devoted to the construction of the railway, including the purchase of land required for it, the construction of stations and the working of the line and to the payment of interest on the Loan during the period of construction.

**Article 4.** The Imperial Government shall guarantee the interest and principal of this Loan, and the said sum shall be paid in gold by the Imperial Chinese Government and the proceeds of the Railway and all other property, rights and privileges...
