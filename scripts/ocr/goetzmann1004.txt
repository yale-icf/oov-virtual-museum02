## Notable markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 236902
- **Imperial Double-Headed Eagle:** Displayed prominently in the center and in decorative corner medallions
- **Red Stamp:** Visible in upper right corner with text "50% COMPLEMENT DE DROIT DE TIMBRE" (50% Stamp Duty Supplement)
- **Ornamental Border:** Elaborate decorative frame in traditional Imperial Russian style
- **Series Designation:** 1st Series A, denomination 125 Gold Rubles
- **Signature Line:** [illegible signature] at bottom of terms section
- **Multiple Administrative Seals:** Visible throughout the document
