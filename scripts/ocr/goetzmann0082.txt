# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Heer

Laurier Konning

't Heb de Achtes ceirst bedagt, maar een krâde Artiekans, Smit al mijn lof omwer, en schent mijn lauswer krans.

**English translation:**

Lord

Laurier King

I have the Eights first considered, but a crude Articans [or similar], Smite all my praise around, and shame my Laurel wreath.

---

- Border frame around the entire composition
- Historical engraving/etching style typical of 17th-century Dutch playing cards

- Small cherub or putti figures visible in the background scene
