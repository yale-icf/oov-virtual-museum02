**РУССКИЙ ТЕКСТ:**

4½% ОБЛИГАЦИОННЫЙ ЗАЁМ 1911 г.
ОБЩЕСТВА ЧЕРНОМОРСКО-КУБАНСКОЙ

со безусловною гарантией ИМПЕРАТОРСКОГО Российского Правительства в уплате процентов и обслуживания.

№ А13852                    ОБЛИГАЦИЯ                    № А13852

ВЪ СТО ВОСЬМИДЕСЯТИ ДЕВЯТЬ РУБЛЕЙ=20 ФУНТАМЪ СТЕРЛИНГОВ

## English Translation:

**4½% BOND LOAN OF 1911**
**OF THE BLACK-SEA-KUBAN RAILWAY COMPANY**

with the nominal capital of 18,085.032 rubles = 1,913.760 pounds sterling,
with the absolute guarantee of the IMPERIAL Russian Government for the payment of interest and principal.

No. A13852                    BOND                    No. A13852

OF ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY-NINE RUBLES = 20 POUNDS STERLING
TO THE BEARER.

[Bond terms detailing that the bearer of this bond is entitled to a share of £20 sterling or 189 rubles (1 ruble = 1/3 imperial) in the loan which has been issued for the general needs of the Black-Sea-Kuban Railway Company; payment of principal and interest is guaranteed by the Imperial Russian Government; detailed payment schedule and conditions...]

Interest payments are made on the 1st of November each year.
Dated, 1911 — Yekaterinodary.

Board of Directors of the Black-Sea-Kuban Railway Company:

President of the Board: [signature]
Directors: [signatures]
Chief Accountant: [signature]
Delegate of the IMPERIAL Russian Government: [signature]

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** A13852 (appears twice on document)
- **Imperial Russian Double-Headed Eagle Seal** (left side)
- **Ornamental Decorative Seals** (four corner medallions with geometric patterns)
- **Red Revenue Stamp** (upper right, Russian Imperial markings)
- **Multiple Signatures** in cursive script from company officers and government delegate
- **Decorative Border** with intricate geometric patterns throughout
- **Dual Language Text** (Russian and English)
- **Date and Place:** Yekaterinodary, 1911
- **Bond Denomination:** £20 Sterling = 189 Rubles
