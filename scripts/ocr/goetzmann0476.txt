## English Translation:

We, the undersigned, as Lords of ZAAMSLAG, and interested parties in the completed new Dyke work called *the Eendracht Polder*, in the Island of *Axel*, acknowledge and declare that we are justly and truly indebted to [handwritten] *Mr. Pieter Paul van Gelce* — or the lawful Bearer hereof, in a Capital Sum of *One Hundred Pounds Flemish*, arising from monies justly loaned and furnished on Interest, received and accepted by Us in the aforementioned capacity; renouncing thereby the exception of uncounted money; and so that those Monies together with further Capital may be applied to the completion of the aforementioned Dike works with their dependencies to be employed: Promising thereby that the aforementioned Capital of £100:--:-- together with the Interest thereof at 3 per Cent in proper Currency annually and for a longer term in proportion to the time, shall be paid and redeemed to the aforementioned

*Mr. Pieter Paul van Gelce* — or his assignee, by Lottery against further Capitals from the Products which from the aforementioned Dike work shall be able to profit, or by assessment on the Lands thereof, which shall be done specially from time to time. Binding ourselves, the undersigned, in the aforementioned capacity to the security of the payment and restitution of the aforementioned Capital and

## Notable Markings:

- Document number: **No. 153** (printed at top center)
- Handwritten inscription at top (partially illegible, dated July 1779)
- Handwritten name insertion: *Dhr. Pieter Paul van Gelce* (appears twice, indicating the bond holder's name)
- Capital amount in italics: *£ 100*
- Polder name in italics: *den Eendracht Polder*
- Location in italics: *Axel*
- Interest rate clearly stated: **à 3 per Cento vry Geld jaarlyks** (3% per annum in proper Currency annually)
