# Transcription of MZA Stock Certificate (1859)

## Original Text:

**Header (top, partially illegible):**
[Ornamental text - illegible]

**Main Body:**

COMPAÑIA DE LOS FERRO-CARRILES | COMPAGNIE DES CHEMINS DE FER
DE | DE

MADRID A ZARAGOZA Y A ALICANTE | MADRID A SARAGOSSE ET A ALICANTE

CAPITAL SOCIAL: 456,000,000 DE REALES V°ⁿ | CAPITAL SOCIAL: 120,000,000 DE FRANCS
DIVIDIDOS EN 240,000 ACCIONES DE 1,900 REALES V°ⁿ | DIVISÉS EN 240,000 ACTIONS DE 500 FRANCS

accion de mil novecientos reales v°ⁿ | action de cinq cents francs
AL PORTADOR | AU PORTEUR

DANDO DERECHO A 1/240,000° DEL ACTIVO SOCIAL | DONNANT DROIT A 1/240,000° DE L'ACTIF SOCIAL

N° **233055**

POR DELEGACION DEL CONSEJO, | UM ADMINISTRADOR,

[Signature - appears to read: Eugenia Aguirre del Hoyo] | [Signature - appears to read: Areole]

Madrid, el 1° de Julio de 1859

ESTE TITULO SUBSTITUYE AL PRIMITIVO DE IGUAL NUMERO Y FECHA

**Bottom text:**
AYMUNDETRIA S.A. MADRID

---

## English Translation:

RAILROAD COMPANY | RAILWAY COMPANY
OF | OF

MADRID TO ZARAGOZA AND TO ALICANTE | MADRID TO SARAGOSSA AND TO ALICANTE

SHARE CAPITAL: 456,000,000 REALES VELLÓN | SHARE CAPITAL: 120,000,000 FRANCS
DIVIDED INTO 240,000 SHARES OF 1,900 REALES VELLÓN | DIVIDED INTO 240,000 SHARES OF 500 FRANCS

share of one thousand nine hundred reales vellón | share of five hundred francs
TO BEARER | TO BEARER

GIVING RIGHT TO 1/240,000 OF THE COMPANY'S ASSETS | GIVING RIGHT TO 1/240,000 OF THE COMPANY'S ASSETS

By delegation of the council, | One administrator,

Madrid, July 1st, 1859

THIS TITLE REPLACES THE ORIGINAL OF THE SAME NUMBER AND DATE

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 233055
- **Date:** July 1, 1859 (1° de Julio de 1859)
- **Signatures:** Two handwritten signatures (delegation from council on left; administrator signature on right)
- **Printer:** AYMUNDETRIA S.A., MADRID
- **Stamp/Notation:** Purple stamp or library marking in upper right corner (partially visible)
- **Design:** Elaborate decorative border with heraldic shields, city skylines, and ornamental engravings throughout
- **Bilingual:** Document presented in both Spanish and French
