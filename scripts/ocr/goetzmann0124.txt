# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Door't maken
Moet ik't halen.

't Waaid sterk daar ieder een nu leeft bij
alle winden
Die menig 't aapen land, of zijne dood
doen vinden

**English translation:**

By making [it]
I must obtain it.

It blew strongly there, each one now lives by
all winds
That many [go to] the ape land, or find their death

---

**Notable markings:**

- Playing card suit symbol: **Four of Clubs** (visible in top right corner)
- This is page 46 of 55 from "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Chart), a historical Dutch satirical playing card series from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Theater)
- Engraved illustration in black ink showing a figure in period dress with cherub heads above and a windmill in the background
- Horizontal line engraving technique throughout
