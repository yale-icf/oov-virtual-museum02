## English Translation:

**Title:**
PRINCIPALITY OF BULGARIA / 6% State Hypothecary Loan of 1892 / Bearer Bond (Obligation)

**Denomination:** Five Hundred Leva / Five Hundred Francs

**Loan Description:**
This is a bearer bond for the 6% State Hypothecary Loan of the Principality of Bulgaria from 1892. The total loan amount is 142,780,000 leva (equivalent to 5,711,000 sterling or 115,651,000 marks), represented by 255,500 obligations.

[The document contains extensive terms and conditions regarding]:
- Payment terms and methods
- Interest payment schedules
- Security and guarantees
- Redemption procedures
- Rights and obligations of bondholders
- Administrative provisions
- References to financial backing through state property mortgages

**Interest Coupons:**
Sterling 20 shillings or Austrian florins of 200 (equivalent value)
