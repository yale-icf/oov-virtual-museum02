# Stock Certificate Transcription

## Original (Chinese):

**Header:**
上海浦东强生出租汽车股份有限公司
SHANGHAI PU DONG QIANG SHENG TAXI CO., LTD.

**Main Text:**
股票

中国人民银行上海市分行（92）人金股字第（1）号文批準發行

股份總額：壹佰拾陸股
每股金額：計人民幣壹佰元整
總計金額：壹仟拾佰万元整

**Central text (on certificate):**
股東

本股票拾股计人民市壹佰元整

**Signatures and markings:**
法人代表：[signature]
[Red official seal/chop]

發行日期：一九九二年二月十二日

證號：XII 0068056

---

## English Translation:

**Header:**
Shanghai Pu Dong Qiang Sheng Taxi Co., Ltd.

**Main Text:**
Stock Certificate

Authorized and issued by the People's Bank of China Shanghai Branch (Document No. 92, Personnel Fund Stocks Character No. 1)

Total Share Capital: 176 shares
Par Value per Share: 100 RMB (Chinese Yuan)
Total Capitalization: 17,600 RMB

**Central text:**
Shareholder

This stock certificate represents 10 shares valued at 100 RMB

**Issue date:** February 12, 1992

Certificate Number: XII 0068056

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Red official seal/chop** (印章) in center-left area
- **Signature** of legal representative (法人代表) in lower left
- **Serial number:** XII 0068056 (in red, lower right)
- **Decorative border** in green with traditional Chinese patterns
- **Vignette illustration** of buses/taxis in the center
- **Denomination:** "100" (壹佰) displayed in green corners (top-left and bottom-right)
