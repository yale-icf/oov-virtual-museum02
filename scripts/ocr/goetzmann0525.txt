# Transcription

## Original (Dutch/French):

**LEFT SIDE (Dutch):**

La A.                                    N° 5320.

**Certificaat.**

Houder dezes is geregtigd tot een **Kapitaal** van
**DUIZEND ROEBELS,
IN ASSIGNATIËN,**

Inschrijving, rentende 6 p℃., in het **Grootboek der Keizerlijke Schuld** van de **Keizerlijke Commissie van Amortisatie te St. Petersburgh,** welk fonds, door dit Certificaat gerepresenteerd, in bewaring is bij het

**Algemeen Kantoor van Administratie te Amsterdam,**

onder directie van:

STADNITSKI & VAN HEUKELOM,
JACOB VAN BEECK VOLLENHOEN,
SAMUEL & DAVID SAPORTAS,
LAMAISON & BOUWER,
JOHANNES SAMUEL WURFBAIN,
HENDRIK OYENS

en

DE LANOY & BURLAGE.

En heeft houder dezes, ingevolge bericht den 30 Junij 1824 uitgegeven, het regt om het bovengenoemde fonds ten allen tijde opte vragen, tegen terugave van dit **Certificaat** met bijvoeging der nog niet vervallene **Coupons.**

## English Translation:

**LEFT SIDE (English):**

The A.                                    No. 5320.

**Certificate.**

The holder of this is entitled to a **Capital** of
**ONE THOUSAND ROUBLES,
IN ASSIGNATIONS,**

Registration bearing 6%, in the **General Ledger of the Imperial State Debt** of the **Imperial Amortization Commission in St. Petersburg**, which fund, represented by this Certificate, is held in deposit at the

**General Administration Office in Amsterdam,**

under the direction of:

STADNITSKI & VAN HEUKELOM,
JACOB VAN BEECK VOLLENHOEN,
SAMUEL & DAVID SAPORTAS,
LAMAISON & BOUWER,
JOHANNES SAMUEL WURFBAIN,
HENDRIK OYENS

and

DE LANOY & BURLAGE.

And the holder of this, in accordance with notice issued on June 30, 1824, has the right to claim the aforementioned fund at any time, against the return of this **Certificate** together with any coupons not yet matured.

---

**RIGHT SIDE (English):**

**Certificate.**

The **Bearer** is entitled to a **Sum of capital of ONE THOUSAND ROUBLES, IN ASSIGNATS,**

Registrations bearing 6% Interest, in the **General Ledger of the Public Debt of the Imperial Amortization Commission in St. Petersburg**, of which the title is deposited at the

**General Administration Bureau, established in Amsterdam,**

under the direction of:

STADNITSKI & VAN HEUKELOM,
JACOB VAN BEECK VOLLENHOEN,
SAMUEL & DAVID SAPORTAS,
LAMAISON & BOUWER,
JOHANNES SAMUEL WURFBAIN,
HENDRIK OYENS

and

DE LANOY & BURLAGE.

According to the prospectus published on June 30, 1824, the Bearer may claim at any time the original fund mentioned above, against the return of the present **Certificate** accompanied by its unmatured **Coupons**.

---

**SIGNATURE SECTION:**

Amsterdam, the 30th of June, 1825

[Three signatures with title] **Directors.**

---

**FOOTER:**

LEFT: CAPITAL R° 1000 in Assignation.
Issued twenty half-yearly Coupon certificates, signed by [signature]

RIGHT: CAPITAL R° 1000 in Assignats.
Delivered twelve Interest Coupons of six months, signed by [signature]

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** L.A. No. 5320 (top right)
- **Three handwritten signatures** in the middle section, with "Directeuren" (Directors) notation
- **Ornamental dotted border** separating Dutch and French text
- **Capital amounts:** R° 1000 in both assignations and assignats
- **Date:** Amsterdam, June 30, 1825 (handwritten)
- **Interest rate:** 6% p℃ (per cent)
- **Additional signatures** at bottom of each column
