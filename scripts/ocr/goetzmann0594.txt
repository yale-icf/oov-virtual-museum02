Such illustration will be effected by means of the certificates issued in the form hereof as required.

2. The Company will furnish to the holder of the Company distributable as aforesaid amount of not less to the sum of £1,000, three times in succession with the approval of the Meetings: the first advertisement in the London and one Paris daily paper, to be repeated in each paper specifying the title and object of the Company, and the three price, the sharecopies payment will be made on presentation and delivery of the relevant coupon in the Bank in hand, and according to the terms and conditions provided by such order.

3. All Certificates will be fully paid without any preference of priority the one over the other. All Certificates and Coupons are transferable by delivery or endorsement. The Company will pay all sums due on the Certificates, issued to the bearer of this Certificate on or when it may become due.

4. Meetings of the Company will be held in London or Paris as determined upon by the Board of Certificates with all unpaid Coupons attached will be entitled to be present and vote at all such meetings, and will have equal voting powers as other holders of the Certificates produced by them respectively.

5. The seal of the Company will be kept at the registered office of the Company.
