and be it further enacted by the Authority aforesaid, That all and every such Person or Persons, Bodies Politick or Corporate, that have any Annuities, and shall be intitled, by virtue of this Act, to have any receipt, an Interest of Twopence, after the Rate of Eight Pounds per Centum per Annum, to the said principal Sum not exceeding One hundred seventy six thousand eight hundred ninety three Pounds, Eleven Shillings, and Seven Pence, to to be advanced into him for such Annuities of Exchequer, to commence from the respective Time or Times of advancing the same as aforesaid; which said payable Quar-terly, Interceir of Twopencehall, from time to time, by paid Payments, out of the monies arising at the said Receipt, or to the Supremacy thereof, of Duerplus Spencies of the said Sinking Fund as aforesaid, until such Times as the said Exchequer Bills to to be issued, or any Part thereof, shall be redeemed and cancelled in the Manner and Form by this Act hereafter provided.

And be it further enacted by the Authority aforesaid, That it shall and may be lawful to and for the said Commissioners of the Exchequer, or any of the more now being, or the said High Creature, or any Three or more of the Commissioners of the Creatury for the time being, to cause Exchequer Bills to be made forth at the said Receipt, for any Sum of Sums of Money, not exceeding the said Sum of One hundred ninety three Pounds, Eleven Shillings, and Seven Pence, in the same of the Manner, Form, and Dyder, and agreeing to the Time of the Rules and Directions, as are herein before enacted and prescribed concerning the Exchequer Bills herein before directed to be made forth; and that all and every the Clauses, Powders, Powders, Privileges, Advantages, Penalties, Forfeitures, and Disabilities, herein before contained or referred unto, relating to the said Exchequer Bills (except as before excepted), shall be applied and extended to the Exchequer Bills to be made forth by the said Sum, not exceeding One hundred seventy six thousand eight hundred ninety three Pounds, Eleven Shillings, and Seven Pence, as fully and effectually, to all Intents and Purposes, as if the said several Clauses or Provisions had been herein again particularly repeated and renewed.
