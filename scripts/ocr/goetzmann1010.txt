COUPON

BETAALD.

Deze kwitantie dient bewaard te worden voor eene eventuele afstempeling.

OVERGENOMEN van de Heer [handwritten: L.C. Burdet, Druyvestejne]

— Een Talons van 3 pCt. Obligatien Groote Russische Spoorweg Maatschappij, 3de uitgitte 1881. [handwritten: m 213779]

**English Translation:**

COUPON
1/14 JUNE 1917
PAID.
8 cps.
L.R & CO.

This receipt should be preserved for any eventual stamping/cancellation.

RECEIVED from the Gentleman [handwritten: L.C. Burdet, Druyvestejne]

— One Talon of 3% Bonds Grand Russian Railway Company, 3rd emission 1881. [handwritten: No. 213779]

AMSTERDAM, [handwritten: 12 June 1924]
ORIGINALLY DATED
LIPPMANN, ROSENTHAL & CO.

[Handwritten notations for amounts:]
Talon at Rb. 125.—
" " 625.—

[Handwritten signature]

---

**Notable markings:**

- Rectangular stamp (top left): "COUPON 1/14 JUNI 1917 BETAALD. 8 cps. L.R & CO."
- Green rectangular box (top right): Instruction text in Dutch
- Handwritten annotations in red and black ink throughout
- Handwritten signature (cursive, illegible) at bottom right
- Serial number visible: 213779
- Reference number: 7629 - 33/2
