# Transcription: Province of Nova Scotia Government Redeemable Stock

## Main Text (Repeated across coupon sections):

THE BEARER OF THE PROVINCE OF NOVA SCOTIA ALONG AND CABLE IS HEREBY AUTHORIZED TO RECEIVE FROM THE RECEIVER GENERAL OF THE DOMINION OF CANADA OR FROM THE UNITED KINGDOM AND THE COMMISSIONERS OF HIS MAJESTY'S TREASURY THE INTEREST DUE UPON THIS STOCK AT THE RATE OF THREE AND ONE HALF PER CENTUM PER ANNUM PAYABLE HALF-YEARLY ON THE FIRST DAY OF MAY AND THE FIRST DAY OF NOVEMBER IN EACH YEAR UPON PRESENTATION OF THIS COUPON AND THE PRODUCTION OF THE STOCK OF WHICH THIS IS A COUPON AND DULY ENDORSED FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE STOCK OF ONE OF THE FOLLOWING TREASURIES—40 & 41 VICT.
