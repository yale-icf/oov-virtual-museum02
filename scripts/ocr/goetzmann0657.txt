## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** IV SEERIA № 013136
- **Date:** 1927
- **Signatures:** Chairman Karl Heint and two board members (signatures present but names partially illegible)
- **Decorative Elements:** Brown ornamental border with Estonian national emblem (coat of arms) at top center and corner medallions marked "250"
- **Watermark:** "Eesti Maapank" visible in background
- **Languages:** Estonian (primary), French (left column), English (right column)
- **Handwritten notations:** Various administrative annotations and signatures on form fields
