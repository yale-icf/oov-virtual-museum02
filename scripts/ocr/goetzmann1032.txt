# Transcription

No. 15 representing Witnesses
from 1st to 130.

This is to Certify, that James Greenleaf
is entitled to ten __________ Shares in the entire Property
of the NORTH AMERICAN LAND COMPANY, the Dividend whereof shall not
be less than Six Dollars, on each Share Annually, conformable to
Articles of Agreement Duly Executed, dated at PHILADELPHIA
the twentieth __ day of February 1795, Transferable only at the
Company's Office in that CITY, by the Owner in Person, or by his Executor,
Administrator, Attorney, or legal Representative, Signed in the presence,
and by Order of the Board of Managers, at Philadelphia, this tenth
day of March— One thousand seven hundred and ninety five

James Marshall Secretary B. Morris President—
