# Transcription of Mississippi Union Bank Bond

## Main Bond Text (Top):

**2000** UNITED STATES OF AMERICA **2000**

STATE OF MISSISSIPPI

FIVE PER CENT LOAN

Know all Men by these Presents that the State of Mississippi undertakes to be indebted to the **MISSISSIPPI UNION BANK**, in the Sum of

**TWO THOUSAND DOLLARS**

Which sum the People of Mississippi promise to pay, without money of the United States, to the order of the President of the Company on the 1st day of [date], and in the rate of five per cent per annum, payable half yearly at the place named in the Bond until the final payment of said principal sum.

**In Testimony** [whereof] the Governor of the State of Mississippi has signed... [remainder illegible/faded]

[Signatures present but largely illegible]

**Dated, [date unclear]**
