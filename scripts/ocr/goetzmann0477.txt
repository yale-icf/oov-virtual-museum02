# Russian 4% Gold Loan Bond - Coupon Sheet Transcription

## Original (Russian & French):

**Header Section:**

РОССIЙСКIЙ 2% ЗОЛОТОЙ ЗАЕМЪ, 6' ВЫП. 1894 Г.
187-й выпускъ въ облигаціи №059944

[Russian Government 2% Gold Loan, 6th Issue 1894
187th Coupon in Obligation No. 059944]

Emprunt Russe 4% or, sixième émission, 1894. TALON de l'obligation de 187 roubles 50. (1 rouble = 1/15 impériale)
= 300 fr. = 401 reichsmark = 19 £ 15 shill. 6 pence = 229 f. des Pays-Bas = 96.25 dollars or des États-Unis.

**Coupon Blocks (16 sections numbered 101-120):**

Each block contains:
- РОССIЙСКIЙ 2% ЗОЛОТОЙ ЗАЕМЪ, 6' ВЫП. 1894 Г. [Russian 2% Gold Loan, 6th Issue 1894]
- Coupon number and obligation number (№059944)
- Dates (19 Марта / Августа [March/August] with years 1922-1923)
- Emprunt Russe 4% Or, 6e Émission, 1894
- Value statement: "300 coupon de l'obligation, payable le [date] Mars / Août 1922-[1923]"
- Distribution locations: "Paris S.O. - Berlin a.d.S. - Londres 3 ch. - Amsterdam 2 & 39"
- "Valable pendant 10 ans" (Valid for 10 years)

**Notable Markings:**

- Serial number: №059944 (repeated throughout)
- Stamps/seals in corners of each coupon block
- Bilingual text (Russian/French)
- Multiple currency equivalents listed
- Validation period: 10 years from issue
- Multiple distribution centers for redemption
