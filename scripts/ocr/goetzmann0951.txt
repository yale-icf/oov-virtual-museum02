D Matthew Lawler

Do hereby assign and transfer to Peter Stephen du Ponceau D M Shares in the Capital or Joint Stock of the Insurance Company of the State of Pennsylvania.

In witness whereof I have hereunto set my Hand the twenty ninth day of August 1795

witness,                                Mat Lawler
