# Sherman & Barnsdall Oil Company Stock Certificate Transcription

## Main Text:

**SHERMAN & BARNSDALL OIL COMPANY**

THIS IS TO CERTIFY that [handwritten: James H. Arbona] is entitled to One Hundred Shares of

Two and Dollars cash, in the Capital Stock of SHERMAN & BARNSDALL OIL COMPANY, transferable personally or by Attorney on the Books of the Company upon the surrender of this Certificate.

**In testimony Whereof,** the said Company have caused this Certificate to be signed by the President and Countersigned by the Secretary, and affixed the Seal of said Corporation at

**NEW YORK, [handwritten: May] 1865.**

[Signatures for Secretary and President with decorative marks]

## Transfer Information (Handwritten):

For value received 9 forty [?] Ranges and Assign to **James H. Arbona**
the shares of Stock above mentioned, and authorize **James Irving** to make
the necessary transfer on the Books of the Company New York 27th June
**1868**
In Presume of **H.C. Brolasky** [signature]

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Red wax seal** with company seal impression (center left)
- **U.S. Internal Revenue Civil War revenue stamp** (25 cents denomination, red, upper right)
- Ornate decorative border with corner medallions
- Engraved vignette depicting an oil derrick/drilling operation in center
- Multiple handwritten signatures and annotations
- Decorative cross-hatch pattern signatures at bottom
