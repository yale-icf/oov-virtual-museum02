## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 0324809 (consistent across all coupons and main bond)
- **Series Designation:** Φ (Phi)
- **Denomination:** 100 Dinara
- **Date of Issue:** February 1990, Belgrade
- **Signature:** Jovan Jebić, Republican Secretariat for Finance
- **National Emblem:** Socialist coat of arms (visible on bond certificate)
- **Coupon Dates:** Semi-annual payments from June 30, 1995 through June 30, 1999
- **Document Type:** Bearer Bond (Obveznica) - payable to whoever holds it
- **Ornamental Borders:** Intricate decorative frames in mauve/purple throughout
