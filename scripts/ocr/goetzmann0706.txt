# Transcription

**Liberian Government 7% External Loan of 1871.**

---

This Certificate represents the interest in arrear (amounting to £176 : 3 : 4) on Bond No. 0865 for £100 of the above Loan, from 1st February, 1874, to 31st March, 1899, and is redeemable in accordance with the conditions of the Arrangement approved by the Law of the Liberian Congress of 23rd January, 1899, an extract from which is printed on the back hereof.

FOR THE COUNCIL OF FOREIGN BONDHOLDERS,

[Signature]
Secretary.

COUNCIL HOUSE,
17, MOORGATE STREET, LONDON, E.C.,
1st October, 1899.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Ornate decorative green border on left margin with text "COUNCIL OF FOREIGN BONDHOLDERS" running vertically
- Handwritten signature of Secretary
- Printed by Bradbury, Wilkinson & Co., Limited, London (noted in margins)
- Bond No. 0865
- Serial/reference number visible in decorative border
