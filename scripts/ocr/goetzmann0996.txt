## English Translation:

**[Top of page - Repayment table header]**

Recapitulation of Repayments

**[Main document text]**

№14725

**Partial Bond Certificate**

for

*Twenty Guilders Conventional Coinage at 20 fl. Standard,*

which, as a share of the loan of **One Million Guilders Conventional Coinage at 20 fl. Standard** contracted by me, **Count Casimir Esterházy of Galantha**, Imperial Royal Chamberlain, Lord and Captain-Proprietor of Frágnó and Semptén, with the established Imperial Royal privileged wholesale house **Hammer & Karoly**, is hereby acknowledged to be issued as one of 50,000 equal partial bond certificates. I acknowledge that I shall repay the sum stated herein, in accordance with the statutory repayment plan, within the period specified, with five percent annual compound interest throughout the entire term of this partial bond certificate, and that upon the drawing of lots of this partial bond certificate, at the time also specified in the prospectus, together with the amount of interest accrued, it shall be paid without objection to every presenter by the wholesale banking house **Hammer & Karoly** in Vienna. This partial bond certificate carries neither mortgage nor other security on the wholesale banking house **Hammer & Karoly** or its legal successors, yet it may be pledged by every holder of this partial bond certificate at the rate specified in this drawing of lots, should he wish to do so prior to the maturity date.

In witness whereof I sign this document, which serves as the basis for this loan, in Vienna on November 6, 1847, **One Million Guilders Conv.-Coinage.** [Imperial Royal] Duke Esterházy Esterházy [unclear], mathematical and book-keeping statements including: [unclear] in the Duchy of Carinthia, Villacher documentation, Pedersen, and [unclear] Tarig, all: of the Portfolio holder Goggan, Count.
