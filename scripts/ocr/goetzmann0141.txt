# Transcription

**CELESTINA QUAVERSIDES.**

This damsel, whose strains are me'odious and clear,
The Syrens' true notes may repeat thro' the year,
May please like sweet Philomel, tuned to delight,
And add a fresh charm to the joys of Twelfth-Night.
But Music's vibrations are sounds that expire,—
More permanent blessings are those I admire;
While from the State Lottery bright gold we can have,
And Land from the City,—'tis those that I crave.
