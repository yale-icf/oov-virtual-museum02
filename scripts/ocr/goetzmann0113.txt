# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

*Top text:*
Hier kost de goud ook gilden
By shift op deze schilden

*Bottom text:*
Dit brits der steden houd de Koopgod
in zijn stand,
Omhelsd Neptuijn, en sluit het sal-
lend Vaderland.

**English translation:**

*Top text:*
Here the gold also gilds
By shift on these shields

*Bottom text:*
This brittle [thing] of the cities holds the God of Commerce
in its position,
Embraced Neptune, and closes the salt-
lending Fatherland.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Number "3" in upper right corner (indicating card number: Three of Diamonds)
- Diamond shape symbol in upper right
- Engraved coat of arms on the figure's chest
- This appears to be from a playing card series ("Pasquins Windkaart") used as a financial/satirical document from Het Groote Tafereel (The Great Picture Show)
