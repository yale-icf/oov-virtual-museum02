# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Top text:
"Hebben de Guijzarheid,
Door misdruk  ksy't"

Main figure text (left side):
"Mijn Agtbaarheid is met de zuid,
Door schraag-en woekerzugt verbruid"

**English translation:**

Top text:
"Have the Cowardliness,
Through misprint ksy't" [unclear/partially illegible]

Main figure text:
"My Dignity is with the south,
Ruined by greed and usurious desire"

**Notable markings:**

- Card designation indicator: "8" (Eight of Diamonds)
- A diamond symbol in the upper right corner
- Engraved illustration in the style of 17th-century Dutch satirical playing cards
- The figure depicted is a well-dressed man in period clothing holding various items
- A small building visible in the lower left background
- This is from Pasquins Windkaart (Pasquin's Wind Cards), a series of allegorical playing cards from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Spectacle)
