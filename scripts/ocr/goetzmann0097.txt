# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Drie en vier
Betaald laltheer

Zie de een en twe, en uyf en ues gaan uyf, maar ach !
De drie en vier betaald in Quinqueme poix t. gelag.

**English translation:**

Three and four
Paid by the gentleman

See the one and two, and five and six go five, but alas!
The three and four paid in Quinqueme poix the bill.

**Notable markings:**

- Number "7" in upper right corner (indicating this is card 7 of the series)
- Spade symbol (♠) in upper right
- This is page 19 of 55 from "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Cards), a Dutch satirical playing card series
- Engraving-style illustration with period Dutch clothing and a table with coins/payment visible
