# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

kleer Vossen en aapen
Om gelt te schraopen

t.Is vrugtcloos op winst gedagt,
Daar de Aep en Vos het voordeel magt

**English translation:**

Clever Foxes and Apes
To scrape for money

It is fruitless to think of profit,
When the Ape and Fox have the advantage

**Notable markings:**

- Decorative heart symbol in upper right corner
- Engraved illustration in woodcut/etching style showing two figures (an ape and a fox) in a tropical setting with palm trees
- One figure appears to be at or near a box or container
- Border frame around entire composition
- Page number notation indicates this is "Page 30 of 55" from "Pasquins Windkaart" playing cards
