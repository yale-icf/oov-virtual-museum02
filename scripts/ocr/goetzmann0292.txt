# Transcription of Baltimore and Ohio Railroad Company Stock Certificate

**COMMON STOCK**

100 [top left and right corners]

NUMBER: C243475

SHARES: 100

**The Baltimore and Ohio Railroad Company**

*Incorporated by the State of Maryland, February 28, 1827.*

This Certifies that

**EVANS, STILLMAN & CO.**

is the owner of

*One Hundred Shares of the Perpetual Preferred Letters and half paid and assessable of the Common Capital Stock in and Ohio Railroad Company transferred to and standing on the books of the Company in the name of [the holder hereof upon and subject to all the conditions upon which the same were issued and to all assessments and calls made thereon. The holder of Preferred Capital Stock is the owner of Forty Million Dollars ($40,000,000) now issued and such additional amounts as may be lawfully issued from time to time by the Board and Directors of said Railroad Company the property and franchise and rights granted to receive in each year out of the surplus net profits of the Company for the current year such yearly dividend (commutative as the Board of Directors of said Railroad Company may declare) to be not exceeding [for per centum] upon all dividends then declared. No transfer of said stock shall be valid until endorsed by or under the direction of the Transfer Agent. The holder of Preferred Capital Stock is the owner of Forty Million Dollars ($40,000,000) now issued and such additional amounts as may be lawfully issued from time to time by the Board and Directors of said Railroad Company.*

Dated: **JUN 19 1934**

**SHARES $100. EACH**
