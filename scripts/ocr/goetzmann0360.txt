# Transcription

**Original (French):**

COMPAGNIE FRANÇAISE
DU HAUT & DU BAS CONGO

CFNBC
[Circular stamp with "CHAMBRE DU HAUT & DU BAS CONGO"]

SOCIÉTÉ ANONYME AGRICOLE INDUSTRIELLE ET COMMERCIALE DE L'AFRIQUE ÉQUATORIALE

SIÈGE SOCIAL À PARIS

CAPITAL SOCIAL 850.000.000 DE FRANCS
Divisé en 170.000 Actions de 5.000 francs chacune dont 34.000 Actions de priorité
et 136.000 Actions ordinaires.

STATUTS DÉPOSÉS EN L'ÉTUDE DE M° AUBRON, NOTAIRE À PARIS

ACTION ORDINAIRE DE 5000 FRANCS

AU PORTEUR ENTIÈREMENT LIBÉRÉE N° 068.570

LE PRÉSIDENT-DIRECTEUR GÉNÉRAL          PAR DÉLÉGATION DU CONSEIL
                                         D'ADMINISTRATION.

[Signature: J Cousin]                    [Signature: illegible]

MORAX IMP. PARIS

---

**English translation:**

FRENCH COMPANY
OF UPPER & LOWER CONGO

ANONYMOUS JOINT-STOCK COMPANY FOR AGRICULTURE, INDUSTRY AND COMMERCE OF EQUATORIAL AFRICA

HEAD OFFICE IN PARIS

CAPITAL STOCK 850,000,000 FRANCS
Divided into 170,000 Shares of 5,000 francs each, of which 34,000 Priority Shares
and 136,000 Ordinary Shares.

BYLAWS DEPOSITED WITH THE OFFICE OF M° AUBRON, NOTARY IN PARIS

ORDINARY SHARE OF 5,000 FRANCS

BEARER FULLY PAID-UP No. 068,570

THE PRESIDENT-GENERAL MANAGER          BY DELEGATION OF THE BOARD
                                        OF DIRECTORS.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **N° 068,570**
- Circular stamp (red): "CHAMBRE DU HAUT & DU BAS CONGO" with "CFNBC"
- Two signatures: "J Cousin" (President-General Manager) and one illegible signature (Board delegation)
- Ornate border decorations featuring elephant heads, palm trees, colonial scenes, and tropical imagery
- Printer mark: "MORAX IMP. PARIS" (bottom center)
- Document printed on aged, cream-colored paper with blue ink engraving
