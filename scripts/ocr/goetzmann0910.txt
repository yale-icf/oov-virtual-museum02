**English translation:**

No 259.

The undersigned Director of the then by Royal Privilege authorized Swedish West India Company hereby certifies that
in very highly esteemed [person] Waldonia
C. G. [illegible] on Dalarne
for one Hundred Reichsthaler as the Capital of [the company]—
Bohemia, whereby as noted below
duly and faithfully acknowledges receipt, with the enclosed [document] securing the owner's rights
of this Company— hereupon for general
Transport— the carrier's right to the aforesaid Company's assets, in proportion
to the ordinary share in the individual Capital of Dalarne; and to all the advantages belonging
thereto. Furthermore, all the Trade which the Company shall enjoy
in accordance with the privilege obtained in October 1786 shall be
constantly [in effect] and available, even as and authorized
to [participate] in the said Trade and [Company]'s direction in the ordinary and
according to the rules which the said Royal Majesty for the Company's
use has prescribed, [these are the terms]. The Regulations of the 18 [items or points]
mentioned above are hereby in all respects binding on the holder and [others]. And to the truth hereof this Company's Seal is affixed at Stockholm this 15th December 1787.
