## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** №0349
- **Denomination:** 1,000 Rubles (Акция в 1000 рублей / Aktie à et tusen Rubel)
- **Date:** December 29, 1916 (charter approval); 1917 (issue date, Petrograd)
- **Circular Stamp:** "СЪЕЗД ЛЕСОПРОМЫШЛЕННИКОВ" [Congress of Timber Industrialists] with "ПЕТРОГРАД" (Petrograd) visible
- **Ornate decorative borders** with floral motifs throughout
- **Multiple handwritten signatures** of directors, administrators, and financial officers
- **Text in two languages:** Russian (Cyrillic) and Norwegian (Latin script)
- **Issued to:** Herr Jorgen Bogaland (Mr. Jorgen Bogaland)
- **Capital information:** Aktiekapital 3,000,000 Rubel (Share Capital of 3,000,000 Rubles)
