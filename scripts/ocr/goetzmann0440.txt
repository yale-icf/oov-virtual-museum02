## English Translation:

**Header:**
TO THE BEARER

**Main Title:**
SIBERIAN COMMERCIAL BANK

**Subheading:**
Charter approved June 28, 1872.
[USED/UTILIZED]

**Main text:**
SHARE
No. 10891

IN TWO HUNDRED FIFTY RUBLES
TO THE BEARER.

BANQUE DE COMMERCE DE SIBÉRIE.
Statutes sanctioned June 28, 1872.

ONE SHARE
OF TWO HUNDRED FIFTY ROUBLES

to bearer—

Chairman of the Board of Directors;
President of the Direction

RUBLES

Members of the Board of Directors
Members of the Direction

**Footer:**
St. Petersburg, 1905, St. Petersburg.

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 10891 (printed) / No. 10691 (as noted in document context)
- **Circular stamp (top center):** Library or institutional archive stamp in Russian (Cyrillic), appears to include date references "1915" and "1821"
- **Rectangular stamp (center right):** "Право на выдачу 1907 использовано" (Right of issue 1907, used)
- **Oval stamp (left center):** "М.Ф." monogram with coat of arms
- **Circular seal (lower left):** Heraldic seal with Cyrillic text
- **Handwritten signatures:** Multiple signatures of board members at bottom (largely illegible but present)
- **Location markings:** Both Russian (С.-Петербургъ) and French (St-Pétersbourg) designations
- **Quadrilingual borders:** Russian, German, French, and English text arranged around margins
- **Decorative elements:** Allegorical figures representing commerce and industry at top; ornamental border design throughout
