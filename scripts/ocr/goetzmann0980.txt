## English Translation:

**[Header]**
GRAND RUSSIAN RAILWAY COMPANY, By Imperial Decree, November 26, 1857

125 r.                                                                                    3%

**GRAND RUSSIAN RAILWAY COMPANY,**
Established by Imperial Decree, November 26, 1857.

**HEADQUARTERS OF THE COMPANY IN ST. PETERSBURG.**

The Company's Capital according to the Charter approved by Imperial decree on November 3, 1861:
Capital consists of: 1,600,000 shares at 125 rubles in silver equivalent to 75,000,000 rubles in silver; and 1,000,000 bonds; First emission: 70,000 bonds at 4 1/2% on 50 rubles equivalent to 35,000,000 rubles in silver (metallic); Second emission: 19,877 bonds at 4 1/2% on 125 rubles equivalent to 2,359,625 rubles in silver (metallic).

**THIRD EMISSION OF BONDS:**
105,176 bonds at 125 rubles in silver equivalent to 13,147,000 rubles in silver (metallic).

Issued at parity price, distributed through the Russian Telegraph Company, over a period of 70 days, one and a half years from each date. Holders receive income from November 1 to December 30 as specified in the bond terms and conditions for general presentation.

Annual income: 3 rubles 75 kopeks = 6 rubles 12 1/2 kopeks in silver = 7 guilders, paid semi-annually.

**BOND FOR ONE HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE RUBLES IN SILVER METALLIC,
or 500 francs = 20 pounds sterling = 402 German marks = 238 Dutch guilders.**

No. 210979

[Followed by extensive terms and conditions regarding redemption, payment procedures, and company obligations]

For the Grand Russian Railway Company:

**Chairman** [signature]
**Member** [signature]

**By order of the Board** [signature]

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number**: 210979
- **Date**: December 4, 1861 (4 Декабря 1861 г.)
- **Signatures**: Three handwritten signatures present (Chairman, Member, and Board representative)
- **Denomination**: 125 Rubles Silver (equivalent values listed: 500 francs, 20 pounds sterling, 402 German marks, 238 Dutch guilders)
- **Interest Rate**: 3% annual
- **Imperial Double-Headed Eagle**: Visible in multiple decorative locations
- **Ornamental Border**: Elaborate engraved decorative frame with allegorical figures
- **Printing Color**: Teal/green monochrome
- **Seal/Emblem**: Company emblem with "МФ" marking visible
