# Register MIDDELBURG – Plantation Bond Transcription

## Original Text (Dutch):

Ik Ondergeschreeve KORNELIS VAN DEN HELM BODDAERT, als Directeur en gequalificeerde van de geld Opsfieters op diversche Plantagien in de Colonie van Estequebo en Demerary, volgens de Hypotheeken ter Secretarie aldaar, in beholpzake Ordres alle rest van dit fonds gepasteerd, waar van de Coypen Authentieq met 'sCompagnie's Zegel bezegeld, en door den Secretaris der Colonie geteekend onder my zyn beruftsende, Bekenne in die qualiteit aggervolgens het gebruikte Project en Conditien (waar van den inhoud hier agter is geïncercert) Ontfangen te hebben

van **[illegible] Anna Brianna Van Citlacht**

Eene Somma van **[illegible Ducaton/Dukaten?] Loons** [unclear]

Welke somma ik Ondergeschreeve, beloove dan dezevlve of deszelfs of deszelfs Regt en Actie vervogde, en zullen doen Restitueeren na Expiratie van den tyd in het Plan hier agter vermeld, en inmiddels daar van te zullen, uitgegeeve, den Intrest tot [illegible] p Cento in 't Jaar ingaande primo January 1700 **[& te Cloosen?]** gedurende alzoo van Jaar tot Jaar, tot de volle en Effectueele betaaling der Hypotheeken, waar van de cerste vervolgde zou zijn op primo January 1700 op de Plantagien en zoo vervolgends alle Jaar, en dat van alles vry-geld zondert eenige kortinge aan. Capitaal of Interesten te mogen doen, zullende de Interesten werden betaald ten Comptoire van den Ondergeschreeve, tot nakominge van al het welke de Geld-opnemers boven hunne [speciale?] Hypotheeken hebben verbonden, hunne Perzoonen en goederen, ten

Ter Oirkonde dezes by my onderteekend
Middelburg den **[8?]sten Januarij 1794**

---

## English Translation:

I, the undersigned KORNELIS VAN DEN HELM BODDAERT, as Director and qualified [official] of the money receivers on various plantations in the Colony of Essequebo and Demerary, according to the mortgages at the Secretariat there, in the matter of Orders all remainder of this fund granted, of which the Coypen Authentic with the Company's Seal sealed, and signed by the Secretary of the Colony under my [authority] being, acknowledge in that capacity according to the used Project and Conditions (of which the contents are certified here after) to have received

from **[illegible] Anna Brianna Van Citlacht**

A Sum of **[illegible amount] [currency]**

Which sum I, the undersigned, promise the same or its or its right and action moreover, and shall do restore after Expiration of the time in the Plan stated here after, and meanwhile thereof to shall, issued, the Interest to [illegible] per Cent in the Year commencing primo January 1700 [& to Close?] during thus from Year to Year, to the full and Effectual payment of the mortgages, of which the first continued would be on primo January 1700 on the plantations and so successively all year, and that of all free-money without any abridgement to. Capital or Interest can do, the Interest will be paid at the Office of the undersigned, for the fulfillment of all which the money-takers above their [special?] mortgages have obligated, their persons and goods, to

In witness hereof signed by me
Middelburg **[8th] January 1794**
