## Notable markings:

- **Serial Number:** 008,679 (appears multiple times)
- **Signatures:** Two handwritten signatures present (dated Paris, 31 August 1931)
- **Official Seal/Stamp:** Circular red stamp of the Gouvernement de la Nouvelle-Calédonie et Dépendances visible on both the main bond and coupon sheets
- **Decorative Border:** Ornate geometric Art Deco-style border throughout
- **Coupon Stamps:** Red stamps on each attached coupon certificate
- **Reference Mark:** "8699" visible in top left corner
- **Printer Mark:** "IMPRIMERIE CHAIX, RUE BERGÈRE, 20, PARIS" noted at bottom
- **Coupons:** Numbered 68, 54, 67, 50, 66, 65, 64, 63, 62, 61 (right side, for interest payments through October 1962)
