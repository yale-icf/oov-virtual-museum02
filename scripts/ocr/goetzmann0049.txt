# Transcription

**Title (top of card):**
Providing for and employ-ing all the Poor in Gr: Britain

**Caption (bottom of card):**
The Poor when manag'd, and employ'd in Trade, —
Are to the public Welfare usefull made ;
But if kept Idle from their Vices spring
Whores for the Stews, and Soldiers for the King
