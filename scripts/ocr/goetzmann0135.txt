# Transcription

**LADY SNEERWELL.—School for Scandal.**

"There is no possibility of being witty without a little ill-nature; the *malice* in a *good thing* is the *barb* that *makes it stick*."

---

To say that your Ladyship's wrong I won't dare—
'Tis a dangerous subject to handle;
Gain but Fortune's favours, then what need you care?
Not a fig for the whole "School for Scandal."

---

The Lottery will be drawn 14th THIS MONTH, (January) and following Day.

---
