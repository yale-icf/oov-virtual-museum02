## English Translation:

**Header:**
Selim and Samaan Sednaoui Company

SOCIÉTÉ SELIM ET SAMAAN SEDNAOUI
ANONYMOUS COMPANY R.A.U.
Registration Certificate No. 377 - CAIRO

Year 1909

**Main Text:**
The Company's Capital: 620,000 Egyptian Pounds

**Coupon sections:**
Interest and Profits Coupon for the Year

- Serial number references visible: "٢٥٠...251" to "٢٥٠...250"
- Grid of 20 identical coupons at bottom (5 rows × 4 columns)
- Document appears aged with visible creasing and wear
