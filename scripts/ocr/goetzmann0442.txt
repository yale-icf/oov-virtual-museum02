## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** №07520
- **Monetary Value:** 50 Rubles (Pуб. 50 Рбл. / 50 Roubles)
- **Date:** 1911 (St. Petersburg / St-Pétersbourg)
- **Imperial Seal:** Circular seal with Cyrillic text (top right) indicating imperial approval
- **Company Seal:** Circular seal with Chinese characters (center left, dated 1907)
- **Multiple Signatures:** Board members' signatures visible on document (largely illegible in reproduction)
- **Decorative Border:** Ornamental frame with geometric patterns in corners
- **Trilingual Format:** Russian (left), French (center), Chinese (right margin) - reflecting multinational commercial interests
- **Charter Approval Date:** 12 February 1907 by Imperial decree
