# Transcription

**Title (top):** Bahama Islands

**Caption (bottom):**
Rare fruitfull Isles, where not an Able can find
A verdent Lust, or Thistle, to his Mind!
How then, must those Poor Silly Asses fare,
that leave their Native Land to Settle here?
