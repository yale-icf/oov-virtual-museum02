**Coupon 2 (7th):**

## Notable markings:

- **Circular seal/emblem** on left side of each coupon bearing Cyrillic text and decorative elements (bank's official seal)
- **Share certificate numbers**: 17747 and 17748 (repeated on all five coupons)
- **Handwritten signatures** on each coupon (appears to be the same signature repeated, likely "Прус" or similar)
- **Sequential coupon numbering**: 6th through 10th coupons
- **Years covered**: 1917–1921 (spanning the Russian Revolution period)
- **Green color scheme** throughout document
