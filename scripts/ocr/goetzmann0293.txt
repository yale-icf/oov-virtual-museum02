# Baltimore and Ohio Rail Road Company Stock Certificate - Transcription

**TITLE AND HEADER:**
The Baltimore and Ohio Rail Road Company.
Six Per Cent. Preferred Stock.
Shares

**MAIN TEXT:**

This is to Certify, That A. Gregg & Co. [handwritten names]
are entitled to [handwritten number] Sixty [unclear] Shares
in the Joint Purse of the Preferred Stock of the BALTIMORE AND OHIO RAILROAD COMPANY, being a part of the Twenty Thousand Shares of the said Preferred Stock, which were authorized by a general meeting of the Stockholders of the said Company, held on the eleventh day of May, in the year Eighteen Hundred and Sixty Eight, under and by virtue of the provisions of an Act of the General Assembly of Maryland, passed at the January Session, Eighteen Hundred and Sixty Eight, enacting a substitute for Article Twenty-Six, of the Public General Laws of the said State, relating to Corporations; upon which the said [handwritten] A. Gregg & Co.
and [unclear] assigns are entitled, under all the terms of the creation of the said second series of said Preferred Stock, and of the agreement made by the subscribers thereto, and of the guarantee of the said Company, dated on the 25th day of May, in the year Eighteen Hundred and Sixty Eight, to a perpetual annuity of six per centum per annum, and so long as the same shall be payable out of the Net Income of the Stock and Profit in each and every year, before any dividend is declared to any of the Stockholders of the said Company. When the holders of the Preferred Stocks thereof, or their assigns, in the order in which the said Preferred Stocks were issued, it being hereby expressly understood to be the condition of the issue of this Certificate, and of the guarantee aforesaid, that the holder of this Certificate, and their assigns shall not be entitled to any dividend, of any nature or description, declared upon the Stock of the said Company, other than the said dividend of six per centum per annum, and no more, guaranteed as aforesaid, to be paid upon said Preferred Stock; and that the Stock evidenced by this Certificate is held subject to the conditions of its original issue, and of the subscription thereto, and of the guarantee thereof. The said Stock is transferable on the Books of the Company on or before [unclear] with an assignment endorsed thereon.

**WITNESS,** the Seal of the Company, attested by the Signature of the Treasurer thereof.

Baltimore, Sept. 7th, 1863 [handwritten date]

**SIGNATURES:**
[Illegible] Treasurer [handwritten signature]
[Illegible] President [handwritten signature]

**PRINTER MARK:**
Kell & Fitt, Print, Baltimore.

**CERTIFICATE NUMBER:**
No. 466
