# Transcription of Share Certificate

## English Text (Left Section):

CHARLES LAFFITTE & COMPANY,
LIMITED.

Name _______________

Address _______________

No. 67906

£20 Share.

## French Text (Center - on ornamental banner):

Original:
"Il est Certifié que le Propriétaire dûment enregistré au Registre de la Société CHARLES LAFFITTE & COMPANY, LIMITED, est le propriétaire d'Une Action de Vingt Livres Sterling numérotée comme ci-dessus, conformément aux clauses et conditions de cette Société. Il est certifié en outre que jusqu'à ce jour la somme de DIX LIVRES STERLING, a été versée sur cette action."

English translation:
"It is Certified that the duly registered Owner in the Register of the Society CHARLES LAFFITTE & COMPANY, LIMITED, is the owner of One Share of Twenty Pounds Sterling numbered as above, in accordance with the clauses and conditions of this Society. It is further certified that up to this day the sum of TEN POUNDS STERLING has been paid on this share."

## English Text (Right Section):

"This is to Certify that the Person whose name is duly entered on the Register of Members of CHARLES LAFFITTE AND COMPANY, LIMITED, is the registered Proprietor of One Share of Twenty Pounds numbered 67906 in the Capital thereof, subject to the Bye Laws and Regulations of the Company, and that on this day the sum of TEN POUNDS has been paid.

Given under the Common Seal of the said Company the fifteenth day of January 1866."

DIRECTORS.

Specimen

## Notable Markings:

- Serial Number: No. 67906
- Date: 15th January 1866
- Ornamental decorative banner with "CHARLES LAFFITTE & COMPANY LIMITED" and denomination details
- Text notation at bottom: "NO TRANSFER OF THE SHARE CAN BE REGISTERED WITHOUT THE PRODUCTION OF THIS CERTIFICATE."
- "Specimen" printed in bottom right corner
- Blank spaces for: Name, Address, and signature lines
- "DIRECTORS." label on right side
