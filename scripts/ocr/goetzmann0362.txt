# Transcription

**No 72249**

**£50 SHARE**

**Overend, Gurney and Company, Limited,**

*Registered and Incorporated on the 12th day of July 1865.*

UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE COMPANIES ACT, 1862.

*This is to Certify that* **John Metcalfe Smith, Esq.,**
*of* **Leeds,**

*is at the date hereof the registered*

**Proprietor of ONE SHARE of £50 numbered 72249** *in the*

*Capital Stock of* **Overend, Gurney and Company, Limited,** *subject to the Articles of Association of the Company, and that up to this day there has been paid up in respect of such Share the sum of* **Fifteen Pounds.**

*Given under the Common Seal of the said Company this 15th day of November, 1865.*

**Wm. Bos**
SECRETARY.

*This Certificate must accompany the transfer of this Share.*
