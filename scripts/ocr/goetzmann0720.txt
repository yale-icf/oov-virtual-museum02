# Transcription of USSR State Loan Bond, 1946

## Original (Russian):

**Header:**
№ 012594 СССР № 33
СЕРИЯ ОБЛИГАЦИЯ

**Center Emblem:**
[USSR State Seal]

**Main Title:**
ГОСУДАРСТВЕННЫЙ ЗАЕМ ВОССТАНОВЛЕНИЯ
И РАЗВИТИЯ НАРОДНОГО ХОЗЯЙСТВА СССР
ОБЛИГАЦИЯ НА СУММУ

**Denomination:**
Двадцать пять рублей
[25 rubles in numerals on left and right corners]

**Date:**
1946
ОБЛИГАЦИЯ СЕРИЯ

**Bottom text band:**
ДВАДЦАТЬ ПЯТЬ
РУБЛЕЙ

**Serial/Classification Numbers:**
№ 012594 (top right, in red overprint)
Series 33
Class 98 [appears as "Раздел 98"]
№ 01 [in red overprint at bottom]

---

## English translation:

**Header:**
No. 012594 USSR No. 33
SERIES BOND

**Main Title:**
STATE LOAN FOR RESTORATION
AND DEVELOPMENT OF THE NATIONAL ECONOMY OF THE USSR
BOND FOR THE SUM OF

**Denomination:**
Twenty-five Rubles

**Date:**
1946

**Bottom:**
TWENTY-FIVE
RUBLES

---

## Notable markings:

- USSR State Seal (hammer and sickle emblem) at top center
- Red overprinted serial numbers and identifiers
- Ornate decorative border with classical motifs
- Large numeral "25" in bottom corners
- Fine line engraving throughout typical of official securities
