# Document Transcription

## Original (Spanish):

**Right Document (Main Bond Certificate):**

[Decorative border with ornamental corners]

[Eagle emblem with flourishes]

CONSOLIDADA AL TRES POR CIENTO.

Sèrie sèrie — Letra F — Número 548

BONO DE 5,000 PS.

[Ornamental flourishes]

Emitido en [date - partially illegible] de Junio de 1881.

**Left Document (Wrapper/Envelope):**

[Oval stamp/seal containing:]
5,000

[Decorative border pattern]

DEUDA [text in oval border - partially visible]

---

## English Translation:

**Right Document:**

CONSOLIDATED AT THREE PERCENT.

Series — Letter F — Number 548

BOND OF 5,000 PESOS

Issued in [June] of 1881.

---

## Notable Markings:

- Ornate decorative borders with floral and geometric patterns
- Oval seal/stamp on left document showing "5,000"
- Purple manuscript stamp/annotation (appears to be a library or archive marking)
- Serial number: 548
- Series designation: Letter F
- Decorative eagle emblem with floral elements
- Corner ornamental designs typical of 19th-century financial documents
- Multiple fold lines indicating age and storage
