# English Translation:

We the Princes and Estates of Upper and Lower Silesia, hereby acknowledge, declare, and make known to all those to whom it may concern, in what manner His Most Serene and Royal Majesty, our Most Gracious Emperor, King, and Lord, has most graciously permitted us, that His Imperial Majesty was pleased, on account of His Most Pressing Expenditures, to raise a Capital of seven hundred thousand guilders of the Holland Currency in Amsterdam, to be negotiated and taken up at the rate of Five percent per annum, which shall be repaid within the period of Fifteen Years, namely, in the Years 1739, 1740, 1742, 1743, 1744, 1745, and 1746, from the aforesaid Capital of seven hundred thousand guilders Holland Currency, according to all required stipulations, whereby in the earlier Years the Interest for a quarter of a Year shall be paid, but in the latter five Years, namely, in the Years 1747, 1748, 1749, 1750, and 1751, one fifth of the Capital itself, for a period of twenty percent per annum, from six months to six months, and the Interests calculated pro rata for the time and periods, shall be paid every three months from the aforesaid Silesian levies for the Revenues, collected and paid promptly; His Imperial and Royal Majesty has further desired that we shall bind ourselves for the aforesaid Sum by the Name of the Public to pay the same with and together with His Most Serene Imperial Majesty.
