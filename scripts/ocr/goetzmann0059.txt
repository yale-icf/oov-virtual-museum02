# Transcription

**Title (top of card):**
Exporting Timber from Germany

**Main text (bottom of card):**
You that are Rich, and hasty to be Poor,
Buy Lumber Export from the German Shoar.
for Galloufes, built up of Forraign Wood,
If rightly us'd, may do Change Alley good.

**Notable markings:**
- Ten of Hearts suit indicator (shown as 10 red hearts in upper left corner)
- Engraved illustration showing a German port scene with timber loading operations, vessels, dock workers, and coastal fortifications
- Period engraving style typical of early 18th-century satirical playing cards
- Hand-drawn or printed cursive text in the caption
