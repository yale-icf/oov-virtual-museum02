**LEFT SIDE (BULGARIAN):**

№ 79881 до 79890

"СВ. ГЕОРГИ"
АКЦИОНЕРНО ДРУЖЕСТВО ЗА ТЕКСТИЛНА ИНДУСТРИЯ И ТЪРГОВИЯ С ТЕКСТИЛНИ МАТЕРИАЛИ.
СОФИЯ

ДЕСЕТ АКЦИИ
ПО СТО ЛЕВА ВСЯКА

Предлежащият ми тарифи акции е удостоверение за притежаването на десет акции със стойност от по сто лева всяка едно на Дружеството "Св. Георги". София 1929, София

Вл имело на Управителният съвет на Дружеството
Членове на Съвета (или и Съвета д'Администтуватели)

**RIGHT SIDE (FRENCH):**

№ 79881 до 79890

"SV. GUÉORGU"
SOCIÉTÉ ANONYME POUR L'INDUSTRIE TEXTILE ET LE COMMERCE DES MATÉRIAUX TEXTILES
SOFIA

DIX ACTIONS
DE CENT LEVAS CHACUNE

Le porteur de la présente action est propriétaire de dix actions d'une valeur de cent levas chacune de la Société Anonyme pour l'Industrie Textile et le Commerce des Matériaux Textiles "Sv. Guéorgu".

Il a un droit proportionnel au Capital Social et aux bénéfices réalisés ainsi qu'à la liquidation d'après les statuts de la Société.

Les dividendes seront payés chaque année en échange des coupons attachés à la présente action.

Sofia 1929, Sofia

En vertu de l'Assemblée Générale du Droit Société
Membres du Conseil d'Administration
[Signatures]

## English Translation:

**LEFT SIDE:**

No. 79881 to 79890

"ST. GEORGE"
JOINT-STOCK COMPANY FOR TEXTILE INDUSTRY AND TRADE IN TEXTILE MATERIALS
SOFIA

TEN SHARES
OF ONE HUNDRED LEVAS EACH

The holder of the present share is the owner of ten shares with a value of one hundred levas each of the Company "St. George". According to the regulations and the law on business associations and the basic statutes, [the rights and obligations are as set forth in the regulations of the Joint-Stock Company]. You recognize and acknowledge this as verified.

Sofia, 1929

By authority of the Board of Directors of the Company
Members of the Council [of Administration]
[Signatures]

---

**RIGHT SIDE:**

No. 79881 to 79890

"SV. GUÉORGU"
ANONYMOUS SOCIETY FOR TEXTILE INDUSTRY AND THE TRADE OF TEXTILE MATERIALS
SOFIA

TEN ACTIONS
OF ONE HUNDRED LEVAS EACH

The bearer of the present action is the proprietor of ten actions of a value of one hundred levas each of the Anonymous Society for Textile Industry and the Trade of Textile Materials "Sv. Guéorgu".

He has a proportional right to the Capital Stock and to the profits realized as well as to liquidation according to the statutes of the Society.

The dividends shall be paid each year in exchange for the coupons attached to the present action.

Sofia 1929, Sofia

By virtue of the General Assembly of the Right of the Society
Members of the Administrative Council
[Signatures]
