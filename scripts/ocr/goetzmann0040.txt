# Transcription

**Title (top):** The Freeholder

**Main text (bottom):**
Come all ye Spendthrift Prodigals, that hold
Free Land and want to turn the Same to Gold; —
Wil Buy your all, provided you'll agree
To Drown your Purchase Money in South Sea. —

**Notable markings:**
- Club suit symbol (♣) in upper left corner
- Playing card format with engraved illustration showing a figure in period dress (top left) and a scene of multiple men in 18th-century clothing gathered around a table with large sheets of paper (center)
- Aged paper with visible creasing and wear
