# ALGIERS

Lottery begins January 21, 1817.
Two Grand Prizes of 20,000 Guineas;
And FORTY other Capitals!
All in Sterling Money!—No Stock Prizes!

MOHAMMED STABDALLA
BLOODHOUNDO ALI CUT-THROATO.

"By Mahomet's beard I swear,
"Of that Cake I'll have my share;
"Share?—I mean I'll have it all!
"All your Shares, both large and small!
"If this modest claim should fail me,
"By my whiskers! I'll impale ye!
"More than that—(my fury rises)—
"I'll seize on all your Lottery Prizes!"
