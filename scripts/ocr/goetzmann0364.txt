**English Translation:**

ELECTRIC LIGHTING OF ST. PETERSBURG
ANONYMOUS COMPANY

Constituted by deed executed before Messrs. N. Delcrocher and M. Halloren, both notaries in Brussels, May 1891 and published in the Belgian Official Gazette of June 11, 1891, No. 1288.

Registered Office: BRUSSELS

CAPITAL: TEN MILLION FRANCS
Divided into
40,000 Preference Shares at 250 Francs each
Reserves provided: 20,000 Ordinary Shares without stated value

PREFERENCE SHARE OF 250 FRANCS
FULLY PAID-UP

No. 38859

To the Administrator                To the Administrator
[Signatures]                        [Signatures]
Live for Control                    [illegible]

---

**Notable markings:**

- Red circular stamp (upper right, appears to be a Belgian official seal)
- Black circular stamp (upper center, marked "TITRES FRANCÉS" with date "21 PARIS 1920")
- Blue rectangular stamp (center area, partially visible)
- Serial number: N°. 38859
- Handwritten signatures and notation at bottom
- Decorative Art Nouveau borders with ornamental lamp posts framing an engraved view of St. Petersburg waterfront
- Ornamental banner header with "ÉCLAIRAGE ÉLECTRIQUE DE ST PÉTERSBOURG"
