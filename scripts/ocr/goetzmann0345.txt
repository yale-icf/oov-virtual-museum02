# Transcription of South Carolina Six Per Cent Consolidation Bond

**Central Text:**

DP 1602

STATE OF
SOUTH CAROLINA

SIX PER CENT

Consolidation Bond

$500.

SECURED BY AN
ANNUAL TAX

Coupon
Receivable for Taxes

Interest payable
JANUARY & JULY

Madison Columbia S.C.

PRINCIPAL DUE

JULY 1876

**Side Panels:**

500 (appears on both left and right decorative panels)

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: DP 1602
- Ornate decorative borders with floral motifs on left and right panels
- Denomination "$500" displayed in ornamental cartouches at bottom of each side panel
- Text arranged vertically on spine reading "Consolidation" (partially visible in the center fold)
- Overall sepia/tan coloring typical of 19th-century financial documents
- Decorative corner elements and cross symbols throughout the design
- No visible signatures present on this scan
