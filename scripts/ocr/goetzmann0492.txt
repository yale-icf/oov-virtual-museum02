# Transcription of Liverpool Corn Trade Association Debenture No. 291

## Front Left Page (Handwritten Notes):

"The certificate named George Grossman, died on the 17th Dec 1919 as officer by letters of Administration dated 21st January 1919 Granted to his Widow Agnes Ellen Grossman. Since of deceased.

Established [19th] 1919

Royal Corn
Secretary.

Transfer dated 25th May 1910
To Ernest Clifford Grasman
55 Crofton Road
East County

Registered in the Books of the Association
is third, of June 1919
Roy Grossman
Secretary."

## Reverse Page (Printed Text):

**The Conditions within referred to.**

[This page contains extensive printed legal text detailing the conditions governing the C. Perpetual Mortgage Debenture. The text is organized in numbered sections but is too dense and small to transcribe with complete accuracy in this format. Key headings visible include sections regarding:

- The nature of the Debenture as a series of "C" Perpetual Mortgage Debentures
- Terms and conditions of the mortgage
- Interest payment provisions
- Security provisions over the Association's property
- Conditions for redemption
- Rights and obligations of debenture holders
- Powers of the Association regarding the debentures]
