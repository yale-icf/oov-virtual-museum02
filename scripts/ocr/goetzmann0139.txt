# TIMOTHY TANDEM

In tandem or curricle, drive as you will,
You cannot drive well, lest your pockets you fill;
'Tis money alone sets you four-in-hand going,
'Tis money that makes you both witty and knowing.
Tim. Tandem, 'tis known, tho' he merrily grins,
Seldom laughs with a grace, unless when he wins;
Yet, of dame Fortune, he e'er wants a lift,
He treats his dear self with a Lottery gift:
Which never yet fail'd to insure him a tin,
And produce him some work for the smack of his whip,
Which doubtless next January, the *eighteenth*, he'll try;
And likewise all friends, known to you and to I.

The Lottery will be drawn 18th & 26th This Month Jan.
