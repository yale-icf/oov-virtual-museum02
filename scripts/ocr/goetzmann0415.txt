## Original (French):

**HEADER:**
CECTURE PROVISOIRE DEVRA ETRE ECHANGE A PARTIR DU 16 DÉCEMBRE 1889 CONTRE UN TITRE DÉFINITIF – LES TITRES DÉFINITIFS PRENDRONT SEULS PART AU TIRAGE DU 15 FÉVRIER 1890

**MAIN TEXT:**

COMPAGNIE UNIVERSELLE DU CANAL INTEROCÉANIQUE DE
PANAMA

Société anonyme au capital de TROIS CENTS MILLIONS de Francs
Divisé en 600,000 Actions de 500 Francs

SOCIÉTÉ CIVILE AVEC RESPONSABILITÉ LIMITÉE A LA MISE SOCIALE POUR L'AMORTISSEMENT
DES OBLIGATIONS A LOTS DU CANAL DE PANAMA – SESSION DU 26 JUIN 1888

EMPRUNT DE 720 MILLIONS

EMPRUNT AUTORISÉ CONFORMÉMENT AUX PRESCRIPTIONS DE LA LOI DU 21 MAI 1836,
PAR LA LOI DU 8 JUIN 1888,

MAIS SANS AUCUNE GARANTIE OU RESPONSABILITÉ DE L'ETAT

SOUSCRIPTION PUBLIQUE A LOTS

Rapportant la limite par un payement essentiellement très Le Décembre et 1 Juin de chaque année

## English Translation:

**HEADER:**
THIS PROVISIONAL CERTIFICATE MUST BE EXCHANGED FROM DECEMBER 16, 1889 FOR A DEFINITIVE TITLE – DEFINITIVE TITLES ALONE SHALL PARTICIPATE IN THE DRAWING OF FEBRUARY 15, 1890

**MAIN TEXT:**

UNIVERSAL INTEROCEANIC CANAL COMPANY OF
PANAMA

Joint-stock company with capital of THREE HUNDRED MILLION Francs
Divided into 600,000 Shares of 500 Francs each

CIVIL COMPANY WITH LIMITED LIABILITY RESTRICTED TO SOCIAL CONTRIBUTION FOR THE AMORTIZATION
OF BONDS WITH PRIZES OF THE PANAMA CANAL – SESSION OF JUNE 26, 1888

LOAN OF 720 MILLION

LOAN AUTHORIZED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THE LAW OF MAY 21, 1836,
BY THE LAW OF JUNE 8, 1888,

BUT WITHOUT ANY GUARANTEE OR LIABILITY OF THE STATE

PUBLIC SUBSCRIPTION WITH PRIZES

Yielding the limit through a payment essentially very [on] December and June 1 of each year
Redemption at 400 francs and payment of prizes shall be guaranteed by a deposit of French government bonds
or securities guaranteed by the French government.

PROVISIONAL BEARER BOND - NEGOTIABLE
No. 0,269,304

FOR ONE FULLY PAID 500-FRANC OBLIGATION

[Legal provisions regarding 60 francs allocation between the Universal Company of the Interoceanic Canal and the Civil Amortization Company, with 300 francs noted and specifications for capital amortization at 400 francs]

Paris, June 20, 1888

BY DELEGATION, | THE PRESIDENT-DIRECTOR, | AN ADMINISTRATOR, | A MEMBER OF THE COUNCIL
              |                          |                   | OF THE CIVIL COMPANY,

[Signatures]

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** N° 0,269,304
- **Signatures:** J.F. de Lesseps (President-Director) and H. Cottu (Administrator) [handwritten]
- **Stamps:** Multiple circular and rectangular stamps reading "REMBOURSÉ PAR LE SÉQUESTRE" (Redeemed by Receivership)
- **Official Seals:** Circular seal of "COMPAGNIE UNIVERSELLE DU CANAL INTEROCÉANIQUE DE PANAMA"
- **Postage Stamps:** Multiple 10-centime French stamps affixed throughout document
- **Postal Markings:** Various dated cancellation stamps from Paris and Lyon, dated 1889
- **Document Date:** Paris, June 20, 1888
- **Classification Number:** No 0,269,304 (top right)
