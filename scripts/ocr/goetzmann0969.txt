# Transcription

## Left Column (Russian)

**Original:**
БОЛГАРСКОЕ ЦАРСТВО.
Болгарский 5% золотой-займъ 1902 г.,
ОБЕЗПЕЧЕННЫЙ ДОХОДАМИ СЪ ТАБАКА

ТАЛОНЪ

По предъставлению сего талона, предъявителю будетъ выдавать, въ своё время, Министерством Финансов новый купонный листъ.

**English translation:**
BULGARIAN KINGDOM.
Bulgarian 5% Gold Loan of 1902,
SECURED BY TOBACCO REVENUES

TALON

Upon presentation of this talon, the holder will be issued, in due time, by the Ministry of Finance a new coupon sheet.

---

## Middle Column (German)

**Original:**
KÖNIGREICH BULGARIEN
5 % Bulgarische Staats-Gold-Anleihe von 1902
MIT VERPFÄNDUNG DER TABAKSSTEUERN

TALON

Gegen Uebergabe dieses Talons, wird dem Inhaber, seiner Zeit, ein neuer Couponbogen durch das Finanzministerium ausgehändigt werden.

**English translation:**
KINGDOM OF BULGARIA
5% Bulgarian State Gold Loan of 1902
WITH PLEDGING OF TOBACCO TAXES

TALON

In exchange for surrender of this talon, the holder will be issued, in due time, a new coupon sheet by the Ministry of Finance.

---

## Right Column (English)

BULGARIAN KINGDOM
5 % Gold Loan of 1902
SECURED ON THE TOBACCO DUTIES

TALON

Against surrender of this Talon the Ministry of Finance shall deliver, in due time, to the bondholder, a new sheet of coupons.
