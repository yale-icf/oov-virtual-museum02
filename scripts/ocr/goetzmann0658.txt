## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** No. 467,139
- **Date:** Belgrade, 18/31 October 1913
- **Official Seal:** Circular emblem (top center) with Serbian royal insignia
- **Signatures:** Three handwritten signatures at bottom (Minister of Finance, National Bank Governor/President)
- **Decorative Border:** Ornate Celtic-style patterns in green and cream
- **Language:** Bilingual - French (left) and Serbian Cyrillic (right)
- **Denomination:** 500 Francs / 500 Dinara at 5% per annum
- **Printer Mark:** IMPRIMERIE CHAIX, RUE BERGÈRE, 20, PARIS at bottom
