# Transcription

**Original (French):**

CRÉDIT FONCIER AGRICOLE DU MAROC

SOCIÉTÉ ANONYME
au CAPITAL de 1.000.000 de FRANCS
divisé en Dix Mille Actions de Cent francs

SIÈGE SOCIAL:
92, Rue de Richelieu, PARIS

Statuts approuvés par l'État de Vos Président à Champes
le 17 Mars 1920

ACTION DE 100 FRANCS

AU PORTEUR
entièrement libérée

N° 8497

Un Administrateur.                    Un Administrateur.

[CRÉDIT FONCIER AGRICOLE DU MAROC - circular stamp]

Paris le _____ 19____

---

**English translation:**

AGRICULTURAL LAND CREDIT BANK OF MOROCCO

ANONYMOUS COMPANY
with CAPITAL of 1,000,000 FRANCS
divided into Ten Thousand Shares of One Hundred Francs

REGISTERED OFFICE:
92, Rue de Richelieu, PARIS

By-laws approved by the State of Your President at Champes
March 17, 1920

SHARE OF 100 FRANCS

TO BEARER
fully paid up

No. 8497

One Administrator.                    One Administrator.

[AGRICULTURAL LAND CREDIT BANK OF MOROCCO - circular stamp]

Paris on _____ 19____

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **8497**
- Red crescent and star symbol (top center)
- Red circular stamp with "CRÉDIT FONCIER AGRICOLE DU MAROC" (center bottom)
- Two administrator signature lines
- Decorative Moorish arch border with ornamental patterns
- Illustration of North African landscape with camels and palm trees (center vignette)
- Number "3" in red circle (bottom right corner)
- Ornamental Celtic/Islamic geometric border patterns throughout
