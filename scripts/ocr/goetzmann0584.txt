# TRANSCRIPTION OF BULGARIAN LOAN DOCUMENT (1907)

**УСЛОВИЯ НА ЗАЕМА.**

[Long Russian text describing loan conditions, including references to Bulgarian currency, interest rates, and repayment terms. The text mentions dates including 1/14 November 1907 and various financial obligations.]

Key sections visible include:
- Multiple references to loan amounts and payment schedules
- References to locations: Варна, Русе, Габрово, Габрово, Пловдив, София, Враца, Тырново
- Payment terms and conditions for bond holders
- References to Emperor Boris I (Царь I Август)

## English Translation of Conditions:

**CONDITIONS OF THE LOAN**

The Kingdom loan secured on the rate of 5 per cent a half year and 1/14 & 30 per cent yearly. [payments due] 1st February and 1st August every year against the half yearly coupons...

[The document outlines payment terms, locations for redemption, and conditions for bondholders, including specifications that bonds would be redeemed according to an amortization schedule beginning in specific years with designated annual amounts.]
