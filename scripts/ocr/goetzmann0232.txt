# Transcription

**No. 18                                                                                                           100 Shares**

## AMERICAN & BRITISH SECURITIES COMPANY
INCORPORATED UNDER THE LAWS OF THE STATE OF DELAWARE.

This Certifies that **Hattie J. Barnhill**

is the owner of **ONE HUNDRED (100)** shares of COMMON STOCK of AMERICAN & BRITISH SECURITIES COMPANY, each share without nominal or par value, fully paid and non-assessable, transferable only on the books of the corporation, in person or by attorney, upon the surrender of this certificate properly endorsed.

The holders of preferred stock are entitled to receive on or before the first day of each annum, payable semi-annually in each year, at such times as the board of Directors shall determine, and which said dividends shall be in preference to and shall be payable before any dividends shall be apart on paid upon the common stock. The dividends due upon the preferred stock semi-annually from the date of issue to and including September 15th, 1919, shall not be cumulative, but thereafter, to-wit, after September 15th, 1919, dividends at the rate of seven per cent. (7%) per annum on any deficiency in any of the dividends to be paid on the preferred stock on or before October 15th, 1919, shall be made good out of the earnings of subsequent years before dividends shall be paid upon the common stock.

In the event of a dissolution or liquidation of the corporation or a voluntary or involuntary, or upon any distribution of its capital, there shall be paid to the holders of the preferred stock the par value thereof, to wit, one hundred dollars ($100) per share, and also any assets distributed among the holders of the common stock, after payment to the holders of the preferred stock of its accrued and unpaid, accrued and accumulated dividends as above provided, the remaining funds of the corporation shall be divided among and paid to the holders of the common stock, according to the shares held by each respectively.

The corporation shall have the option of redeeming the preferred stock, in whole or in part, at any time after issue, on any semi-annual dividend period, by payment to the holders thereof of the sum of one hundred dollars ($100) per share for each share of such preferred stock so redeemed, together with all accrued and accumulated dividends thereon, upon thirty days actual or published notice to the holders thereof.

Each share of preferred stock and each share of common stock outstanding shall be entitled to one vote each at all meetings of stockholders for any purpose. The common stock shall have no nominal or par value, and each share of the same shall be entitled in the event of the sale or other disposition of all or substantially all of the assets of the corporation to vote thereon, subject to the payment of all sums due and payable upon the preferred stock, cumulative or non-cumulative according to their nature.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the corporation has caused this certificate to be signed by its duly authorized officers, and its corporate seal to be hereunto affixed this _____ day of _____________, 19___.

**Edward P. Johnson**                                                                                              [illegible signature]
Secretary                                                                                                                    President
