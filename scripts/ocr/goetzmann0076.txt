# Transcription

**Title (top of card):**
Buying Seamens Tickets

**Main text (bottom of card):**
At the Cafe Stands the Wapping Ale Wives buy,
the Seamens Tickets for a small Supply.—
But tis no matter whether Spendthrift Slaves,
Are Chouse'd by Wapping Whores or Bubbling Knaves.

**Notable markings:**
- Suit symbol: Eight of Clubs (displayed in top left corner with eight club symbols arranged in traditional playing card pattern)
- Document type: Playing card from the "John Law and South Seas Bubble Playing Cards" series
- Page notation: Page 49 of 51
- Historical context: These cards satirized the financial speculation and fraud associated with the South Sea Bubble of 1720
