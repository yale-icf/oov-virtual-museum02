# Transcription

**BETSY BUXOM.**

When honest Jack is forc'd from home,
To quit his Bet, abroad to roam;
What are the pangs that are not felt;
Their souls united almost melt:
But sorrows—hence—far hence from me,
I sing of mirth and revelry.
"Laughter, holding both its sides,"
As the Prizes, Joy divides;
Bestow'd by Fortune's lib'ral hand,
At COOPER'S-HALL, in England's happy land!

• Lottery will be drawn 18th & 26th This Month Ju[ly]
