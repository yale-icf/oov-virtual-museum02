# Transcription of Oregon and Transcontinental Company Stock Certificate

**CAPITAL $50,000,000.**

**Oregon and Transcontinental Company**

**NUMBER 40583**

**100 SHARES**

**$100 EACH**

---

**THIS IS TO CERTIFY** that [Halston H. Loom & Oog] is the owner of

**ONE HUNDRED**

Shares of Hundred Dollars each, fully paid and free from assessment on the Capital Stock of the Oregon and Transcontinental Company, transferable only on the books of the Company in person or by duly authorized attorney upon the surrender of this Certificate properly endorsed hereon.

In Witness our hands and the Corporate Seal of said Company this

**22nd day of September 1888**

[Signatures]

Edward Ede                                    Mayor Fiske
Assistant Secretary                          President

**$100 SHARES EACH**

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: 40583
- Date: September 22, 1888
- Two signatures: Edward Ede (Assistant Secretary) and Mayor Fiske (President)
- Multiple stamps and notations in purple/magenta ink
- Decorative green ornamental border with floral patterns
- Central engraving depicting a steam locomotive and Native American figures in a landscape
- "SEP 1888" date stamp visible
- Various handwritten annotations and reference numbers
