**English Translation:**

EGYPTIAN GENERAL SOCIETY
FOR AGRICULTURE & COMMERCE

ANONYMOUS COMPANY

Constituted by notarial act before M Alphonse-Louis-Jean COLS, notary in Antwerp, on 31 May 1905
and published in the annexes of the Belgian Official Gazette, 18 June 1905, no. 3503, and whose capital was increased by notarial act before the same notary on 30 June 1905 and published in the annexes of the Belgian Official Gazette, 14 July 1905, no. 3523.

INITIAL CAPITAL: 12,500,000 FRANCS increased to
15,000,000 FRANCS

Represented by 60,000 Capital Shares of 250 Francs each
and 8,500 Founder's Parts divided into tenths (art. 14)

ONE-TENTH OF A BEARER'S FOUNDER'S PART

No. 02524

WITHOUT DESIGNATION OF VALUE

An Administrator                                An Administrator
