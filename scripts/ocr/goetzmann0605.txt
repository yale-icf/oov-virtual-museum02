# Transcription

£.12. 17. 072                                                          Numb. 11593

STATE of CONNECTICUT.

Treasury-Office, June 1, A. D. 1782.

THE State of Connecticut doth owe unto _________________ who hath served in the [Continental Army], the Sum of Twelve Pounds Seventeen Shillings and [Eight Pence], Being one fourth Part of the Balance found on his Order at this Office, in Gold or Silver, on the first Day of June, A. D. One Thousand Seven Hundred and Eighty-Two, with annual Interest thereof; which Interest shall be paid unto him or his Order annually, at the same Place, on the first Day of June, if demanded, pursuant to an Act of the General Assembly, held at Hartford, the second Thursday of May last.

[Signature]
Treasurer.
