# Transcription

**Title (top right):**
Trade to Harborough

**Main Text (bottom, in italics):**
you that delight to take up foreign Linnen,
At Harb'rough made, a little town in Bremen,
Encourage Trade abroad, for time to come
And like Kind Fools, neglect your own at home.

**Notable Visual Elements:**
- Three of Clubs suit symbol in top left corner
- Engraved illustration showing a harbor scene with a brick fortification/warehouse on the left, a merchant ship with multiple masts in the center, dock workers and figures in the foreground engaged in trade activities, and water/seascape on the right
- Fine line engraving technique typical of early 18th-century playing cards
- Aged, cream-colored paper stock with visible wear and discoloration
