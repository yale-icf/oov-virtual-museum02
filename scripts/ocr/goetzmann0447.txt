**2nd EMISSION.**
No. 3276

**LOVITCH COMPANY OF CHEMICAL PRODUCTS AND FERTILIZING SUBSTANCES.**

The Company's Charter has been approved by Imperial decree on June 9, 1895.

**BASIC CAPITAL: 1,000,000 RUBLES.**

**SHARE**

**OF TWO HUNDRED FIFTY RUBLES**

issued (delivered) or presented to bearer.

**SOCIÉTÉ DE LOVITCH**
**OF PRODUCTS AND CHEMICAL FERTILIZERS.**

The Statutes were sanctioned by His Majesty the Emperor on June 9, 1895.

Capital: 1,000,000 rubles.

**SHARE OF TWO HUNDRED FIFTY RUBLES.**

Directors of the Board / Administrators:
[Multiple signatures]

Accountant / Cashier:
[Signatures]

Warsaw, 1895, Versovie.

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** №3276
- **Denomination:** Руб. 250 Rbls / 250 Rubles
- **Official Seal/Stamp:** Circular seal in Russian Cyrillic reading "ЛОВИЧСКОЕ ОБЩЕСТВО" (Lovitch Company) visible on document
- **Watermark/Decorative Elements:** Ornamental border with geometric patterns and floral designs in green; decorative medallions in corners
- **Company Monogram:** M.Φ. (Cyrillic initials) in octagonal frame at top center
- **Multiple Signatures:** Six handwritten signatures from directors, accountant, and cashier
- **Location:** Warsaw (Варшава/Varsovie), 1895
- **Language Notation:** Bilingual document (Russian and French) reflecting the cosmopolitan nature of Russian Empire commerce
