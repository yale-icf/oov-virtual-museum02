# Transcription of Republic of Texas Government Bond, $100

**Header:**
Government Bond, payable to holder.
RECEIVABLE FOR ALL GOVERNMENT DUES.

**Main Text:**
[Top left seal with ornamental design]

This Certifies that [illegible] of __________ is entitled to __________ Dollars of the Principal of a Loan of $5,000,000

One Hundred Dollars

with interest at the rate of eight percent per annum from [date], payable semi-annually at the Treasury Department in Gold or Silver on presentation of this Certificate and the proper Interest Coupons thereto attached, and is to be paid, on the first day of July, 18__, the principal value redeemable of the Government, after the first day of [month], 18__. This Certificate including the compliments of interest which must have been due shall at all times be receivable in payment of all Taxes due to the Government the revenue from License Tax and Tax on Ressalts [?] is set apart and constituted for the payment of the interest on the foregoing Loan and [illegible]

Signed: [Two signatures visible]
John F. Chalmers, Sec'y Texas    David G. Burnet [or similar]

**Interest Coupon Strips (Bottom):**
Ten coupons arranged in two rows of five, each reading:
"B.S.M. Bond, [date]"
"Texas Exhibit [illegible] ... [date]"
"Sec'y Treasury"

**Notable markings:**
- Serial number designation areas (unclear/illegible in reproduction)
- Two ornamental circular seals with "C" motif
- Engravings depicting: profile portrait (left), standing figure with industrial/agricultural imagery (center), building structure (right), livestock (upper right)
- Two denomination markers: "100" (upper left and lower left corners)
- Text reading "ONE HUNDRED DOLLARS" on right margin
