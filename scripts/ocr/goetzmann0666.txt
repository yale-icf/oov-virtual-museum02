# Transcription: Bulgarian 6% Hypothecary State Loan Bond of 1892

## Original Text (French and Bulgarian):

**Header:**
Nuмmer 069,185 – 069,186

КНЯЖЕСТВО БЪЛГАРИЯ | Principauté de Bulgarie
(Knyazhestvo Balgariya)

**French text (top right):**
Emprunt hypothécaire d'État 6% de l'année 1892

**Bulgarian text (top left):**
6% Дръжавния ипотекарен заем от 1892 година.

---

**Central section:**

ДВЕ ОБЛИГАЦИИ | DEUX OBLIGATIONS
(Two Obligations)

**Left side (Bulgarian):**
ПEТЬ СТОТИНЬ ЛЕВА
ЦАРСК СЛОВОНОСНЕ АВСТРИЙСКО-ФЛОРИНЕ ЗА ЕДИН ЛЕВА.

**Right side (French):**
CINQ CENTS FRANCS
FLORINS D'AUTRICHE DE 3.76, CHACUN.

---

**Main body text:**
[The document contains extensive terms and conditions in both French and Bulgarian, discussing the hypothecary loan, interest rates, repayment terms, and conditions of the bonds. The text is dense and covers multiple paragraphs on both sides of the certificate.]

---

**Bottom signatures and dates:**

Подпис на Председателя на Министерския Съвет:
[Signature] Stambolov

Le Ministre des Finances:
[Signature - illegible]

**Location and date:**
СОФИЯ, в 15 Ноемвре 1892.
SOFIA, le 15 Novembre 1892.

---

## Notable markings:

- **Serial numbers:** 069,185 – 069,186
- **Royal seal:** Circular Bulgarian royal coat of arms (lower left)
- **Official stamp:** Red circular stamp at top center (Principality of Bulgaria official seal)
- **Signatures:** Two handwritten signatures at bottom (Bulgarian Prime Minister Stambolov and Minister of Finance)
- **Handwritten annotations:** Pencil notations at top of page (appears to be archival or collection marks)
- **Decorative border:** Green ornamental frame with repeating pattern around all edges
- **Denomination:** 2 × 500 Francs/Leva (total 1,000 francs/leva)
- **Interest rate:** 6% per annum
