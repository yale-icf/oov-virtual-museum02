## English Translation:

Copy.

On this day, the twenty-first of March in the year seventeen hundred and seventy-seven, appeared before me *Hermannus de Wolff Junior*, Notary Public, admitted by the Court of Holland, residing in Amsterdam, in the presence of the aforementioned witnesses,

The Gentleman *Frans Jacob Heshuysen*, on behalf of his Company calling itself *Adolph Jan Heshuysen and Compagnie*, Merchants within this City and in Haarlem and known to me Notary, who likewise exhibited to me Notary a Mortgage or Hypothec, by the Gentleman *James Ballmer*, Merchant in London, on the thirteenth of August in the year seventeen hundred and seventy-six for the benefit of the Gentlemen *Adolph Jan Heshuysen, Floris Visscher Heshuysen* and him, the Gentleman Present *Frans Jacob Heshuysen*, together, namely as he, the Gentleman Present *Frans Jacob Heshuysen*, [represents] the Members of his Gentleman Present's said Company of *Adolph Jan Heshuysen and Compagnie*, guaranteed, for a sum of seven hundred pounds Sterling, levied on the Plantation *Vauxhall*, located in the Island of Dominica, which Plantation according to the aforementioned Mortgage or Hypothec mentioned in the year seventeen hundred and seventy-two has been provided with a Life Annuity on diverse Lives in Great Britain to, the amount of twelve hundred, ninety and sixty pounds and ten shillings Sterling per year, without any further Hypothec or encumbrance prior to the date of the aforementioned Mortgage or Hypothec on the same Plantation *Vauxhall* having been levied, the ownership of the said Plantation *Vauxhall* according to that mentioned in the aforementioned Mortgage or Hypothec being to the said Gentleman *James Ballmer* and acquired by lawful Purchase for a sum of nine thousand pounds Sterling above the burden of the aforementioned annual Life Annuity of twelve hundred ninety and sixty pounds and ten shillings Sterling per year, or such part thereof as shall still be paid annually, until the death of the named and in the aforementioned Mortgage or Hypothec specifically mentioned Lives, and by the said Gentleman *James Ballmer* by the aforementioned Mortgage or Hypothec for the benefit of the said Gentlemen *Adolph Jan Heshuysen, Floris Visscher Heshuysen* and him,
