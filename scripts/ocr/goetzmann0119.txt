# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Voor den Koning

Sij ruim; swant zook eens mug den Ackerkoning raken,
Lag ik met Zuid en West om mijn fortuin te maken.

**English translation:**

For the King

Clear out; for once a midge hit the Field-king,
I lay with South and West to make my fortune.

**Notable markings:**

- Title at top: "Voor den Koning" (For the King)
- Card designation: "9" (Nine of Diamonds) in upper right corner
- This is page 41 of 55 from Pasquins Windkaart playing cards
- Engraved illustration in classical style showing a well-dressed man in 17th-century attire holding a scroll/parchment in one hand and what appears to be a spherical object in the other
- Landscape background with trees and buildings
- Decorative diamond shape in upper right
- Text in italicized script at bottom of the card
