# Transcription

**Original (French):**

LA PELLETERIE RUSSO-AMÉRICAINE
SOCIÉTÉ ANONYME

Capital Social: 5.000.000 de Francs divisé en 4.000 Actions de Jouissance et 46.000 Actions de Capital.

SIÈGE SOCIAL: 1, Place des Victoires — PARIS

STATUTS DÉPOSÉS EN L'ÉTUDE DE M° DUFOUR, NOTAIRE A PARIS

ACTION DE CAPITAL DE CENT FRANCS
AU PORTEUR

N° 036857

Paris, le 5, Juillet 1926.

UNA [unclear] UR [unclear]

---

**English Translation:**

THE RUSSIAN-AMERICAN FUR COMPANY
JOINT-STOCK COMPANY

Capital: 5,000,000 Francs divided into 4,000 Jouissance Shares and 46,000 Capital Shares.

REGISTERED OFFICE: 1, Place des Victoires — PARIS

BYLAWS FILED WITH THE OFFICE OF MR. DUFOUR, NOTARY IN PARIS

CAPITAL SHARE OF ONE HUNDRED FRANCS
TO BEARER

No. 036857

Paris, July 5, 1926.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **036857**
- Date: **July 5, 1926** (handwritten as "le 5, Juillet 1926")
- Ornate decorative border in blue and cream
- Central seal with radiating sunburst design, featuring the company monogram in red
- "Titres Français Abonnement" circular stamp (upper right)
- Signature marks (partially legible, lower left and right)
- Printer's mark: "Imp. Max-Chesnitz, Paris" (lower right corner)
- Handwritten annotation/signature on left side: [illegible]
