# English translation:

**Conditions of Negotiation for a Fund under the Direction of DANIEL CHANGUION, for certain Planters in Rio Essequebo and Rio Demerara, for the continuation and improvement of their Plantations, a Sum of ƒ400,000 for 10 Years shall be furnished, at an Interest of 6% per annum.**

**ARTICLE 1.**

The Planters in the aforementioned Colonies, who are inclined to take up certain Monies for the improvement and continuation of their Plantations, and who are accepted for this purpose by the Director, shall be bound to cause said Plantations to be inventoried at their own cost by competent Appraisers, namely with respect to the Land, buildings, with the exclusion of Furniture and other Articles unnecessary to the working of a Plantation, and the full and unencumbered Ownership of such Inventory shall be brought to value by the aforementioned Assessors.

**2.**

The Inventory as determined above, having been drawn up and assessed, shall be sent to Daniel Changuion, together with the necessary Deed of Hypothecation, from which it must appear that such Hypothecation has been properly registered at the Secretary of the Colony, and that no other Special or General hypothecation on such Effects is in place [illegible section].

**3.**

To the Planters who shall submit the Inventory and Deed of Hypothecation as above, shall be loaned half, or at most five-eighths of the value upon which their Plantations, with their appurtenances, are assessed, at an Interest of Six percent per annum, which they bind themselves to pay yearly in addition to all other Charges, on penalty as mentioned in the Deed of Hypothecation.

**4.**

The Money-borrowers, who can annually prove by means of Valuable Certificates that their possessions have thereby been improved, shall have the liberty to have a new Appraisal made as above of the additional value, and to take up half of the improvement, provided the same be hypothecated to: Daniel Changuion.

**5.**

The Respective Planters Money-borrowers bind themselves to address all their produce to, offer necessary Provisions from, and draw Bills on Daniel Changuion; and at the time of sending their Produce, to furnish him with a proper account for the arrangement of the necessary supply, with the proviso that if they should fail therein, or should sell their Produce in the respective Colonies in any other manner, and should fall behind in paying their annual Interest, the Capital with the Interest may be immediately collected from their Persons, Plantations with all appurtenances, and all other moveable and immoveable Goods, before the Court of Justice in the Colony, or wherever it may be required, by voluntary Condemnation.

**6.**

The Money-lenders reserve to themselves the liberty to appoint such and as many Persons in the Rivers of Essequebo and Demerara and to qualify them as they...
