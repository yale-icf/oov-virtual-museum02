## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** №364213 (appears twice)
- **Ornate decorative border:** Elaborate geometric pattern in green throughout
- **Imperial Seal:** Double-headed eagle emblem at top center
- **Circular stamp/seal:** Lower left corner (appears to be an institutional seal, partially legible)
- **Multiple signatures:** Director, Deputy Director, and Board Members (all signatures appear to be in cursive Russian script but are not clearly legible in this scan)
- **Date:** "С.-Петербург, 1897 год" (St. Petersburg, 1897)
- **Color:** Green tinted background throughout document
