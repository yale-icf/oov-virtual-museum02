## English Translation:

**Header:**
Certificate of the State Commission for the Repayment of Debts

**Main Text:**

For a Perpetual Capital

I certify that His Majesty [the State Commission for the Repayment of Debts recognizes] the entry made by the Aforementioned Party in the State Debt Book under the name [unclear] under the administration of [unclear] 1st

Perpetual and eternal [status with] eternal rights without privileges [receiving] uninterrupted income at Six per Cent per Annum, for which [there has been] received [unclear] titled roubles, payable semi-annually [for these] titled roubles.

[Payments] on the 15th through [February] and from the 15th through [July]

## Notable Markings:

- **Imperial Double-headed Eagle** at top center with crown
- **Ornamental borders** with repeating eagle pattern at top
- **Two signatures** (Управляющий/Director and Бухгалтер/Bookkeeper)
- **Circular decorative elements** along left and right margins
- **Document Classification:** "ЛИТ. А" (Letter A)
- **Issue Date:** 1839
- **Denomination:** 300 Roubles (both capital and income columns)
- **Interest Rate:** 6% per annum (по Шести на Сто)
- **Payment Schedule:** Semi-annually on January 15 and July 15
