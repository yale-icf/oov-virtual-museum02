# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Heer

cerst gelukkig,
Nu dronkkig.

K.Ben Directeur geweest, maar tot
mijn ongeluk, het geld droop door de zak, en het
mir niets als dronk.

---

**English translation:**

Sir

First lucky,
Now drunk.

I was a Director, but to
my misfortune, the money dripped through my pocket, and
nothing for me but drink.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Title at top: "Heer" (Sir)
- Club symbol (♣) in upper right corner
- Signature or artist mark visible in lower right area: "[artist mark]"
- This appears to be page 10 from a 55-page playing card series titled "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Cards) from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Picture Book)
- Card designation: King of Clubs
