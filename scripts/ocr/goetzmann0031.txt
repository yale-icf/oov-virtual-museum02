# Transcription

**Title:** Baftard Children

**Text at bottom:**

Love on ye jolly Rakers, and burcome Dames, —
A Child is safer than venereal flames ;
Indulge your Senses with the Sweet offence, —
We'll keep your Baftard at a Small expence .

**Notable markings:**

- Suit symbol (Spade) visible in top left corner
- Decorative illustration showing a woman in a doorway (top left) and multiple figures in period dress in an urban courtyard setting (center/bottom)
- Brick buildings depicted in background
- Engraved/etched style typical of 18th-century playing cards
- Aged paper with visible wear and discoloration
