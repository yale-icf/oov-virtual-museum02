# Transcription of Suez Canal Bond Certificate

## Original (French):

**Header:**
CANAL MARITIME DE SUEZ

2e SERIE                                                           2e SERIE

Compagnie Universelle du
CANAL MARITIME DE SUEZ
Emprunt de 100 Millions

Autorisé par l'Assemblée Générale des Actionnaires de l'Août 1863 —
Converti, par tout l'accord par Décret de l'Ordre Publique de France.

Obligation de 500 francs 3% au Porteur.
N° 271,266

Par Délégation,                                Le Président
[illegible signature]                du Conseil d'Administration,
                                      [signature - appears to be "Henri Francias d'Henckels"]

**Bottom Section ("le Porteur / A DROIT"):**

le Porteur
A DROIT:

1° A un intérêt semestriel de 7 fr 50 payable le 1er Mars à le 1er Septembre de chaque Année.

2° Au remboursement du Capital de 500 francs en 75 ans par voie de tirages annuels à partir de l'Août 1857.
Les tirages s'opéreront par Séries de 100 Obligations ou par fractions de Série.

**Coupon Stubs (right margin and bottom):**
Multiple coupons dated from 1946-1961, reading:
CANAL MARITIME DE SUEZ
Coupon de 7 fr 50
Échéant le 1er [Mars/Septembre] [year]

## English Translation:

**Header:**
SUEZ MARITIME CANAL

2nd SERIES                                                         2nd SERIES

Universal Company of the
SUEZ MARITIME CANAL
Loan of 100 Million

Authorized by the General Assembly of Shareholders of August 1863 —
Converted, by complete agreement by Decree in the Public Order of France.

Obligation of 500 francs 3% to Bearer.
No. 271,266

By Delegation,                           The President
[illegible signature]             of the Board of Administration,
                                   [signature]

**Bottom Section (Rights of Bearer):**

The Bearer
IS ENTITLED TO:

1° Semiannual interest of 7 francs 50 cents payable on the 1st of March and 1st of September of each Year.

2° Reimbursement of the Capital of 500 francs in 75 years by means of annual drawings beginning from August 1857.
The drawings shall be made by Series of 100 Obligations or by fractions of Series.

**Coupon Stubs (right margin and bottom):**
Multiple coupons dated from 1946-1961, reading:
SUEZ MARITIME CANAL
Coupon of 7 francs 50 cents
Due [1st of March/1st of September] [year]

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 271,266 (2e Série)
- **Stamps:** Red circular seal reading "CANAL MARITIME DE SUEZ" appears throughout document
- **Signatures:** Two handwritten signatures present (one partially illegible, one at bottom right)
- **Decorative Elements:** Elaborate border with Egyptian and classical maritime imagery, including winged scarab, pyramids, Suez Canal scenes, and nautical symbols
- **Watermark/Background:** Faint background pattern visible throughout
- **Coupon Numbers:** Sequential numbering from 149 (top) to 119 (bottom), documenting payment dates from 1946-1961
