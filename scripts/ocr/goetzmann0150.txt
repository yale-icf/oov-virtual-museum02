# Transcription

**QUEEN.**

The lass who holds this Ticket, we declare,
Our Twelfth-Night Queen, and hail the happy Fair;
But she who gains a Twenty Thousand Prize
In the State Lott'ry, or whose chance shall rise
A noble Freehold from the City's Store,
May be content, should Fortune grant no more.
