## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** Nº 2980 (top center and in margins)
- **Red Wax Seal/Stamp:** Located on left side (appears to be official certification mark)
- **Purple Stamp:** Circular stamp visible on lower left portion
- **Signatures:** Three signature lines at bottom (Tesorero/Treasurer, Presidente/President, Secretario/Secretary) - signatures appear to be handwritten but partially illegible
- **Handwritten Entries:** Cursive handwriting in the title section and date fields, largely [illegible]
- **Decorative Elements:** Ornamental borders with floral/geometric patterns throughout the certificate
- **Central Vignette:** Circular emblem with mining/industrial imagery (crossed pickaxes and other mining tools)
