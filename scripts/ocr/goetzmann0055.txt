# Transcription

**Header (top right):**
Erecting Houfes of Office in N. Britain for Strangers & Travellors

**Main text (bottom):**
A usefull Project merrily advanc'd,
Sho chiefly by Tom-turdmen countencanc'd,
Design'd to fweeten the North British Nation,
And put Clofe Stool, and Bed Pan, out of fafhion.

**Notable markings:**
- Six red hearts arranged vertically on the left side (characteristic of a playing card suit)
- Engraved illustration in the center depicting a landscape scene with figures, buildings, water, and various activities
- Ornamental scrollwork at the top of the illustration frame
