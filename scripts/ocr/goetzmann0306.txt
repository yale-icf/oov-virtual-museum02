# Transcription

**BOSTON AND PROVIDENCE RAIL ROAD CORPORATION**

No. 122                                                                                                          Shares $100

This Certify that Haliburton Fales
is Entitled to Ten Shares
in the Capital Stock of the **BOSTON AND PROVIDENCE RAIL ROAD CORPORATION**, subject to the By-Laws of the Company. No transfer will be valid unless made by the Owner or duly Signed and recorded by the Treasurer or Office in New York duly authorized by the Directors for that purpose; this Certificate being first surrendered. The said Corporation having duly authorized the Treasurer and their said Officer in New York to authenticate certificates of Stock by their respective signatures, the Subscriber has hereunto set his hand this 15th day of **August 1839**

Jno. T. Torring Treasurer
