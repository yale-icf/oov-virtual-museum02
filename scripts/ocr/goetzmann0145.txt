# Transcription

**LIEUTENANT CHEERLY.**

That the Sailor loves fighting we very well know,
But he seldom succeeds without striking a blow;
Yet the battles of Fortune are quietly won,
Without either bloodshed, or firing a gun;
And, with no other noise than the Thousand Pounds
calling,
Your Prizes you get without grappling or hauling;
Which, when they're obtain'd, with their cargoes
complete,
You may sail when you please with next LOTTERY
FLEET.

The Lottery will be drawn 18th & 26th This Month.
