# Transcription of Reichsbanknote

**Original (German):**

Reichsbanknote

Ein Tausend Mark

zahle die Reichsbankhauptkasse in Berlin ohne Legitimationsprüfung dem Einlieferer dieser Banknote.

1000

Berlin, den 21. April 1910.
Reichsbankdirektorium

[Signatures]
[Illegible handwritten signatures of bank officials]

**English Translation:**

Imperial Bank Note

One Thousand Mark

The Reichsbank main cashier in Berlin will pay the bearer of this banknote without identity verification.

1000

Berlin, April 21, 1910.
Reichsbank Directorate

---

**Notable Markings:**

- **Serial Number:** No. 6125588N (printed in red on left and right margins)
- **Date:** 21. April 1910 (April 21, 1910)
- **Red circular seals/stamps:** Two identical imperial seals with eagle emblems, positioned lower left and lower right
- **Handwritten signatures:** Multiple signatures of Reichsbankdirektorium officials (text illegible)
- **Decorative borders:** Ornamental floral patterns frame all sides
- **Denomination:** 1000 (displayed prominently in ornate numerals in center)
