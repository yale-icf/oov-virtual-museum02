# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

(4)

dekt met een Papiere Ruytte, gedrukt het gemelde Keyzerlyck Decreet Zegel, en geteekend W. GRAVE VAN DIEPENBURGSTEIN. (Lagerislod) op Speciaal Bevel van zyn Gehehldigde Keyzerlyke en Catholique Majesteit, (en was geteekend)

CHRIST: v: WIRDENBURG.
DAV: HENRICH v: KOCH.

Getrouwelyk uyt het Hoogtuyts vertaald, Amsteldam dezen 27. September 1736.

J: BARELS.
Notaris Publieq.

Ik ondergeschreve WILLEM GIDEON DEUTZ, als daar toe gequafificeerdt synde door zyne Keyzerlyke Majesteyft, bekennen de somma van [*handwritten: Cooff Duytsen Guldens*]

in de bovenftaande Obligation vermeld, uyt handen van [*handwritten: A.M. Cox*] [*handwritten: Gezamenderf Synd*]

in Grof Zilver gelt, Ontfangen te hebben, belovende mits dezen in qualité voorgefteven, de intereften te betaalen, als mede de Kapitalen al te lofsen, alles in die manieren als in voorenftaande Obligation is vermeld, dus geteekent in Amiterdamm [*handwritten: 19 October 1736*]

[*Signature: Willem Gideon Deutz*]

NB. De Aflossinge van deze Obligation, zal met Grof Zilver Gelt; ter mijsten encklde Guidsens, gefchieden.

[Handwritten notations in margin, including dates and amounts]

---

**English Translation:**

(4)

covered with a Paper Ream, printed the aforesaid Imperial Decree Seal, and signed W. COUNT OF DIEPENBURGSTEIN. (Lagerislod) by Special Order of His Most Serene and Catholic Majesty, (and was signed)

CHRIST: v: WIRDENBURG.
DAV: HENRICH v: KOCH.

Faithfully translated from the High German, Amsterdam this 27th September 1736.

J: BARELS.
Notary Public.

I, the undersigned WILLEM GIDEON DEUTZ, as thereby duly qualified by His Imperial Majesty, acknowledge the sum of [*One Thousand Guilders*]

mentioned in the above Obligation, received from the hands of [*A.M. Cox*] [*Combined Funds*]

in Gross Silver currency, acknowledging receipt thereof, promising hereby in the capacity aforementioned, to pay the interests, and also to redeem the Capital in full, all in the manner as in the foregoing Obligation is stipulated, thus signed in Amsterdam [*19th October 1736*]

[*Signature: Willem Gideon Deutz*]

NB. The Redemption of this Obligation shall be in Gross Silver currency; at the most in Guilders, to be completed.
