# Grand Russian Railway Company 3% Bond – Coupon Sheet

**Original (Russian):**

ГЛАВНОЕ ОБЩЕСТВО РОССИЙСКИХ ЖЕЛЕЗНЫХ ДОРОГ

[Header repeated across document]
3% облигация ГЛАВНОГО ОБЩЕСТВА РОССИЙСКИХ ЖЕЛЕЗНЫХ ДОРОГ
Третьего выпуска 1861 года.

[Multiple coupon sections arranged in grid, each containing:]

К облигации [No. 210979] от 3% облигации
ГЛАВНОГО ОБЩЕСТВА РОССИЙСКИХ ЖЕЛЕЗНЫХ ДОРОГ
Третьего выпуска 1861 года.

[Each coupon section contains text regarding:]
- Semi-annual interest payment (полугодовой процент)

- Signature lines and official stamps
- Serial markings

[Coupons are numbered sequentially and repeat throughout the page in organized rows]

GRAND SOCIETY OF RUSSIAN RAILWAYS

Third Emission of 1861.

[Multiple coupon sections, each containing:]

Coupon [No. 210979] of 3% Bond

[Each coupon section contains:]

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: №210979 (appears on all coupons)
- Interest rate: 3% (repeated throughout)
- Date: 1861 (Third Emission/Третьего выпуска)
- Multiple official stamps and signature lines on each coupon section
- Green printed background with black text
- Organized grid layout of approximately 18 individual coupon sections visible
