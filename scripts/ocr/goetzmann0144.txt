# Transcription

**VULCAN.**

WHO's this with his budget? 'Tis Vulcan—I'll
A wager he's come for a slice of Twelfth Cake:
He once forg'd the thunder-bolts hurl'd by great
Jove,
And marry'd the prostitute Goddess of Love;
Expos'd in a net, once with Mars did he link her,
Then knock'd up his shop, and now strolls as a
tinker;
Though Fortune he courts in the Lott'ry, they say,
And hopes for a Prize on next Valentine's Day.
