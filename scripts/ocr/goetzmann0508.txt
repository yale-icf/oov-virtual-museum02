## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 11639
- **Signatures:** Two handwritten signatures (Chinese and Dutch officials)
- **Seals:** Two red Chinese character seals/stamps
- **Decorative elements:** Ornate turquoise and cream colored border with geometric and floral medallions; central vignette of a bridge/railway structure
- **Currency:** Dutch Guilders (f. 1000)
- **Issue date:** 1923
- **Railway:** Lung-Tsing-U-Hai (Longhai) Railway
