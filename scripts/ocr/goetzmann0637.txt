# Transcription

**Original:**

N°. 2657

REPÚBLICA                                                                                    BOLIVIANA.

FONDO PÚBLICO.

INSTITUIDO POR LA LEY DE 1° DE JUNIO DEL OCHOCIENTOS CUARENTA Y TRES.

VALE SEIS PESOS

A TERCEO EN CUATRIMESTRES POR TERCAS PARTES.

CAPITAL CIEN PESOS.

Ministro de la Corte Suprema,                    Sucre á 1° de Abril de 1846
[signature - appears to be Avelino Vizcarra]
                                                 El Ministro de Hacienda.
El Presidente del Consejo.                       [signature - illegible]
[signature - illegible]

Al C° Venancio Nair

A la orden del C° Vidalicio Nair                 Anotado en el folio n° 70

---

**English Translation:**

No. 2657

REPUBLIC                                                                                    OF BOLIVIA.

PUBLIC FUND.

INSTITUTED BY THE LAW OF JUNE 1st, EIGHTEEN HUNDRED FORTY-THREE.

CERTIFICATE FOR SIX PESOS

PAYABLE IN INSTALLMENTS IN FOUR-MONTH PERIODS BY THIRDS.

CAPITAL ONE HUNDRED PESOS.

Minister of the Supreme Court,                   Sucre, April 1st, 1846
[signature]
                                                 The Minister of Finance.
The President of the Council.                    [signature]
[signature]

To C° Venancio Nair

By order of C° Vidalicio Nair                    Annotated in register book No. 70

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **N°. 2657** (top center)
- Three handwritten signatures from government officials
- Spiral binding visible on left edge
- Handwritten endorsement names: "Venancio Nair" and "Vidalicio Nair"
- Registry notation: "folio n° 70"
- Date: April 1, 1846 (Sucre, Bolivia)
