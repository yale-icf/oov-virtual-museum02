## Notable markings:

- **Serial number:** №82719 (appears on main certificate and all six coupons)
- **Multiple official seals:** Four circular seals with "200 RУБЛЕЙ" / "200 ROUBLES" in corners
- **Ornamental border:** Detailed decorative frame throughout
- **Multiple handwritten signatures** (illegible) from various officials
- **Octagonal stamp/seal** (center-top) with Cyrillic text
- **Bank emblem:** M.Ф. (monogram) in octagonal frame
- **Dates on coupons:** 1912, 1913, 1914, 1917, 1918, 1919 (indicating payment schedule for dividends)
- **Archive marking:** "1920?" in top right corner (possibly indicating cataloging date)
- **Blue oval library/archive stamp** on coupon section
