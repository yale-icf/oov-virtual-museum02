## English translation:

**Left Column:**

£ A.                No. 8021.

**Certificate.**

The bearer of this
is entitled to a **Capital** of
**ONE THOUSAND ROUBLES,**
**IN ASSIGNATIONS,**

Registration, bearing interest at 6%, in the Savings Book
of the Public Debt of the Imperial
Commission of Amortization at St. Petersburg,
which fund, represented by this Certificate,
is held in custody at the

General Administration Office in Amsterdam,

under the direction of:

STADNITSKI & VAN HEUKELOM,
JACOB VAN BEECK VOLLENHOVEN,
SAMUEL & DAVID SAPORTAS,
LAMAISON & BOUWER,
JOHANNES SAMUEL WURFBAIN,
HENDRIK OYENS & ZOON

and

DE LANOY & BURLAGE.

And the bearer of this, according to notice
of the 30th June 1824 issued, has the right to
claim the aforementioned fund at any time
in return for surrender of this Certificate
with the attachment of the not yet due
**Coupons.**

---

**Right Column:**

**Certificate.**

The Bearer

has a right to a **Capital Sum of**
**ONE THOUSAND ROUBLES,**
**IN ASSIGNATIONS,**

Registrations, bearing interest at 6%, in the Grand
**Book** of the Public Debt of the Imperial
Commission of Amortization at St. Petersburg,
of which the title is deposited at the

General Administration Office, located in Amsterdam,

under the direction of:

STADNITSKI & VAN HEUKELOM,
JACOB VAN BEECK VOLLENHOVEN,
SAMUEL & DAVID SAPORTAS,
LAMAISON & BOUWER,
JOHANNES SAMUEL WURFBAIN,
HENDRIK OYENS & SONS

and

DE LANOY & BURLAGE.

According to the prospectus published on the 30th June
1824, the Bearer may claim at any
time the original fund mentioned above,
against return of the present **Certificate**
accompanied by its non-due **Coupons**.

---

**Signature block:**

Amsterdam, the 31st December 1823

[Three signatures]
[Directors.]

---

**Bottom left:**
CAPITAL: Rl 1000. in Assignation.
Issued twelve Half-yearly Coupon Certificates,
signed by [signature]

**Bottom right:**
CAPITAL: Rl 1000. in Assignations.
Delivered twelve Six-Month Interest Coupons,
signed by [signature]

---
