# Imperial Russian Government Bond Talon - Transcription

## Original Text (Russian and French):

**Header (Russian):**
ИМПЕРАТОРСКОЕ РОССИЙСКОЕ ПРАВИТЕЛЬСТВО.
Талон к 3³/₁₀% конверсионной облигации в 150 рублей.

**Header (French):**
GOUVERNEMENT IMPÉRIAL DE RUSSIE.
Talon de l'Obligation de conversion 3³/₁₀% de 150 rbls.=324 reichsmark=400 francs=15 £ 17 shilll.=192 flor. des Pays-Bas.

---

**Individual Coupon Sections (appearing on numbered coupons 38-48):**

Each coupon follows this pattern:

**Russian:**
ИМПЕРАТОРСКОЕ РОССИЙСКОЕ ПРАВИТЕЛЬСТВО. [NUMBER]
[NUMBER]-й купон к 3³/₁₀% конверсионной облигац. №097530
2/15 [Month] 19[XX] года уплачивается по Конторсис в
отделениях Государственного Банка 2 руб. 85 коп.

**French:**
GOUVERNEMENT IMPÉRIAL DE RUSSIE.
Coupon de l'Obligation de conversion 3³/₁₀%, payable
2/15 [Janvier/Juillet] 19[XX], à raison de 6 mark 15½ pl.=7 fr. 60 c.=
6 shilll.=3 fl. 64½ c. ou quittances désignées sur cette obligation.

Valable pendant dix ans.
[Directeur signature]

## English Translation:

**Header:**
IMPERIAL RUSSIAN GOVERNMENT.
Talon for 3.75% Conversion Bond of 150 Rubles.

---

**Individual Coupon Pattern:**

IMPERIAL RUSSIAN GOVERNMENT. [NUMBER]
[NUMBER]-th coupon for 3.75% conversion bond No. 097530
On [date] 19[XX] payable at offices in
branches of the State Bank at 2 rubles 85 kopecks.

IMPERIAL GOVERNMENT OF RUSSIA.
Coupon of the 3.75% Conversion Bond, payable
[date] 19[XX], at the rate of 6 marks 15½ pfennigs = 7 francs 60 centimes =
6 shillings = 3 florins 64½ centimes, or receipts designated on this obligation.

Valid for ten years.
[Director's signature]

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number**: №097530 (appears on all coupons)
- **Denomination equivalents**: 324 reichsmarks = 400 francs = 15 pounds 17 shillings = 192 Dutch florins
- **Signature**: Director's signature (appears as "Директор" in Cyrillic) on each coupon
- **Bilingual format**: All text presented in both Russian (Cyrillic) and French
- **Date range**: Coupons issued from 1917-1922, indicating post-revolutionary period
- **Payment amounts**: 2 rubles 85 kopecks per coupon (Russian valuation) or equivalent in Western currencies
