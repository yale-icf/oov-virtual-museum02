# Transcription: Banque Industrielle de Chine Stock Certificate

## Original (French and Chinese):

**BANQUE INDUSTRIELLE DE CHINE**
中国实业银行

SOCIÉTÉ ANONYME

CAPITAL SOCIAL: 45,000,000 DE FRANCS
DIVISÉ EN 90,000 ACTIONS DE 500 FRANCS
DONT 87,000 ACTIONS ORDINAIRES & 3,000 ACTIONS DE FONDATEUR

Statuts déposés suivant acte reçu par Me LAZARE & Notaires à Paris
tous deux Notaires à Paris, le 13 Mars 1913

SIÈGE SOCIAL À PARIS

**ACTION ORDINAIRE DE 500 FRANCS**
AU PORTEUR
ENTIÈREMENT LIBÉRÉE

N° 47,221

Un Administrateur                          Un Administrateur
[Signature - illegible]                    [Signature - appears to read "de Talleyrand" or similar]

[Text in Chinese characters - appears to be the same information in Chinese, detailing the bank's organization and terms]

## English Translation:

**INDUSTRIAL BANK OF CHINA**

JOINT-STOCK COMPANY

CAPITAL: 45,000,000 FRANCS
DIVIDED INTO 90,000 SHARES OF 500 FRANCS
OF WHICH 87,000 ORDINARY SHARES & 3,000 FOUNDER SHARES

Articles filed according to deed received by Me LAZARE & Notaries in Paris
both Notaries in Paris, March 13, 1913

HEAD OFFICE IN PARIS

**ORDINARY SHARE OF 500 FRANCS**
TO BEARER
FULLY PAID

No. 47,221

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** 47,221
- **Signatures:** Two administrator signatures (one illegible, one partially legible)
- **Seals:** Red circular seal of "Banque Industrielle de Chine" at bottom center
- **Stamp:** Official stamp/seal in upper right corner
- **Additional markings:** Handwritten notations in blue ink
- **Decorative elements:** Ornate Asian-inspired architectural borders featuring pagodas, dragons, and traditional Chinese design motifs; landscape engraving at bottom showing industrial scene with mountains, bridge, and railway
- **Printer credit:** "Charles Skipper & East" (bottom)
