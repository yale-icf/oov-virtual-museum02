# Transcription

**Original:**

N° 2530.

ACCION DE LA COMPAÑIA.

[Decorative text in cursive script - partially legible:]
Conocen todos los Veedores[?] y demas personas a quien tocare, que en esta Accion de quinientos Escudos de à quinze Reales de Vellon, que ha entregado en la Cava de la Compania, Dios los Directores de ella, le otorgamos obligacion de contribuir las Cantidades que de las Ganancias se repartieren, à quien se presentare con ella, à los tiempos que se señalaren. Y para cumplido effi mandamos dar efta Declaracion à su favor, firmada por [?]Señores, sellada con el Sello de la Compania, y refrendada por el Secretario de ella. En Madrid, à primero de Junio de mil setecientos y cincq[?]y dos.

[Signatures and marks in cursive - largely illegible]

Con Acuerdo
[Additional signatures]

---

**English Translation:**

No. 2530.

SHARE OF THE COMPANY.

Know all those who view [this document] and other persons to whom it may concern, that in this Share of five hundred Escudos of fifteen Reales de Vellon, which has been delivered to the Treasury of the Company, we the Directors of it grant the obligation to contribute the quantities from the Profits that shall be distributed, to whoever presents it, at the times that shall be designated. And in order to fulfill this we order this Declaration to be given in his favor, signed by [the] Lords, sealed with the Seal of the Company, and countersigned by its Secretary. In Madrid, on the first of June of one thousand seven hundred and fifty-two.

---

**Notable markings:**

- **Serial number:** No. 2530
- **Royal seal:** Large gold/tan wax seal with scalloped edges bearing the royal arms of Guipuzcoa
- **Decorative elements:** Ornate copper engraving borders on all four sides featuring botanical designs, cherubs, warriors' weapons, and indigenous figures with bow and arrow
- **Date:** 1 June 1752
- **Multiple signatures:** Several handwritten signatures in cursive script at bottom (largely illegible)
- **Text:** Printed on vellum with manuscript additions
