# Transcription

**Original:**

N°7027m

[Coat of arms of Hamburg with two lions and castle]

Leib-Renten-Lotterie der Stadt Hamburg,
Anno 1773 den 23 April publicirtet.

Zeiger dieses hat für Ein Loos in dieser Leib-Renten-Lotterie unter obbemerckter Nummer und mit der Devise

Zwey Hundert Mark Banco, welche heute dato durch

an Löbl. Cämmererey Lotterie-Conto in Banco abgeschrieben worden, bezahlet.
Hamburg, den                          Anno 1773.

---

**English translation:**

No. 7027m

[Coat of arms of Hamburg with two lions and castle]

Life Annuity Lottery of the City of Hamburg,
Published April 23, Anno 1773.

The bearer hereof has for one lot in this Life Annuity Lottery under the above-noted number and with the motto

Two Hundred Mark Banco, which today has been

written off to the Honorable Chamber of Commerce Lottery Account in Banco, paid.
Hamburg, on                          Anno 1773.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Document number: **N°7027m** (upper left)
- Hamburg city coat of arms (centered at top)
- Date: **April 23, 1773** (publication date and closing date)
- Face value: **200 Mark Banco**
- Blank spaces for completion date and official signatures/seals (not filled in on this specimen)
