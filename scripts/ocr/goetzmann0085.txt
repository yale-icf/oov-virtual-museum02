# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Ik ben beschermd, Heer
Daar ieder kermd.

'k Ben voor de Zuid en West hun
windgeblaas beschut
Verwijl een laauwe Goest 't Heel Al
helpt in den dut.

**English translation:**

I am protected, Sir,
While everyone complains.

I am sheltered from the South and West winds' blast
While a lukewarm breeze helps the whole [thing] slumber.

---

**Notable markings:**

This is part of the "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Chart) playing cards series from *Het Groote Tafereel* (The Great Theatre). The image depicts a figure in 17th-century Dutch clothing holding diamond-shaped wind cards, dressed as the King of Spades (Heer/Lord). The illustration uses period engraving technique with crosshatching and line work typical of Dutch satirical prints.
