# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

De Directeuren van de Generale Keijserlijcke Indische Compagnie ordoneren aan Hueren Cassier Jan Baptist Cogels junior om Contant te ontvangen van Anna Rosalia De Bruijn van Holst De Somme van twee hondert—8 Gylden Wisselgelt voor het eerste payement van vijfentwintig ten hondert van eene actie jder van eenduijsent gyldens wisselgelt Soo den selven Anna Rosalia De Bruijn van Holst in het Capitaal der gewijde Compagnie zich heeft geinteressaert Stellende aan den Voet deser Syne quittantie aller op de Condition in het Octroy breeder vermelt datum in Antwerpen den 16. Augusty 1723.

---

**English translation:**

The Directors of the General Imperial East India Company order their Cashier Jan Baptist Cogels junior to receive in cash from Anna Rosalia De Bruijn van Holst the sum of two hundred [and] 50 Gulden exchange money for the first payment of twenty-five per hundred of one share of one thousand gulden exchange money, as the said Anna Rosalia De Bruijn van Holst has interested herself in the Capital of the aforesaid Company, hereupon presenting his receipt for all, upon the conditions more broadly mentioned in the Charter, dated in Antwerp the 16th of August 1723.

Received from
The sum of
Gulden exchange money for the first
Payment of the aforesaid
share [illegible]

**Notable markings:**

- Elaborate engraved seal/coat of arms of the Generale Keijserlijcke Indische Compagnie (top left), featuring an imperial double-headed eagle and crown
- Decorative ornamental line divider on right margin
- Handwritten manuscript in formal 18th-century Dutch script
- Date: Antwerp, August 16, 1723
