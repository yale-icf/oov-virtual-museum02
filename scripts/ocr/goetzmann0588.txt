**English Translation:**

EMPIRE OF MEXICO
1865                                                                                                1865

EXTERNAL PUBLIC DEBT

Obligation of 500 francs.
SECOND SERIES
Number 1,085,428

The Mexican Government commits itself to pay

TO THE BEARER

the annual interest on demand, at the rate of 5 percent francs of this Obligation, in Paris, in the months of April and October of each year, and to return the capital in Paris, by the holder's deadline per the Commission of Treasury of France, in 1890, according to the following conditions:

The bonds shall be numbered in Paris, [presented to the public] by the Commission of Treasury of France, in accordance with what was agreed with the Mexican Government.
