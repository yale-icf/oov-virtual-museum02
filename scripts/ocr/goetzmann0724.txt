## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** Series 317, No. 049
- **Date:** 11 maggio 1900 (11 May 1900)
- **Signatures:** Multiple official signatures present on left page (Mayor/Sindaco and other officials)
- **Ornamental borders:** Elaborate decorative scrollwork and vignettes throughout
- **Watermark/Design elements:** Central allegorical figures and heraldic shields
- **Color:** Green ink on light paper throughout
- **Condition:** Historical fold lines visible; overall legible
