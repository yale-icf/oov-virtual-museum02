# Transcription

**Header (top left):** Cecilia 300

**Header (top center):** Dollars, at [?] Livres Tournois, Payable Number 131

**Header (top right):** [unclear] 1776

---

**Main Text:**

United States of AMERICA, 9 Day of October 1776

AT Thirty Days Sight of this Third Bill, First, Second and Fourth not paid, pay to Annie Brown or Order, Three Hundred Dollars, in Fifteen Hundred Livres Tournois, for Interest due on Money borrowed by the United States.

To the Commissioner or Commissioners of the
United States of America, at Paris.

Countersigned, Wm Smith
DF Commissioner of the Continental Loan Office in the
State of Pennsylvania.

---

**Signature (right side):** H. Atkinson
(Treasurer of Loans)

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **131** (top right and body of document)
- Reference number: **300** (top left)
- Date: **October 9, 1776**
- Handwritten signatures: "Wm Smith" (countersignature) and "H. Atkinson" (Treasurer signature)
- Ornamental borders on left and right edges
- Handwritten endorsements and notations throughout (partially legible)
- Currency notation in both dollars and Livres Tournois
