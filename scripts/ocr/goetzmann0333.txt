# Transcription of Stock Certificate

**Original (Spanish):**

COMPAÑÍA SALITRERA DE TARAPACÁ Y ANTOFAGASTA

CAPITAL AUTORIZADO $ 208.247.800 DIVIDIDO EN 4.164.956 ACCIONES DE VALOR NOMINAL DE CINCUENTA PESOS CADA UNA

CONSTITUIDA MEDIANTE ESCRITURA PÚBLICA EXTENDIDA ANTE EL NOTARIO DE SANTIAGO DE CHILE DON JAVIER ECHEVERRIA VIAL Y AUTORIZADA POR DECRETO SUPREMO Nº 2938 DE 11 DE JULIO DE 1904

El presente Título acredita que el Portador es dueño de Diez Acciones de la Compañía Salitrera de Tarapacá y Antofagasta, valuadas cada una totalmente pagadas.

El Portador de este título, que es dueño de las acciones que representa, esta obligado a acatar la Ley de la Constitución y Estatutos aprobados Decreto y de la Junta General de Accionistas.

Santiago, 30 ABR. 1937

[Signatures present]

Compañía Salitrera de Tarapacá y Antofagasta

---

**English Translation:**

SALTPETER COMPANY OF TARAPACÁ AND ANTOFAGASTA

AUTHORIZED CAPITAL $208,247,800 DIVIDED INTO 4,164,956 SHARES OF NOMINAL VALUE OF FIFTY PESOS EACH

ESTABLISHED BY PUBLIC DEED EXTENDED BEFORE THE NOTARY OF SANTIAGO OF CHILE DON JAVIER ECHEVERRIA VIAL AND AUTHORIZED BY SUPREME DECREE No. 2938 OF 11 JULY 1904

This present Title certifies that the Holder is owner of Ten Shares of the Saltpeter Company of Tarapacá and Antofagasta, valued each one fully paid.

The Holder of this title, who is owner of the shares it represents, is obliged to comply with the Law of the Constitution and Statutes approved by Decree and the General Shareholders' Meeting.

Santiago, 30 APR. 1937

---

**Notable Markings:**

- **Certificate Number:** X 30095 (printed in center)
- **Denomination:** 10 Shares (Diez Acciones) at 50 pesos each
- **Date of Issue:** April 30, 1937 (30 ABR. 1937)
- **Serial Numbers:** Multiple coupon stamps with "N.A 30095" repeated throughout the margin
- **Signatures:** Two signatures present on the certificate (lower portion)
- **Visual Elements:** Decorative purple border with ornamental designs; central engraving of a saltpeter worker; dividend coupon sheet attached with numbered sections
