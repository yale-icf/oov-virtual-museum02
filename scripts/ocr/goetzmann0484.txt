# Transcription

No. 2435 RECEIVED of OLIVER WOLCOTT, Comptroller of the Public Accounts, One pound fifteen shillings and three pence Lawful Money, in Certificates; being for the Interest on 4 State Notes, amounting to £29. 6. 11¼ computed to the first of February. A. D. 1789

Comptroller's-Office, Hartford, April 4th 1789 for John Douglas
£/s/ 15. 3                                                                     y Stephen Crosby
