# REPUBLIC OF NEW GRANADA: DEFERRED BOND

**Letter A N° 7376**
**£100 Sterling**
**DEFERRED**

---

## ENGLISH TEXT (Left Column):

I, the undersigned Chargé d'Affaires of the Republic of New Granada at the Court of London, in pursuance of the Agreement between the Secretary of Finance of the said Republic and the Agents of the Bondholders of the Foreign Debt of Colombia, signed at Bogotá on the 15th January, 1845, of which a copy is subjoined, and in conformity with the Instructions received from my Government, as fiscal Commissioner in London, do hereby declare that the said Republic is indebted to the Bearer hereof in the Sum of One Hundred Pounds Sterling, and hereby, in the most solemn manner, bind the said Republic to pay the same, and the interest thereon, according to the tenor of the said Agreement. The interest of the said Sum of One Hundred Pounds is to be paid Half-yearly, on the first day of June and the first day of December in each year, the first Half-yearly Dividend falling due on 1st December, 1861, according to the rates specified in the Fourth Article of the said Agreement, at the Counting-house of Messrs. Barings, Bevan, Binneys and Co., in London, on delivery of the Warrants or Coupons attached hereto. The Government of New Granada engages to deliver additional Deferred Warrants to the Holder hereof, when those attached shall have been paid; and so from time to time until the Principal of the Bond shall have been redeemed, or payment thereof tendered by the said Government.

### TRANSLATION.

We the undersigned JUAN CLIMACO ORDÓÑEZ, Secretary of the Finance Department of the Government of NEW GRANADA, in the name of the said Government; and POWLES, ILLINGWORTH, WILLSON and CO., as Agents of the Bondholders of the Debt of Colombia, have agreed to the following proposals:—

1. New Granada acknowledges as her Debt Fifty per Cent. of the Loans contracted by the former Republic of Colombia in 1822 and 1824, which Debt had been repudiated by the Government of Venezuela as being consequently cancelled as respects on the half of the value, and New Granada assumes the other half upon condition which are set forth hereinafter.

2. The Bonds which New Granada may issue, in conformity with the following articles, will bear Interest at One per Cent per annum for the first four years, which Interest shall be loaned upon itself; and in each subsequent year, will it transfer at One per Cent designated for four years.

3. In payment of the Interest due and not paid, and of that which shall become payable until the Bond shall have been paid off, New Granada agrees to issue the rate of Four per Cent, of the Principal, as that the Bonds now issued in its name shall be developed for.

4. These last-mentioned Bonds shall not bear Interest during the first six years after their issue; but, in the seventh year, they shall bear One per Cent, which shall be increased by one eighth part in each of the following years, until it reaches Three per Cent, which is the maximum rate of Interest.

5. The Interest on the New Granada Bonds that may be issued in conformity with the stipulation of Article 3, shall be paid in London on the first and fifteenth of June, concurrently eight hundred and forty-five, and to this account may be paid the Government Deferred Bonds of former loans of Powles, Heritors, of London.

6. The Bonds of both Debts shall be redeemed, on the payment of one percent. of the amount of the nominal value of the said property at the proper period of the foreign debt.

7. The net produce of the Tobacco Revenue and the half of the produce of the Customs Revenue, are chargeable for the execution of this contract.

8. These Proposals shall bind the Government of New Granada after the ratification thereof by the Government of New Granada shall have been issued in the condition which have except that the First shall draw interest from the date hereof, and none of the Credits shall not affect thereof, this Agreement shall be null and void of express lich portion.

In faith whereof we sign these presents at Bogotá, on the fifteenth of January, one thousand eight hundred and forty-five.

JUAN CLIMACO ORDÓÑEZ,
POWLES, ILLINGWORTH, WILLSON, and CO.

[Seal A Copy]
The Secretary of Finance, J. C. ORDÓÑEZ.

**Dated in London, this First day of June, 1845**

## SPANISH TEXT (Right Column):

### ORIGINAL:

Los infrascriptos, á saber, JUAN CLIMACO ORDÓÑEZ, Secretario de Hacienda del Gobierno de NUEVA GRANADA, á nombre de dicho Gobierno; y POWLES, ILLINGWORTH, WILLSON i Cª, como Agentes de los Tenedores de valores de la Deuda de Colombia, hemos convenido en las siguientes proposiciones:

1º. La Nueva Granada reconoce como deuda suya cincuenta por ciento de los empréstitos que contrató la República de Colombia en 1822 y 1824; cuya Deuda habia sido repudiada por el Gobierno de Venezuela como consiguientemente cancelada respecto de la mitad de su valor, y toma la Nueva Granada la otra mitad bajo las condiciones que se expresan enseguida.

2º. Los obligaciones que emita la Nueva Granada en conformidad con el artículo siguiente, devengará interes al uno por ciento anual durante los primeros años; el cual interés se aumentará en un cuarto por ciento en cada uno de los años siguientes, hasta que llegue al tres por ciento, que es el máximum de interés.

3º. En pago de los intereses vencidos i no pagados, y de los que vayan devengándose subsecuentemente hasta que se redima esta Deuda, se obligation New Granada á emitir obligaciones al cuatro por ciento del Principal, en la forma que se expresará enseguida.

4º. Estas billetes no devengará interes alguno en los diez i seis primeros años de su emisión. Pero en el diez i seis año devengará el uno por ciento, que se aumentará en un octavo por ciento en cada uno de los años siguientes, hasta que llegue al tres por ciento, que es el máximum de interés en la Nueva Granada.

5º. El pago de los intereses de las obligaciones Granadinas que se emitan según lo estipulado en el artículo 3º se hará en Londres por semestres vencidos; verificándose el pago del primer semestre desde primero de Junio de mil ochocientos cuarenta i cinco, i en las oficinas que se designe en Londres para esto, tomando por base la inversión de los fondos que se destinen al pago de esta obligación.

6º. Los valores de ambas Deudas serán admitidos á la par en pago de los derechos de Aduana que se causen después de remitida esta Deuda, destinada por los tesoreros á Amortización de la misma.

7º. El producto neto de la Renta del Tabaco i la mitad del Producto de los Aduanas, quedan afectos al cumplimiento de este contrato.

8º. Estas proposiciones obligarán al Gobierno de la Nueva Granada desde que este fuere santificada por los interesados, al afecto d Gobierno Granadino dado inmediatamente sus ordenes para que se observe las disposiciones que las suscriben se cumpla en todas sus partes; i en caso de que alguno de los suscriptores falta al cumplimiento de lo que en este contrato se conviene, Sí los suscriptores se las suscriben, todo vida convenio será salo i ninguno sobre respecto de ambas partes.

En fé de lo cual firmamos el presente en Bogotá á quince de Enero de mil ochocientos cuarenta i cinco.

JUAN CLIMACO ORDÓÑEZ
POWLES, ILLINGWORTH, WILLSON i Cª

En Copia,
El Secretario de Hacienda, J. C. ORDÓÑEZ.
