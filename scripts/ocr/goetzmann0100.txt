# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Que donne moiun sou, zo zult ge aanstonias aanschouwen
Mat grafstoé, dat de tijdt voor de Acheprins zal bouwen.

**English translation:**

"Who gives me a penny, so shall you soon behold
A monument, which time for the Archduke shall build."

**Notable markings:**

- Card number "10" in top right corner
- Spade suit symbol (♠) in top right
- Engraved illustration showing a standing male figure in 17th-century dress holding a bag/pouch, wearing a cap and long coat
- Figure is gesturing with one hand raised
- Landscape background with ground vegetation and distant terrain
- This appears to be from "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Cards), a Dutch satirical playing card series from "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Spectacle)
