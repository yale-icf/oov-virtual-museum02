---

## English Translation:

**Top Banner:**
"Republic of China Ministry of Finance Third National Treasury Bond"

**Center Denomination:**
"Five Yuan"

**Document Purpose:**
This is a government treasury bond (國庫券) for five yuan, issued as part of the Third National Treasury Bond series in Year 15 of the Republic of China (1926).

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** No 0439850 (in red text at bottom)
- **Red Seals:** Two small red official seals visible in left margin of text blocks
- **Decorative Elements:** Ornamental purple borders throughout, floral/scrollwork designs in corners
- **Denomination markers:** "伍圓" (5 Yuan) appears in all four corner sections within decorative frames
- **Color:** Printed entirely in purple/magenta on cream/beige paper
