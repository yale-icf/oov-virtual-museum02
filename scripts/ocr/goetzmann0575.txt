# Transcription of Chinese Republic Industrial Loan Document

## Original (French):

**EMPRUNT INDUSTRIEL DE LA REPUBLIQUE CHINOISE**

**5% OR 1914**

**OBLIGATION N° [varying numbers, e.g., 026,111]**

**500 FRANCS AU PORTEUR**

[Repeated across multiple bond certificates in sheet format]

Each certificate contains:
- Header: "EMPRUNT INDUSTRIEL DE LA REPUBLIQUE CHINOISE" / "5% OR 1914"
- Text: "OBLIGATION N°" followed by serial number
- Denomination: "500 FRANCS AU PORTEUR"
- Interest rate notation: "5%" 
- Date references: Various dates including "1er" (1st) with months
- Serial numbers visible: 026,111 (repeated across multiple coupons)
- Coupon numbers visible: ranging from approximately 26-70+ (indicating multiple interest payment coupons)
- Text fragments: "DE MARS" (March), "SEPTEMBRE" (September), "OCTOBRE" (October)

## English Translation:

**INDUSTRIAL LOAN OF THE CHINESE REPUBLIC**

**5% GOLD 1914**

**BOND No. [varying numbers]**

**500 FRANCS TO BEARER**

The document represents a sheet of bearer bonds with attached interest coupons, issued by the Chinese Republic in 1914, denominated in 500 French francs each, bearing 5% interest payable in gold.

## Notable markings:

- Multiple serial numbers printed in red ink: **026,111** (visible throughout)
- Coupon numbers in oval frames
- Interest payment dates marked with months (March, September, October)
- Professional engraving typical of early 20th-century financial securities
- Decorative borders and official formatting characteristic of period bonds
