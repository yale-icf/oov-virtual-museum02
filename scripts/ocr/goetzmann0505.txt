# Transcription

Cash for 120 Dollars at five Livres Tournois Dollar Hunt. N°43

United States of AMERICA, 1st Day of October 1780

AT Thirty Days Sight of this First, Second, Third and Fourth and paid, pay to Jonathan D. Sergeant or Order, One Hundred and Twenty Dollars, in Six Hundred Livres Tournois, for Interest due on Money borrowed by the United States.

To the Commissioner or Commissioners of the
United States of America, at Paris.

H. Atkinson
(Treas. of Loans)

Countersigned Wm. Smith
Commissioner of the Loan-Office in the
State of Pennsylvania

---
