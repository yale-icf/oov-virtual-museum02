# VOOR DEN ARMEN

**Original:**

Num. 110. — Ik ondergeschrevene, Ontvanger van de STEDELKE BELASTING HAARLEM, geurresteerd den 3de April 1804, bekenne in die qualiteit, uit krachte der speciale qualificatie van den RAAD dezer Stad, bij Resolutie van den 5de June 1804 daartoe verleend, ontvangen te hebben van

de Somma van VYF-HONDERD GULDENS, geliggt op Infveft tegen den Penning 40. in 't jaar, te betalen over één Jaar na dato dezes, tot de effectueele Aflosing der Hoofdsomme toe, en tot meerdere verzekering is deeze bij STEDTS Wedouwdert dezer Stad gesgrocëerd en geteekend.
In oircode deze geteekend den eersten January 1805.

Capitaal f. 500 - - -
In Intresse = f.2-50-

Hierla afgeaam TEENEN
Striks COUPONS cum No. 1.
tot 16s, ledet groef f. 12-10-:
beginnende primo January 1806: Als izo de voorichteere Somma van VYF-HONDERD GULDENS, op Intrest geliggt op Penning 40. in 't jaar, aan dienste der Armen, aen de Stad verkrekt is: Z00 is 'T, dat wy 300 af goudert der Sad Haarlem / als daartoe special gequalificeerd, bij Resolutie van den RAAD, in dato den 14de December 1804, tot meerdere verzekering van de betaling van het vooricheve Capitaal en Intresfen, tot de effectueele Aflosling toe, verbonden hebben en verbinden bij dezen, ten behoeve van den voornoemden

alle de Stads-Inkomsten en Goederen, en speciaal de Inkomsten der voormalige GILDENS ingevolghe het 56e Articul der bovengemelde Publicate.

[Signature] Uting van Berthuall & S: Druijsteeÿn

In kennisse van my,

[Signature] Willm Spallang?

[Handwritten annotations below:]
1822 in August No Coupons
[unclear dates and amounts - 1822-1833 in Notaken?]
[unclear] in Januari van 1839-1853 Ingesloten
[unclear] 1854-1860[?]
[unclear] Munchers in Januari opgelegd apo Bladen

---

**English translation:**

No. 110. — I, the undersigned, Receiver of the CITY TAX of HAARLEM, appointed on the 3rd of April 1804, acknowledge in that capacity, by authority of the special qualification of the COUNCIL of this City, by Resolution of the 5th of June 1804 granted therefor, to have received

the Sum of FIVE HUNDRED GUILDERS, placed at interest at the rate of 40 Pennies per year, to be paid in full after one year from the date hereof, until the effectual Redemption of the Principal Sum, and for further security this is registered and signed by the CITY [authority] of this City.
Executed in duplicate this first day of January 1805.

Capital f. 500
Annual Interest = f. 12.50

Hereupon affixed COUPONS
Strict COUPONS cum No. 1.
to 16s, each coupon f. 12.10:
beginning primo January 1806: Now, as the aforesaid Sum of FIVE HUNDRED GUILDERS, placed at interest at 40 Pennies per year, in the service of the Poor, has been received by the City: SO it is, that we, [the official authority] of the City of Haarlem / being thereto specially qualified, by Resolution of the COUNCIL, dated the 14th of December 1804, for further security of the payment of the aforementioned Principal and Interest, until the effectual Redemption, bound ourselves and bind ourselves by these presents, for the benefit of the aforesaid

all the City-Revenues and Goods, and especially the Revenues of the former GUILDS in accordance with the 56th Article of the aforementioned Publication.

[Signature] [unclear]

In witness whereof,

[Signature] [unclear]
