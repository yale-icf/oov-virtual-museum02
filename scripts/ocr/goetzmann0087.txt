# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Knegt

't Papier zo duur
Verdrookt door
't ruin'

Mijn Actiebrief verteend, hij kangeen
zon verdragen;
't Is ruin voor kleiner tijdj als al
de Bubbelylagen.

---

**English translation:**

Knave/Servant

The paper so expensive
Dries out through
The ruin

My stock certificate is ruined, he cannot
bear the sun;
It is ruin for a shorter time as all
the bubble schemes.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Title at top: "Knegt" (Knave/Servant)
- Diamond symbol in upper right (representing the suit)
- Sun with radiating lines in background
- Engraved illustration showing a figure in period dress (17th century) holding documents
- Landscape with rolling hills in background
- This appears to be page 9 from "Pasquins Windkaart" playing cards, part of "Het Groote Tafereel" (The Great Theater), a Dutch satirical work
- Text references financial/economic themes, likely commentary on the Dutch economic bubble of the period
