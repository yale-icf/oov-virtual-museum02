# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Kneght

't Geluk brengt mij
Uit slavernij.

Mijn Aotiestar beeft mij van
knegt tot Heer gemaakt,
Schoon menig Heer daar door tot
breed' len is geraekt.

---

**English translation:**

Knight

Fortune brings me
Out of slavery.

My godfather has made me from
servant to Lord,
Although many a Lord has been
reduced to rags by it.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Title at top: "Kneght" (Knight)
- Club suit symbol (♣) in upper right corner
- This is identified as page 12 of 55 from Pasquins Windkaart playing cards from *Het Groote Tafereel*
- Engraved illustration in classical Dutch style with period clothing and maritime imagery (ship visible in background)
- Chain and shackles depicted at figure's feet
