# Transcription: The Egyptian Enterprise and Development Company Deferred Share Warrant

## English Text (Left side):

**DEFERRED SHARE WARRANT TO BEARER**

FOR 1 DEFERRED SHARE

No. 08527

This is to certify that the Bearer of this Warrant is entitled to One deferred Share in the above Company, numbered as above, subject to the Statutes of the Company, of which an extract is endorsed hereon.

Cairo, the 5th of March 1906

---

## French Text (Right side):

**CERTIFICAT DE PART DE DIVIDENDE AU PORTEUR**

POUR UNE PART DE DIVIDENDE

No. 08527

Le Porteur du présent certificat est propriétaire de Une part de dividende de la susdite Société, portant le numéro ci-dessus, aux clauses et conditions du Statut, dont extrait d'aura part.

Cairo 5 Mars 1906

---

## Header Text (Bilingual):

**DEFERRED SHARE WARRANT** (centered top)

**THE EGYPTIAN ENTERPRISE AND DEVELOPMENT COMPANY**

Empowered by Decree of the 26th November 1904 | Autorisées par Décret Khédivial du 26 Novembre 1904

Capital L.E. 160,000 | Capital L.E. 160.000
Increased to L.E. 400,000 by decision of the General Meeting of the Shareholders held on the 15th of December 1905 and divided into 40,000 ordinary shares of L.E. 10 each. | porté à L.E. 400,000 par décision de l'Assemblée Générale des Actionnaires du 15 Décembre 1905 et divisé en 40,000 Actions de L.E. 10 chacune.

Deux Administrateurs: [Two Administrators - signature line]
