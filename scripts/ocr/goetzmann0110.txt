# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Windbellen om Nul
te koop

itz Wind en Nul en anders niet,
Gelyk men klaar op't einde ziet,

**English translation:**

Wind Bells for Nothing
for sale

it's Wind and Nothing and nothing else,
As one clearly sees at the end,

**Notable markings:**

- Number "10" in top right corner (indicating card number)
- Heart symbol in top right corner
- Engraved illustration showing a figure in period clothing blowing through a horn/pipe, seated outdoors with vegetation and a bowl nearby
- Decorative border frame around entire composition
- This appears to be plate 32 from a series of satirical playing cards (Pasquins Windkaart from Het Groote Tafereel)
