**COLONIAL EXHIBITION
International
PARIS
1931

Emission authorized by the Law of 22 July 1927

LOTTERY BOND

SIXTY FRANCS
TO BEARER

Series 018 — No. 08682

This Bond, free of all taxes at the bearer's charge, entitles [the holder to] Twenty Entrance Tickets to the Exhibition, valued at 3 francs per ticket, and to the other benefits stated below.

It participates in 12 Drawings comprising 2,136 Lots, net of all taxes, totaling 24,264,000 francs.

Seen at the CRÉDIT FONCIER DE FRANCE:

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** Série 018, No. 08682
- **Three signatures** at bottom (Chief of Control, General Secretary, Head of Lands)
- **Decorative border** with red ornamental patterns and palm tree motifs
- **Printer mark:** "Imp. Paul Dupont (G) — 914.10.28"
- **Compound document:** Includes discount coupons for railways (1st and 2nd journey), maritime transport (outbound and return), and airplanes (outbound and return)
