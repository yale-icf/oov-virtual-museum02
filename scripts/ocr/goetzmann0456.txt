# Transcription of Massachusetts Financial Documents (1785-1786)

## UPPER DOCUMENT: Tax Certificate

**Treasurer's Office, Boston, April 1, 1786. (No. 1582)**

PURSUANT to a Law of this Commonwealth, I do hereby Certify that there is due to [handwritten: P. Seek] or Bearer, the Sum of [handwritten: Three Pounds] and this Certificate shall be received in Payment for one third of the Tax (No. 5) granted in *March 1786*, equal to the same Sum in Gold and Silver.

[Signature: handwritten] £9. 13. 0 [handwritten: Alex Hodgdon] Treasurer.

---

## LOWER DOCUMENT: State Bill

**FIVE DOLLARS**

**STATE of MASSACHUSETTS-BAY.**

No. [handwritten: 1541] Five Dollars.

THE Possessor of this BILL shall be paid Five Spanish milled Dollars by the Thirty-first Day of *December*, One Thousand Seven Hundred and Eighty-six, with Interest in like Money, at the Rate of *Five per Centum per Annum*, by the State of MASSACHUSETTS-BAY, according to an Act of the Legislature of the said State, of the Fifth Day of *May*, 1785.

Payable [unclear] 1786.
Annually, 1 60
Monthly, 0 12

[Signature: handwritten] A. Cranch

---

## Notable markings:

- **Upper document**: Handwritten entries; serial number 1582; ornamental border design (left margin)
- **Lower document**: Serial number 1541; ornamental border design throughout; hole punch (center); handwritten signature "A. Cranch"; notation of interest rates (annually and monthly)
- Both documents show age-related discoloration and wear
