# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Negotiatie "Concordia Res Parvae Crescunt".

REÇU N°. 54

Overgenomen van de Heer 4. [Martonokle?]

een à f 500.— Aandeelen in bovengenoemde Negotiatie, 
overeenkomstig het besluit, genomen in de Vergadering van 
Aandeelhouders, gehouden op 4 Decemter 1893.

Amsterdam,                                Voor de Associatiekas:
[4 Jan 1904]                             [Signature]
[Amount notation: 430.553]

---

**English translation:**

Negotiation "Concordia Res Parvae Crescunt"

RECEIPT No. 54

Taken over from the Gentleman [Martonokle?]

one [share certificate] at f 500.— Shares in the aforementioned Negotiation, 
in accordance with the resolution, taken at the Meeting of 
Shareholders, held on December 4, 1893.

Amsterdam,                                On behalf of the Associatiekas [Association Fund]:
[January 4, 1904]                        [Signature]
[Amount notation: 430.553]

---

**Notable markings:**

- Ornamental floral border frame around entire document
- Handwritten signatures and dates in period script
- Numerical notations: "430.553" (amount reference)
- Document appears to be a duplicate/retained copy of a share transfer receipt
- Formal Dutch financial document format typical of late 19th/early 20th-century Dutch investment societies
