# Transcription

**Title:** Grand Filhery

**Text at bottom:**

Well might this Bubble claim the Style of Grand,
Whilst they that rais'd the same could Shift by Land;
But now the Town does at the Project Pish,
They've nothing else to cry but Stinking Fish.

**Notable markings:**

- Two spade symbols in a box on the left side of the card
- This is a playing card from a satirical deck mocking the South Sea Bubble financial crisis
- Engraved illustration showing a large merchant ship with multiple masts and sails, accompanied by smaller rowing boats in the water
- The ship appears to be depicted in a style typical of 17th-18th century maritime engravings
