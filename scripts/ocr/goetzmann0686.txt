## English Translation:

**Header:**
KINGDOM OF YUGOSLAVIA

**Main Title:**
INTERNATIONAL GOLD LOAN 7% 1931 / INTERNATIONAL STABILIZATION LOAN IN GOLD 7% FROM 1931
OF STABILISATION

**Loan Details:**

The nominal amount of the Loan:
1,025,000,000 French francs = 40,158,525 United States dollars = 8,291,058-13-8 pounds sterling = 988,220,307 French francs of Switzerland = 99,907,324.41 Dutch florins = 149,850,827 Czechoslovak crowns = 7,355,404,000 Belgian francs = 288,817,209 Belgian francs = 2,280,141,510 stabilization dinars.

Represented by:
1,025,000 Bearer Bonds exclusively at bearer, of 1,000 French francs = 39,135 United States dollars = 8-1-6 pounds sterling = 964.99 Swiss francs = 97,565 florins = 146 Czechoslovak crowns = 7,172 Belgian francs = 223 stabilization dinars.

Divided into:
736,605 Single Bond Certificates / 27,380 Five-Bond Certificates
5,882 Ten-Bond Certificates / 3,701 Twenty-Five Bond Certificates

The Loan has been duly executed in accordance with the terms dated June 1, 1931.

---

**Bond Certificate Title:**

Bearer Bond Certificate / One Bearer Bond
OF 1,000 FRENCH FRANCS / OF 1,000 FRENCH FRANCS

Serial Number: **No. 237,381**

**Interest Terms:**

ANNUAL INTEREST: / Annual interest:
Payable semi-annually (on June 1 and December 1 of each year) at the rate of 35 French francs per Bond at the United States Bank, in PARIS, at the Bank of Belgium, in BRUSSELS, in BERLIN, at J.P. MORGAN & Co., in NEW-YORK, at the Bank of America N.A., and at the corresponding banks of the Bank of Belgium in AMSTERDAM, PRAGUE, STOCKHOLM, ZURICH, STOCKHOLM, and at the Nederlandsche Handel-Maatschappij N.V. at their respective successors.
