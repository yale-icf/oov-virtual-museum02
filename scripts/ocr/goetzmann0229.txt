# Russian Imperial Treasury Note Transcription

## Original (Russian):

**Main Document:**

КУПОНЬ БИЛЕТА ГОСУДАРСТВ. КАЗНАЧЕЙСТВА
(Four coupon boxes, left side)

Д-йствителень въ теченiе 10 лѣтъ отъ
[1-го февраля 1919 г.] / [1-го января 1918 г.] / [1-го августа 1918 г.] / [1-го февраля 1918 г.]
Серiя четвертая шестьдесять пятая
CDILXV (465)
№852814  1 РУБ.

(Four similar coupons numbered 8, 7, 6, 5 respectively, each marked "1 РУБ." and "4%")

**Center Section:**

БИЛЕТА
ГОСУДАРСТВЕННАГО КАЗНАЧЕЙСТВА
ВЪ
ПЯТЬДЕСЯТЬ РУБЛЕЙ

Действителен государственной
казначейской погашеньях самоѐ

Директоръ [signature] Бухгалтеръ

№352814

Билеть дѣйствителень по 1 Августа 1929 г.

**4%** (marked in circles at bottom corners)

---

## English Translation:

**Main Document:**

COUPON OF STATE TREASURY BILL
(Four coupon boxes, left side)

Valid for a period of 10 years from
[February 1, 1919 / January 1, 1918 / August 1, 1918 / February 1, 1918]
Fourth Series, Sixty-Fifth Issue
CDILXV (465)
No. 852814  1 RUBLE

(Four similar coupons numbered 8, 7, 6, 5 respectively, each marked "1 RUBLE" and "4%")

**Center Section:**

TREASURY BILL
OF THE STATE TREASURY
FOR
FIFTY RUBLES

Valid [by] State Treasury
[with] interest payments

Director [signature] Bookkeeper

No. 352814

Note valid until August 1, 1929

**4%** (marked in circles at bottom corners)

---

## Notable Markings:

- **Serial Number:** №352814
- **Denomination:** 50 Rubles (Пятьдесят Рублей)
- **Interest Rate:** 4% annual
- **Year of Issue:** 1915
- **Validity Period:** Until August 1, 1929
- **Four attached coupons:** Numbered 5, 6, 7, and 8, each worth 1 Ruble in interest
- **Official signatures:** Director and Bookkeeper (signatures in manuscript)
- **Decorative elements:** Double-headed imperial eagle, ornamental borders, floral motifs
- **Color:** Printed in green and dark brown/black ink
- **Imperial seal/eagle:** Displayed prominently at top center
