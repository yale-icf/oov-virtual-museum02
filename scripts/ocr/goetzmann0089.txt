# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Vrouw
Met Ikarus, en Facton,
Vergnoegelynum
man zijndediezon

Schoon't lot van Ikarus mijn man
valt tot een deel,
Ik hou nog moet, zo lang ik met
de klavers speel.

**English translation:**

Woman
With Icarus and Phaeton,
Pleasure[?]
man being the sun

Though the fate of Icarus [befalls] my man
in part,
I still have courage, as long as I [play]
with the clubs.

**Notable markings:**

- Club suit symbol (♣) in upper right corner
- Engraved illustration of a woman in elaborate 17th-century Dutch dress, holding a child and gesturing to a basket containing figures
- Classical mythological references (Icarus and Phaeton) in the text
- This appears to be page 11 of a 55-page playing card set called "Pasquins Windkaart" from "Het Groote Tafereel"
- The document is catalogued as the Queen of Clubs
