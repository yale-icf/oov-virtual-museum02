**English Translation:**

ANONYMOUS COMPANY

NATIONAL PETROLEUM COMPANY

ESTABLISHED BY PUBLIC DEED EXECUTED BEFORE THE NOTARY D. EMILIO FERNANDEZ SANCHEZ, DATED SEPTEMBER 29, 1924.

REGISTERED OFFICE: SAN SEBASTIAN - PLAZA DEL CENTENARIO, 5

SHARE CAPITAL
100,000,000 PESETAS

DIVIDED INTO 140,000 SHARES OF 500 PTAS. SERIES A.
AND 600,000 SHARES OF 50 PTAS. SERIES B. EACH.

This certifies that the bearer is the owner of one share of

FIVE HUNDRED PESETAS

No. 17341   SERIES A

SAN SEBASTIAN, May 25, 1928

ONE BOARD MEMBER                        ONE BOARD MEMBER

[Signatures - illegible]

500

---

**Notable markings:**

- Serial number: **17341**, Series A
- Date: **May 25, 1928**
- Decorative engraved border in green
- Central vignette depicting an oil derrick with industrial landscape and waterway
- Two signature lines for board members (signatures present but illegible)
- Ornamental oval seals in corners containing striped shield designs
- Document printed in green and black on cream-colored paper
