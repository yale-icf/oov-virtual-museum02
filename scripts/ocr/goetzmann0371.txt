## Notable Markings:

- **Red stamp**: Large characters overlaid on the note (appears to be 銀札 - "ginsatsu"/silver note)
- **Circular seal**: Present at bottom of note with radial text pattern
- **Decorative borders**: Cloud/geometric patterns at top and bottom of note
- **Deity figure**: Upper portion shows illustrated figure of Ebisu (god of fortune)
- **Overall condition**: Aged, creased paper with visible wear
