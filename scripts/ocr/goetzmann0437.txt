## English Translation:

**LEFT SIDE (SPANISH):**

ANONYMOUS COMPANY OF THE RAILWAYS
OF
SEVILLE–JEREZ–CADIZ

Authorized by Royal Decree of March 4, 1857.

THE BYLAWS WERE EXECUTED BEFORE THE NOTARY OF THE MADRID COLLEGE, D. LEON MUÑOZ.

Corporate headquarters in Madrid — Offices in Paris, Taitbout Street, 57.

Capital ; 266,000,000 reales vellón
DIVIDED INTO 140,000 SHARES OF 1,900 REALES Vª EACH.

DURATION OF THE COMPANY : 99 YEARS

No. 40,721

share of one thousand nine hundred reales
OR FIVE HUNDRED FRANCS

THE ADMINISTRATORS.
