# French Royal Tontine Certificate - Page 2 of 2

**Original:**

Enregistrée au Contrôle général des Finances par nous Chevalier, Conseiller du Roi en ses Conseils, Garde des Registres du Contrôle général des Finances, commis à cet effet.

A Paris, le vingt neuf jour de septembre mil sept cent soixante.

Dervoille

[Additional faint handwritten text and notations are visible throughout the document but are largely illegible in this scan]

---

**English translation:**

Registered at the General Control of Finances by us, Knight, Counselor of the King in his Councils, Keeper of the Registers of the General Control of Finances, commissioned for this purpose.

In Paris, the twenty-ninth day of September, one thousand seven hundred sixty.

Dervoille

---

**Notable markings:**

- Signature: "Dervoille" (handwritten in cursive)
- Various archival reference marks and notations in the margins
- Faint handwritten annotations throughout the document
- Document appears aged with water staining and foxing
