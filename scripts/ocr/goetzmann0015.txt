**English translation:**

...nor shall the bank enter into Banco credit for him if it is not possible, and the following day, and afterwards (300 pieces as it pleases him) the same may be disposed / 300 well 00z assignment good to himself, or another, to those who also have a Banco credit; also through payments of assignment on the spot or Derlage to those who have a Banco credit; and shall for the discharged, these parts deducted therefrom with two stivers, and through the monies to him or his old account paid, with one guilder for one thousand guilders. or two stivers for every hundred guilders.

6.

In the mornings, from eight to nine o'clock, shall every one, without let, be instructed, be notified of what has come on his account; but if delayed, there for three stivers must be given.
