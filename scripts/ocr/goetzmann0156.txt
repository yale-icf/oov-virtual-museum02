# Transcription

**SPLENDOUR.**

[Engraving of an ornately dressed figure labeled "Splendour" holding a fleur-de-lis staff, wearing elaborate Renaissance-style clothing and headdress]

Lottery will Finish NEXT TUESDAY, January 12, when
TWO £50,000, and all the other Capitals, Must be drawn.

---

**QUEEN.**

Avannt, ye gloomy fiends of spleen!
Vivat Regina! Live the Queen!
And let me, whilst I reign, impart
Felicity to every heart.

I give you welcome to my court,
To join in Fortune's golden sport,
At CARROLL'S Office, where you'll see
The Goddess hold her jubilee.

Go, taste the joy that there abounds—
The sweets of *Thirty Thousand Pounds*.
Your Queen invites—her voice obey—
NEXT TUESDAY is the levee day.

---
