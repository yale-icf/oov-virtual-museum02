# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

Zie hoe de zijfde stadt van Holland,
de Acties plet.
Waarom een lauwerkrans mij werd
op 't hoofd gezet.

**English translation:**

See how the fifth city of Holland,
the Shares crushes.
Why a laurel wreath was placed
upon my head.

**Notable markings:**

- This is page 17 of 55 from "Pasquins Windkaart" (Pasquin's Wind Chart) playing cards
- Identified as the Five of Spades
- Classical engraving style with fine line hatching
- Decorative border frame around the entire composition
- A coat of arms with crossed lines visible on the left side (on the container/bucket being held)
- The image shows a figure in 17th-century dress holding wind-related implements with cherubs above
