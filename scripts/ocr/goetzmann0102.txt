# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

*Top right:* 2♥ (Two of Hearts symbol)

*Center right:* Zij deynen op en onder

*Bottom text:*
Mijn harten hobb lende op't verdwij-
nend Bubbel water.
Zijnals al't Activwerk, zo vals
gelijk een Sater.

---

## English translation:

*Center right:* They sway up and down

*Bottom text:*
My hearts lend [themselves] to the vanishing
Bubble water.
They are like all the Active works, as false
as a Satyr.

---

## Notable markings:
- Playing card designation: Two of Hearts (2♥) in upper right corner
- Publisher's mark/signature in lower left corner (appears to be maker's initials)
- Horizontal line borders framing the entire composition
- Engraved/etched illustration style typical of 17th-century Dutch playing cards
