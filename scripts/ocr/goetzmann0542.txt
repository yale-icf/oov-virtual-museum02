# Transcription

## Original Text (French and Dutch):

DONNÉ À HAMM EN WESTPHALIE (sous nos sceaux ordinaires & tius le contrefacing du Maréchal Duc de Broglie, Ministre affEtat, le vingt cinquième jour de Septembre l'an de Grace Mille fait cent quatrevingt treize, & du Regne du Roi le premier.

LOUIS STANISLAS XAVIER,
CHARLES PHILIPPE.

(L. S.)

LE MARECHAL, DUC DE BROGLIE.

---

N° 34

Wy ondergestechtevene I. BOURCOURD en Weduwe E: CROEESE & COMP., als daar toe, gelyck in vorenstaande Obligatie breeder staat gemeld, geauthhoriseert zynde, bekennen voor rekening van hunne Koninglycke Hoogheden de Prineen van Vrankryk, ontfangen te hebben uit handen van

de Somma van EEN DUIZEND GULDENS Hollands Courant voor Aandeel in dezelve Obligatie, en zyn de Coupons hiertoe behorende geteekend

*Actum Amsterdam, den 28. September; 1793.*

ƒ 1000:--:

Deze recontrafiaerd en ten Prohocolle aangetekend, belopende de door my getekende Aandeelen niet boven het getal van Twaaif Duyzend Nummers. En zyn de *Lettres Patentes* dato 7 September 1793, benevens de-Originele Obligatie van dato 25 September 1793 gedeponeerd en/ berustenде onder my.

Par ordre de leur Altesses Royales.

Garde du Tréfor Royal.

---

## English Translation:

GIVEN AT HAMM IN WESTPHALIA (under our ordinary seals & under the countersigning of the Marshal Duke of Broglie, Minister of State, on the twenty-fifth day of September in the year of Grace One Thousand Seven Hundred Ninety-three, & of the Reign of the King the first.

LOUIS STANISLAS XAVIER,
CHARLES PHILIPPE.

(L. S.)

THE MARSHAL, DUKE OF BROGLIE.

---

N° 34

We undersigned I. BOURCOURD and Widow E: CROESE & COMP., as thereto authorized, as appears stated more fully in the foregoing Obligation, hereby acknowledge receipt for the account of Their Royal Highnesses the Princes of France, from the hands of

the Sum of ONE THOUSAND GUILDERS Dutch Currency for a share in the said Obligation, and the Coupons belonging hereto are signed

*Done in Amsterdam, the 28th of September; 1793.*

ƒ 1000:--:

This is re-authenticated and registered in the Protocol, the number of Shares signed by me not exceeding the total of Twelve Thousand Numbers. And the *Letters Patent* dated 7 September 1793, together with the Original Obligation dated 25 September 1793 are deposited and rest in my custody.

By order of Their Royal Highnesses.

Keeper of the Royal Treasury.

---

## Notable Markings:

- Document number: **N° 34**
- Denomination: **ƒ 1000:--:** (1,000 Dutch Guilders)
- Date: **Amsterdam, 28 September 1793**
- Multiple handwritten signatures and notarial marks
- Circular library stamp (lower left corner)
- Handwritten annotations and endorsements throughout the document
- Official seal impression notation (L. S.) indicating "Locus Sigilli" (place of seal)
