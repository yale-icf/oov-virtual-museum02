# Transcription

**Original (Dutch):**

eenryke srek
Is dubbell gek

3 ♥

Hoe hoog mijn kapitaal door de
Achies is gerezeen ,
't Ben door mijn gierigheid niet
rijker als voor dezen .

---

**English translation:**

A rich streak
Is double madness

3 ♥

However much my capital has risen
Through stocks,
I am not richer through my greed
than before.

---

**Notable markings:**

- Heart symbol (♥) in upper right corner
- Playing card designation: "3" (Three of Hearts)
- Engraving-style illustration of a figure in period dress (approximately 17th century Dutch clothing) holding a staff/cane and what appears to be a document
- Hatched cross-hatching technique typical of woodcut or engraved playing cards
- Simple rectangular border frame around the entire image
- Text appears to be printed in period typeface/script
