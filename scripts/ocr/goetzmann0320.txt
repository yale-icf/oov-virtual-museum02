# Transcription of Chinese Government Loan Bond

## Main Text Transcription:

**The Chinese Government Loan**

of £ 6,866,046.10/10

bearing interest at the rate of £ 8.-/- per cent per annum,

being in substitution for and a consolidation of the unpaid portion of the following duly authorized Chinese Government Loans: Loan for £ 300,000.- dated the 1st day of March 1912, Loan for £ 1,200,000.- dated the 16th day of January 1912, Loan for £ 2,000,000.- dated the 27th day of April 1912, Loan for £ 500,000.- dated the 27th May 1912 Loan for £ 500,000.- dated the 8th day of June 1912.

Authorization of this Loan has been officially communicated by the Wai Chiao Pu to the Ministers in Peking for France and Italy.

**Notable Markings:**

- **Serial Numbers:** No. 0005102 (appears twice, top and bottom of bond number section)
- **Signature/Seal:** Chinese characters "李思浩" (Li Sihao) as Minister of Finance, with red official seal (approximately 1.5" x 1.5")
- **Decorative Border:** Ornate purple and green Art Nouveau-style border with geometric patterns
- **Top Right:** Orange/red decorative stamp with Chinese characters and dragon motif
- **Large Red Stamp/Overlay:** A large red chop or validation stamp overlays much of the central text (making portions illegible)
- **Currency Denomination:** "10" appears in circular medallions at top and bottom left and right corners
- **Color Scheme:** Purple borders, cream/beige background, blue and green underprinting
