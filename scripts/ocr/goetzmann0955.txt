**TO**
**THE PRESIDENT AND DIRECTORS**
**OF THE**
**MISSISSIPPI UNION BANK.**

Do hereby designate that *[House] of the Bank of the United States*
in the city of *London*

as the place of payment of the within Bond and Interest, and hereby
assign and transfer the same for value received as *[Sworn King, Esqr]*
*[For his $d in $* *Barbadoes,]* and guarantee the payment
of the same at the place designated.

*Stevens* [signature] *H.S Runnels* [signature]
